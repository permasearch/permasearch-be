The university endeavors to become a "Top 20" public institution, undergoing a process of enhancing its graduate programs while continuing to emphasize the quality of the undergraduate experience. It has steadily moved up the rankings for public universities from 34,[33] to 30,[34] to 27,[35] to 22[36] in 2005, 2006, 2007, and 2008 respectively: according to the U.S. News & World Report. In 2012, U.S. News & World Report ranked Clemson as the 25th top public school in the US,[37] and in 2015 the ranking improved to a tie for 21st. As of 2016, Clemson is ranked 23rd.[38][39]
