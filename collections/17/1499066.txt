Social exchange theory is not one theory but a frame of reference within which many theories can speak to another, whether in argument or mutual support.[4] All these theories are built upon several assumptions about human nature and the nature of relationships. Thibaut and Kelley have based their theory on two conceptualizations: one that focuses on the nature of individuals and one that describes the relationships between two people.
