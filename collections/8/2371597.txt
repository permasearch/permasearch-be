The Z4 M Coupe/Roadster was introduced in 2006 and is powered by the S54 straight-six engine from the E46 M3. The S54 was also on the Ward’s 10 Best Engines list for 2001 through 2004.[32] North American Z4 M models produce 330 bhp (246 kW), 3 hp less than the North American M3.[33] In other markets, the power output is the same 252 kW (338 bhp) as the M3.
