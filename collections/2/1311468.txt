In April 2012, Nugent pleaded guilty to illegally killing and transporting an American black bear in Alaska.[90]  His sentence included two years of probation, a prohibition on hunting and fishing in Alaska and on any U.S. Forest Service lands for one year, and a fine of $10,000, and he was required to produce and broadcast at his own expense a 30-60 second Public Service Announcement (PSA) on the responsibilities of hunters.[91][92]
