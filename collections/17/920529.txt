The library of the institute functions in a separate block, where you can make use of one of the best collections of books and journals. It has more than 8000 volumes monographs and 80 printed journals. The institute has subscribed to e-journals and has access to over 5000 international journals in the areas of management EBSCO and computer science IEEE. The library is fully automated and you can search the holdings by using the OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue). The library also provides Digital Library (Greenstone) and Reprographic Services.
