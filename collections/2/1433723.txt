Kabul (Pashto/Dari: کابل‎‎, Kâbol, pronounced [ˈkɒːbul]; English: /ˈkɑːbʊl/) is the capital of Afghanistan as well as its largest city, located in the eastern section of the country. According to latest estimates, the population of the city is about 4.6 million,[1] which includes all the major ethnic groups.[2] Rapid urbanization had made Kabul the world's 64th largest city[3] and the fifth fastest-growing city in the world.[4]
