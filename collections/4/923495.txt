On July 13, 2013, Toyota, with Nickelodeon, unveiled a SpongeBob-inspired Toyota Highlander.[352] The 2014 Toyota Highlander was launched on SpongeBob Day at the San Diego's Giants v. Padres game.[353][354][355] The SpongeBob Toyota Highlander visited seven U.S. locations during its release, including the Nickelodeon Suites Resort Orlando in Florida.[356]
