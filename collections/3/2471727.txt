"Special Lady" is a 1979 single by vocal trio Ray, Goodman & Brown, formerly known as The Moments. In the U.S., it was a number one R&B hit and reached number five on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1980.[1] The single marked their first release under the name Ray, Goodman & Brown.  The song was written by Harry Ray, Al Goodman and Lee Walter and produced by Vincent Castellano.[2]
