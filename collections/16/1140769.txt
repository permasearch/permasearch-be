Turner was born on September 27, 1954 in Houston, Texas, as the sixth of nine children, and was raised in the Acres Homes community in northwest Houston by his father, a commercial painter, and his mother, a maid at the Rice Hotel.[6] Turner was elected senior class president by his peers and awarded valedictorian of his graduating class from Klein High School.[7] At the University of Houston he was Speaker of the Student Senate and graduated Magna Cum Laude with a B.A. degree in Political Science. In addition, Turner achieved a finalist position in the Ames Moot Court Competition while obtaining a J.D. degree from Harvard Law School. Turner is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity.[8]
