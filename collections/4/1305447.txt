Golda Meir and the Israeli Defense Committee secretly authorized the Mossad to track down and kill those allegedly responsible for the Munich massacre.[74] The accusation that this was motivated by a desire for vengeance was disputed by Zvi Zamir, who described the mission as "putting an end to the type of terror that was perpetrated" (in Europe).[75] To this end the Mossad set up a number of special teams to locate and kill these fedayeen, aided by the agency's stations in Europe.[76]
