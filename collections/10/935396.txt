In December 2011, Deadline.com reported that Netflix and Gaumont International Television were finalizing a deal for a 13-episode series order for Hemlock Grove.[14] The project was officially announced in March 2012.[15] Eli Roth serves as an executive producer and will direct the pilot, Jellyfish In the Sky.[14][16] Deran Sarafian, who signed on in mid-March as an executive producer/series director, directed six episodes.[17][18]
