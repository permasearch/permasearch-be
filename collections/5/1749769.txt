Burr was a well-known philanthropist.[3]:149[89] He gave enormous sums of money, including his salaries from the Perry Mason movies, to charity. He was also known for sharing his wealth with friends. He sponsored 26 foster children through the Foster Parents' Plan or Save The Children, many with the greatest medical needs.[8] He also gave money and some of his Perry Mason scripts to the McGeorge School of Law in Sacramento, California.[90]
