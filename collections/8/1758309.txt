During the Bears' 2010 season, the team wore a #99 sticker on the back of their helmets to mourn the death of Gaines Adams,[10] as well as wearing a #34 patch in 1999 to honor Walter Payton, who died that year.[11] In their 1991 year, the team wore a #91 patch in honor of rookie Fred Washington, who died in a car accident, along with a #50 patch in 1992, in which they paid tribute to Mike Singletary, who retired that year. In 2001, the Bears wore a "Salute to Soldier Field" patch in honor of their stadium Soldier Field, which began its reconstruction that year.
