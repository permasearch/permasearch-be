On March 23, 2018, MLB announced that Wright would be suspended for 15 games for violating the league's personal conduct policy.[10] The suspension took effect on April 28, when Wright completed a rehabilitation assignment with Triple-A Pawtucket.[11] On May 14, Wright was activated by Boston, his suspension having been completed.[12][13] After 10 appearances (including four starts), he was placed on the disabled list with a left knee injury on June 26;[14] he received a PRP injection in the knee on July 2.[15]
