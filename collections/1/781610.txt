In late December, Scott Hoying stated that for 2015, "Pentatonix is transitioning towards original music."[77] A release of a full-length album consisting of only original material is planned.[78][79] Hoying also stated, "We're at the point in our careers now [where] we must go to original music, and we want to go to original music; we have so much to say, and I think it's gonna be quite a journey."[80]
