Ready Player One was initially scheduled to be released on December 15, 2017,[53] but was pushed back to March 30, 2018, to avoid competition with Star Wars: The Last Jedi.[54] In January 2018, it was announced the film's release date had been moved up one day to March 29, 2018.[55] The film had its world premiere at the Paramount Theatre in Austin, Texas on March 11, 2018 (as part of the South by Southwest Film Festival).[4]
