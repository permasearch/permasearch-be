Leah Marie Remini (/ˈliːə ˈrɛmɪni/; born June 15, 1970) is an American actress, producer, and anti-Scientology activist. She is known for her roles as Carrie Heffernan on the comedy series The King of Queens (1998–2007) and Lara in the film Old School, as well as co-hosting The Talk in 2010–11. She is currently involved in and hosting the Emmy Award-winning documentary series "Leah Remini: Scientology and the Aftermath".
