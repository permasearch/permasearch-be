"Into the Mystic" has featured in several movies beginning in 1971 with Sweets McGee and two movies in 1989: Dream a Little Dream and Immediate Family.[12] It was one of the songs played in the 1998 movie Patch Adams and again in the 1999 movie Kate's Addiction.  It was played as Jim and Michelle's first dance as a married couple in the 2003 movie American Wedding, along with a cover version of the song by The Wallflowers.[12] The song most recently can be heard on the soundtrack of the HBO television show The Newsroom in the 2013 Season 2 premiere.
