Chiwetel Umeadi Ejiofor,[3] CBE (/ˈtʃuːɪtɛl ˈɛdʒi.oʊfɔːr/ CHOO-i-tel EJ-ee-oh-for; born 10 July 1977)[3][4] is a British actor.[5] After enrolling at the National Youth Theatre in 1995, and gaining a scholarship to the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, at age 19 and three months into his course, Ejiofor was cast by Steven Spielberg to play a supporting role in the film Amistad (1997) as James Covey.[6]
