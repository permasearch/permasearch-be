"Run Joey Run" was released in the late summer of 1975, by October the song had peaked at #4 on the Billboard Hot 100. It would be Geddes' only Top 10 hit; his only other hit, "Last Game of the Season (Blind Man in the Bleachers)" would peak at #18 in Billboard (December 27, 1975)[3] and #23 in Cashbox (December 6, 1975).[4]
