The Eh Bee Family is known for its social media accounts, including Vine, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. They have also starred in sponsored advertisements for such companies as Johnson & Johnson, Toyota, and Regal Cinemas and have also been featured in the New York Daily News,[5] on Buzzfeed,[6] Good Morning America,[7] Today.com,[8] and other media outlets. The family's social media was nominated for a Streamy Award in 2015[9] and a Shorty Award in 2016.[10] On February 17, 2018, Gabriela Bee[11] released her song "Sound in Color". On September 1st, 2018,Gabriela Bee[12] released another song, "Something More".
