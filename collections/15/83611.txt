Zabriskie was born in New Orleans, Louisiana, the daughter of Marion Grace (Zabriskie) of Wyckoff, New Jersey, and Roger Thomas "Tom" Caplinger.[2] Her mother had distant Polish ancestry,[3] and was a relative of James Zabriskie, a 19th-century railroad industrialist and early pioneer of California.[4] Zabriskie has said that her family was visited by Tennessee Williams, Gore Vidal and Truman Capote during her childhood.[5] Her father owned the famous French quarter gay bar, Cafe Lafitte in Exile.[1]
