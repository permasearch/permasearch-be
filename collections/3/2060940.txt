"The Correct Way to Kill" is the ninth episode of the fifth series of the 1960s cult British spy-fi television series The Avengers, starring Patrick Macnee and Diana Rigg, and guest starring Anna Quayle, Michael Gough, Philip Madoc, and Terence Alexander.[1] It was first aired on ABC on 11 March 1967. The episode was directed by Charles Crichton, and written by Brian Clemens.
