Alumni have collectively won at least 25 Emmy Awards: Jon Else (BA 1968) for cinematography; Andrew Schneider (BA 1973) for screenwriting; Linda Schacht (BA 1966, MA 1981), two for broadcast journalism;[303][304] Christine Chen (dual BA's 1990), two for broadcast journalism;[305] Kristen Sze (BA), two for broadcast journalism;[306] Kathy Baker (BA 1977), three for acting; Ken Milnes (BS 1977), four for broadcasting technology; and Leroy Sievers (BA),[307] twelve for production.
