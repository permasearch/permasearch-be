The choral refrain and title's namesake is used in the refrain of Amon Amarth's "Tattered Banners and Bloody Flags" with the accompanying variation or the melody. "The Earth moves under our feet the sky comes tumbling down (...) trembles to its knees". The entire chorus is aso a melodic reinterpretation of the original's chorus. The melody is reused in the same band's track "For Victory or Death".[citation needed]
