In 2010 Lorde and McDonald performed covers live on a regular basis as a duet called "Ella & Louis", playing at The Leigh Sawmill Cafe on 15 August, at Roasted Addiqtion Cafe in Kingsland on 20 August, at The Vic Unplugged at Victoria Theatre, Devonport on 27 October, and at Devonstock in Devonport on 12 December.[20] While working on her music career, she attended Takapuna Grammar School from 2010 to 2013, completing Year twelve.[21] She later chose not to return in 2014 to finish Year thirteen.[22]
