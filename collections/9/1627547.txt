Dr. Misty Calhoun is invited to consult on a new, top secret project run by pharmaceutical billionaire Carl Durant. She believes the project, performed at a remote, sea-based facility, focuses on extracting shark antibodies to help work toward cures for human diseases. However, Dr. Calhoun is shocked to learn that the company is using unpredictable bull sharks as its test subjects, and Durant has bio-engineered a pack of highly intelligent, super-aggressive bull sharks.
