In March 2015, Johnson & Johnson announced a deal for Jennings to appear in Clean & Clear commercials.[26] Jennings became a spokesmodel for Clean & Clear's "See The Real Me" digital campaign and shared "the trials of growing up transgender." She also modeled for the NOH8 Campaign.[4] She also authored a piece for Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People List, writing the entry for Laverne Cox.[27]
