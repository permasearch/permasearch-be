Christian Dior signed Kunis in 2012 to be the face of its Spring fashion campaign.[158][159] In February 2013, she was named Gemfields global brand ambassador and the face of their advertising campaign.[160] Gemfields is a luxury company that produces emeralds, rubies, and amethysts. She visited Gemfields' mine in Zambia.[161] Kunis appeared wearing Gemfields's Rubies for the world premiere of Jupiter Ascending.[162]
