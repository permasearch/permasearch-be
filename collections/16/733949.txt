In late 2013, McAdams starred in Richard Curtis' romantic comedy-drama About Time opposite Domhnall Gleeson.[163][163] Zooey Deschanel was originally slated to play McAdams's role but dropped out shortly before filming began.[164] A fan of Curtis for years, McAdams wanted to work with him on what he stated would be his last project as a director.[165] The film was a commercial success at the international box office,[166] and McAdams had a positive reception among critics, with Leslie Felperin of Variety praising her and Gleeson for their "radiant, believable chemistry" which "keeps the film aloft."[167]
