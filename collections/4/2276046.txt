However Simmons had a big success in The Big Country  (1958), directed by William Wyler. She did Home Before Dark (1959) at Warners and This Earth Is Mine (1959) with Rock Hudson at Universal. In the opinion of film critic Philip French, Home Before Dark (1958) was "perhaps her finest performance as a housewife driven into a breakdown in Mervyn LeRoy's psychodrama".[23]
