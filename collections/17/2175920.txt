Maattrraan had a good opening at the domestic box office, collecting ₹19.15 crore (US$2.9 million) nett in Tamil Nadu,[50] with ₹2.27 crore (US$350,000) in Chennai alone in its opening weekend.[51] The film also collected ₹2.86 crore (US$440,000) in Kerala in the opening weekend.[50] The film stayed in the first position for three consecutive weeks in Chennai but was later overtaken by horror film Pizza at the box office.[52] The film was declared as an 'Above average' by Behindwoods. The film is reported to have completed a 50-day run in a few theatres across Tamil Nadu.[53][54]
