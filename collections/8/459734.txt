On March 26, 2013, ABC Family announced that a spin-off, Ravenswood, would air in October 2013. The spin-off show aired 10 episodes before being canceled. On June 10, 2014, Pretty Little Liars was renewed for its sixth and seventh seasons. On August 29, 2016, it was confirmed that the series will be ending with Season 7 in 2017.[2] Filming of the series officially wrapped on October 26, 2016.[3]
