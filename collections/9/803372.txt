A 2015 Gallup (company) poll found that 32% of Americans agreed that animals should have the ”same rights as people”, up from 25% in 2008. 54% were Somewhat or Very Concerned about animals raised for food and 67% about animals in research.[65] A 2014 Pew Research Center poll found that 50% of respondents oppose animal testing, up from 43% in 2009.[66]
