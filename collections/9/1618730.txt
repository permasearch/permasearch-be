As of the 2017 national census, Liberia was home to 4,694,608 people.[101] Of those, 1,118,241 lived in Montserrado County, the most populous county in the country and home to the capital of Monrovia. The Greater Monrovia District has 970,824 residents.[102] Nimba County is the next most populous county, with 462,026 residents.[102] As revealed in the 2008 census, Monrovia is more than four times more populous than all the county capitals combined.[48]
