LG and Google announced the Android Wear-based smartwatch, the LG G Watch, that was in June 2014.[32] In August 2014, the LG G Watch R that has a circular face (similar to the Moto 360) was released.[33] The LG Watch Urbane that LG's third Android Wear-based smart watch has released in April 2015. This is the first device to support new features such as Wi-Fi, and new parts of Android Wear's software interface, like the ability to draw emoji to friends.[34]
