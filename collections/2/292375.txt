"Bugatti" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Ace Hood. It was released on January 29, 2013, as the first single from his fourth studio album, Trials & Tribulations.[1] The song, produced by Mike WiLL Made It, and features guest appearances from fellow American rappers Future and Rick Ross.[2] The song peaked at number 33 on the Billboard Hot 100, making it Ace Hood's most successful single of his career. The single has sold over one million copies and was certified platinum by the RIAA.[3][4]
