In the Japanese version, Ira is voiced by Yuichiro Umehara, Invi is voiced by Kana Hanazawa, Aced is voiced by Subaru Kimura, Gula is voiced by Kaito Ishikawa, and Ava is voiced by Yume Miyamato.
