After the Single Audit is concluded, the recipient prepares two documents: a “Data Collection Form and a “Reporting Package”. The data collection form, Form SF-SAC, is a standard form which is basically a summary of the Single Audit. It includes details of the auditor, a list of the federal programs audited, and a summary of any audit findings reported by the auditor.
