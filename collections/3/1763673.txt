There are no taxes on coffee exports  from Brazil, but importing green and roasted coffee into the country is taxed by 10% and soluble coffee by 16%.[65] Unprocessed coffee can be exported duty-free into the three largest markets: the United States, the European Union and Japan,[66] but processed coffee such as roasted beans, instant coffee and decaffeinated coffee is taxed 7.5% into the EU and 10% into Japan. Exports to the United States are tariff-free.[66]
