Grouchy typing lady: Principal's secretary who works at the front office. Junie B. often fears her because she is "grouchy", though she is shown to be considerate to Junie B. at times, such as in Junie B. Jones is Not a Crook, where she is proud of Junie B. for returning the colorful pen she found to the Lost and Found box. Junie B.’s response to her telling her to sit down is often “Yeah, only I’m not even bad” or “Yeah, only that’s the bad kid’s chair. And I’m not even bad.”
