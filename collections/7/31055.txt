Wood was married and widowed twice. Her first husband (poet/writer John V.A. Weaver) died at the age of 44. She gave birth to their son (David Weaver) in 1927 at the age of 32. Her second husband (in 1946 William H. Walling) was an executive in the printing business who died in 1973. They were married for 27 years.[3] She was a devout Episcopalian and a member of the Episcopal Actors Guild.[4]
