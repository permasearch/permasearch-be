On 23 October 2013, Dornan was cast as Christian Grey[20] in the film adaptation of Fifty Shades of Grey, replacing Charlie Hunnam.[21] Initially announced to be released on 1 August 2014,[22] the film was later rescheduled and released on 13 February 2015.[23] On 27 August 2014, it was announced that Dornan would play Dr. Allan Pascal in a Miramax film titled The 9th Life of Louis Drax, a supernatural thriller based on a book of the same name. It was released on 2 September 2016.[24] In November 2014, Dornan was #3 in People Magazine's Sexiest Man Alive rankings.[25]
