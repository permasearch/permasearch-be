Part three of the four-part comic adaptation of The Lost World: Jurassic Park, published by Topps Comics in July 1997, confirmed to readers that a cartoon series based on the film was in development.[133][134] In November 1997, it was reported that the cartoon would be accompanied by Jurassic Park: Chaos Effect, a series of dinosaur toys produced by Kenner and based on a premise that scientists had created dinosaur hybrids consisting of DNA from different creatures.[135][136] The new toys were based on the upcoming cartoon.[135] That month, it was also reported that the cartoon could be ready by March 1998, as a mid-season replacement.[135] The Chaos Effect toyline was released in June 1998,[136] but the animated series was never produced, for unknown reasons.[137]
