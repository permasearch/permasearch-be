The Institute runs a Boys and Girls hostel in the Campus under the aegis of Ekasila Education Society, which accommodates 750 students.Hostels are also provided with Guest Room facilities for the stay of Parents. A separate room is provided for watching Television and for reading News papers. Television is connected with Tata Sky and boarders of each room are at their disposal a Hindu daily news paper. Boarders are provided with Mineral Water for drinking purpose. An ambulance along with a driver is at their disposal during the night times for use at the time of any emergency. Other Facilities available in the Hostel: 24 x 7 Internet Facility, Gym & Indoor Games Facility in the Mornings and Evenings.
