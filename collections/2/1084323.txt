The sixteenth series began on 4 October 2015. This is the first series to be included under the new contract which was confirmed on 3 June 2015, renewing the show for a further six series.[19] Ahead of the series, it was announced that cast member Gemma Collins had quit the show. She had appeared on the show since the second series.[20] For the Essexmas special, Gemma Collins, Mario Falcone and Lauren Goodger each made a brief return to the show. This was the last series to feature Nanny Pat who died on 16 December 2015, and Lauren Pope who made her exit during the series.
