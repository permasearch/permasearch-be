As of 2009, India envisages to increase the contribution of nuclear power to overall electricity generation capacity from 2.8% to 9% within 25 years.[95]  By 2020, India's installed nuclear power generation capacity is expected to increase to 20 GW.[96]   As of  2009[update], India stands 9th in the world in terms of number of operational nuclear power reactors.  Indigenous atomic reactors include TAPS-3, and -4, both of which are 540 MW reactors.[97]
