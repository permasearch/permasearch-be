The Cincinnati Public School (CPS) district includes 16 high schools accepting students on a citywide basis. CPS is the third-largest Ohio school district by student population, and the largest one to garner an overall 'effective' rating from the state.[124] The district includes public Montessori schools, including the first public Montessori high school established in the United States, Clark Montessori.[125] Cincinnati Public Schools' top-rated school is Walnut Hills High School, ranked 34th on the national list of best public schools by Newsweek.
