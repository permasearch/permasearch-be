Gibson's five Wimbledon trophies are displayed at the Smithsonian Institution's National Museum of American History.[94] The Althea Gibson Cup seniors tournament is held annually in Croatia, under the auspices of the International Tennis Federation (ITF).[95] The Althea Gibson Foundation identifies and supports gifted golf and tennis players who live in urban environments.[96] In 2005 Gibson's friend Bill Cosby endowed the Althea Gibson Scholarship at her alma mater, Florida A&M University.[97]
