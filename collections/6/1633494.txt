"When It Rains It Pours" first entered the Billboard's Country Airplay on chart date of July 1, 2017 at No. 56,[3] and Hot Country Songs at No. 43 the same week.[4] The song was certified Gold by the RIAA on October 23, 2017,[5] and has sold 369,000 copies in the United States as of February 2018.[6]
