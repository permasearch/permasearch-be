"Weird Al" Yankovic parodied the song as "Handy" for his 2014 album Mandatory Fun. The parody's music video was released on 17 July 2014 through Yahoo! Screen's "Sketchy" channel.[79] On 7 July 2014, host Jimmy Fallon covered the song in the manner and appearance of Neil Young on The Tonight Show, backed up by the real Crosby, Stills and Nash.[80] On 2 September 2014, he did an impression of the track while singing "Old McDonald Had a Farm" as a part of The Tonight Show's "Musical Impressions Generator" segment.[81]
