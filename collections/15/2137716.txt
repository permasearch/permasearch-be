In 2010, coinciding with the second IS line refresh, the revised diesel IS 220d was detuned for improved fuel consumption figures but lowered power output by 27 bhp (20 kW; 27 PS).[34] Building on its "F Sport" line of parts and accessories for the IS 250/350,[35][36] Lexus added factory-produced F Sport IS models in 2010.[34] The second refresh also includes further interior updates for the IS line.
