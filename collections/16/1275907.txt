Federer has won the most grand slam tournament titles for a male player (19). He has reached the most finals (29), semifinals (42) and quarterfinals (51). He has participated in the highest number of majors (71), and the most consecutively (65). He has won the most matches at these tournaments (325). He is the only man to reach each final at least five times. He is the only man to win three tournaments five times each. He is the only man to win two of these tournaments five times consecutively. He is one of eight men to win all four majors.
