A new group called "White Flags", made up of former ISIL members and Kurdish mafia groups, pushed out of Tuz Khurmatu, rose in Saladin after the ouster of Peshmerga controlling the region, and started carrying out attacks in the region.[188] An operation was launched on 7 February to hunt down the remnants of ISIL, as well as members of Ansar al-Sunna and White Flags.[189]
