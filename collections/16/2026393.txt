On May 8, 2013, Universal Orlando announced the expansion of Wizarding World of Harry Potter, Diagon Alley, along with the Hogwarts Express attraction, to be located on the former site of the Jaws attraction.[8][9][10] By the end of August 2013, all six passenger cars and both tenders had been spotted in the backstage area, waiting to be assembled on the track.[11] The first of the two trains was installed on the track on October 24, 2013.[12] By the beginning of December 2013, the second train had been placed on the track.[13] In January, the resort previewed the interior of the Hogwarts Express through a live cast.[14][15] On December 2, 2013, Orlando Attractions Magazine, an amusement park website, spotted one of the trains being tested for the first time.[16] In mid-March 2014, Universal Orlando Resort released further information about the ride, including concept animations.[17] On June 24, 2014, the resort announced that the Diagon Alley expansion, including the Hogwarts Express, would officially open to the public on July 8, 2014.[18] A week later, Universal Orlando soft-opened the Hogwarts Express, without any announcement.[19][20] Within a month of the Hogwarts Express' opening, one million riders had ridden the attraction.[21] It had taken approximately two and a half years to develop.[22]
