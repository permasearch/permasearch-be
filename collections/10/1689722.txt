In Crazy Antics they are shown to be religious, as they were saying grace before Sniffles' tongue burst into the dining room. Ironically, soon after, they torture Sniffles to death. They appeared twice in each the first and second internet season, and the TV series. However, they were completely absent from internet season 3 and have yet to appear in the current season. They're all voiced by Misha Klein, Jessica Teach, Jennifer Hansen, Sarah Castelblanco, Warren Graff, Kenn Navarro, and Ken Pontac.
