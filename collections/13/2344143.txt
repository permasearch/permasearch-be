In a June 2009 interview with Digital Spy, series producer Cameron Welsh said a few new cast members would arrive during the coming weeks.[1] The following month, it was announced that the four-strong Walker family would be introduced to Home and Away. The family consists of father Sid (Robert Mammone), mother Jody (Victoria Haralabidou), son Dexter (Tom Green) and daughter Indigo or "Indi".[2] Former Out of the Blue actress Samara Weaving was cast as Indigo.[3] She initially appeared for five weeks in a recurring capacity.[4] The Walker family departed at the conclusion of their storyline.[5]
