Macquarie Ice Rink is an ice rink and opened in 1981 and is home to Sydney Bears and Sydney Ice Dogs ice hockey teams. The ice rink is used for ice skating, ice dance, speed skating and ice hockey. It is the only facility in Sydney to provide a full size Olympic rink (60m x 30m) with seating for approximately 2000 people.[4] Macquarie Ice Rink was used for the 1991 World short track speed skating Championships.[5]
