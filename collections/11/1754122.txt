"Cherry Bomb" is a 1976 punk-influenced hard rock single by the all-girl band the Runaways from their self-titled debut album, and is often regarded as the band's signature song.[1] "Cherry Bomb" was also ranked 52nd on VH1's 100 Greatest Hard Rock Songs.[2] It peaked at number 106 on the Billboard Bubbling Under the Hot 100 chart in the U.S.
