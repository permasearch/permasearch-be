The fictional Haxby Park, the estate Sir Richard Carlisle intends to buy in Series 2, is part of Waddesdon Manor in Buckinghamshire.[18] Byfleet Manor in Surrey is the location for the Dower house, home to Violet, Dowager Countess of Grantham,[19] while West Wycombe Park in Buckinghamshire is used for the interior scenes of Lady Rosamund (Samantha Bond)'s London residence in Belgrave Square.[20] A house in Belgrave Square is used for exterior shots.[21]
