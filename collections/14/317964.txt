The film received mixed to positive reviews from critics. Review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes reports that 61% of 57 film critics have given it a positive review, with a rating average of 5.7 out of 10. The site's consensus states, "Pretty Woman may be a yuppie fantasy, but the film's slick comedy, soundtrack, and casting can overcome misgivings."[22] Metacritic, which assigns a weighted average score out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, gives it a score of 51 based on 17 reviews, indicating "mixed or average reviews."[23]
