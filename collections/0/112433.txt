In 2004, "Papa's Got a Brand New Bag" was ranked number 72 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 greatest songs of all time. (In 2010 the magazine updated its list, and the song was moved up a rank to number 71.)[8] The song is currently ranked as the 80th greatest song of all time, as well as the sixth best song of 1965, by Acclaimed Music.[9]
