Dangal (2016), following its Chinese release on 5 May 2017,[12] became the highest-grossing Indian film and fifth highest-grossing non-English language film of all time, crossing ₹2,000 crore (US$290 million) worldwide, making it the first Indian film to gross $300 million worldwide and one of the top 30 highest-grossing 2016 films (surpassing the $299.5 million gross of Alice Through the Looking Glass, starring Johnny Depp and Mia Wasikowska).[13] Dangal is also the highest-grossing sports film of 2017, and Disney's fourth highest-grossing film of 2017.[14]
