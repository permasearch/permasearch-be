The E46 GTR came to life in February 2001, powered by the P60B40 a 3,997 cc V8 producing 330 kW (449 PS; 443 hp).[32][33] Unlike the straight-six powered M3 versions, which were outpaced by the Porsche 996 GT3, the racing version of the E46 M3 GTR, entered by Schnitzer Motorsport, was very successful in the American Le Mans Series (ALMS) with BMW factory driver Jörg Müller securing the 2001 GT title.
