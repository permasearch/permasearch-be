People with this surname include: [Bruce Barkley Downs Sr.] (born April 17,1930) (died May 21,1983). Traffic Operations for Florida Department of Transportation for 29 years. Assistant County Administrator for Hillborough County from 1979 until his sudden death. There is a large highway named after him which runs beside USF. He holds the honor of being selected "A Great Floridian", chosen by Governor Jeb Bush in 2000.
