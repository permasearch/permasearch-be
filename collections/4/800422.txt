Freeland issued a statement via Twitter on 2 August 2018 expressing Canada's concern over the recent arrest of Samar Badawi, a human rights activist and sister of imprisoned Saudi blogger Raif Badawi. She advocated their release.[49] In response to Canada's criticism, Saudi Arabia expelled Canada's ambassador, and froze trade with Canada.[50] Freeland asked for help from allies including Germany, Sweden, the United Arab Emirates and the United Kingdom.[51][52]
