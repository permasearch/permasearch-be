The Amazing Spider-Man was originally reported as being the first of at least three planned sequels to the original trilogy.[290] The second sequel, The Amazing Spider-Man 2 was released on May 2, 2014.[291] On June 17, 2013 Sony announced two more sequels to follow The Amazing Spider-Man 2, which were scheduled to release on June 10, 2016 and May 4, 2018, respectively.[292] However, in July 2014, the third film was delayed to an unspecified date in 2018.[293]
