The Sunday Times described Welch as "the most peculiar and most highly acclaimed female singer of the moment" and "the latest in a line of great English pop eccentrics".[19] AllMusic referred to Lungs as "one of the most musically mature and emotionally mesmerising albums of 2009".[141] Spin magazine rated Lungs eight out of ten and wrote, "You've gotta hand it to the girl: She always makes you feel something."[142] The magazine named it the eighth best album of 2009.[143] In December 2010, Florence and the Machine appeared on one of Spin's three holiday issue covers as Artist of the Year.[144][145]
