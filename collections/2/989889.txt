On election day, two presidential candidates were seriously beaten by police[63] in different opposition rallies.[64][65][66] On the night of the election, opposition protesters chanting "Out!," "Long live Belarus!" and other similar slogans attempted to storm the building of the government of Belarus, smashing windows and doors before riot police were able to push them back.[67] The number of protesters was reported by major news media as being around or above 10,000 people.[68][69][70][71] At least seven of the opposition presidential candidates were arrested.[63]
