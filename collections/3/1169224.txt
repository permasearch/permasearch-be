The explanation for the green and red colours that make up the background field arose during the Estado Novo period, the nationalist authoritarian regime that held power from 1933 to 1974. It claims that the green represented the hope of the Portuguese people, while the red represented the blood of those who died serving the nation.[8] Sources believe these noble meanings are far from the truth and are nothing more than propaganda, to provide an honourable justification to their choice.[9]
