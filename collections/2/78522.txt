Fargo holds a 93% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes, based on 88 reviews, with an average rating of 8.7/10. The website's critical consensus reads, "Violent, quirky, and darkly funny, Fargo delivers an original crime story and a wonderful performance by McDormand".[23] The film scores 85 out of 100 on Metacritic, based on 24 reviews, indicating "universal acclaim".[24]
