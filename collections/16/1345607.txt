Globally, up to 35% of the population over the age of 60 years is estimated to be incontinent.[31] In 2014, urinary leakage affected between 30% and 40% of people over 65 years of age living in their own homes or apartments in the U.S.[32] Twenty-four percent of older adults in the U.S. have moderate or severe urinary incontinence that should be treated medically.[32]
