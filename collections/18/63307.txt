Carnegie Hall (/ˈkɑːrnɪɡi/,[3] also frequently /kɑːrˈneɪɡi/[4] or /kɑːrˈnɛɡi/) is a concert venue in Midtown Manhattan in New York City, United States, located at 881 Seventh Avenue, occupying the east side of Seventh Avenue between West 56th Street and West 57th Street, two blocks south of Central Park.
