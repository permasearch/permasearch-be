In April 2010, World of Wonder started seeking real estate agents within New York City for a Million Dollar Listing spin-off.[2] The official series debut date was announced on January 27, 2012, with Michael Lorber (son of Howard Lorber), Ryan Serhant and Fredrik Eklund as the cast.[3] Eklund left CORE Group, another broker firm, for Prudential Douglas Elliman in order to be on the series.[4] Ryan Serhant was added to the cast last minute, replacing another potential cast member.[4] The first season averaged 1.04 million total viewers.[5]
