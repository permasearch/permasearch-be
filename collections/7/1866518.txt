Hockey is a moderately popular recreational sport in England. The Great Britain women's hockey team won the 2016 Rio Olympics with over 10 million viewers on TV, the men's team won the hockey tournament at the 1988 Olympics. Women's hockey at International, Premier League and Conference League level is sponsored by Investec. The England Hockey, Men's Hockey League and Investec Women's Hockey League contain very high levels of club hockey ability and usually at least 2 clubs from both the men's and women's leagues proceed to European Competitions. Each week highlights of that weeks games get posted on YouTube.
