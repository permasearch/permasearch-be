America's Army: Proving Grounds also debuts a new story line in a full digital comic series through IDW Publishing. The America's Army Comics series,[14] available on the web and for mobile devices, unveils the story line that influences the plot for the game's missions and maps and gives the player a better understanding of their assignment and the challenges they will face. In AA Comics, players learn the saga of American forces deployed to the Ostregal Islands, a tiny foreign nation in the middle of a chaotic conflict.
