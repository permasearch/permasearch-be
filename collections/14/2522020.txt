In 2012, Ty Dolla Sign signed a recording contract with Atlantic Records.[15] After signing with Atlantic Records, he released his first solo mixtape, titled Beach House, on October 1, 2012.[16] He followed with the mixtape's sequel, Beach House 2, on July 1, 2013. Beach House 2 features guest appearances from Too $hort, Wiz Khalifa, Juicy J and Kirko Bangz, among others.[17] The following day, it was revealed that Ty Dolla Sign signed a deal with Wiz Khalifa's Taylor Gang Records.[18] In mid-2013, he toured with Khalifa and A$AP Rocky, among others, on the "Under the Influence of Music 2" concert tour.[19]
