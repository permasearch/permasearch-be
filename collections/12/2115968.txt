The Toyota Avalon is a full-size car produced by Toyota in the United States and Japan, and is Toyota's largest front-wheel-drive sedan in the United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, and the Middle East. It was also produced in Australia from April 2000 until June 2005, when it was replaced in November 2006 by the Toyota Aurion. The first production Avalon rolled off the TMMK assembly line in Georgetown, Kentucky, in September 1994.[2] A second-generation model was released in the United States and Japan in 1999.
