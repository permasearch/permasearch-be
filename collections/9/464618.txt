The episode "The Incident" was written by Steven Levitan and directed by Jason Winer. The episode is Steven Levitan's third writing credit after the "Pilot" and "The Bicycle Thief".[2] The episode is also Jason Winer's fourth directors credit after "Pilot", "The Bicycle Thief" and "Come Fly with Me.[3][4] It is also Shelley Long's first guest appearance on the show as Jay's Ex-wife and Mitchell & Claire's mother.[5]
