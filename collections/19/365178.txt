The success of the series led to SBS One and the National Archives of Australia jointly organising a Shake Your Family Tree day on 27 February 2008 to promote genealogy.[2] SBS One renewed the series for a sixth series on 29 August 2012.[3] The seventh season aired on the SBS from 4 August 2015, with Geoffrey Rush featured on Episode 1.[4] The eighth season aired on Tuesday, 13 September 2016.[5]
