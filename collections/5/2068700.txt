The Trixie & Katya Show is an American television series featuring former RuPaul's Drag Race contestants Katya Zamolodchikova and Trixie Mattel. The show is a spin-off of the YouTube series UNHhhh,[1] which had turned both drag queens into "viral internet stars".[2] Ahead of its premiere on November 15, 2017, Viceland and World of Wonder made the show's first episode available online on November 3.[3][4]
