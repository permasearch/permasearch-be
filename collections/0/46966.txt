4 Your Eyez Only World Tour was a concert tour by American rapper J. Cole, in support of his fourth studio album 4 Your Eyez Only (2016).[1] Dreamville artists J.I.D, Ari Lennox and Lute served as opening acts on the first leg of the tour.[2] Singer Anderson .Paak and rapper Bas served as opening acts on the second leg of the tour, along with J.I.D and Ari Lennox.[3] Dreamville artists J.I.D., Ari Lennox and EarthGang served as opening acts on the European leg of the tour.[4]
