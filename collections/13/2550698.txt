Benvicktor in the first three series or Frankenstrike in Ben 10: Omniverse, is a Transylian from the planet Anur Transyl in the Anur system. Transylians are based on Frankenstein's monster, resembling patch-work humanoids with machine parts on their bodies while possessing super-strength, agility and durability. They also can generate electricity and blast it at others, adhere to metal surfaces through electromagnetism and survive in the vacuum of space.
