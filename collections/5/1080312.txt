It has some psychological benefits. The aroma of the incense stick has healing power that has a soothing effect on the mind. The calming effect relaxes the mind and helps in performing rituals with better concentration. Prayer offered with a calm mind acts like a meditation process.[17] Agarbatti made up of bamboo is not used because to burn bamboo is strictly prohibited in Hinduism. Incense has its own spiritual significance. The incense stick burns itself completely into ashes and yet fills the air with a pleasant smell. This ritual basically denotes human virtue of sacrificing oneself for society.[18] The sticks are used as air fresheners during normal days as well and integral part of every Hindu ceremonies.[19]
