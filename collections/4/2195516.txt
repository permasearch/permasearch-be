Knight Squad received a 20-episode order from Nickelodeon in May 2017.[1][3] Production on the series began in October 2017,[4][5][6] and it premiered in February 2018.[7] Tenzing Norgay Trainor has appeared on the series, playing Jimbo in the episode "The Dork Knight Returns".[8] On July 27, 2018, the series was renewed for a second season of 10 episodes.[9][10]
