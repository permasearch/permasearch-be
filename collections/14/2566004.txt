Metal Gear Solid was shown to the public at E3 1997 as a short video. It was playable for the first time at the Tokyo Game Show in 1998 and released the same year in Japan[52] with an extensive promotional campaign.[45] Television and magazine advertisements, in-store samples, and demo giveaways contributed to a total of $8 million in promotional costs.[53] An estimated 12 million demos for the game were distributed in 1998.[54]
