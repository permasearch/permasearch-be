After dating for six years, Hardrict and actress Tia Mowry were engaged on Christmas Day 2006.[2] They married on April 20, 2008 in Santa Barbara, California.[3] On January 11, 2011, People announced that Hardrict and Mowry were expecting their first child. The pregnancy was documented on Mowry's reality TV show called Tia & Tamera starring her and her twin sister on the Style Network.[4] Hardrict spoke at Victor J. Andrew High School on March 9, 2011 stating, "...she's six months pregnant". Their son, Cree Taylor Hardrict, was born on June 28, 2011 in Los Angeles.
