"Snuff" is a power ballad by American metal band Slipknot.[1] Released as the fifth single from their fourth album All Hope Is Gone on September 28, 2009,[2] the song charted at number two on the Billboard Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, their highest-charted single to date, surpassing "Dead Memories".[3] It was not included in the All Hope Is Gone World Tour setlist but the band performed the song live at the October 11, 2009 performance in Kennewick, Washington.[4]
