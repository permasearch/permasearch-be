Starting around 2000, several police departments started reporting problems with high speed instability on the Harley-Davidson Touring motorcycles.[83]  A Raleigh, North Carolina police officer, Charles Paul, was killed when his 2002 police touring motorcycle crashed after reportedly experiencing a high speed wobble.[84]  The California Highway Patrol conducted testing of the Police Touring motorcycles in 2006.  The CHP test riders reported experiencing wobble or weave instability while operating the motorcycles on the test track.[85]
