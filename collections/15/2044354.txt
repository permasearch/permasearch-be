Ellsbury was born on September 11, 1983, to Jim and Margie Ellsbury, and is the eldest of four children. His mother is a Navajo, a descendant from 19th-century tribal leader Ganado Mucho. Her father Franklin McCabe was a silversmith and her mother a traditional rug weaver.[3] His father, Jim Ellsbury, is of English and German descent.[7] The Ellsburys lived on the Warm Springs Indian Reservation until they moved to Madras, Oregon, when he was in kindergarten.[7] He was raised in the Mormon religion.[8]
