Deuteronomy 14:19 specifies that all "flying creeping things" were to be considered ritually unclean[97] and Leviticus 11:20 goes further, describing all flying creeping things as filth, Hebrew sheqets.[98] Leviticus goes on to list four exceptions, which Deuteronomy does not.
