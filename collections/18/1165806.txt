The Freedom Trophy has been awarded to the winner of the Nebraska-Wisconsin game since the 2014 season. The teams have met only twelve times, with the series dating back to 1901, an 18–0 Wisconsin win. The Badgers lead the series, 8-4. Wisconsin currently holds the Freedom Trophy, having defeated the Cornhuskers 38–17 in 2017. The teams play annually and will meet next on October 6, 2018 at Camp Randall Stadium.
