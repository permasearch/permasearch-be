Rapid City, with a 2010 population of 67,956,[159] and a metropolitan area population of 124,766,[160] is the second-largest city in the state. It is on the eastern edge of the Black Hills, and was founded in 1876.[163] Rapid City's economy is largely based on tourism and defense spending,[162] because of the proximity of many tourist attractions in the Black Hills and Ellsworth Air Force Base.
