On January 8, 2014, WWE announced the network would launch on February 24, 2014 in the United States. On July 31, 2014, WWE announced that the network would be expected to go live in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Hong Kong, Singapore, Mexico, Spain, and the Nordics, among other countries starting on August 12.[26] WWE Network arrived in the United Kingdom on January 13, 2015, with expansion to Italy, United Arab Emirates, Germany, Japan, India, China, Thailand, Philippines, and Malaysia following at a future date.
