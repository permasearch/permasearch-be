The song is featured in Oliver Stone's 1986 film Salvador and the 1997 film Boogie Nights, as well as in the video game Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas in which it plays on the fictional radio station K-Rose. It also appears as source music in the first episode of The Americans, a 2013 television series set during the first Reagan administration. The song is also featured in the 1998 King of the Hill episode Hank's Dirty Laundry and the 2017 South Park episode "Splatty Tomato".
