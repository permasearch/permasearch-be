By the ninth grade, Rodger was "increasingly bullied", and he wrote that he "cried by [himself] at school every day".[73] During his time at Crespi Carmelite High, he was bullied by other students including an incident that involved his head getting taped to his desk while he was asleep.[18][74] According to Rodger, in 2012, "the one friend [he] had in the whole world who truly understood [him]" "blatantly said he didn't want to be friends anymore" without offering him a reason for ending the friendship.[73]
