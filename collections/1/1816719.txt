"Duncan" was first included on Dusty's 1981 album, No. 50: The Golden Anniversary Album[15] and appeared on eight subsequent albums: Beer Drinking Songs of Australia (1986), 91 Over 50 (1996), The Very Best of Slim Dusty (1998), The Man Who Is Australia (2000), A Piece of Australia (2001), Slim Dusty Live (2006), Pubs, Trucks & Plains (2008) and The Very Best of Slim Dusty (2013).
