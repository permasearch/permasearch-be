Kyara was a female orca,That was born at Seaworld San Antonio on April 19, 2017. her mother is Takara and her father Kyuquot, Her name was a combination of her parents names. On july 22th It was announced that she was showing signs of pneumonia. Because of this, she had been moved to SeaWorld's animal care hospital for treatment. Veterinarians and trainers were providing care for Kyara with hopes she would recover soon and be reunited with Takara and the rest of the pod. Sadly, she began to deteriorate and passed away on July 24, 2017.
