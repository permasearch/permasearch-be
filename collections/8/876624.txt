In November 2010, Stanley Tucci stated that he had completed filming his scenes and that the rest of the production would wrap in about three weeks.[96] In February 2011, it was announced that Alan Silvestri had been chosen to compose the film score.[97] In March 2011, it was reported that Captain America: The First Avenger would be undergoing reshoots in the United Kingdom and in Los Angeles in April 2011.[98][99] A scene was also filmed in New York City's Times Square on April 23, 2011.[100]
