Coimbatore is one of major manufacturers of automotive components in India with car manufacturers Maruti Udyog and Tata Motors sourcing up to 30% of their automotive components from the city.[55][56] India's first indigenously developed diesel engines for cars was manufactured in Coimbatore in 1972.[57] The district also has a number of tier-I, II and III suppliers catering to the needs of the entire gamut of the automobile industry, ranging from two-wheelers and four-wheelers to commercial vehicles and tractors.[58] Coimbatore district has more than 700 wet grinder manufacturers with a monthly output 75,000 units as of March, 2005[59] and the term "Coimbatore Wet Grinder" has been given a Geographical indication.[57][60]
