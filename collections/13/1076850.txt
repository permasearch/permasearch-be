Virginia Elizabeth "Geena" Davis (born January 21, 1956)[7] is an American actress, film producer, writer, voice actress, former model, and former archer.[8] She is known for her roles in The Fly (1986), Beetlejuice (1988), Thelma & Louise (1991), A League of Their Own (1992), The Long Kiss Goodnight (1996), Stuart Little (1999), and The Accidental Tourist, for which she won the 1988 Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.
