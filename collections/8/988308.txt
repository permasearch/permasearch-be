As of the 2000 United States Census, there were 46,756 people, 16,372 households, and 13,081 families residing in the township.  The population density was 2,129.7/mi2 (822.4/km2). There were 16,640 housing units at an average density of 758.0/mi2 (292.7/km2). The racial makeup of the township was 77.56% White, 2.83% African American, 0.09% Native American, 16.27% Asian, 0.01% Pacific Islander, 1.12% from other races, and 2.12% from two or more races.  4.19% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race.[39][40]
