Ma He was captured by the Ming armies at Yunnan in 1381.[11] General Fu Youde saw Ma He on a road and approached him in order to inquire about the location of the Mongol pretender.[21] Ma He responded defiantly by saying that the Mongol pretender had jumped into a lake.[21] Afterwards, the general took him prisoner.[21] He was castrated before he, at age 10, was placed in servitude of the Prince of Yan,[22] but another source indicates that the castration occurred in 1385.[21]
