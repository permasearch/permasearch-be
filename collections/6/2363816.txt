On review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, the film has an approval rating of 62% based on 185 reviews, with an average rating of 5.9/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "Extract has some very funny moments and several fine performances, but the film feels slighter and more uneven than Mike Judge's previous work."[7] On Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating to reviews, the film has a weighted average score of 61 out of 100, based on 33 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews."[8]
