James Stewart was born on October 12, 1975 in Melbourne. He grew up on Queenslands Gold Coast, alongside his identical twin brother, Nicholas and older half-sister Annie.[2] His mother Beverly raised him and his siblings as a single parent while studying to become a teacher. She eventually graduated with honors in English and literature at Monash University.[3] His estranged father Arthur Koo is a maths teacher and was a former Australian soldier who served in the Vietnam War. Stewart has Irish and Chinese ancestry through his Australian-born parents.[4]
