To facilitate transport by Elgin, the columns' capitals and many metopes and frieze slabs were either hacked off the main structure or sawn and sliced into smaller sections, causing irreparable damage to the Parthenon itself.[47][48] One shipload of marbles on board the British brig Mentor [49] was caught in a storm off Cape Matapan and sank near Kythera, but was salvaged at the Earl's personal expense;[50] it took two years to bring them to the surface.
