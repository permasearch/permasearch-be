"Weird Al" Yankovic wrote and performed the theme song for the film, which was featured in a lyric video. It currently stands at over 7 million views (as of September 17).[14] Andy Grammer wrote another original song for the film, titled "A Friend Like You".[15] The film also features music from Adam Lambert, Cold War Kids member Nathan Willett, and Lil Yachty.[15] An 11-track soundtrack album was released digitally on June 2, 2017, by Virgin Records.[16]
