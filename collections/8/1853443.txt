The series featured numerous famous and well known Hollywood actors (particularly of the period), with Robin Williams and Teri Garr in the first episode, "The Tale of the Frog Prince". The creator, executive producer and narrator Shelly Duvall, occasionally appeared in episodes. Others, including Jean Stapleton, Mark Blankfield, Charlie Dell, Donovan Scott and Daniel Frishman, were among those who had credited appearances in more than three episodes.
