The United States Postal Service issued a stamp that featured the light on June 17, 1995.  It was one of five lighthouses chosen for the "Lighthouses of the Great Lakes" series[8] postage stamp designed by Howard Koslow in 1995.  There was one lighthouse chosen on each of the Great Lakes.[9]  The five lighthouses are Split Rock Light on Lake Superior,[10] St Joseph Light on Lake Michigan, Spectacle Reef Light on Lake Huron,[11] Marblehead Light (Ohio) on Lake Erie[12] and Thirty Mile Point Light on Lake Ontario.[13]
