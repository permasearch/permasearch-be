The four truths are best known from their presentation in the Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta text,[note 7] which contains two sets of the four truths,[33][2] while various other sets can be found in the Pali Canon, a collection of scriptures in the Theravadan Buddhist tradition.[20] According to the Buddhist tradition, the Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta, "Setting the Wheel of Dhamma in Motion,"[web 4] contains the first teachings that the Buddha gave after attaining enlightenment, and liberation from rebirth.
