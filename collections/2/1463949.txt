In 1992, White began a relationship with Matilde Conejero, the bartender at The Canteen [41] and went on to have two sons and a daughter. The couple married at the Belvedere on 7 April 2000. After White became friends with city financier Robin Saunders, Conejero suspected an affair between the two. White and his wife had a fight, after which White spent 14 hours in the cells of Notting Hill police station in January 2005.[42] White and Conejero began divorce proceedings in 2007. In 2011 the divorce proceedings were withdrawn but in October 2012 White and Conejero separated again.
