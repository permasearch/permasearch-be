"Centuries" was used by WWE as the official theme song for Friday Night SmackDown's 15th anniversary show.[31][32][33] "Centuries" was also performed at the 2015 NHL All-Star game at Nationwide Arena, Columbus, Ohio in between periods.[34] On April 5, 2015, the band performed "Centuries" as part of pre-game festivities at Wrigley Field in their hometown of Chicago for the opening game of the 2015 Major League Baseball season; the performance was televised by ESPN2 as part of its coverage.[28][35][36] "Centuries" was performed at the 2015 MTV Movie Awards on April 12, 2015.[37][38] "Centuries" was also used for an intro video of the 2015 Chicago White Sox season home games and Calgary Flames playoff home games.
