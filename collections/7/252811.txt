Midtown TDR Ventures, LLC, an investment group controlled by Argent Ventures,[103] purchased the station from American Financial in December 2006.[104] As part of the transaction the lease with the MTA was renegotiated through February 28, 2274. The MTA pays $2.24 million in rent and has an option to buy the station and tracks in 2017, although Argent could extend the date another 15 years to 2032.[103] The transferable air rights remain the property of Midtown TDR Ventures.
