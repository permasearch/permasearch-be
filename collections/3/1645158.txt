A seventh series comprising ten episodes began airing on 17 October 2014 and ended with a Christmas special on 24 December.[3] A further Christmas special, which was shot at West London Film Studios, was aired on 24 December 2015. On 24 August 2016, it was confirmed that Not Going Out would return for an eighth series,[4] which began airing on 13 January 2017 and ended on 3 March 2017. On 14 November 2017, it was confirmed that a Christmas Special will air in 2017.[5] On 4 December 2017, it was confirmed that the Christmas Special will air on 24 December 2017.[6]
