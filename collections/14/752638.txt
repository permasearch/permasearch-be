On 21 September 2014 Spain evacuated a Catholic priest who had contracted Ebola while working in Sierra Leone with Hospital Order of San Juan de Dios.[185] He died on the 25th September in Madrid.[186] On 6 October 2014 a nurse who treated the priest tested positive for Ebola.[187] By 20 October 2014 the nurse seemed to have recovered after many days battling the disease in the hospital, with tests coming back negative.[188]
