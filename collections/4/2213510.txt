Following the remastered re-release of The Joshua Tree in 2007, it was rumoured that the band would also remaster and re-release War, along with the albums Boy, and October.[48] This was confirmed by U2.com on 9 April 2008 (2008-04-09).[49] The remastered album was released on 21 July 2008 (2008-07-21) in the UK, with the US version following it the next day.
