Look What the Cat Dragged In is the debut studio album by American glam metal band Poison, released on August 2, 1986[2] through Enigma Records. Though not a success at first, it steadily built momentum and peaked at #3 on the US Billboard 200 on 23 May 1987.[3] The album spawned four singles, three of them charting successfully: "Talk Dirty to Me", "I Want Action", "I Won't Forget You" and "Cry Tough".[4] It serves as a prime example of the glam metal genre both musically and image-wise. The album ultimately has sold four million copies worldwide,[5] one of their best-selling albums.
