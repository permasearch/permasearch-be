Snickers has been an official sponsor of WWE's WrestleMania events, including WrestleMania 2000, 22, 32, 33 and 34, while its Cruncher variant sponsored WrestleMania X-Seven, XIX, XX and 21. Since then, Snickers has sponsored superstars such as Enzo Amore and Big Cass with their signature term, SAWFT, which is labelled at the back of the chocolate bar.
