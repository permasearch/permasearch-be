Canada has participated in almost every Olympic Games since its Olympic debut in 1900,[336] and has hosted several high-profile international sporting events, including the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal,[337] the 1988 Winter Olympics in Calgary,[338] the 1994 Basketball World Championship,[339] the 2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup,[340] the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver,[341] and Whistler, British Columbia,[342] and the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup.[343]
