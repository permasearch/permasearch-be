Reggae and dancehall stars Dillinger, Trinity, Ranking Joe, Scorcher, Little John, Super Cat and countless others had sung about Clarks in the past. Some of the most famous songs written about the Clarks shoes in Jamaica are Little John’s "Clarks Booty" and Vybz Kartel’s "Clarks" (over 2.6 million hits on YouTube).[66][67]
