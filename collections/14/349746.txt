On 12 January 2017, Madrid's draw against Sevilla FC in the second leg of the Copa del Rey round of 16 saw him with 40 consecutive matches without a loss – creating a new Spanish record, beating Luis Enrique's record of 39 matches undefeated with Barcelona.[126][127] Zidane later led Madrid to the La Liga title, their 33rd in history, by defeating Málaga 2–0.[128]
