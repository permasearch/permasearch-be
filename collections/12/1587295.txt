In the UK, ITV4 has broadcast colour episodes. In the US, FamilyNet and RTV have airied both the black-and-white and colour episodes. Me-TV has also broadcast the series. In March 2015, the CBS-owned Decades digital cable network aired a "Series Binge" marathon of the show as part of "Countdown to Decades", a soft-launch prelude to the network's official launch in May 2015. The marathon featured every episode of the series aired back to back. The marathon began March 30 at 5 pm Eastern Time and ended April 3 at 11 pm.
