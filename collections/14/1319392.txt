Varanasi is served by Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport, which is approximately 26 km (16 mi) from the city centre in Babatpur.[171] The airport inaugurated a new terminal in 2010, and it was granted international airport status on 4 October 2012.[172][173] Air India, Buddha Air, Jet Airways, IndiGo, Thai Smile, SriLankan Airlines and SpiceJet operate flights from Varanasi to Bangkok, Colombo, Delhi, Gaya, Kathmandu, Khajuraho, Sharjah, Yangon, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Goa and Kolkata.[174] Over 1,916,454 passengers passed through the airport in 2016-17, making it the 21st busiest airport in India.
