On September 22, 2011, Lifetime picked up the show for a 4th season featuring 13 episodes, which premiered on June 3, 2012.[1] Lex Medlin who played the recurring role of Judge Owen French, has been upgraded to a series regular in season four.[2] Carter MacIntyre also joined the main cast for season four. He plays the role of Luke Daniels, Jane's new guardian angel.[3] Kim Kardashian will recur as Nikki, Stacy's business partner.[4] Brandy is confirmed to be reprising her role as Elisa Shayne for season four.[5]
