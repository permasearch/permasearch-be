On review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes, World War Z has a 66% approval rating, based on 262 reviews, with an average rating of 6.2/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "It's uneven and diverges from the source book, but World War Z still brings smart, fast-moving thrills and a solid performance from Brad Pitt to the zombie genre."[80] Metacritic, which uses a weighted mean, assigned a score of 63 out of 100, based on reviews from 46 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[81]
