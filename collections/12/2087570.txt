The Greater Binghamton Airport (IATA code BGM, ICAO code KBGM) is a medium-size regional airport, and the only area airport that offers scheduled airline service. Located 10 miles (16 km) north of downtown, the airport has non-stop flights to Detroit on Delta Air Lines.[147] The region also has a general aviation airport, Tri-Cities Airport, which is 10.5 miles (16.9 km) to the west.
