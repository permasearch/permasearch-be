The film was screened at more than 131 territories[52] and was commercially released on 9 October 2013 in France with a "12" rating.[53] In the United States, the film was rated NC-17 by the Motion Picture Association of America for "explicit sexual content". It had a limited release at four theatres in New York City and Los Angeles on 25 October 2013, and expanded gradually in subsequent weeks.[54][55][56][57] The film was released on 15 November 2013 in the United Kingdom[58] and in Australia and New Zealand on 13 February 2014.[59][60][61]
