March 2017, the Energy Department prohibited the use of the term "climate change".[443] In December 2017, the Trump administration sent a list to the Center for Disease Control (CDC) on words that the agency that was prohibited from using in its official communications.[428] These words included “transgender,” “fetus,” “evidence-based,” “science-based,” “vulnerable,” “entitlement,” and “diversity.”[428] The Director of the CDC denied these reports.[429]
