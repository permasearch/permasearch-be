On April 11, 2014, Summit Entertainment announced that a two-part film based on the final book in the Divergent trilogy, Allegiant, would be made. The first part, The Divergent Series: Allegiant, will be released on March 18, 2016 while the second part, The Divergent Series: Ascendant, on June 9, 2017.[12][89] Shailene Woodley, Theo James, and Naomi Watts will reprise their roles.[90] On December 5, 2014, it was announced that Robert Schwentke will return to direct Part 1.[91] Principal photography for Allegiant began in Atlanta on May 18, 2015,[92][93] and concluded on August 23, 2015. On September 10, 2015, it was announced that the two films would be re-titled, with Part 1 being renamed as The Divergent Series: Allegiant and Part 2 as The Divergent Series: Ascendant.[94] Lee Toland Krieger will serve as the director for Ascendant.[95]
