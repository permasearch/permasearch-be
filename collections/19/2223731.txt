On May 6, 2018, Drake was spotted with Lil Baby at OVO affiliate Preme's album release party in an Atlanta nightclub, where a snippet of the track surfaced on Quality Control CEO Pierre "Pee" Thomas's Instagram story.[2] The next day, Lil Baby took to Twitter and tweeted: "Imagine Me And Drake Dropping A Song".[3] On May 12, the song was premiered on OVO Sound Radio, originally surfacing under the title "Pikachu (No Keys)".[4][5] The song was officially released to streaming services on May 15, 2018, under its current title, "Yes Indeed".[1][5][6][7]
