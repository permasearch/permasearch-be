The Model S exists in several versions, differing in energy capacity (battery size), power (motor size), and equipment. It is classified as a full-size luxury car in the US, or as a "Large Car"[61] (120 cu ft or more) or "Luxury Sedan"[62] by the EPA. The Euro Car Segment classification is S-segment (sports car),[63][64] or "Oberklasse" (F-segment) in Germany.[65]
