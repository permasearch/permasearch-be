Cybersecurity measures include firewalls, anti-virus software, intrusion detection and prevention systems, encryption, and login passwords.[2] There have been attempts to improve cybersecurity through regulation and collaborative efforts between the government and the private sector to encourage voluntary improvements to cybersecurity. [1] Industry regulators, including banking regulators, have taken notice of the risk from cybersecurity and have either begun or planned to begin to include cybersecurity as an aspect of regulatory examinations.[1]
