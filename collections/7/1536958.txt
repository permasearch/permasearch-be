Damon is a fan of the Boston Red Sox.[129] After the team won the 2007 World Series, he narrated the commemorative DVD release of the event.[130] He has competed in several World Series of Poker (WSOP) events,[131][132] including the 2010 World Series of Poker main event.[133] He was eliminated from the 1998 WSOP by poker professional Doyle Brunson.[134]
