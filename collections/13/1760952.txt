The launch of the Ertiga has been announced for the export market in July 2014. The available variants at introduction will be GA manual transmission, GL manual or automatic and GLX automatic and LDi variant for Algeria. All models will be fitted with the 1.4 liter petrol engine. One of the export Markets of Ertiga is The Republic of the Philippines which is an intense market for MPV's and AUV's with a culture of being family oriented and design conscious clientele.[17]
