On September 25, 2017, it was revealed that Sproles suffered a broken arm and a torn ACL on the same play in a game against the New York Giants in Week 3.[88] He was officially placed on injured reserve that day, prematurely ending his season.[89] In three games, he rushed 15 times for 61 yards with seven receptions for 73 yards.[90] The Eagles went on to win Super Bowl LII.[91] After the season ended, Sproles stated his interest to return to the Eagles for one more season.
