Politicians from Eugene include U.S. Senators Wayne Morse and Paul Martin Simon, as well as Congressmen Jim Weaver and Peter DeFazio. Also born in Eugene was Joan Mondale, wife of Vice President Walter Mondale. Actors of note include River Phoenix, born August 23, 1970 Jenny Wade, Howard Hesseman and David Ogden Stiers, while Bryce Zabel chaired the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences.
