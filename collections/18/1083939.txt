Combs signed a deal with Sony Music Nashville, and the debut single from This One's For You, "Hurricane" was re-released on Columbia Nashville.[7] "Hurricane," reached No. 1 on the Country Radio airplay charts on May 15, 2017 - holding that spot for 2 weeks.[9]  The album reached No. 1 on the Billboard Country Albums Chart[10] and No. 5 on the Billboard Top 200 Albums[11] in its first week.
