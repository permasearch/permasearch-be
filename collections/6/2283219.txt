Even though Terry was suspended for the Champions League Final, along with teammates Ramires, Branislav Ivanović and Raul Meireles,[55] he took part in celebrations at the final whistle after Chelsea triumphed 4–3 on penalty kicks, with striker Didier Drogba netting the decisive penalty.[56] His celebrations led to a popular internet meme lampooning the fact that Terry wore a full kit despite not playing in the match.[57]
