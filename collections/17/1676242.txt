Anne embarks on a road trip across the United States, in search of a husband to fund a new life for her and her boys. George serves as the chauffeur.  They first travel to Boston and Pittsburgh. Anne has a string of disastrous attempts at relationship.  After finding that a former suitor now deems her too old to be of interest she becomes desperate and dispirited and chats up a man in a bar who turns out to be an undercover house detective.  He takes her for a prostitute and charges her with solicitation.
