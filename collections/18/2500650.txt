In several countries, the album attained gold status within a month of its release. In Canada, the album shot to the top of the charts, selling 30,000 copies in its first week according to Nielsen SoundScan data, Houston's best opening totals since Nielsen SoundScan began tracking sales in 1991. Her last album, Just Whitney, debuted at number 85 with just 13,000 in sales.[61] After three weeks the album had sold around 26,000 copies in Canada.[62][63] According to Arista Records, the album also debuted at number one in the Netherlands, Poland, Germany, Italy and Switzerland.[58] In the UK, the album debuted at three with first week sales of 51,632.[64] The album also debuted at three in France with first week sales of 19,000.[65] In Korea the album reached number one on the monthly chart and sold 12,429 copies.[66] According to Houston's official website, the album and singles combined have sold a total of 500,000 copies in the UK and Ireland.[67] The album also finished the 29th biggest selling album of 2009 in the United States.[68]
