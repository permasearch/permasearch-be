Mary Riggans, who played Isa's friend Sadie in this episode, also starred in the popular CBeebies programme Balamory. There are a number of associations between Balamory and Still Game. Greg Hemphill guest-starred in one episode; his wife, Julie Wilson Nimmo, played the teacher Miss Hoolie. Nimmo was also featured in Kiernan and Hemphill's earlier sketch shows, Pulp Video and Chewin' the Fat.
