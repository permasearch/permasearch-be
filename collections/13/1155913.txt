In July 1994, Abbey National purchased James Hay, one of the United Kingdom's foremost independent providers of self-administered pensions.[6] James Hay then went on to grow in straight[clarification needed] and launched Abbey Wrap, the first Wrap a service in which IFAs can keep the clients' ISAs, PEPs, Offshore bonds, and SIPP in one place. Abbey Wrap Managers was FSA approved in August 2003. This was relaunched as James Hay Wrap in June 2005.[7]
