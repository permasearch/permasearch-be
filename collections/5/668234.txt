The Climate Summit was introduced by UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon, who invited world leaders in finance, politics and business as well as civil society to Climate Summit 2014. It was held on September 23 of 2014 and its aim was the effective approaches in climate action.[15] The Climate Summit also acted as an impulse on politicians decisions regarding the important universal climate agreement in Paris in 2015 . It encouraged all countries to reduce greenhouse gases while corresponding with adaptation to the negative impacts of climate change.
