Flair teamed with Razor Ramon to take on Savage and Perfect at the Survivor Series in November 1992.[29] Flair appeared in the Royal Rumble in January 1993, then lost a Loser Leaves the WWF match to Mr. Perfect on the next night's (January 25) Monday Night Raw in a match taped six days earlier.[32] Flair then fulfilled his remaining house show commitments, making his last appearance on February 10, 1993, before returning to WCW.[33]
