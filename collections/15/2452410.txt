The Netherlands Development Organisation, SNV,[74] supports national programmes on domestic biogas that aim to establish commercial-viable domestic biogas sectors in which local companies market, install and service biogas plants for households. In Asia, SNV is working in Nepal,[75] Vietnam,[76] Bangladesh,[77] Bhutan, Cambodia,[77] Lao PDR,[78] Pakistan[79] and Indonesia,[80] and in Africa; Rwanda,[81] Senegal, Burkina Faso, Ethiopia,[82] Tanzania,[83] Uganda, Kenya,[84] Benin and Cameroon.
