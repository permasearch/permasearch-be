After the release of the game, sequels, spin-offs, and a crossover have been made. The second and third games in the series, Justice for All and Trials and Tribulations, were released in 2002 and 2004.[77][78] The fourth game, Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney, which features the new protagonist Apollo Justice, was released in 2007.[79][80] The fifth game, Dual Destinies, was released in 2013,[81] and a sixth game, Spirit of Justice, was released in 2016.[82] Two spin-off games starring Miles Edgeworth, Ace Attorney Investigations and Ace Attorney Investigations 2, were released in 2009 and 2011,[83][84][85] and two featuring Phoenix's ancestor Ryūnosuke Naruhodō, Dai Gyakuten Saiban and Dai Gyakuten Saiban 2, were released in 2015 and 2017.[86] A crossover with the Professor Layton series, titled Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney, was released in 2012.[87][88]
