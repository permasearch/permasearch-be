In 1994, Bonham Carter and Kenneth Branagh met while filming Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. They began an affair, while Branagh was still married to Emma Thompson, whom he had met in 1987 while filming the BBC Series Fortunes of War and married in 1989.[50] At the time, Thompson's career was soaring, while Branagh was struggling to make a success of his first big-budget film (Mary Shelley's Frankenstein).[50] Following his affair with Bonham Carter, Branagh and Thompson divorced in 1995.[51] However, in 1999, after five years together, Bonham Carter and Branagh also called it quits.[52]
