Brady dated actress Bridget Moynahan from 2004 until late 2006.[397] On February 18, 2007, Moynahan confirmed to People magazine that she was more than three months pregnant with Brady's child.[397][398] Brady and Moynahan ended their relationship sometime in early December 2006, around the time Moynahan became pregnant.[399] John Edward Thomas Moynahan[400] was born on August 22, 2007, at Saint John's Health Center in Santa Monica, California.[401] John's middle names are Brady's own first and middle names in reverse order, while Moynahan's father's first name is Edward.
