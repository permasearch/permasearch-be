In 2010, New England had a population of 14,444,865, a growth of 3.8% from 2000.[77] This grew to an estimated 14,727,584 by 2015.[78] Massachusetts is the most populous state with 6,794,422 residents, while Vermont is the least populous state with 626,042 residents.[77] Boston is by far the region's most populous city and metropolitan area.
