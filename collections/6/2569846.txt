The Kraft Heinz Company is an American food company formed by the merger of Kraft Foods Group and Heinz in 2015.[4] The merger was backed by 3G Capital and Berkshire Hathaway, which invested US$10 billion in the deal, making Kraft Heinz worth about US$46 billion.[5][6] In 2015, the Kraft Heinz Company had 13 different brands with $500 million or more each in annual sales.[7]
