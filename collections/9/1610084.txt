Devi Sri Prasad is an Indian music composer, lyricist, singer, and director.[1][2] He is best known for his works in the cinema of South India, especially in Telugu cinema.[3][4] He has won eight Filmfare Awards, seven of which are Filmfare Best Music Director South Awards and one Special Jury Award, five CineMAA Awards - Best Music Director,[5][6] & one Nandi Award for Best Music Director. Devi also choreographed a song in the Telugu film Kumari 21F.[7][8]
