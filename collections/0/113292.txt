Tonioli was born in Ferrara, in northeast Italy.[9] His father Werther was a bus driver and his mother Fulvia stitched car-seat upholstery.[9] The family lived with his paternal grandparents until he was 12.[9] Tonioli is fluent in five languages: Italian, English, Portuguese, Spanish, and French.[10] He has lived in London since 1975.[11]
