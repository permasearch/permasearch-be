Singer and actress Amii Stewart, who is most famous for her cover of the Eddie Floyd song "Knock on Wood", also covered this is in 1979 as a double A-side with "137 Disco Heaven". It was a substantial hit getting to number 5 in the UK Singles Chart as well as number 26 in Germany, number 69 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number 36 on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. It was later re-released as a remix in 1985 as a double A-side with a remix of "Knock on Wood", this remix was also successful returning Stewart to the British Top 10.[citation needed]
