Johnny English Strikes Again is an upcoming British spy action comedy film directed by David Kerr. It is a sequel to the 2011 film Johnny English Reborn and the third installment of the Johnny English series. The film's screenplay was written by William Davies and produced by Rowan Atkinson, who reprises his role as the title character. The film will be released in theaters in the United Kingdom on 12 October 2018, by Universal Pictures.
