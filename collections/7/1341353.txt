The Tom and Jerry Show is a 2014 American animated television series produced by Warner Bros. Animation and Renegade Animation, based on the Tom and Jerry characters and theatrical cartoon series created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera in 1940. It premiered in the United States on Cartoon Network on April 9, 2014 and on Boomerang. It also premiered on March 1, 2014 in Canada on Teletoon.
