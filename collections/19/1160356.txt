Kimball was a co-founder and has been editor and publisher of America's Test Kitchen, which produces television and radio shows, and publishes magazines, including Cook's Illustrated,[6] which Kimball launched in 1993. It also publishes Cook's Country magazine, which was launched in 2004,[1] and former publisher of the now-defunct Cook's Magazine.[citation needed] The company's revenue comes from its readers, rather than advertisers, which differentiates them from the competitors.[1]
