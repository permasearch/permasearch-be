Redmond Derby Days is an annual community festival held the second full weekend of July and celebrated its 75th anniversary in 2015.[26] It began as a race around Lake Sammamish called the Redmond Bicycle Derby in 1939, and since then has become a multi-day event including a bicycle criterium, parade, entertainment stages, beer garden, local food offerings and activities. It also includes a carnival with rides and attractions and a fireworks display at dusk on Saturday.[27]
