On October 19, 2017, Swift announced she would be releasing a new song titled "Gorgeous".[58] It became available the next day through digital retailers and streaming services as a promotional single from the album.[7] The song reached the top ten in Canada and Australia and the top twenty in the United Kingdom and United States. On November 2, Swift announced she would be releasing the second promotional song from Reputation, while posting teasers on her official Twitter and Instagram accounts. The track, titled "Call It What You Want" was released at midnight on November 3, 2017.[8]
