Micky & the Motorcars is an alternative country band formed in Stanley, Idaho that is now based in Austin, Texas. Formed in Idaho originally, the band has it's roots from two of the founding members, Mickey and Gary Braun, are the sons of outlaw country and western swing musical icon Muzzie Braun of Stanley, Idaho and are the brothers of Cody Braun and Willy Braun of the Texas based roots rock band Reckless Kelly.
