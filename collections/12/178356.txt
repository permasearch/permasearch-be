The film depicts the apes and humans in armed conflict, and follows Caesar and his followers as they track down the mysterious Colonel, a human paramilitary leader, and search for a new home. Serkis returned as Caesar, Woody Harrelson played the villainous Colonel, and Steve Zahn played Bad Ape.[97][98] It earned widespread critical acclaim; reviewers praised the effects and narrative, and found the film a fitting conclusion to Caesar's story.[99][100] It earned $491 million around the globe.[96] In October 2016, it was reported that a fourth film in the new series was being discussed.[101][102]
