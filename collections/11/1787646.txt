A second season was announced and began airing on October 4, 2015.[5] The first opening theme song is "I'm A Believer" (アイム•ア•ビリーバー) by SPYAIR; the ending theme song is "Climber" (クライマー) by Galileo Galilei. From episode 14 on, the opening is "FLY HIGH" performed by Burnout Syndromes and the ending is "Hatsunetsu (Fever)" (発熱 - はつねつ) by Tacica.
