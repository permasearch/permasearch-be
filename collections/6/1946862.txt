On 24 April 2015, Wakefield received two standing ovations from the students at Life Chiropractic College West when he told them to oppose Senate Bill SB277, a bill which proposes elimination of non-medical vaccine exemptions.[136] Wakefield had previously been a featured speaker at a 2014 "California Jam" gathering of chiropractors,[137] as well as a 2015 "California Jam" seminar, with continuing education credits, sponsored by Life Chiropractic College West.[138] On 3 July 2015, Wakefield participated in a protest held in Santa Monica, California, against SB 277,[139] a recently enacted bill which removed the personal belief exemption to school vaccine requirements in California state law.[140]
