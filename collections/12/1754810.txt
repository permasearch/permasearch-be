Descendants 3 is written and produced by Sara Parriott and Josann McGibbon, and is directed and executive produced by Kenny Ortega.[1] Wendy Japhet is a producer on the film,[2] with Ortega, Sara Parriott, Josann McGibbon, and Japhet also serving as executive producers.[3] Mark Hofeling and Kara Saun return as production designer and costume designer on the sequel, respectively.[2] In addition to playing Dr. Facilier, Jamal Sims also serves as the film's choreographer,[5][7] along with Ortega who has served as choreographer for all three Descendants films.[1]
