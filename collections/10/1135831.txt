The first film in the DCEU was Man of Steel (2013) followed by Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016), Suicide Squad (2016), Wonder Woman (2017), and Justice League (2017). Future films with scheduled release dates include: Aquaman in 2018, Shazam! in 2019 and Birds of Prey, Wonder Woman 1984, Cyborg and Green Lantern Corps in 2020. A multitude of other projects are in various stages of development.
