Black Gives Way to Blue debuted at No. 5 on the Billboard 200.[183] On May 18, 2010, the album was certified gold by the RIAA[13] for selling over 500,000 copies in the U.S.[184] The singles "Check My Brain" and "Your Decision" reached No. 1 on Billboard's Mainstream Rock Tracks, while "Lesson Learned" reached No. 4.[15] "Check My Brain" was also the band's first #1 song on the Alternative Songs chart,[185] and on the Hot Rock Songs chart,[186] it also reached No. 92 on Billboard's Hot 100, becoming the band's first single to appear on the chart.[23]
