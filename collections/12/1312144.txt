At the end of the season, De Gea was shortlisted for the PFA Players' Player of the Year and the PFA Young Player of the Year, before losing out to Eden Hazard and Harry Kane respectively.[95][96][97] On 26 April 2015, De Gea was named in the PFA Team of the Year as its goalkeeper and its only Manchester United player.[98] He also won both Fans' Player of the Year and Players' Player of the Year awards for the second consecutive season.[99][100] His save against Everton was voted as Premier League Save of the Season by Match of the Day, the second consecutive year he won the award.[101]
