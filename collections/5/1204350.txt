In the United Kingdom, Family Guy premiered in September 1999, originally on Channel 4 and Sky One. In January 2005, FX (now Fox) began broadcasting the show.[211] From October 2005, BBC Two[212] started screening Family Guy before switching to BBC Three on September 2006.[213] In March 2015, it was announced that season 14 of Family Guy and all of MacFarlane's other cartoons will transfer to ITV2, premiering on February 29, 2016,[214][215] while the BBC would continue to hold the rights until 2017 for older episodes.[216][217]
