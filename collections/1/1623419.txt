The first -300ER was delivered to Air France on April 29, 2004.[201] The -300ER is the best-selling 777 variant, having surpassed the -200ER in orders in 2010 and deliveries in 2013.[1] As of September 2017, -300ER deliveries to 45 different customers totalled 758, with 71 unfilled orders.[1] As of July 2017, 739 -300ER aircraft were in service.[10]
