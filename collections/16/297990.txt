The fourth major update, titled "Next", will be released for all platforms on 24 July 2018, alongside the game's release for the Xbox One, which will include all four updates.[29] The update will have a "full multiplayer experience" which includes exploration, base building, and ground and space travel and combat. Additionally, the Next update will include support for Tencent's WeGame distribution platform in China, which Hello Games says hosts a significant number of No Man's Sky players.[30]
