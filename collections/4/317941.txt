This version of the song, with session musicians Paul Griffin on piano and Gary Chester on drums, as of 2009 was ranked as the 162nd greatest song of all time, as well as the best song of 1960, by Acclaimed Music.[3] It was ranked at #126 among Rolling Stone 's list of The 500 Greatest Songs of All Time.
