Diana made her debut as a Sloane Ranger in 1979 with a gown by Regamus.[339] Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, the Princess wore outfits and ensembles by numerous notable fashion designers.[343][344][345][346] She also wore ensembles by fashion companies such as Versace, Armani, Chanel, Dior and Clarks.[331][334][344][347] Among her iconic outfits are a décolleté by David and Elizabeth Emanuel worn by a newly engaged Diana at a charity event,[341] a cocktail dress by Christina Stambolian, commonly known as the "Revenge Dress", which she wore after Charles's admission of adultery,[348] an evening gown by Victor Edelstein that she wore to a reception at the White House and later became known as the "Travolta dress",[331][316][339] and a Catherine Walker pearl-encrusted gown and jacket dubbed the "Elvis Dress",[347][339] which she wore for the first time on an official visit to Hong Kong.[327][349]
