A revival at the Regent's Park Open Air Theatre from 3 August to 15 September 2018 is directed by Maria Aberg, choreographed by Lizzi Gee and designed by Tom Scutt. It stars Marc Antolin as Seymour, Jemima Rooper as Audrey, Forbes Masson as Mr Mushnik, Matt Willis as Orin and American drag performer Vicky Vox as Audrey II.[37][38] The production includes the song "Mean Green Mother from Outer Space", written for the 1986 film.[39]
