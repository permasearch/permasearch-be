The song has been covered many times since. The Exciters' version charted at number 58 on the Billboard Hot 100 in February 1966. Paul Davis's rendition reached number 52 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the summer of 1970, while Nigel Olsson's version hit number 34 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number 9 on the adult contemporary chart in June 1979.[2] Both Davis and Olsson used ballad arrangements. The song was a success in Britain in the 1978 version by Showaddywaddy, peaking at number 5 in UK Singles Chart in July 1978.
