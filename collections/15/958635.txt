Netflix announced the new series in June 2017. The show stars Tony Danza as "a disgraced, former NYPD officer who never followed the rules",[7] and Josh Groban as his son, Tony Jr., "an earnest, obsessively honest NYPD detective who makes a point of always following the rules".[8] The show was created and is written by Andy Breckman, who created and wrote the Emmy Award-winning USA Network series Monk.
