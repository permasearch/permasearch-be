Michael Gregory Mizanin was born on October 8, 1980[4] in Parma, Ohio,[4][5] where he was raised. His parents are divorced; he has a step-father and two half-siblings.[6][7][8][9][10] He attended Normandy High School and was the captain of its basketball and cross country teams.[4][5] He also participated in swimming, was a member of the student government and edited the yearbook.[4] He then attended Miami University, where he was a member of the Theta Chi fraternity and studied business at the Richard T. Farmer School of Business before he was cast as a member of The Real World (2001).[11]
