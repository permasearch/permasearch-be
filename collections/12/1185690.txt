In the 2012 National League Championship Series Beltrán batted .300 with 2 RBI and 2 stolen bases.[82] He played in 6 of the 7 games in the series, missing most of game 3 and all of game 4 after suffering a knee injury.[83] Playing in his 3rd NLCS game 7 in his career, Beltrán went 1-4 at the plate as the Cardinals lost 0-9 to the Giants.[80]
