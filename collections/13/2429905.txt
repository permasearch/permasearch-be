The Office of the Ombudsman for Bermuda[25] was established by the Bermuda Constitution and is governed by the Ombudsman Act 2004.[26] The first National Ombudsman for Bermuda, Arlene Brock, was appointed on 1 August 2005 by the Governor after consultation with the Premier who first consulted with the Opposition Leader. The Ombudsman investigates complaints about the administrative actions of Public Authorities, including Government Departments, Boards and Bodies established or funded by the Legislature.
