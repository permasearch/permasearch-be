With Doublelift coming back from his break as the TSM's starting ADC, the team looked once again to make it to the League of Legends World Championship. They ended the season at 14-4[75] being tied with Immortals in wins but having lesser losses, making them ending the season in first place. TSM met with Team Dignitas in the semifinals and defeated them 3-1. TSM then continued on to represent North America as their first seed, defeating Immortals 3-1 in the finals, becoming the first organization to win three NA LCS finals in a row.[76]
