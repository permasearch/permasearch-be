The miniseries Convergence released in April 2015, features a pre-New 52 version of a married Superman and Lois Lane. The couple is expecting the impending birth of their child, as Superman is called to protect the city.[18] Convergence shows the birth of their son, Jonathan Samuel Kent (Clark and Lois' biological child in DC Comics canon).[19] Following Convergence, DC announced a spin-off comic book series Superman: Lois and Clark.[20] The eight-issue series debuting in October 2015 by Dan Jurgens and Lee Weeks is set several years after the Convergence event. The series focus on Clark and Lois' relationship and their son, Jonathan, living in the post-Flashpoint DC Universe.
