The APRA Music Awards are presented by the Australasian Performing Right Association (APRA). The awards recognise achievements by ARPA's "outstanding songwriter, composer and publisher members in Popular Contemporary, Art Music and Screen Music".[28] Nominations and winners are determined by the more than 60,000 eligible voting members of APRA, by members of its board, or by "APRA based statistical analysis".[29] Mars has won one award of one nomination.
