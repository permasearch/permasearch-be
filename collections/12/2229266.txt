The company was incorporated in New Jersey in 1970.[9] It has an operating subsidiary, KBI Inc., which was originally formed as a joint venture with AstraZeneca.[9] In November 1993, Merck & Co. completed a $6 billion purchase of Medco Containment Services Inc., one of the largest mail-order pharmacy and managed-care drug companies.[10] Merck & Co. spun Medco off ten years later, and on August 20, 2003, Medco Health Solutions began trading on the New York Stock Exchange.[11] (In 2012, Medco merged with, and became a wholly owned subsidiary of, Express Scripts Holding Co.)[12]
