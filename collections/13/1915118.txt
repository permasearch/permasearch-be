Gifford Pinchot died on October 4, 1946, aged 81, from leukemia. He had a wife, Cornelia Bryce,[37] and a son, Gifford Bryce Pinchot. He is interred at Milford Cemetery, Pike County, Pennsylvania.[38] Gifford Pinchot was named for Hudson River School artist Sanford Robinson Gifford.[39] Proud of the first Gifford Pinchot's legacy, the family has continued to name their sons Gifford, down to Gifford Pinchot IV.[40]
