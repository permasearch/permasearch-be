Silent Hill 2 was risen by critical acclaim. It was named the fourteenth best game of the PS2 by IGN, saying "It preserved most of the original game's what-might-be-out-there fear, but with major advances to the graphics and sound, the game was able to deliver a far more immersive, frightful and compelling storyline."[108] Silent Hill 2 is considered one of the best horror games of all time by many, as it features on several "best games ever" lists by critics.
