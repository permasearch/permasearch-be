The tour was originally scheduled to begin in Columbus on September 7, 2017. However, Perry revealed on August 17 that due to production delays, the tour had been rescheduled to begin September 19, 2017, with the opening night set in Montreal at the Bell Centre. She also confirmed that Noah Cyrus, Purity Ring, and Carly Rae Jepsen would respectively serve as opening acts from September 19 to November 1, November 7 to December 20, and January 5, 2018 to February 5, 2018.[11] The singer later added a final Canadian date for February 6 with Jepsen.[12] Dates in Latin America were later added to the itinerary, including three dates in Brazil, one in Chile, Argentina and Peru, and five in Mexico.[13]
