Los Angeles International Airport (IATA: LAX, ICAO: KLAX, FAA LID: LAX) is the largest and busiest airport in both the Greater Los Angeles Area and in the state of California, third busiest airport in the United States, and one of the largest international airports in the world. The airport is most often referred to by its IATA airport code (and FAA LID) LAX, with the letters pronounced individually.
