IIFL (formerly known as India Infoline Limited), is an Indian diversified financial services company headquartered in Mumbai. The organisation was founded by Nirmal Jain.[citation needed] IIFL and its group companies are backed by Canadian investor Prem Watsa, private Equity firm General Atlantic and CDC Group, the UK Government's private equity arm.[1][2][3] IIFL is ranked among the top seven financial conglomerates in India [4] and as the top independent financial services firm in India in terms of market capitalisation.[5] Nirmal Jain is the Chairman of the group, while R Venkataraman is the Group Managing Director and Co-Promoter.[6]
