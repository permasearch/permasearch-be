The following year featured Jones solidifying his position as the team's starting shooting guard, as Jones started 66 of 70 games and averaged 12.8 points a game for the season.  The Lakers improved their total record to 53 wins, bolstered by the midseason return of franchise legend Magic Johnson out of retirement.  The Lakers made the playoffs again, but could not defeat the defending NBA champion Houston Rockets, who defeated Los Angeles 3 games to 1 in the first round.  Jones averaged 17.3 points in the series.
