South Carolina Highway 9 (SC 9) is a 258.3-mile-long (416Â km) major state highway in the U.S. state of South Carolina. The route runs from North Myrtle Beach to the North Carolina border upstate. The road is currently the longest state highway in South Carolina. It is signed as a north-south highway (with North Myrtle Beach as its southern terminus and the North Carolina border as its northern) though overall it runs in a east-west direction.[1]
