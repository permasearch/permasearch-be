Mircea Monroe (born March 11, 1982)[1] is an American model and actress, known for her roles on the TV series Episodes as Morning Randolph (2011–2017), Hart of Dixie as Tansy Truitt (2011–2015), Impastor as Alexa Cummings (2015–2016) and Sing It! as Stacey Needles (2016–present). She has also starred in films including Cellular (2004), House of the Dead II (2005), Just Friends (2005), The Change-Up (2011) and Magic Mike (2012).
