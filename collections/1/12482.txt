Luftwaffe losses for August numbered 774 aircraft to all causes, representing 18.5% of all combat aircraft at the beginning of the month.[267] Fighter Command's losses in August were 426 fighters destroyed,[268] amounting to 40 per cent of 1,061 fighters available on 3 August.[269] In addition, 99 German bombers and 27 other types were destroyed between 1 and 29 August.[270]
