In the Half-Life series, the Scientists of Black Mesa are frequently shown operating Retinal Scanners to access locked doors or hidden devices. Characters in the films GoldenEye (1995), Mission: Impossible (1996), Barb Wire (1996), Entrapment (1999), Minority Report (2002) and Paycheck (2003) and Charlie's Angels (film) (2000) utilize or try to deceive retinal scanners.[5]
