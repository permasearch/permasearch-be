"Blye, K., Part 2" was seen by 15.85 million live viewers following its broadcast on February 28, 2012, with a 9.9/15 share among all households, and 3.1/8 share among adults aged 18 to 49.[1][4] A rating point represents one percent of the total number of television sets in American households, and a share means the percentage of television sets in use tuned to the program. In total viewers, "Blye, K., Part 2" came third, behind the original NCIS and American Idol.[5]
