Internationally, the university was ranked 171-180 in the QS University Rankings BRICS University Rankings of 2018.[12] In India, the National Institutional Ranking Framework ranked it 29 overall[13] and 18 among universities in 2018.[14] A study performed by the National Institute of Science, Technology and Development Studies on the performance of universities in India in 1998-2008 listed Madras University as #5 based on publication for that period.[15]
