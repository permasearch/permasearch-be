On the memorial are the names of service members classified as "declared dead" (as the memorial contains names of individuals that died of circumstances other than KIA (Killed in Action), including murder, jeep accidents, drowning, heart attack, tiger attack, snake bites, etc.) [10] and the names of those whose status is unknown, which typically means "missing in action" (MIA). The names are inscribed in Optima typeface. Information about rank, unit, and decorations is not given.
