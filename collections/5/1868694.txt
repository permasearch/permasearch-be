The Yaris Hybrid shares the same powertrain as the Toyota Prius c sold in North America and as Toyota Aqua in Japan. The Prius c will not be available in Europe.[58] It has a 1.5 L gasoline engine with a maximum output of 75 PS (55 kW) and an 45 kW electric motor which combine for a total output of 74 kW. Its NiMH battery has a capacity of 0.9 kWh.
