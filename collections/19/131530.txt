The fees for premium parking at Dallas Cowboys games are estimated at $75 per game, based on season ticket holder parking charges.[105] The fees to park at major concerts and other sporting events will be nearly $40 per space at the new stadium.[106] A shuttle operates between the T&P Station and Cowboys Stadium for all Cowboys regular season and postseason games and selected college football games,[107] which averages approximately 900 riders per game.[107]  For special events like Super Bowl XLV parking prices can increase to as much as $990.[108]
