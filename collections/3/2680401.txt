In September 2017, Ekana International Cricket Stadium was opened to public as it hosted 2017-18 Duleep Trophy. On 06th November 2018 Ekana International Cricket Stadium will host its first T20 international match between Indian national cricket team and  West Indies cricket team. It is the second largest stadium in India by capacity after Kolkata's Eden Gardens.[204][205] For decades Lucknow hosted the Sheesh Mahal Cricket Tournament.
