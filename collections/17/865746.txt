The music for Great Grand Masti is composed by Sanjeev-Darshan, Sharib-Toshi, Superbia (Shaan, Gourov, Roshni). The lyrics have been penned by Sameer Anjaan, Manoj Yadav and Kumaar. The music rights have been acquired by Zee Music Company. The first song "Teri Kamar Ko" was released on 21 June 2016. The song 'Lipstick Laga Ke' had become so much popular and was one of the top 20 songs of 2016.
