In 2008, Hazen and Sawyer,P.C. submitted a report titled "Indian River Lagoon Economic Assessment and Analysis Update" to Troy Rice, Director of the Indian River Lagoon National Estuary Program, St. Johns River Water Management District. The report described the estimated 2007 recreational uses and economic value of the Indian River Lagoon to residents and visitors of the five counties that comprise the Lagoon system. The sum of recreational expenditures and recreational use value was estimated at $2.1 billion.[26]
