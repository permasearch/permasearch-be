Internationally, IIT Kharagpur was ranked 308 in the QS World University Rankings of 2018[45] The same rankings ranked it 62 in Asia[47] and 24 among BRICS nations.[46] It was ranked 501-600 in the world by the Times Higher Education World University Rankings of 2018,[48] 87 in Asia[50] and 71 among BRICS & Emerging Economies University Rankings in 2017.[49]
