The Spy Who Loved Me opened with a Royal Premiere attended by Princess Anne at the Odeon Leicester Square in London on 7 July 1977. It grossed $185.4 million worldwide,[18] with $46 million in the United States alone.[19] On 25 August 2006, the film was re-released at the Empire Leicester Square Cinema for one week.[20] It was again shown at the Empire Leicester Square 20 April 2008 when Director Lewis Gilbert attended the first digital screening of the film.
