On review aggregation Rotten Tomatoes, the series holds an approval rating of 48% with an average rating of 5.8/10, based on 25 reviews. The website's critical consensus reads, "Despite a decent ensemble and a few intriguing elements, A MillIon Little Things breaks under the weight of its own emotionally lofty ambitions."[31] Metacritic, which uses a weighted average, assigned the series a score of 51 out of 100 based on 15 critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews."[32]
