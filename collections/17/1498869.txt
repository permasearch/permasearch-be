On December 8, 2009, Perry's mother, Willie Maxine Perry, died at age 64, following an illness.[81] As of 2016, he is unmarried.[82] He lives and works in Southwest Atlanta where he operates the Tyler Perry film and TV studios.[83] In August 2010, it was reported that he had purchased Dean Gardens, a 58-acre (23 ha) estate in the Atlanta suburb of Johns Creek. He tore down the existing 32,000-square-foot (3,000 m2) mansion and built a new, environmentally-friendly home on the property.[84][85]
