San Marcos has also become known for its abundance of open space and parks in recent years.[citation needed] Miles of trails have been planned and constructed to facilitate biking throughout the city.[citation needed] A popular destination is the top of Double Peak Park, which features a panorama of northern San Diego County from the highest coastal peak in the county.[citation needed] Park at Discovery Lake for lake and trail access, one of which leads up to Double Peak Park.  On a clear day one can see the San Bernardino Mountains over Greater Los Angeles and the skyscrapers of downtown San Diego.[citation needed]
