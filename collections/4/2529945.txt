Elements from the Halo universe have also appeared in other games. A Halo-based character, SPARTAN Nicole-458, appeared in Dead or Alive 4, a product of the collaboration between Tecmo's Team Ninja and Microsoft's Bungie Studios.[74] The Halo theme tune was also available as downloadable content for Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock.[75] An Arbiter appears as a playable character in Killer Instinct: Season Three, alongside a Halo-themed stage.[76]
