Wildmutt/Wildpup is a Vulpimancer from the planet Vulpin; he is a large dog-like creature with no visible eyes or tail. The eyeless Vulpimancers use echolocation, heightened smell, and thermographic neck gills to navigate. Wildmutt's porcupine-like quills supplement his sonar and can be used as projectiles or protect him while he is rolling.
