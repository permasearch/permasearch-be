Afghanistan, Iran, Iraq, Syria,[107] Turkey, and former members of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, such as Azerbaijan,[3][4] were reported to have had Asiatic lions[63][68] and Caspian or Siberian[c] tigers.[4][70][109][110] Besides Asia, lions and tigers had occurred in Europe, in the region of the Black Sea, with tigers occurring in Ciscaucasia, and lions occurring in the Balkans,[111] up to Thrace and Macedonia, and possibly the Danube River, at least.[3][4][64]
