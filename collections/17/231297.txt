The highest recorded temperature was 106 °F (41 °C) on August 6, 1918, and on July 20, 1930.[74] while the lowest recorded temperature was −15 °F (−26 °C) on February 11, 1899, right before the Great Blizzard of 1899.[68] During a typical year, the city averages about 37 days at or above 90 °F (32 °C) and 64 nights at or below 32 °F (0 °C).[65] On average, the first day at or below 32 °F (0 °C) is November 18 and the last day is March 27.[75][76]
