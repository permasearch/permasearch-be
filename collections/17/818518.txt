The first local production, after the Off-Broadway show, opened at the Playbox Theatre, Melbourne on 15 November 1971. The cast included Colleen Hewett, Collette Mann, Christopher Pate and George Spartels. The producers, Aztec Services and Williamson Edgley Theatres, launched a second production in Sydney, opening 10 April 1972, with Peta Toppano, John Waters and Marty Rhone. Melbourne played 504 performances and Sydney 507, before the two companies went on tour performing another 700 shows.[16]
