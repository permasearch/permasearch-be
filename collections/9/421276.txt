In 2017, Thompson appeared as Mrs. Potts (originally played by Angela Lansbury in the 1991 animated film) in Disney's live action film Beauty and the Beast directed by Bill Condon.[137] It grossed $1.2 billion worldwide, making it the 10th highest-grossing film of all time as of mid-2017. She also had a supporting role as a hippy in Noah Baumbach's dramedy The Meyerowitz Stories, which played in competition at Cannes and received critical acclaim.[138] She followed it with a starring role in the Richard Eyre film The Children Act, a drama about a family who refuse cancer treatment for their son based on religious beliefs.
