On June 29, 2017, Tyler released a song titled "Who Dat Boy" featuring A$AP Rocky on a new YouTube channel following many promotional countdown posts on his social media accounts. Later that night, he released the song on streaming services with a new song titled "911 / Mr. Lonely" featuring Steve Lacy, Frank Ocean, and Anna of the North. On July 6, 2017, he announced the name, track-list and release date of his fourth album, Flower Boy,[48] which was released on July 21, 2017.[49]
