Her first job as a staff writer was for the unaired NBC post-apocalyptic series Day One, which she got coming out of graduate school.[3] Shortly thereafter, she was hired as a staff writer on the short-lived FX series Terriers, of which she wrote two episodes. In 2011, she joined the writing staff of the acclaimed AMC horror drama series The Walking Dead as a story editor. She was promoted to producer for season three (2012–2013), co-executive producer for season five (2014–2015), and showrunner for the upcoming ninth season in 2018.
