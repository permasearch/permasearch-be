In 1981, the Heels compiled a 10–2 record and finished the season by beating Arkansas in the Gator Bowl.[83] That would be Carolina's last season of double digit wins for 15 years. The next two seasons saw Carolina finish 8–4.[84][85] After the 1982 season, Carolina upset Texas in the Sun Bowl.[86] In 1983, they would lose to Florida State in the Peach Bowl.[87]
