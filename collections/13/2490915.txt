Carl is the son of a cruel father who worked in a carpet factory, and made him work there as a child, even on Christmas.[5] His father was also the former star of a series of amateur porn videos on VHS that Carl owns and treasures.[6] Carl's mother lives at a VA Hospital, and occasionally asks him for very small amounts of money such as $10 for food, which Carl immediately dismisses. Carl sometimes loses tolerance when she asks him for favors, but has been shown on occasions that he genuinely loves her.
