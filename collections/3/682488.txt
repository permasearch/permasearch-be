Forbes magazine rated Albuquerque as the best city in America for business and careers in 2006[75] and as the 13th best (out of 200 metro areas) in 2008.[76] The city was rated seventh among America's Engineering Capitals in 2014 by Forbes magazine.[77] Albuquerque ranked among the Top 10 Best Cities to Live by U.S. News & World Report in 2009[78] and was recognized as the fourth best place to live for families by the TLC network.[79] It was ranked among the Top Best Cities for Jobs in 2007[80] and among the Top 50 Best Places to Live and Play by National Geographic Adventure magazine.[81]
