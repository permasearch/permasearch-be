Alexander was killed on June 4, 2008.[20] He sustained 27 to 29 stab wounds, his throat had been slit, and he had suffered a gunshot wound to the head.[21][22] Medical Examiner Kevin Horn testified that Alexander's jugular vein, common carotid artery, and trachea had been slashed and that Alexander had defensive wounds on his hands. Horn further testified that Alexander "may have" been dead at the time the gunshot was inflicted, and that the back wounds were shallow.[23][24] Alexander's death was ruled a homicide. He was buried at the Olivewood Cemetery in Riverside, California.[25]
