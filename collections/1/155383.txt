Former Jets star cornerback Darrelle Revis signed with the Patriots in the offseason, giving New England a lift to their defense and secondary. Rex Ryan was quoted as being "sick to his stomach" seeing Revis in a Patriots uniform.[48] The first meeting of the season came on October 16, with the Patriots hosting the Jets on Thursday Night Football for the second year in a row. Despite being 1–5, the Jets battled closely with the 4–2 Patriots, nearly completing a come from behind victory, but their 58-yard field goal attempt was blocked by Chris Jones in the game's final seconds; the final score 27–25.[49]
