Lagavulin 16 is the preferred scotch of Ron Swanson, a character on NBC's Parks and Recreation. In "London Part II", the second episode of the show's sixth season, he visits the distillery itself. Nick Offerman, the actor who plays the character, is a Lagavulin drinker in real life, referring to it in an interview as "mother's milk".[4] In the show's finale, Ron purchases a 51% share in the distillery when urged to diversify his personal portfolio of assets.
