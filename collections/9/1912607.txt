Cetera was born and raised in the Morgan Park section located on the far South Side of Chicago, Illinois, United States, North America.[18] He was the second of six children and is of Polish[1][19] and Hungarian[20] descent. His father worked as a machinist.[21][22] Cetera's siblings include two brothers, Tim Cetera (who also recorded an album with Ricky Nelson in the early 1970s)[23] and Kenny Cetera,[18] who are listed as contributing musicians on some of the recordings he made with Chicago[24][25] and on some of his solo recordings.[26][27][28][29] Kenny remembers the family harmonizing together while he was growing up.[30]
