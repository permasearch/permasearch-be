In January 2007, The Weinstein Company acquired U.S distribution rights to the film.[33] I'm Not There had its world premiere at the Telluride Film Festival on August 31, 2007.[34] The film went onto screen at the Toronto International Film Festival,[35] London Film Festival,[36] and the New York Film Festival.[37] The film opened in limited release in the United States on November 21, 2007.[38] It was then released in Germany on February 28, 2008, by Tobis Film.[39]
