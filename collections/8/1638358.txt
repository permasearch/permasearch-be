Shruti is the yesteryear, multilingual star actress and politician. She was the most prominent and promising actress of the 90's. She has been cast more than 150 films. She has bagged more number of awards in her career. She is a house hold name. She had starred many women oriented movies. She was famous for her tear jerking roles. She has acted with all the leading stars of the 90's.She has not only acted in Kannada but also in tamil and malayalam. She is serving as the chief Secretary in BJP women's wing of Karnataka. She won the Bigg Boss 3 thereby fulfilling her daughter's dream.
