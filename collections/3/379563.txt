SXSW is the highest revenue-producing event (outside of athletic and other events associated with the University of Texas) for the Austin economy, with an estimated economic impact of $190.3 million in 2012[132] increasing to $218 million in 2013,[133] $315 million in 2014,[134] $317 million in 2015,[135] and $325 million in 2016.[136] (In comparison, Super Bowl LI brought a $347 million economic impact to the Houston economy[137] and the 2016 Final Four brought a $324 million economic impact to the economy of Phoenix, Arizona.[138])
