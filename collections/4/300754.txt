"Stand by You" is the second single by American singer and songwriter Rachel Platten's major label debut studio album, Wildfire (2016). The song was released on September 11, 2015, by Columbia Records.[1] The song peaked at number 37 on the Billboard Hot 100, making this her second consecutive top-40 song. "Stand by You" reached the top of the Adult Top 40 chart in February 2016.[2]
