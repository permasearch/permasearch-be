Kenneth inadvertently starts up a mini-cab business whilst Joyce is asked to go to a conference in the city of Barcelona, but panics when she finds out she has to deliver a speech in Spanish. Elsewhere, Sheron's mother Loretta arrives at the Solana to join the family on holiday, and it turns out she has already made quite an impression in Benidorm from the night before. Joey thinks he has managed to sign Tiger and himself up for a free trip to Ibiza, but things do not go according to plan.
