Lone Survivor grossed $125,095,601 in the United States and Canada;[2] coupled with its international take of $29,707,311, the film accumulated $154,802,912 in worldwide box office totals.[2][66] Outside of North America, the film's biggest markets were in Australia, the United Kingdom, Spain, Japan, France, South Korea, and Germany; the film grossed approximately $3.5 million in Australia, $3.4 million in the United Kingdom, $2.5 million in Spain, $2.2 million in Japan, $1.5 million in France, $1.2 million in South Korea, and $1 million in Germany.[66] In North America, Lone Survivor is the 24th-highest-grossing film of 2013,[86] and the sixth-highest-grossing R-rated film of that year.[87]
