Bengal Tiger is a 2015 Indian Telugu action comedy film written and directed by Sampath Nandi. It was produced by K. K. Radha Mohan's Sri Satya Sai Arts and features Ravi Teja, Tamannaah and Raashi Khanna in the lead roles. Boman Irani, Nagineedu, Rao Ramesh, and Sayaji Shinde play supporting roles.
