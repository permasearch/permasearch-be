Aldrin was born January 20, 1930, in Mountainside Hospital, in Glen Ridge, New Jersey.[3][4] His parents were Edwin Eugene Aldrin Sr. (1896–1974), a career military man, and Marion Aldrin (née Moon, 1903–1968), who lived in neighboring Montclair.[5][6] He is of Scottish,[7] Swedish, and German ancestry. Aldrin was a Boy Scout and earned the rank of Tenderfoot Scout.[8]
