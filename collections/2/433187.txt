As an actress she has appeared in the movies 2 Fast 2 Furious,[7] D.E.B.S., Sin City, Dead or Alive, War and Mutant Chronicles.[citation needed] She was cast as Tatsu Yamashiro in Arrow season 3,[8] but was replaced by Rila Fukushima due to a scheduling conflict.[9] In September 2007, she designed a line of jeans for Levi's Asian market called + D.[10][11] In 2017, she returned to the runway for Moschino's fashion show.[12]
