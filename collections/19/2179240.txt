Since 2010,[10] either the Municipality Agoo has been regularly declared an election hotspot[10][11][12] due to incidences of violence[11][12][13] during national and local election periods. Major incidents include the murder of former Tubao Vice Mayor Lazaro Gayo outside his law office near Agoo's Municipal Hall,[11] and an alleged assassination attempt on Tubao Mayor Dante Garcia in the same year;[14][13] and an alleged assassination attempt on former Congressman Eufranio Eriguel in 2016.[15]
