With the impending Brexit negotiations, the House of Lords has produced a report entitled "Brexit: the Crown Dependencies" which states the "UK Government must continue to fulfil its constitutional obligations to represent the interests of the Crown Dependencies in international relations, even where these differ from those of the UK, both during the Brexit negotiations and beyond." [45] In the Great Repeal Bill white paper published on 30 March 2017 the UK government states "The Government is committed to engaging with the Crown Dependencies, Gibraltar and the other Overseas Territories as we leave the EU."[46]:ch.5
