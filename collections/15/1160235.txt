The man was known as Elendil the Tall (Tolkien put his height at "more than man high by nearly half a ranga",[1] almost 8' or 240 cm), Elendil the Faithful ('Elendil Voronda' or 'Vorondo') or Elendil the Fair, he was the father of Isildur and Anárion, last lord of Andúnië, and the first High King of Arnor and Gondor.
