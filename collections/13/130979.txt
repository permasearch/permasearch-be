Over the seasons, Tony and Ziva's flirtation attracted much media attention, commentary, and praise.[24][48][87][88] Maureen Ryan of the Chicago Tribune wrote that the characters' "love-hate banter gives the show extra zip" during Cote de Pablo's first year on the show.[11] They have been called a "power couple" by Entertainment Weekly[78] and "one of TV's hottest couples" by TV Guide.[3] In May 2009, they were pictured on the cover of TV Guide Magazine and described as "TV's hottest love-hate relationship".[89]
