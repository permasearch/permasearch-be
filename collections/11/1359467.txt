Episode Summary: Alexandria "Alex" Sekella is an aspiring dancer from Allentown, Pennsylvania who is pregnant by her boyfriend, Matt McCann. Prior to the birth, the episode focuses on McCann's undependability and Sekella's mother making it clear that if Sekella does not put the baby up for adoption, Sekella and the baby will have no place to live. Sekella struggles with the decision of whether to keep the baby or to allow her close friend's parents to adopt the baby and execute a very open adoption. Their daughter, Arabella "Bella" Elizabeth Sekella-McCann, is born healthy on July 18, 2011. The remainder of the episode focuses on Alex's dire financial and housing troubles due to her decision to keep the baby, and her eventual relationship problems with McCann.
