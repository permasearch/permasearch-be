"Maria Maria" is a song by Santana featuring The Product G&B.[1] The song was produced by Wyclef Jean and Jerry Duplessis.[2]  At the 2000 Grammy Awards, the song won the Grammy Award for Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal. Despite other lead singers from the album receiving awards, The Product G&B did not receive a Grammy Award.[3] Marc Anthony also sang on a version of the song.
