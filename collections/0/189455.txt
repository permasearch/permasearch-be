In January 2018, Kiernan Shipka was cast as Sabrina Spellman.[1] Shipka was Aguirre-Sacasa's first choice for the role.[19] The next month, Jaz Sinclair was cast as Rosalind "Roz" Walker,[6] along with Michelle Gomez as Mary Wardwell / Madam Satan, Chance Perdomo as Ambrose Spellman,[4] Lucy Davis as Hilda Spellman,[3] Miranda Otto as Zelda Spellman,[10] and Richard Coyle as Father Faustus Blackwood.[9] In March 2018, Ross Lynch joined the cast as Harvey Kinkle,[2] while Tati Gabrielle was cast as Prudence Night.[7]
