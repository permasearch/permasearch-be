Evancho promoted the album on television and at concerts.[102][103] Her first solo concert television special for the 2011 PBS Great Performances series consisted of performances of largely the same songs as the album.[104][105] The special, titled Dream With Me In Concert, was the most broadcast program of the year on the PBS network.[106] It "became one of the most viewed specials in the 38-year-history of the Great Performances series [and] raised record amounts of money for PBS stations."[107] Evancho was the youngest person ever to have a special on the series.[108] The concert was hosted by David Foster,[109] with guest Conrad Tao.[110][111] The CD/DVD release[112] reached No. 1 on Billboard's Top Music Video chart.[93][113] Evancho performed "Nessun dorma" as a guest artist at the June 2011 season finale of Britain's Got Talent,[36][114] and at the September 2011 season finale of America's Got Talent.[115]
