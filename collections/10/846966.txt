In January 2016, a list of actors was revealed for the role of young Han Solo, including Miles Teller, Ansel Elgort, Dave Franco, Jack Reynor, Scott Eastwood, Logan Lerman, Emory Cohen and Blake Jenner.[59] In March 2016, it was reported that Alden Ehrenreich, Reynor and Taron Egerton were on a shortlist for the role.[60] In May 2016, Ehrenreich was reported to have been cast as the young Han Solo,[18] and was revealed in the role at Star Wars Celebration: Celebration Europe III two months later.[19] Miller called casting the role one of "the hardest casting challenges of all time", adding that they "saw over 3,000 people for the part".[61]
