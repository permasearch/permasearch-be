"Parition" and "Yoncé" were both performed by Beyoncé at the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards on August 25 as part of a medley consisting of songs from her fifth studio album; she appeared wearing a bejeweled bodysuit and performed a dance choreography on a pole for the former song; "Yoncé" was performed later during her set.[107] HitFix writer Melinda Newman noted that the performance of "Partition" emulated looks and poses from its clip.[108] Nadeska Alexis from MTV News felt that the singer kept the audience "lifted" with the performance of "Yoncé".[109] In a review of her performance, Hilary Hughes of Fuse noted the performance "elicited nothing but chills and rapturous screams".[110]
