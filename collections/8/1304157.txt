Reviews for "Whatever You Like" were generally favorable. Jared W. Dillon of Sputnikmusic called the song a "more sophisticated take" on Lil Wayne's "Lollipop".[6] Ian Cohen of Pitchfork Media noted that the song contained virtually no rapping while praising the synth-driven beat.[7] Blender magazine ranked the song number 14 their Top 144 Songs of 2008[8] and number 18 on MTV's Commercially Released Hip-Hop Singles.[9]
