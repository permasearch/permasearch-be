In 1947, India attain independence from the British. Further in 1956, the reorganization of the states of India took place and state boundaries were redrawn based on linguistic demographics.  Kodagu or Coorg (the birthplace of the Cauvery), became a part of Mysore state[12]. Parts of Malabar which earlier formed part of Madras Presidency went to Kerala[13].  Puducherry had already become a de facto Union territory in 1954.
