With the coming of free agency after the 1976 season, and with Oakland owner Finley unwilling to pay the higher salary that Jackson would ask for, he was traded on April 2 in a six-player deal just prior to the start of the season. Along with pitcher Ken Holtzman and minor leaguer Bill VanBommell, Jackson went to the Baltimore Orioles for Don Baylor, Mike Torrez, and Paul Mitchell.[38]
