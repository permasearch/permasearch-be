The North Stars would achieve success in their first year of existence by finishing in fourth place in the West Division with a record of 27-32-15, and advancing to the playoffs.[5] During the 1968 playoffs, the North Stars defeated the Los Angeles Kings in seven games after losing the first two in the series.[5] In the next round, the West finals, the North Stars faced the St. Louis Blues in a series which would also go to a seventh game.[5] Minnesota was one game away from advancing to the Stanley Cup Finals, but in the deciding game, they lost in double overtime.[5]
