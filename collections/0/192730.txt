The film premiered at the Odeon Leicester Square theater in London on November 11, 2013.[64] The film was later released on November 22, 2013, in the United States in conventional and IMAX theaters. The film was also shown in the 4DX format in selected international territories. It features motion-enhanced seating, wind, water sprays, strobe lightning, and scent-based special effects.[65][66][67][68] The film was released in 4,165 theaters in the US alone.[2]
