Erin Kellyman appears as Enfys Nest, the leader of a gang of pirates called Cloud Riders.[36] Jon Favreau voices Rio Durant, "a very cool and important alien character" and member of Beckett's crew,[37][38] and Linda Hunt voices Lady Proxima, the serpent-like leader of the gang to which teenage Han and Qi'ra belong. Ian Kenny portrays Rebolt[39] while Clint Howard portrays Ralakili.[40] Additionally, Anthony Daniels cameos as Tak, enslaved alongside Chewbacca,[41] Kiran Shah plays Karjj and Warwick Davis briefly reprises his role from the film The Phantom Menace as Weazel, a Cloud Rider.[42] Ray Park reprises his role as Maul,[43] with Sam Witwer providing the character's voice, reprising the role from the Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Star Wars Rebels animated TV series.[44]
