Hum Tumhare Hain Sanam grossed ₹22.26 crore (US$3.5 million) in India and $2.55 million (₹12.49 crore) in other countries, for a worldwide total of ₹34.76 crore (US$5.4 million), against its ₹12 crore (US$1.9 million) budget. It had a worldwide weekend opening of ₹11.11 crore (US$1.7 million), and grossed ₹17.23 crore (US$2.7 million) in its first week.[2] It is the 4th-highest-grossing Bollywood film of 2002 worldwide.[12]
