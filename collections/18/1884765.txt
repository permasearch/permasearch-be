Many musicians are keeping the free jazz style alive in the 21st century. Two major scenes are based in New York and Chicago. In New York, players include Charles Gayle, William Parker, Matana Roberts, Chad Taylor, John Zorn, Assif Tsahar, Tom Abbs, Kenny Werner, and Chris Speed. In Chicago, notable performers are Fred Anderson (deceased 2010), Nicole Mitchell, Ernest Dawkins, Ken Vandermark, and Hamid Drake. There are also many free-jazz musicians in Europe.
