A 25' styrofoam Unconditional Surrender sculpture, built in 2005 and on loan from Seward Johnson's Sculpture Foundation was installed at the 42-acre Grounds for Sculpture (GFS) in Hamilton, New Jersey as part of a retrospective honoring Johnson which ran from May 4, 2014 through July 1, 2015.[20][21][22] Writing up this event, a staff writer for The Trentonian described Unconditional Surrender as a "masterpiece".[22]
