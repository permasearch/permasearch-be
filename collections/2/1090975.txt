"Turn Up the Music" also reached the top twenty in some European countries. It peaked at number fifteen on the Hungarian Airplay Chart,[69] and number 20 on the Slovak Airplay Chart.[70] On the Irish Singles Chart, "Turn Up the Music" peaked at number 12 and spent 18 weeks in the chart.[71][72] In the United Kingdom, the song first charted on March 10, 2012 in a cover version by Beautiful People, reaching the top 100.[73] On April 7, 2012, the Chris Brown version, having sold 83,777 copies in its first week, became Brown's first UK number one single.[74][75] That same week, it also debuted at number one on the Scottish Singles Chart.[76] "Turn Up the Music" has sold 273,000 copies in the UK as of December 2012.[77]
