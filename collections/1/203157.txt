Minsk has the highest crime rate in Belarus – 193.5 crimes per 10,000 citizens.[23][24] 20–25% of all serious crimes in Belarus, 55% of bribes and 67% of mobile phone thefts are committed in Minsk.[23][25] However, attorney general Grigory Vasilevich stated that homicide rate in Minsk in 2008 was "relatively fine".[26]
