Marshall was signed by the Jacksonville Jaguars as a free-agent after going undrafted in the 2015 NFL Draft[13] He played primarily on special teams including returning kickoffs for most of the season but saw his role on defense increase the last four weeks of the season. He started his first NFL game week 17 against the Houston Texans.[14] On September 3, 2016, he was released by the Jaguars.[15] He was signed to the Jaguars' practice squad the next day.[16]
