NE1fm launched on 8 June 2007, the first full-time community radio station in the area.[272] Newcastle Student Radio is run by students from both of the city's universities, broadcasting from Newcastle University's students' union building during term time.[273] Radio Tyneside[274] has been the voluntary hospital radio service for most hospitals across Newcastle and Gateshead since 1951, broadcasting on Hospedia [275] and online. The city also has a Radio Lollipop station based at the Great North Children's Hospital in the Newcastle Royal Victoria Infirmary.
