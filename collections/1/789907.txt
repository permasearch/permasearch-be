After the 2012–13 season, the Hornets renamed themselves the Pelicans.[172] Davis donated $65,000 toward the construction of a basketball court at his alma mater, Perspectives.[173][174] On October 16, 2013, during the preseason of Davis' sophomore year for the 2013–14 New Orleans Pelicans', the Pelicans exercised the team's 3rd-year option on Davis' contract.[169]
