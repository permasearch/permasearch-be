Throughout the 2010s, there has been a resurgence of popularity in thrash metal, with all the Big Four bands (Metallica, Megadeth, Slayer and Anthrax) debuting within the top 5 or top 10 on the Billboard 200,[15][16][17][18][19][20] while other veteran thrash metal acts, such as Testament, Overkill, Exodus, Metal Church and Death Angel, began to achieve more greater success on the aforementioned chart.[21][22][23][24][25] Some of the most successful thrash metal acts that got their start in the 21st century are Bonded by Blood, Evile, Gama Bomb, Havok, Municipal Waste, Suicidal Angels, Vektor, Warbringer and others, thus giving birth to the "thrash metal revival movement".
