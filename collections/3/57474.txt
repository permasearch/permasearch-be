In this episode Mehrotra and Singh gave the first dance performance of the day, dedicating it to Shetty. The did a freestyle Bollywood dance on the tune of Shetty’s super hit song ‘’Golmaal.’’ The dance had a lot of moves from South Indian movies. Shetty loved the performance, and he complimented stating that it looked like a Rohit Shetty film. However, he also felt that the dancers were hurrying the steps. Both scored four points each. The auto rickshaw which the dancers used in this act was later used by both the judges for a little bit of fun. The audience really enjoyed this fun part.
