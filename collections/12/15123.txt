The Crown of Ptolemy is the third and last book in the Percy Jackson and the Olympians and The Kane Chronicles crossover series. It was published in the back of the paperback version of The House of Hades on March 31, 2015, and later as an ebook and audiobook.[18] The ebook edition was released May 12, 2015.[19] It was later republished as part of a collection entitled Demigods & Magicians, released in 2016.[15]
