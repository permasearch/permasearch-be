Ahimsa does not merely indicate absence of physical violence, but also indicates absence of desire to indulge in any sort of violence.[3] Jains have strongly advocated vegetarianism and nonviolence throughout the ages.[4] Ahimsa being central to the Jain philosophy, Jain Ācāryas have produced, through ages, quite elaborate and detailed doctrinal materials concerning its various aspects.
