The Vow received mixed reviews from critics. Review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes gives the film an approval rating of 29% based on 126 reviews, with an average rating of 4.9/10. The site's consensus reads, "Channing Tatum and Rachel McAdams do their best with what they're given, but The Vow is too shallow and familiar to satisfy the discriminating date-night filmgoer."[10] On Metacritic, which assigns a weighted mean rating to reviews, the film received an average score of 43 out of 100, based on 28 critics, which indicates "mixed or average reviews".[11]
