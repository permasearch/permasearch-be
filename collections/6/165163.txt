On October 21, 2014, cast member Robyn Paris launched a Kickstarter campaign to raise the budget for her comedy mockumentary web series, The Room Actors: Where Are They Now? A Mockumentary. On completion, the campaign had raised US$31,556 (equivalent to $32,621 in 2017) from 385 backers.[81] Although a number of the original cast appeared in the series, Wiseau, Sestero and Holmes are not involved.[82] The series premiered at the 24th Raindance Film Festival on September 30, 2016,[83][84] and debuted on the website Funny or Die on November 30, 2017.[85]
