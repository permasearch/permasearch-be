There were rumours that Madhuri Dixit (Bhansali's heroine from Devdas), would be performing an item number for the film.[20] Other actresses were also speculated to be signed for the song including Aishwarya Rai Bachchan (Bhansali's heroine from Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam, Devdas, and Guzaarish) and Priyanka Chopra.[21] Chopra was eventually confirmed to be performing the song.[22]
