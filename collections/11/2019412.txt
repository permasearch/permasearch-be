Ninenth Year Plan(1997-2002) The main objectives of Ninenth plan was poverty alleviation. The target of the ninenth plan was to increase industrial production by 6% per annum the investment both domestic and foreign was estimated to be Rs. 35 billion. The plan also estimated creation of additional 0.35 million job. Industrial contribution to the GDP was expected to reach 14% by the end of the plan period. Many of these targets could not be achieved during the plan period.
