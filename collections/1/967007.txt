Capcom released Marvel vs. Capcom: Infinite for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows in September 2017. While it features elements from the series, it is not a direct adaptation.[105] To coincide with the game's release, Capcom held a global tournament series called "Battle for the Stones".[106] The winner of the tournament received a cash prize and a light-up Infinity Gauntlet trophy.[107]
