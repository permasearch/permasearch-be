John B. Watson first married Mary Amelia Ickes on 1 October 1904 in Cook County, Illinois.[18]  They had at least two children, also named John and Mary Watson.[19]As an adult, the child Mary was not affectionate with her own child, and "... made several suicide attempts."[20]  Mary's husband was Paul Hartley, and their daughter is the actress, bipolar disorder advocate, and founder of the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention, Mariette Hartley.
