Riz Ahmed was in talks to join the film in August,[45] with Matt Smith, Pedro Pascal, and Matthias Schoenaerts also up for the same role.[46] In September, Michelle Williams entered talks to join the film as a district attorney and Brock's love interest.[47] By October, Jenny Slate,[48] Reid Scott,[49] and Scott Haze were in negotiations to join the film,[50] with Slate up for a role as a scientist.[48] Kelly Marcel was writing the latest draft of the film's script.[51]
