A Special Collector's Edition of the film was released on DVD in 2005.[24] Paramount released Fatal Attraction on Blu-ray Disc on June 9, 2009.[25] The Blu-ray release contained several bonus features from the 2005 DVD, including commentary by director Adrian Lyne, cast and crew interviews, a look at the film's cultural phenomenon, a behind-the-scenes look, rehearsal footage, the alternate ending, and the original theatrical trailer.
