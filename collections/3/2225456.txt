The film opened on 7 December 2005 in New Zealand to positive reviews,[1] and quickly became the highest grossing local film at the New Zealand box-office taking in NZ$7,043,000;[2] and taking in over US$18,297,690 worldwide.[3]  The film was theatrically released on 3 February 2006 in the United States by Magnolia Pictures; and was subsequently released on DVD on 22 December 2006 by Magnolia Home Entertainment.
