The first win for the GT3-R spec Viper came on July 13, 2014 in the Tudor United SportsCar Championship at Mosport[85] followed by a win in the Pirelli World Challenge at the Streets of Toronto in the second race on the 20th of July 2014.[86] Dutch Supercar Challenge squad Team RaceArt won the 2014 and 2015 Super GT class championship in a Viper GT3-R.[87]
