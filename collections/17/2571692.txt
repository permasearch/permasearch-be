The 2018 Women's County Championship began on 6 May and will be completed on 3 June. Each of the eight sides in Division One of the Championship will play seven one-day fixtures, once against each of the other teams.[21] A separate Twenty20 competition will follow the County Championship between 10 June and 1 July with teams organised into a different set of divisions. Each Division One team playing eight matches, one against each of the other teams in the Division.[21]
