In 1993, Bonds left the Pirates to sign a lucrative free agent contract worth a then-record $43.75 million ($72.5 million today) over six years with the Giants, with whom his father had spent the first seven years of his career, and with whom his godfather Willie Mays played 22 of his 24 Major League seasons. The deal was at that time the largest in baseball history, in terms of both total value and average annual salary.[42]
