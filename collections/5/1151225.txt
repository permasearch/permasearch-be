The album debuted at number one on the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums, scoring Foxx's third chart-topper and first since 2009. It also debuted number 10 on the US Billboard 200, with first-week sales of 31,000 copies.[16] It was Foxx's fourth top ten on the Billboard 200. In its second week of sales, the album dropped to number 32 on the chart, selling 11,000 copies, bringing total copies to 42,000 copies.[17]
