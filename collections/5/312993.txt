TCM's library of films spans several decades of cinema and includes thousands of film titles. Besides its deals to broadcast film releases from Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer[10] and Warner Bros. Entertainment, Turner Classic Movies also maintains movie licensing rights agreements with Universal Studios, Paramount Pictures,[11] 20th Century Fox as FX Movie Channel,[12] Walt Disney Studios (primarily film content from Walt Disney Pictures), as well as most of the Selznick International Pictures library,[13] Sony Pictures Entertainment (primarily film content from Columbia Pictures), StudioCanal, and Janus Films.
