The film was released in theaters in Japan on April 23, 2016,[24] later receiving 4DX and MX4D screenings on September 24, 2016.[25] The film received limited screenings in North America from January 27, 2017 to February 9, 2017, offering limited edition trading cards for attendees, while Manga Entertainment screened the film for a limited time in the United Kingdom in February 2017.[26][27]
