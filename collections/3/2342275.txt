Maya is a 2015 Indian Tamil language neo noir horror film written and directed by Ashwin Saravanan.[2] It features Nayanthara starring her 50th film and Aari in the lead.[3] Lakshmi Priyaa Chandramouli and Amzath Khan were in supporting roles. It was one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of 2015.[1] A dubbed Telugu version, Mayuri, was also released. Maya was produced by Potential Studios. The film was remade in Kannada as Aake by K. M. Chaitanya, starring Chiranjeevi Sarja.[4] Maya is undoubtedly one of the best horror movies of Tamil cinema.[5]
