A U.S. tour of Mackintosh's 2001 West End production ran from September 12, 2007, to June 22, 2008.[40] The production starred Christopher Cazenove as Higgins, Lisa O'Hare as Eliza, Walter Charles as Pickering, Tim Jerome as Alfred Doolittle[41] and Nixon as Mrs. Higgins, replacing Sally Ann Howes.[42]
