In December 2009, the PPL announced "Chasing Cars" was the most widely played song of the decade in the UK.[57] The same month, in a UK poll conducted by Channel 4, it was voted the nation's favourite "song of the noughties".[58] In January 2010, the band were nominated in three categories in the annual Meteor Awards.[59] The band also played at the event, scheduled on 19 February 2010 at The RDS.[60]
