Sai Dharam Tej's first debut was Rey as hero, produced and directed, by YVS Chowdary on his Bommarillu films banner. The film was launched at Ramanaidu Studios in Hyderabad on 17 October 2010 on the eve of Vijayadasami.[3] The filming ended on 6 August 2013.[4] Rey movie delayed due to financial issues and it released on 27 March 2015.
