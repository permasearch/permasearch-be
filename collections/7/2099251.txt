On September 28, 1947, Hall married a distant cousin, Marilyn Doreen Plottel (May 17, 1927 - June 5, 2017); the two had been introduced by a mutual cousin, Norman Shnier, the previous year. They became United States citizens in 1949.[1] They have three children: actress Joanna Gleason; Sharon Hall Kessler, head of television at Alcon Entertainment; and Richard Hall, a television producer. They have lived in Beverly Hills, California since 1962.[13]
