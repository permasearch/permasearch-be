Evanescence performed the song at the 2004 Billboard Music Awards on December 8, 2004.[56] The band was joined by an eight-piece string section during the performance and a stage backdrop of knotted, decaying trees were placed on stage in order to showcase the "powerful vocals" by Amy Lee as stated by a writer of Billboard.[56] The band additionally performed the song at Late Show with David Letterman in March 2004.[57]
