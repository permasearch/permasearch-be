"Courtesy of the Red, White and Blue (The Angry American)" debuted at number 41 on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Songs for the week of May 25, 2002.  The album was certified Gold by the RIAA on January 3, 2006, and platinum on March 27, 2012. The song has sold 1,528,000 digital copies in the U.S. as of July 2017.[11]
