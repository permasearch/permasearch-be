In addition to aircraft carriers and large amphibious assault ships, the Indian Navy is acquiring numerous surface combatants such as; the Visakhapatnam-class destroyers, the Project 17A-class frigates,[244] ASW shallow water corvettes,[245] ASuW corvettes,[246] and MCM vessels.[247] New submarine types include; the conventional Kalvari-class,[248] Project 75I,[249] and the nuclear Arihant-class.[250] New auxiliary ships include; five Replenishment Oilers, a Missile Range Instrumentation Ship and an Ocean Surveillance Ship.[251]
