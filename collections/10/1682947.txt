Doby and his wife, Helyn, had five children, six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.[117][121] When the Dobys moved to Montclair, Yogi Berra and his wife became neighborhood friends and children of the two families played baseball and football together.[9] Doby had a kidney removed in 1997 after a cancerous tumour was detected. Helyn, married to Doby for 55 years, died in 2001 after a six-month battle with cancer.[82]
