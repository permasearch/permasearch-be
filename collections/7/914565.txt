Del Rey grew up in Lake Placid, New York,[18] and was raised Roman Catholic.[19] She attended a Catholic elementary school[15] and for one year, a high school where her mother taught.[20] She began singing in her church choir when she was a child, where she was the cantor.[15][21] At age fifteen,[8][20][22] she was sent to Kent School by her parents[22] to resolve a budding drinking problem.[23] Her uncle, an admissions officer at the boarding school, secured her financial aid to attend.[a]
