Princess Daisy (Japanese: デイジー姫, Hepburn: Deijī-hime, [deː.ʑiː çi.me]) is the princess of the region of Sarasaland. She first appeared in Super Mario Land as the game's damsel in distress, a role usually reserved for Princess Peach. Since her appearance in Mario Tennis, she has become a staple playable character in the Mario spin-off games, It is often speculated that she is Luigi's love interest.
