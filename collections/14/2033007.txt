Meluan Lackless: Heiress of the Lackless family, an ancient family of Vintas. In the oldest part of their estates there is rumored to be a secret door without a handle or hinges. She possesses an unusual box without handle, lid, or hinges. It seems that Kvothe has stolen her box. There is a probability the she is Kvothe's aunt because she resents the Edema Ruh (Kvothe's ethnicity) for taking her sister away.
