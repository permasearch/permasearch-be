On June 23, Batista was drafted from SmackDown to the Raw brand during the 2008 WWE Draft.[96] Batista became a World Tag Team Champion for a third time on August 4 episode of Raw when he teamed up with John Cena to defeat Cody Rhodes and Ted DiBiase for the title,[97][98] but they failed to retain the titles against the former champions on the next episode of Raw.[99] Batista defeated Cena at SummerSlam.[100]
