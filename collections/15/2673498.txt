The Latin Kings have a hierarchical organizational structure: they have numerous "chapters" or "tribes" across the country,[9] which adhere to a regional, state, and a national system. Officers ( Inca, El-Cacique and War Lord ) are supported by a "Crown Council" of the 5 Crowns Council members.
