Sunrisers Hyderabad replaced the Deccan Chargers in 2012 and debuted in 2013. The squad was announced in Chennai on 18 December 2012. The team is owned by Sun TV Network who won the bid with ₹850.5 million (US$13 million) per year for a five-year deal, a week after the Chargers were terminated due to prolonged financial issues. Sun TV Network Limited, which is headquartered in Chennai, is one of India's biggest television networks with 32 TV channels and 45 FM radio stations, making it India's largest media and entertainment company.[8]
