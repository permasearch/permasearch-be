Kirby also stars in several manga series, most of which have only been released in Japanese. The longest running series is titled 星のカービィ デデデでプププなものがたり (lit. Kirby of the Stars: The Story of Dedede In Pupupu) running up to 25 books, and was written by Hirokazu Hikawa (ひかわ 博一 Hikawa Hirokazu).[13] While Viz Media originally had plans to release the manga in English on September 2010.[14] This release date was pushed back to October 4, 2011 before being canceled completely on May 16, 2011.
