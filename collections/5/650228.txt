Reprise Records released the soundtrack on May 19, 2009, which included 15 tracks. While Common had expressed interest in writing a song for the soundtrack,[83] Alice in Chains' "Rooster" is the only featured song.[84] Although not included in the soundtrack, "You Could Be Mine" by Guns N' Roses, which was featured in Terminator 2: Judgment Day, can be heard briefly in a scene of the film as well.[85] A remix version of Nine Inch Nails' "The Day the World Went Away" is played on the film's third theatrical trailer, but is not included in the film or soundtrack.
