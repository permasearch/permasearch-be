Quantico, initially scheduled to air Tuesdays at 10:00 pm, was moved to Sundays at 10:00 pm due to a retooling of Of Kings and Prophets.[80][81] The series premiered on ABC on Sunday, September 27, 2015[82] The series debuted in Canada on CTV on the same day as its American premiere.[83] The episodes, about 43 minutes in length, are broadcast in standard and high definition. In Australia, it premiered on the Seven Network on October 11, 2015.[84] The series was acquired by Alibi in the United Kingdom.[85] The season-two premiered on September 25, 2016.[86] After the mid-season finale, it moved to Mondays at 10:00 p.m. on January 23, 2017.[87]
