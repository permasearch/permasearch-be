Shortly after the airing of the season one finale in December 2011, Fox Broadcasting stated that no decision would be made until 2012 regarding the continuation of the series.[54] Fox announced the cancellation of Terra Nova on March 6, 2012. 20th Century Fox Television stated that it would try to sell the show to other networks.[55] Following the decision 20th Century Fox entered negotiations with Netflix to possibly create a second season.[56] However nothing ultimately came of this.[57]
