Murphy was shipped to Casablanca in French Morocco on 20 February 1943. He was assigned to Company B, 1st Battalion, 15th Infantry Regiment, 3rd Infantry Division,[22] which trained under the command of Major General Lucian Truscott.[23] [22][23] He participated as a platoon messenger with his division at Arzew in Algeria in rigorous training for the Allied assault landings in Sicily.[22][24] He was promoted to private first class on 7 May and corporal on 15 July.[25][26]
