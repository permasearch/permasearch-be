In their 41 seasons since joining the NBA, the Spurs have achieved a winning record 35 times, have appeared in the NBA playoffs 37 times, and have won 5 NBA Championships, with the most recent one coming in 2014.[2] The only NBA franchises that have won more championships than the Spurs are the Boston Celtics (17 championships), the Los Angeles Lakers (16), the Golden State Warriors (6) and the Chicago Bulls (6).[3]
