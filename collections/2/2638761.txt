228,000 sq ft (21,200 m2) was opened to the public October 26, 2007. It consists of about 40 retailers and 4 restaurants. Some of the tenants of this expansion include Abercrombie kids, Aldo, Barnes & Noble, Betsey Johnson, Brooks Brothers, Clarks, Coach, GUESS, Juicy Couture, Lacoste, Louis Vuitton, MAC Cosmetics, Pottery Barn, Swarovski, Talbots, Tommy Bahama, Urban Outfitters, and West Elm. In 2008 True Religion, Cole Haan, Kate Spade, The Capital Grille, J. Alexander's and Mitchell's Fish Market were opened.[2]
