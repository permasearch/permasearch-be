The soundtrack peaked at number one on the ARIA Charts,[6] three on the US Billboard 200 album chart, four on the New Zealand Albums Chart,[7] and four on the UK Albums Chart.[8] It received Platinum certifications by the Recording Industry Association of America[9] and the Australian Recording Industry Association,[10] and a Gold certification by the British Phonographic Industry.[11] In the United States, it has sold over half a million copies (573,000) by April 2017.[12]
