A former Army chaplain and Catholic priest who now owns a bar called "Paradise Lost". He has known Booth from his time in the service. After being blackmailed by Christopher Pelant into breaking his engagement to Brennan, Booth goes to Aldo's bar to drown his sorrows and confide in him, even calling him "Father", despite the fact that Aldo was no longer in the ministry. Brennan also goes to Aldo for advice several times for an explanation to Booth's uncharacteristic behavior. He presides over the couple's wedding in the episode "The Woman in White". Brennan and Booth investigate his murder(season 12 episode 4).
