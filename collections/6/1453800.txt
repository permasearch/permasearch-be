William & Mary's graduate business program (Mason School of Business) ranked 37th by Business Week in 2015,[64] 41st by Forbes in 2011,[65] 40th nationally by the Financial Times in 2008[66] and 17th nationally by the Wall Street Journal.[67] It is also ranked tied for 74th by U.S. News & World Report in its rankings for 2016.[58] In 2017, it was ranked 2nd in "Best MBA Professors" by The Princeton Review.[68]
