Saving Face was nominated for the Breakthrough Director Award at the 2005 Gotham Awards,[12] the Viewer's Choice Award and the Best Actress Award for Michelle Krusiec at the 2005 Golden Horse Film Festival,[12] and the GLAAD Media Award for Outstanding Film in Limited Release at the 2006 GLAAD Media Awards.[12] Out of these, it won the Viewer's Choice Award at the 2005 Golden Horse Film Festival.[12]
