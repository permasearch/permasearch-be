Until the end of October the fighting will rage inside the Barricades then the Red October Factory.[61] The Soviet-controlled area shrinks down to a few strips of land along the Volga, and in November the fighting concentrates around what newspapers of all the Soviet Union call "Lyudnikov's Island", a patch of ground behind the Barricades Factory where the remnants of Colonel Ivan Lyudnikov's 138th Division will resist all assaults and became a symbol of the stout defense of Stalingrad.[62]
