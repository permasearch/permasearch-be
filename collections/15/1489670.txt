About 96.4% of Pakistanis are Muslim. Pakistan has the second-largest number of Muslims in the world after Indonesia.[529][530] The majority of them are Sunni (estimated between 75 and 95%)[531][532][533][534][535] while Shias represent between 5–20%.[531][532][536][537] Pakistan, like India, is said to have at least 16 million Shias.[537][533][538] A PEW survey in 2012 found that only 6% of Pakistani Muslims were Shia.[536]
