Nefera de Nile (voiced by Wendee Lee)[143] is the daughter of Ramses de Nile and Cleo's older sister by three years. She has blue, black, and gold streaked hair, and purple eyes. Her clothes consist of a wrap top and wrap skirt. She wears a snake necklace, jeweled earrings and a gold hairpiece. She has a pet scarab beetle named Azura.[144]
