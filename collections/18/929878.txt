In late 2007, the company announced the new Plum Card as the latest addition to their card line for small business owners.[54] The card provides a 1.5% early pay discount or up to two months to defer payment on purchases. The 1.5% discount is available for billing periods where the cardmember spends at least $5,000. The first 10,000 cards were issued to members on December 16, 2007.[55]
