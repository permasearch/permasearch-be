The country's only international airport, and the hub of all air service in the country, is Tribhuvan International Airport, which serves the nation's capital and the country's largest metropolitan city of Kathmandu. However, two of the domestic airport namely, Pokhara airport and Gautam Buddha Airport, located in Pokhara, Kaski and Siddharthanagar, Rupandehi are all set to be converted into second and third international airport in Nepal. Both the constructions are scheduled to finish within upcoming five years.
