In February 2014, Flynn landed a guest role on the hit CBS television series, Hawaii Five-0, in an episode entitled, "Hoku Welowelo"; Flynn played the role of Nick Spitz, opposite actress Melanie Griffith. Flynn also co-produced his short movie called Solely.[8][9] In August 2014, DAYS co-executive producer, Greg Meng confirmed to Soap Opera Digest that Flynn had joined the cast of NBC soap opera Days of Our Lives, as a recast of Chad DiMera, previously played by actor Casey Deidrick. Flynn made his on-screen debut on September 12, 2014.[10][11][12] In October 2016 Deadline confirmed that Flynn has booked a role in the thriller movie called "D.O.A. Blood River"[13]
