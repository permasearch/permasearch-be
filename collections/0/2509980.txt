So I Married an Anti-fan (also known as:No One's Life Is Easy) is a Chinese romantic comedy film directed by Kim Jae-young and starring Park Chanyeol, Yuan Shanshan, Seo Ju-hyun and Jiang Chao.[1][2][3][4][5][6] It is based on the manhwa So I Married An Anti-fan (Hangul: 그래서 나는 안티 팬과 결혼했다) by Jiwan. The film was released in China by Bona Film Group on June 30, 2016.[1]
