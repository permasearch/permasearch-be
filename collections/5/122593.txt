Good Morning Britain was broadcast from Studio 5 at The London Studios where GMTV was brought from during its seventeen year run. In February 2017, it was announced that The London Studios, where Good Morning Britain is filmed, would be closed for some years beginning in 2018 as ITV decided to go through with demolishing the building and having it rebuilt. It was also announced that ITV's daytime shows, including Good Morning Britain, would be moving to free studio space (TC3) at Television Centre, previously used by the BBC before moving to New Broadcasting House in March 2013. They made the move to TVC on Monday 16 April 2018; With its last programme from The London Studios aired on 13 April.
