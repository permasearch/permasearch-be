First Frontier shows the Master (apparently the Ainley version) finally acquiring a new body,[73] who according to McIntee is based on the cinema persona of Basil Rathbone.[citation needed] This incarnation reappears in Happy Endings by Paul Cornell, Virgin Publishing's celebratory fiftieth Virgin New Adventures novel. After the broadcast of the television movie, some fans[vague][who?][original research?] suggested that this is the incarnation briefly played by Gordon Tipple in the prologue, eventually succumbing once again to the cheetah virus in the first Eighth Doctor novel The Eight Doctors.[clarification needed][original research?]
