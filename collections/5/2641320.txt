Canadian broadcasts premiered on CHRGD and Family Channel in April 2016.[26] Taiwan broadcast was premiered in PTS Taiwan on 14 September 2016 (as 新雷鳥神機隊[27]).[28] In Brazil, the show has broadcast on Gloob since 4 July 2016. In Latin America, the show broadcasts on Disney XD. The Nordic broadcasts were premiered in SVT (SVT Barnkanalen) in Sweden (as Thunderbirds[29]), NRK Super in Norway (as Thunderbirds[30]), DR (Ramasjang and Ultra) in Denmark (as Thunderbirds[31]), MTV Juniori in Finland (as Thunderbirds), with Alicom appointed as the Nordic licensing agent.[21][32]
