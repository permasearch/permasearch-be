A memorial wall at the DIA headquarters is dedicated to those agency employees who lost their lives in the line of their intelligence work[66] and whose deaths are not classified. The wall was first dedicated on December 14, 1988 by Director Leonard Perroots. It "commemorates the profound individual sacrifices made on behalf of the United States by DIA members and acts as a reminder of the selflessness, dedication, and courage required to confront national challenges..."[66]
