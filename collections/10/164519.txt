The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, which plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team plays its home matches at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. After serving a two-year suspension from the IPL starting July 2015 for the alleged involvement of their owners in the 2013 IPL betting case, the Super Kings returned to the league in 2018.[2] The team is captained by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and coached by Stephen Fleming. It won it's comeback season by winning it's 3rd season and also becoming the team which has won the most number of IPL with MI.
