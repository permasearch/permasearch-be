After four years at HB Studio, Pacino successfully auditioned for the Actors Studio.[6] The Actors Studio is a membership organization of professional actors, theatre directors and playwrights in the Hell's Kitchen neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City.[15] Pacino studied "method acting"[3] under acting coach Lee Strasberg, who appeared with Pacino in the films The Godfather Part II and in ...And Justice for All.[4]
