Diaz's first film of 2014 was the romantic revenge comedy The Other Woman opposite Leslie Mann and Kate Upton. While The Other Woman received mostly negative reviews from critics, who felt that it settled for cheap laughs,[85] it opened atop the US weekend box office with earnings of US$24.7 million across the three days;[86] it eventually made US$83.9 million in North America and US$196.7 million globally.[87]
