Additional filming began in Chicago, Illinois in November 2014.[152] Other locations included the Michigan Motion Picture Studios, the Eli and Edythe Broad Art Museum at Michigan State University, Yorkville, Illinois, and New Mexico.[153][154][155] Sequences of the film, including a scene depicting the murder of Bruce Wayne's parents, were filmed using IMAX cameras.[156] The planned shoot in Morocco was shifted to New Mexico due to incidents related to the 2014 Ebola outbreak.[157] Principal photography wrapped on December 5, 2014.[158]
