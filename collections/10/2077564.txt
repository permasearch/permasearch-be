Gadar: Ek Prem Katha (English: Revolt: A Love Story) is a 2001 Indian period action film starring Sunny Deol, Amisha Patel, and Amrish Puri, set in the time of the Partition of India in 1947. Made for ₹185 million (US$2.9 million), Gadar has made more than Rs. 130 crore during its initial theatrical run with a distributor share of 54.6 crores[citation needed] and after its theatrical run, was commercially one of the most successful movies in India when it was released.[4][5] Gadar: Ek Prem Katha is the second most watched Hindi language film in India as it recorded 5.05 crore footfalls in India.[6][7] The shy role of Sunny Deol was admired and led to the Filmfare nomination for the best actor. According to Box office India, its adjusted gross is 468 crores in 2017 ticket sales.[8] The story of this film is loosely based on the life of Boota Singh.[9]
