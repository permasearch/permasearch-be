Resistance Is Futile was released on 13 April 2018 in standard CD format, deluxe CD, cassette and vinyl.[11] The album sold around 24.000 copies in the first week, entering the UK Albums Chart at number 2,[3] despite being number 1 during the week,[12] the record spent a total of 6 weeks in the chart.[3] However on physical sales the album peaked at number 1, both on CD[13] and Vinyl.[14]
