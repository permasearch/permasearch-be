Rollins resides in the Tampa, Florida area,[92] and is married to Johari Smith. They were married on January 23, 2010, in the Cayman Islands.[93] On May 21, 2012, Johari gave birth to the couple's first daughter, Camryn Drew Rollins.[94] Their second daughter, Logan Aliya, was born in early April 2014.[95] When he played for the Phillies, Rollins resided in Woolwich Township, New Jersey.[96]
