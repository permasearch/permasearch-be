Flight evaluation of the fighters started in August 2009 at Bangalore.[111] The F/A-18E/F and F-16IN completed their field trials by mid-September 2009.[112] The Rafale began trials in late September 2009.[113] As of late October 2009, the IAF has completed the trials of F/A-18, F-16, Rafale, and MiG-35. According to Air Chief Marshal P.V. Naik, all the aircraft tested so far "are going neck and neck".[114]
