Rainbow Six was met with mostly positive reviews on PC. However, the console versions received average to negative response upon release. GameRankings gave it a score of 81.81% for the PC version;[2] 73.71% for the Nintendo 64 version;[3] 72.28% for the Dreamcast version;[4] 53.83% for the Game Boy Color version;[5] and 48.07% for the PlayStation version.[6] Metacritic gave only the PC version a score of 85 out of 100.[7]
