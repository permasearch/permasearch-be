The band's eighth full-length album, Nonagon Infinity, was released worldwide on 29 April 2016.[30] Described by Mackenzie as a "never-ending album", it features nine songs connected by musical motifs which flow "seamlessly" into each other with the last track "linking straight back into the top of the opener like a sonic mobius strip".[31] On 8 March the band released a video for the first single, "Gamma Knife".[32] It contained a riff from the song "People Vultures" which was premiered on April 4[33] with a music video released on May 6.[34] The album received high praise among critics, with Pitchfork stating that it "yields some of the most outrageous, exhilarating rock 'n' roll in recent memory."[35]
