The United States Special Operations Command Medal was introduced in 1994 to recognize individuals for outstanding contributions to, and in support of, special operations.[citation needed] Since it was created, there have been more than 50 recipients, four of whom are not American, namely Generał broni Włodzimierz Potasiński (Poland, 2010, posthumously),[96][97] Kaptein Gunnar Sønsteby (Norway, 2008), Generał brygady Jerzy Gut (Poland, June 2014)[98] and Generał dywizji Piotr Patalong (Poland, October 2014).[99]
