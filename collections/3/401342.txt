Hachikō is also the subject of a 2004 children's book entitled Hachikō: The True Story of a Loyal Dog, written by Pamela S. Turner and illustrated by Yan Nascimbene.[29] Another children's book, a short novel for readers of all ages called Hachiko Waits, written by Lesléa Newman[30] and illustrated by Machiyo Kodaira, was published by Henry Holt & Co. in 2004. Hachiko Waits was released in paperback by Square Fish (an imprint of MacMillan) in 2008.[31] Hachikō is featured prominently in the 2008 novel The Story of Edgar Sawtelle by David Wroblewski.[32] Hachikō plays an important part in the 1967 children's book Taka-chan and I: A Dog's Journey to Japan.[33]
