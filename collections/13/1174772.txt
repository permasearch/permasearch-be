He submitted a report on "Renovation and Rejuvenation of Higher Education in India" to the Ministry of Human Resource Development on June 24, 2009. He filed the case, Prof. Yashpal & Anr vs State Of Chhattisgarh & Ors in the Supreme Court against a law, Chhattisgarh Niji Kshetra Vishwavidyalaya (Sthapana Aur Viniyaman) Adhiniyam, 2002 which is used to establish universities without regulatory approvals and necessary infrastructure. He won the case in February 2005 and 112 private universities formed unded the said law in Chhattisgarh had to be closed.[12]
