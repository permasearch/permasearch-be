The Chinese government declared martial law on 20 May, and mobilized at least 30 divisions from five of the country's seven military regions.[81] At least 14 of PLA's 24 army corps contributed troops.[81] As many as 250,000 troops were eventually sent to the capital, some arriving by air and others by rail.[82] Guangzhou's civil aviation authorities put regular airline tickets on hold to prepare for transporting military units.[83]
