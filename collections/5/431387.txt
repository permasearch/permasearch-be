Originally scheduled for release on July 22, 2016,[55] Lionsgate delayed it to March 24, 2017.[56] The film received its world premiere in Berlin, Germany on March 17, 2017.[57] All five of the surviving actors who originally portrayed the Rangers in the series attended the film's Los Angeles premiere on March 22, 2017.[58] It was the first time they had been together publicly since 1995.[59]
