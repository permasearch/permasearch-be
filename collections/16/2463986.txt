Hunt was born on April 2, 1945 in Morristown, New Jersey to Raymond Davy Hunter (d. 1985), the vice president of Harper Fuel Oil, and Elsie Doying Hunter (d. ~1994), a piano teacher who taught at the Westport School of Music and performed with the Saugatuck Congregational Church Choir in Westport, Connecticut, where Hunt was raised.[3] She has one sibling, an older sister named Marcia (b. 1940). [4] Hunt attended the Interlochen Arts Academy[5] and the Goodman School of Drama in Chicago, Illinois, which now part of DePaul University.[6][7]
