The one-hour pilot, titled Woo-oo!, premiered on August 12 at 12:00am EST and was repeated consecutively for the next 24 hours. Two days later on August 14, the pilot was also released on YouTube.[28] The pilot also aired on Disney Channel on September 17, 2017.[29] The series officially premiered on September 23, 2017, coinciding with the original show's 30th anniversary.[30]
