Royston Vasey draws on the upbringing of all the League's members - Mark Gatiss, Steve Pemberton, Reece Shearsmith and Jeremy Dyson - all of whom were raised in the north of England.[3] Royston Vasey is often said to be the real name of British stand-up comedian Roy 'Chubby' Brown.[3] Brown played the part of the town's mayor in a cameo appearance.
