In April 2017, Ryan Penagos, Vice President and Executive Editor of Marvel Digital Media, stated that the game would be released in 2017.[19] At Sony's E3 2017 conference, the first gameplay demo was shown and it was announced that the game would be released in 2018.[20] Shawn Layden, the president of SIE America and chairman of SIE Worldwide Studios later confirmed that Spider-Man, and SIE's other games announced for 2018 at the conference, will be released in the first half of 2018.[21] The game is scheduled to be released on September 7, 2018.[22]
