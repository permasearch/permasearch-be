All the Hunger Games films finished first at the North American box office during both their opening and second weekend.[43][44][45] In North America, The Hunger Games film series is the second highest-grossing film series based on young adult books, after the Harry Potter series, earning over $1.4 billion.[46] Worldwide, it is the third highest-grossing film series based on young-adult books after the film series of Harry Potter and The Twilight Saga, respectively, having grossed over $2.9 billion.[47] In North America, it is the eighth highest-grossing film franchise of all time.[48] Worldwide, it is the 15th or 16th highest-grossing film franchise of all time.[49][50]
