On May 8, 2014, ABC renewed the series for an eleventh season that aired from September 2014 to May 2015).[339] In addition, the show was relocated to the Thursday 8:00pm EST time slot. After four seasons outside the top 25 rated shows, Grey's Anatomy was the number 15 show in the 2013–2014 season, the show's tenth. The show also re-entered the top five shows in the 18–49 viewer demographic.
