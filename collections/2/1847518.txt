One day, a man named Ted Narracott buys a young horse for 39 guineas when he was supposed to buy a calf at an auction. Ted's son, Albert, names the horse Joey and grows to love him and protecting the young horse from Ted when he is drunk and caring for Joey. While with the Narracotts, Joey also meets a horse named Zoey, who was a source of comfort to Joey, and whose name partially inspired his name.
