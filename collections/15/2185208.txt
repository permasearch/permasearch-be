South Park: The Stick of Truth is set in the fictional town South Park in the Colorado Rocky Mountains.[26] The main character, whom the player controls, is the New Kid—nicknamed "Douchebag"[3]—a silent protagonist who has recently moved to the town.[21][27] Befriending the local boys, he becomes involved in an epic role-playing fantasy game featuring wizards and warriors battling for control of the Stick of Truth, a twig that possesses limitless power.[17][21]
