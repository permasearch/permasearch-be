Clear AFS construction began in August 1958[42] with 700 workers[42] (completed July 1, 1961),[42] and Thule Site J construction began by May 18, 1960,[65] with radar pedestals complete by June 2.[61] Thule testing began on May 16, 1960,[66] IOC was on September 30,[55] and the initial operational radar transmission was in October 1960[67] (initially duplex vacuum tube IBM 709s occupied 2 floors).[citation needed]
