Kochi was home to some of the most influential figures in Malayalam literature, including Changampuzha Krishna Pillai,[168] Kesari Balakrishna Pillai,[169] G. Sankara Kurup,[170] and Vyloppilli Sreedhara Menon.[171] Prominent social reformers such as Sahodaran Ayyappan[172] and Pandit Karuppan[173] also are from Kochi. The Kochi International Book Festival is an annual event.
