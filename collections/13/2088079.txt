Toby Huss (born December 9, 1966)[1] is an American actor known for portraying Artie in the Nickelodeon series The Adventures of Pete & Pete (1993–1996). He is also known for his voice-over work on the long-running animated series King of the Hill (1997–2010) and his role as Felix "Stumpy" Dreifuss on HBO's Carnivàle (2003–2005).[2] He played John Bosworth on the AMC original period drama Halt and Catch Fire.[3]
