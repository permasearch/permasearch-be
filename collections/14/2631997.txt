Famitsu awarded Irozuki Tingle no Koi no Balloon Trip a score of 34 out of 40.[11] It was the 9th best-selling game in Japan during the week of its release, selling 33,498 units.[12] The following week it placed 9th again with 17,956 copies sold.[13] In August 2009, the game sold 70,544 units in total, placing 10th in the Japanese sales charts for that month.[14]
