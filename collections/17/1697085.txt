Free updates have been released to add additional features to the game. TPC Scottsdale, East Lake Golf Club, the fantasy course Treasure Island, and Quail Hollow Club were added in August, September, October, and November 2015, respectively.[11] The Quail Hollow update re-introduced alternate shot, best ball, four ball, and skins play.[11] Henrik Stenson and Dustin Johnson were also added as playable characters in October and November 2015 respectively.[11] PGA West Stadium Course was added in January 2016.[12]
