This season was slated to see the introductions of villains Granny Goodness, Desaad, and Glorious Godfrey.[38] Lindsay Hartley was cast as another of Darkseid's minions, Mad Harriet, who works for Granny Goodness.[59] Hartley explained the traditional claws her characters wears are powered by kryptonite, which give her an advantage over Clark. Hartley also stated that Harriet is the leader of Granny Goodness' Female Furies.[60]
