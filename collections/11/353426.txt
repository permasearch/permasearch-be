Spears confirmed her engagement to Aldridge in March 2008 after it was reported she was seen wearing an engagement ring.[21] In May 2008, the couple moved back to Liberty, Mississippi, and purchased a house, with Spears stating they would raise their child there.[22] Spears and Aldridge called off their engagement in March 2009,[23][24] and Spears ended the relationship and moved out in February 2010.[25] Spears and Aldridge reconciled in August 2010,[26] but ended their relationship a second time in November 2010.[27][28]
