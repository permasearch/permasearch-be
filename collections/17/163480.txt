Chemical industry of India earned revenue of $155–160 billion in 2013.[30] 100% FDI is allowed in Chemical sector under automatic route. Except Hydrocynic acid, Phosgene, Isocynates and their derivatives, production of all other chemicals is de-licensed in India.[31] India's share in global specialty chemical industry is expected to rise from 2.8% in 2013 to 6–7% in 2023.[32]
