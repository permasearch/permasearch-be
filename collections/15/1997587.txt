Throughout 2003, "Clocks" was featured in various commercials, movies and television programs: from the BBC using a sample to advertise Freeview TV, WWE promos featuring the return of American professional wrestler Kurt Angle, HBO, the 2002 Irish drama film In America, and an episode of the American medical drama television series ER. The song was played in its entirety during the ending credits for the 2003 film Confidence and was also featured in the TV shows The Sopranos, Third Watch,[28] Hindsight and Family Guy. In late 2003 the song was used in a trailer for the movie Peter Pan. It was also used in the 2006 Disney film The Wild, the 2010 Lionsgate film Alpha and Omega and the 2014 Blue Sky Studios film Rio 2.
