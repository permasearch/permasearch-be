Over 1.6 million people watched Tubridy's first episode, a figure which peaked during the Cowen interview.[34][35][36] Pat Kenny was not one of these, having decided to eat pizza instead.[37] Tubridy described it as "easily" the largest audience of his career—Pat Kenny attracted similar figures for his debut in 1999.[24][38] These figures made this the most watched episode – apart from the annual Toy Show – since Gay Byrne retired as presenter in 1999.[38]
