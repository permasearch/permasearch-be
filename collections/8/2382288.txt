On November 29, 2015, it was announced that Justin Fuente was leaving Memphis to become head coach at Virginia Tech.[677] Offensive coordinator Darrell Dickey was named interim head coach for the 2015 Birmingham Bowl,[678] a game the Tigers lost to Auburn by a score of 31–10.[679] In April 2016, Paxton Lynch was selected by the Denver Broncos in the first round of the 2016 NFL Draft with the 26th overall selection.[680] Fuente left Memphis with a 26–23 record,[681] becoming the first head coach in 41 years, since Fred Pancoast, to leave Memphis with a winning record.[682]
