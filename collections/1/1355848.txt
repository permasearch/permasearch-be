In 2007, Emirates made an order worth over $34.9 billion, at the Dubai Air Show. The airline signed contracts for 120 Airbus A350s, 11 A380s and 12 Boeing 777-300ERs.[31] By opening flights to São Paulo in 2007, Emirates began the first non-stop flight between the Middle East and South America;[32] it also began operations of its $120 million Flight Catering Centre at Dubai Airport.[33]
