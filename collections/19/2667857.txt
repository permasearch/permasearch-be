Dr. Cares is a spin-off game of the Delicious series. The game was released on April 12, 2017. Dr. Amy Cares is a veterinary graduate who returns to Snuggford to her grandfather's vet clinic and eventually takes over the vet business. Both Emily and Paige O'Malley make a cameo appearance when Paige's bunny is sick and Amy cares for him. This is Amy's first game in the series.
