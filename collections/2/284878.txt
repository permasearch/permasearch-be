Rito Revolto, the brother of Rita, pays a visit to Lord Zedd and Rita and plants some eggs nearby. After that, Rito teleports to the planet Earth and lures the Rangers into a trap. Just when the Rangers think they get to destroy Rito, Rito gets help from four other monsters made by Lord Zedd. The Rangers are totally outclassed and ultimately, the Thunder Megazord and Tigerzord are destroyed.
