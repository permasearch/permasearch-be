Led Zeppelin have collected many honours and awards throughout the course of their career. They were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1995,[97] and the UK Music Hall of Fame in 2006.[197] Among the band's awards are an American Music Award in 2005, and the Polar Music Prize in 2006.[198] Led Zeppelin were the recipient of a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award in 2005,[199] and four of their recordings have been inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame.[200] They have been awarded five Diamond albums, as well as fourteen Multi-Platinum, four Platinum and one Gold album in the United States,[48] while in the UK they have five Multi-Platinum, six Platinum, one Gold and four Silver albums.[201] In addition to listing five of their albums among "the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time", Rolling Stone named Led Zeppelin the 14th-greatest artist of all time in 2004.[202][203]
