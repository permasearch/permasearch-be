After some initial delays, the game was released worldwide in March 2014, with the Microsoft Windows version being released on April 24, 2014.[3][5] An updated version of the game, subtitled Scholar of the First Sin, was released for PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Microsoft Windows in April 2015. The title is a compilation of the original game and all of its downloadable content with upgraded graphics, expanded online multiplayer capacity, and various other changes. A sequel, Dark Souls III, was released in 2016.
