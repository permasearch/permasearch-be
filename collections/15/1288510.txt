In 2017 fines issued by courts for the most serious speeding offences have increased to better reflect the seriousness of the offence.[91] On April 24, 2017 new rules came into force which see the maximum fine for being caught speeding increase by 150 per cent to £2,500 from £1,000 for the most serious offenders.[92] The minimum penalty for speeding remains a £100 fine and 3 penalty points added to your licence.[93]
