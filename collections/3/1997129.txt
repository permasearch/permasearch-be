"Daydreaming" was released as a download on 6 May 2016 on Radiohead's site and on streaming and digital media services.[22][23] Anderson and the band sent 35 mm prints of the "Daydreaming" music video to select theatres inviting them to screen it.[24] On 16 July 2016, Radiohead announced a fan competition to create a music video vignette for an alternative version of the song with additional strings.[25]
