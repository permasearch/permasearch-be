The Rutgers Business School is ranked 39th in the Wall Street Journal's Ranking of Top Business Schools.[101] The full-time Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.) program is ranked 48th in United States according to U.S. News and World Report,[102] with speciality at Pharma, Biotech and Healthcare industries. The Master of Quantitative Finance (M.Q.F.) program at Rutgers Business School and Master of Mathematical Finance (M.S.M.F) program at the department of Mathematics, is ranked 7th in the United States.[103]
