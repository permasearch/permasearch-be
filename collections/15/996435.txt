Manning was inducted into the Tennessee Athletics Hall of Fame in 2016.[49] He was also named the 2016 Tennessean of the Year by the Tennessee Sports Hall of Fame.[50] Manning was elected to the College Football Hall of Fame in 2017, his first year of eligibility for the honor, and will be inducted during a ceremony in December. He will join his father Archie in the hall, making them the first father and son duo to both be inducted as players.[51]
