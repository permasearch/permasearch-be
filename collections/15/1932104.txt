In December 2005, the company received final regulatory approval for its $9.2 billion purchase of PacifiCare Health Systems. It agreed to divest parts of PacifiCare's commercial health insurance business in Tucson, Arizona and Boulder, Colorado to satisfy antitrust regulator concerns, and also agreed to end its network access agreement with Blue Shield of California.[19] The Tucson business was sold to Cigna.[19] Also in December 2005, UnitedHealth Group acquired John Deere Health Care.[20]
