On 22 June 1974, she married the banker André-Louis Auzière, with whom she had three children: Sébastien Auzière (born 1975), an engineer, Dr Laurence Auzière-Jourdan (born in 1977), a cardiologist, and Tiphaine Auzière (born in 1984), a lawyer.[16] They resided in Truchtersheim until 1991, when they moved to Amiens.[7] She met Emmanuel Macron in La Providence High School where she was a teacher and he was a student. She divorced Auzière in January 2006 and married Macron in October 2007. Macron is 24 years younger than she. [17][18]
