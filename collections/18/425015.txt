In the United States, "Lonely Is the Night" spent 8 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, peaking at number 76, in 1986.[6] To date "Lonely Is the Night" is both the lowest-charting single from the band as well as the band's most recent song to reach the Billboard Hot 100.[6] On the Adult Contemporary chart, the song was better, peaking at number 12, remaining eleven weeks inside the adult chart.[7]
