The soundtrack for the film is composed by Vishal-Shekhar. The original score is composed by Vishal-Shekhar and John Stewart Eduri. The first single from the film, "Sainika", was released on Indian Republic Day, 26 January 2018 as a tribute to the soldiers of the Indian Army.[7] The film's second single "Lover Also, Fighter Also" was released on 14 February 2018.[8] The Naa Peru Surya Naa Illu India audio launch event was held on 22 April at Military Madhavaram, West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh. [9] The Naa Peru Surya- Naa Illu India pre-release event was organized on 29 April at Gachibowli Stadium, Hyderabad. [10]
