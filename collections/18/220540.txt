In the United Kingdom, "Cry Me a River", debuted at number two on February 15, 2003.[61] The next week it fell to number three, before returning to its original peak on March 1, 2003; it stayed on the chart for 13 weeks.[62] The song has sold over 365,000 copies in the country as of 2015.[63] It debuted at number 14 on the French Singles Chart on April 5, 2003. After three weeks, "Cry Me a River" peaked at number six, and it stayed on the chart for 21 weeks.[64] It was less successful on the Italian Singles Chart, where it peaked at number 14 and stayed on the chart for four weeks.[65] "Cry Me a River" also peaked at number five in Belgium (Wallonia),[66] at number six in the Republic of Ireland[67] and the Netherlands,[68] at number seven in Belgium (Flanders),[69] and at number 10 in Germany,[70] Norway,[71] and Sweden.[72]
