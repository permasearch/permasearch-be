Vyom attacks Shiv in the presence of his parents and Shiv's mother and Sharanya. He tries to forcefully marry Radhika. Shiv comes to her rescue. Vyom threatens Radhika to kill Shiv. In a turn of events, Shiva able to trick Vyom and kills Freddy instead. Shiva and Sharanya marry in front of Vyom. Vyom unable to process this commits suicide by jumping off a cliff. As Krish and Radhika spend time together, Vyom's ghost comes and taunts them that his craze and love for Sharanya will be another story. Krish and Radhika watch helpless around them as they are unable to see Vyom but sense that something is amiss.
