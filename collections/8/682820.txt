The entire public transit network and most Boston taxi services[c] were suspended, as was Amtrak service to and from Boston.[73][113] Logan International Airport remained open under heightened security.[113] Universities, schools, many businesses, and other facilities were closed as thousands of law enforcement personnel participated in the door-to-door manhunt in Watertown, as well as following up other leads, including at the house that the brothers shared in Cambridge. Seven improvised explosive devices were recovered by bomb squads.[114]
