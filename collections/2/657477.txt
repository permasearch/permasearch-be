Microsoft Office 2010 (Office 14.0, because Microsoft skipped 13.0[107]) was finalized on 15 April 2010 and made available to consumers on 15 June 2010.[108][109] The main features of Office 2010 include the backstage file menu, new collaboration tools, a customizable ribbon, protected view and a navigation panel. This is the first version to ship in 32-bit and 64-bit variants. Microsoft Office 2010 featured a new logo, which resembled the 2007 logo, except in gold, and with a modification in shape.[110]
