On August 17, Kian Loyd delos Santos, a 17-year-old Grade 11 student, was shot dead on an antidrug operation in Caloocan.[107][108] CCTV footage appeared to show Kian being dragged by two policemen. Police say they killed him in self-defense, and retrieved a gun and two packets of methamphetamine.[107] Delos Santos was the son of an overseas Filipino worker, a key demographic in support of Duterte.[109] The teenager's death caused condemnation by senators.[110][111] His funeral on August 25, attended by more than a thousand people, was one of the largest protests to date against the Drug War.[112]
