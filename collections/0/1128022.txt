Frank and Cora patch up their relationship and the publicity from the murder makes their diner a raging success. Kennedy, an investigator who used to work for Cora's lawyer, attempts to blackmail them with her confession but she and Frank beat him up and force him to give them the copies. After an argument, Cora reveals to Frank that she is pregnant and speculates that bringing a new life into the world may help make up for the one they took. The two spend the dayat the beach and agree to rededicate themselves to each other. On the drive home, Frank becomes distracted by Cora and crashes the car, killing her.
