Langford auditioned for the role of American high school student Hannah Baker in the mystery teen drama TV series 13 Reasons Why, over Skype. She had only 10 days to get an O-1 visa as she had not worked in the United States before.[22] She has received critical acclaim for her work on the show.[23] Langford researched the role, speaking with a representative of the sexual assault awareness campaign "It's On Us" and a psychiatrist who specializes in adolescence.[24] In December 2016, she signed with the William Morris Endeavor agency.[25] Langford reprised the role in the second season of the series, released on May 18, 2018.[26] On May 25, 2018, Langford confirmed that she will not return as Hannah Baker in the third season of the series.[27]
