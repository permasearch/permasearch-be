Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, 15 km (9.3 mi) from the city centre, provides domestic and international flights.[181] It is the busiest airport in Gujarat and the eighth busiest in India with an average of 250 aircraft movements a day.[182] The Dholera International Airport is proposed near Fedara. It will be the largest airport in India with a total area of 7,500 hectares.[183]
