On 27 April 2014, it was announced that Foote had been written out of the show.[13] Foote stated that while she was sad to be leaving, she was excited about the challenge of future roles. She also praised her character's storylines and her co-stars.[13] Executive producer Stuart Blackburn confirmed that Katy would not be killed-off and that the door would be left open for a possible return in the future.[13] Foote filmed her final scenes in February 2015.[14] Katy was killed off-screen in a car accident on 18 October 2017.[15]
