In December 2014, authorization for a national historical park designation was incorporated in the 2015 National Defense Authorization Act.[204] Despite opposition from some legislators,[205][206] the bill passed with bipartisan support, and was signed into law by the President on December 19, 2014.[207][208][209] In March 2017 the Harriet Tubman Underground Railroad Visitor Center was inaugurated in Maryland within Harriet Tubman Underground Railroad State Park.[210]
