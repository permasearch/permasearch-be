The game's announcement came following a standstill in Wii sales, which had led to a 52% drop in Nintendo's first-half earnings for 2009. Nintendo hoped that the game would help to increase sales of the Wii in the coming holiday season.[56][8] In a Japanese retail briefing event prior to its release, Shigeru Miyamoto expressed his faith that the game would express strong sales stretching beyond its first year on the market.[57]
