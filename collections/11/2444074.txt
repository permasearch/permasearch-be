AIG began an advertising campaign on January 1, 2013, called "Thank You America," in which several company employees, including AIG President and CEO Robert Benmosche, talked directly to the camera and offered their thanks for the government assistance.[88] Peter Hancock succeeded Benmosche as President and CEO of AIG in September 2014.[89] While Benmosche stayed on in an advisory role,[89] he passed away in February the following year.[90]
