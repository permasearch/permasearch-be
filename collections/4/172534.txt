Solar power in Japan has been expanding since the late 1990s. The country is a leading manufacturer of solar panels and is in the top 5 ranking for countries with the most solar PV installed, with 4,914 MW installed at the end of 2011, making it third in the world in total solar power (behind Germany and Italy),[29] with most of it grid connected.[30][31][32] The insolation is good at about 4.3 to 4.8 kWh/(m²·day).
