On 18 April, Sánchez scored both of Arsenal's goals in the FA Cup semi-final as Arsenal defeated Reading 2–1 after extra time.[70] Eight days later, he was the only Arsenal player named in the PFA Team of the Year.[71] On 4 May, Sánchez scored another brace, this time against Hull City in a 3–1 away win.[72] On 29 May, he was voted as the 2015 PFA Fans' Player of the Year.[73]
