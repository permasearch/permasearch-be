The film had its world premiere on February 13, 2015 at the 65th Berlin International Film Festival,[40][41] and was released on March 13, 2015.[42] Theatrically, it was accompanied by Walt Disney Animation Studios' short film Frozen Fever, featuring the characters from Frozen.[43] On February 10, 2015, IMAX Corporation and Disney announced plans to digitally re-master the film into the IMAX format and release it in IMAX theaters globally on the scheduled release date.[44]
