This article is a list of seasons completed by the Utah Jazz of the National Basketball Association (NBA).  The Jazz joined the NBA as the New Orleans Jazz, an expansion team that began play in the 1974–75 season.  The Jazz relocated from New Orleans to Salt Lake City, Utah for the 1979–80 season.  As of April 15, 2015, the Utah Jazz are the last franchise to not record a 60-loss season.
