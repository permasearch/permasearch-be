Kauffman told TV Guide the writers felt it was important that the trivia contest reveal new information about the characters "otherwise it's just exposition."[7] The answer "Viva Las Gay-gas" in response to 'What is the name of Chandler's dad's show in Vegas?' changed "about a million times" in drafts according to Crane.[5] On the night the show was being filmed, writers continued to pitch for different answers in order to receive a better response from the audience.[5] The staff found it difficult coming up with different points of view for each character as all wanted to win the game.[5]
