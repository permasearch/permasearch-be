Richard Stokes produced series 1 and 2 of Torchwood; Originally, James Hawes (a Doctor Who director) was lined up as the producer, but he later withdrew from this project.[40][50] Series 3 was produced by Peter Bennett.[51] Series 4 was produced by Kelly A Manners,[52] with UK filming produced by Brian Minchin, producer of Series 4 and 5 of The Sarah Jane Adventures.[53][54] The series also shares Doctor Who's production designer, Edward Thomas.[55][56] Music for the series was composed by Ben Foster[57] and Doctor Who's composer Murray Gold,[58] with composer Stu Kennedy assisting on Series 4.[59]
