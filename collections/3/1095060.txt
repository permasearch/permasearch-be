Iron Man 2 premiered at the El Capitan Theatre in Los Angeles, California on April 26, 2010,[94] and was released in 6,764 theaters (48 IMAX) across 54 countries between April 28 and May 7,[95][6][96] before going into general release in the United States on May 7, 2010.[95] In the United States, it opened at 4,380 theaters, 181 of which were IMAX.[97] The international release date of the film was moved forward to increase interest ahead of the 2010 FIFA World Cup association football tournament.[98]
