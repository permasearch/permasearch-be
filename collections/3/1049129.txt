Native American leaders who signed the treaty included leaders of these bands and tribes: Wyandot Chiefs Tarhe, Leatherlips, Roundhead (Wyandot), Delaware (Lenape; several bands). Shawnee, Chief Blue Jacket, Ottawa (several bands), Chippewa, Potawatomi (several bands), Miami (several bands), Chief Little Turtle, Wea, Kickapoo, and Kaskaskia.[8]
