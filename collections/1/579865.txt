In 2012, Cass announced that The CW had optioned the rights to her series with the intent of turning it into a television series. The series would star Aimee Teegarden[17] as America Singer, but the pilot was not picked up to series for the fall 2012 television season.[18][19] A second pilot was ordered for the CW in 2013,[20] starring Yael Grobglas as the same character[21][22] but was also not picked up by the network.[23]
