To sponsor the Communist Party's 1936 candidate for the California State Assembly's 57th District, Ball signed a certificate stating, "I am registered as affiliated with the Communist Party."[60] The same year, she was appointed to the State Central Committee of the Communist Party of California, according to records of the California Secretary of State.
