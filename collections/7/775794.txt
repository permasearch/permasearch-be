His daughters, Eugenie and Beatrice, were noted in 2008 as having earned the best grades so far in the royal family in their A-levels, better than their father and uncle, and than their older cousins William and Harry.[30] Eugenie ranked first with her two As and a B in art, English literature, and history of art from Marlborough College.[30] Beatrice, who is dyslexic,[71] studied at Goldsmiths, University of London, graduating in 2011 with a 2:1 degree in history and the history of ideas.[72] Eugenie studied at Newcastle University, graduating in 2012 with a 2:1 degree in English and history of art.[73]
