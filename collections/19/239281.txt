Firearms known as top-o-tufak also existed in the Vijayanagara Empire of India by as early as 1366 AD.[135] By 1442 guns had a clearly felt presence in India as attested to by historical records.[71] From then on the employment of gunpowder warfare in India was prevalent, with events such as the siege of Belgaum in 1473 AD by the Sultan Muhammad Shah Bahmani.[140]
