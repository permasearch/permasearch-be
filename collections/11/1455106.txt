Aside from sporting uses, the Emirates has been used as a music venue which increases the maximum capacity to 72,000.[120] Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band became the first band to play a concert at the stadium on 30 May 2008.[121] They played a second gig the following night.[121] British band Coldplay played three concerts at the Emirates in the June 2012, having sold out the first two dates within 30 minutes of going on sale. They were the first band to sell out the stadium for music purposes. Green Day set an gig attendance record when performing at the Emirates in June 2013.[122]
