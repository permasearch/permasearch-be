"How Can I Be Sure" was revived in 1972 with a version from David Cassidy, which became a major hit on both sides of the Atlantic, reaching No. 25 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 3 on the Easy Listening chart. [9] In the UK, "How Can I Be Sure" spent two weeks at No. 1 on the UK Singles Chart in September and October 1972.[10]
