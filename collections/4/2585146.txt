"Bang" first aired on CBS in the United States on March 2, 2010, marking the first episode of The Good Wife in three weeks after a break due to the 2010 Winter Olympics.[10][11] At the same time, it was simulcast on the Global Television Network in Canada.[12] The episode aired on NET 5 in the Netherlands on April 2, 2010.[13] The day following its American broadcast, "Bang" was made available for download at the digital media store iTunes.[14]
