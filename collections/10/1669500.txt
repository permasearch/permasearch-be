"Grenade" received several nominations. In 2011, it was nominated for "Choice Music: Break-Up Song" at the 2011 Teen Choice Awards[38] and at the 2011 MTV Europe Music Awards the song received a nomination in the category of Best Song.[39] In 2012, "Grenade" was nominated for three Grammy Awards, including: Record of the Year, Song of the Year, and the newly introduced Best Pop Solo Performance, losing all of them to British singer Adele.[40] In the same year, the single received a nomination for "Best Hit International" at the Swiss Music Awards.[41] At the 2012 ASCAP Pop Music Awards the song was able to win the title of "Most Performed Song"[42] The song was nominated for the 2011 The Record of the Year ceremony.[43]
