In late 2012, Mac Miller confirmed an early 2013 release date for the album.[18] In a March 1, 2013, interview with MTV, Miller announced that he was close to finishing the album and that he would be releasing music videos in a few weeks.[12] In late March 2013 Mac Miller announced a release date of either May or June 2013, and said that the album was one song away from completion. He turned the album in to the label in late April 2013.[19] On May 2, he announced via Ustream and Twitter that the album would be released on, June 18, 2013.[20]
