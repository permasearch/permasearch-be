Mandy Milkovich (Jane Levy season 1) (Emma Greenwell season 2 to 6) is a recurring character in season 1, 2 and 5, a regular character in seasons 3 and 4, and a guest character in season 6. Mandy is Mickey's sister who has a reputation at school for her sexuality. She originally wanted to date Ian but was humiliated when he rebuffed her advances. She sent her brothers to beat him up but he confessed to her that he's actually gay. She offers to be his beard and they become close friends.
