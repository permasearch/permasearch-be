All ethnicities who were of the literati were possibly called Haner because ethnic Xianbei were referred to as "damned Chinese" by the Northern Qi.[26] Appearing as culturally Xianbei and at the same time declaring Han Chinese ancestry was done by Gao Huan and the Han family.[27] The Han Chinese Gao family of Bohai (渤海高氏[28][29]) was claimed by Gao Huan as his ancestors.
