Luke Cage is available on the streaming service Netflix, in all territories where it is available,[38] in Ultra HD 4K and high dynamic range.[80] The first season was enhanced to be available in HDR after its initial release by post-production vendor Deluxe.[81] The episodes of each season were released simultaneously, as opposed to a serialized format, to encourage binge-watching, a format which has been successful for other Netflix original series.[58][61]
