Emerging from Los Angeles' glam metal and goth scene,[71][78] Jane's Addiction's music contains elements from heavy metal, punk rock, folk, jazz fusion, funk rock, psychedelic rock and progressive rock.[73][78][79][80][81][82][83][84] The band's other prominent influences included '70s heavy metal, art rock,[85] post-punk  and gender bendering glam rock.[83] The band was also labeled as "funk-punk".[86] Their album, Strays was labeled as "nu metal",[87][88][89] a genre which the band was a precursor to.[90]
