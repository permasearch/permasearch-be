Major opera companies have begun presenting their performances in local cinemas throughout the United States and many other countries. The Metropolitan Opera began a series of live high-definition video transmissions to cinemas around the world in 2006.[73] In 2007, Met performances were shown in over 424 theaters in 350 U.S. cities.
