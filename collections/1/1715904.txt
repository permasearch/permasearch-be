Hernandez began dating Shayanna Jenkins in 2007. They had a daughter, Avielle Janelle Jenkins-Hernandez, who was born in November 2012.[30] They became engaged in the same month their daughter was born. That month, Hernandez purchased a 7,100 square foot (660 m2) four-story home, with an in-ground pool, in North Attleborough, Massachusetts, for $1.3 million, where the family lived together.[31]
