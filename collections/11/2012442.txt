Several of Kay's advertisements are well known. While at Needham Worldwide he created the Brother Dominic campaign for Xerox Corporation which premiered during the 1976 Super Bowl.[9] In developing the Xerox campaigns Kay worked with the computer scientist, Alan Kay (no relation), who led the development team at Xerox PARC (now PARC).[10] Advertising Age named Brother Dominic one of the top 50 campaigns of the 20th century and ESPN called the spot the prelude to every boundary-pushing ad that followed.[11][12] The New York Times called it one of the top 25 ads of the 20th century.[13] The Brother Dominic campaign was inducted into the Clio Hall of Fame.[6] In all, there were six Brother Dominic spots that aired on television between 1976 and 1982.[13]
