Wayne-Westland operates David Hicks Elementary School in Inkster.[19] The portions of Inkster that are located in the Wayne-Westland district are zoned to Hicks.[20][21] One portion of the Hicks zone is assigned to Marshall Upper Elementary School,[22] Stevenson Middle School in Westland,[23] John Glenn High School, all in Westland.[24] Another portion of the Hicks zone is assigned to Adams Upper Elementary School in Westland,[25] Franklin Middle School in Wayne,[26] and Wayne Memorial High School in Wayne.[27]
