The U.S. Army formally accepted its first production AH-64A in January 1984 and training of the first pilots began later that year.[81][82] The first operational Apache unit, 7th Battalion, 17th Cavalry Brigade, began training on the AH-64A in April 1986 at Fort Hood, Texas.[83][84] Two operational units with 68 AH-64s first deployed to Europe in September 1987 and took part in large military exercises there.[83][85]
