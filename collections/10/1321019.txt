Despite Saudi Arabia asking for Pakistan's support to join the coalition,[380] the Pakistan government has also called for a ceasefire in order to help negotiate a diplomatic solution.[381] Alongside Turkey, Pakistan has taken initiatives to arrange a ceasefire in Yemen.[382] Analysis written in U.S. News, Pakistan's strategic calculations firmly believes that if the Saudis enter into a ground war in Yemen– with or without Pakistani military– it will become a stalemate; therefore, Pakistan is increasing its efforts to potentially help engineer a face-saving solution to achieve a ceasefire and end the war.[380]
