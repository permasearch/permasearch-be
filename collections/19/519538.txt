Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle was released on Digital HD March 6, 2018 and on DVD, Blu-ray and 4K Ultra HD Blu-ray March 20, 2018 despite the film was still in theatres. The Blu-ray and digital versions include two additional featurettes: “Surviving the Jungle: Spectacular Stunts!” and “Book to Board Game to Big Screen & Beyond! Celebrating The Legacy of Jumanji.”[35]
