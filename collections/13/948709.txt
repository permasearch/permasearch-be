The Red Sox acquired starting pitcher Josh Beckett, who pitched a complete game shutout for the Marlins against the Yankees to end the 2003 World Series, the end of the 2005 season.[270] The Yankees would follow with their own off-season acquisition of former Red Sox outfielder Johnny Damon, a fan-favorite during his four years in Boston.[271][272] Damon returned to Fenway Park the following May to a mix of cheers and boos as he tipped his helmet to the fans.[273][274]
