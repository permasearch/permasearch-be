It is generally believed that the first game of the Brazilian national football team was a 1914 match between a Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo select team and the English club Exeter City, held in Fluminense's stadium.[27][28] Brazil won 2–0 with goals by Oswaldo Gomes and Osman,[27][28][29] though it is claimed that the match was a 3–3 draw.[30][31]
