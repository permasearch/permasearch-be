On February 9, 1947, the 482th Artillery Battalion was organized under Lt. Col. Jacinto Hidalgo, its batteries were organized in San Juan and Cayey throughout the year and one in 1948.[93] Along the 225th Engineering Battalion, reorganized on January 22, 1948, the 296th was fully reorganized.[94] In May 1947, Esteves re-designated several companies of the 295th and 296th in order to facilitate their training with tanks and mortars.[95]
