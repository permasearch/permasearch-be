The original Chromecast measures 2.83 inches (72 mm) in length and has an HDMI plug built into the body.[13] It contains the Marvell Armada 1500-mini 88DE3005  system on a chip running an ARM Cortex-A9 processor. The SoC includes codecs for hardware decoding of the VP8 and H.264 video compression formats. Radio communication is handled by AzureWave NH–387 Wi-Fi which implements 802.11 b/g/n (2.4 GHz). The device has 512 MB of Micron DDR3L RAM and 2 GB of flash storage.[14][15]
