In September 2018, the Duchess announced her support for a charity cookbook (for which she wrote the Foreword) titled Together: Our Community Cookbook.[125][126] The book, which is written by women from the Grenfell community, features recipes from around the world.[127] Meghan, who has regularly visited the Hubb Community Kitchen since January 2018,[127] said in a statement: "Through this charitable endeavour, the proceeds will allow the kitchen to thrive and keep the global spirit of community alive."[125]
