Progress of civil works up to February 2018 was 99.12%.[6] The line was initially targeted for completion in December 2016, however, this date was extended. The trials of the line began in August 2017.[7] The line got inaugurated in two stages where the first section from Botanical Garden to Kalkaji Mandir was opened for public by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 25 December 2017[8] and the second stage got inaugurated by Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on 28 May 2018. The remaining section of the line became functional and opened for public use from 29 May 2018 at 06:00 hrs IST.[9]
