Since 1961, several tests of anti-ballistic missiles were conducted on Kwajalein. Therefore, there are launchpads on Illeginni Island ( 9°05′11″N 167°28′23″E﻿ / ﻿9.08631°N 167.47303°E﻿ / 9.08631; 167.47303﻿ (Illeginni)), Roi-Namur Island ( 9°24′04″N 167°27′59″E﻿ / ﻿9.4012°N 167.4663°E﻿ / 9.4012; 167.4663﻿ (Roi-Namur)) and Kwajalein Drop Zone, Pacific Ocean ( 7°39′00″N 167°42′00″E﻿ / ﻿7.6500°N 167.7000°E﻿ / 7.6500; 167.7000﻿ (Kwajalein Drop Zone)).[citation needed]
