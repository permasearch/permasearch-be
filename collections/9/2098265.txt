Underwood has been the recipient of seven Grammy Awards,[208] ten Billboard Music Awards,[305] fourteen Academy of Country Music Awards,[306] twelve American Music Awards,[307] eight Country Music Association Awards,[308] six Teen Choice Awards, three CMT Artists of the Year awards,[309] and seventeen CMT Music Awards, among others. As a songwriter, she has also received a Golden Globe Award nomination h [310] and won ten BMI Awards.[311][312] Underwood was inducted into the Grand Ole Opry in 2008,[313] for her many accomplishments in country music, and into the Oklahoma Music Hall of Fame in 2009.[314] Further, she was inducted into the Oklahoma Hall of Fame in 2017, the highest honor one can receive from the state. In December 2009, she was awarded the prestigious Harmony Award from the Nashville Symphony Orchestra for her achievements in music across many genres.[315] In 2013, she received Artist Achievement Award at the 38th Annual New York Honors Gala held by the T.J. Martell Foundation.[316] In 2014, she was honored by the Country Radio Broadcasters' Board of Directors with the CRB Artist Humanitarian Award at the 2014 Country Radio Seminar.[317]
