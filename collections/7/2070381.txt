Raonic began 2012 with titles in two of his first three tournaments, starting with his second ATP title at the Chennai Open in India. He had back-to-back wins over top 10 players at a tournament for the first time, beating Nicolás Almagro in the semifinals and Janko Tipsarević in the final.[93] Raonic held serve during the entire tournament, becoming the first player to do so since Federer at the 2008 Halle Open.[94] He lost in the third round of the Australian Open to Lleyton Hewitt.[95] In February, Raonic defended his title at the Pacific Coast Championships, defeating Denis Istomin in the final for his third ATP title.[96] The following week in Memphis, Raonic reached the final of the U.S. National Indoor Tennis Championships for the second straight year, but lost to Melzer.[96]
