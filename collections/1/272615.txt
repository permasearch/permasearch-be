Holt tractors were also the inspiration for the development of the British tank, which profoundly altered ground warfare tactics.[14][26] Major Ernest Swinton, sent to France as an army war correspondent, very soon saw the potential of a track-laying tractor.[27]:116 Although the British later chose an English firm to build its first tanks, the Holt tractor became "one of the most important military vehicles of all time."[25]
