There is no grave for Maugham. His ashes were scattered near the Maugham Library, The King's School, Canterbury.[44] Liza Maugham, Lady Glendevon, died aged 83 in 1998, survived by her four children (a son and a daughter by her first marriage to Vincent Paravicini, and two more sons to Lord Glendevon). One of her grandchildren is Derek Paravicini, who is a musical prodigy and autistic savant.[45][46]
