Chennai houses several theme parks, namely MGM Dizzee World and Queens Land.[248][249] The safety of several amusement parks has been questioned after several fatal accidents occurred.[250] Wonderla is planning to open an amusement park in 2017.[251] Other important recreation centres include Madras Boat Club, which is over 140 years old, and Gymkhana Club, which is famous for its 18-hole golf courses. Built in 1867, Madras Boat Club is the second oldest surviving Indian rowing club.[252]
