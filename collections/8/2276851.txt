The natural reserve of the thousand-year-old Cardon Cactus has become a major attraction after the transport of one of these giant specimens to Seville, Spain for Seville Expo '92. The area has also become a favorite spot for photographers worldwide. Local environmentalists are lobbying for the protection of the valley in the form of a Nature Reserve. The entrance to the park is located at 30°52′38″N 114°44′27″W﻿ / ﻿30.8771944°N 114.7407916°W﻿ / 30.8771944; -114.7407916﻿ (Valley of the Giants).
