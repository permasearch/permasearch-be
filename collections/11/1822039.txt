Victoria is Ted's ex-girlfriend. They meet at a wedding and fall in love.[32] After two months of dating, she moves to Germany and they try to have a long distance relationship.[33] They break up after Ted kisses Robin.[34] Six years later, they meet again, and Victoria ends up leaving a man at the altar for Ted.[35] Ultimately, however, they break up again after Victoria insists that Ted end his friendship with Robin.[36] Victoria is portrayed by Ashley Williams.
