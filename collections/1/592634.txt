In January 2018, Buc-ee's broke ground on second out of Texas location in Baldwin County Alabama, [14] At 50,000 square feet with 120 gas pumps, it will be the second Buc-ee's to be built outside of Texas and the first in Alabama. A few weeks after the Baldwin County location began construction, Buc-ee's announced that a second Southern Alabama location will be built in Mobile. [15]
