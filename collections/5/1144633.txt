Globally, the U.S. recovery boosted Ireland's economy due to Ireland's close economic ties to the US.[citation needed] The decline in tourism as a result of foot and mouth disease and the 11 September 2001 attacks had reversed itself.[53][better source needed] The recovery of the global information technology industry was also a factor; Ireland produced[citation needed] 25% of all European PCs, and Apple, Dell (whose major European manufacturing plant was in Limerick),  HP, and IBM all had sizeable Irish operations.
