In the season two episode, "Morality Bites", Piper travels ten years into the future from the year 1999 and inhabits her future self's body.[28] In the episode, Piper discovers that in ten years time, her freezing power becomes ten times stronger allowing her to instantly freeze an entire city block.[28] After returning to the present, Piper never realizes the growth of this level of power before the series comes to an end.
