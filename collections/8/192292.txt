Hazard made his Champions League debut for Chelsea in the team's opening group stage game against Juventus.[156] On 6 October, he scored his second goal for Chelsea in a 4–1 win against Norwich City.[157] During December, he scored in back-to-back games, in a 5–1 away victory over Leeds United in the League Cup,[158] and an 8–0 league win against Aston Villa.[159]
