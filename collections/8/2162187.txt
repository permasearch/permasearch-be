Ice hockey is particularly popular in the interior regions of Croatia's Pannonia, Slavonia, Zagreb and Zagorje, where winters are as cold as in more prolific hockey nations such as the Slovak and Czech Republics. Today, KHL Medveščak competes in the Russian Kontinental Hockey League or KHL, the top-tier Russian tournament and the 2nd highest-ranking ice hockey league in the world.[6] Before joining KHL, Medveščak played in the Austrian  EBEL, Medveščak debuted in EBEL in the 2009 season and it has qualified for the playoffs every year.
