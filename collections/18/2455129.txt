As of the census[11] of 2000, there were 9,022 people, 3,372 households, and 2,319 families residing in the city.  The population density was 1,412.0 people per square mile (545.1/km²).  There were 3,579 housing units at an average density of 560.2 per square mile (216.3/km²).  The racial makeup of the city was 86.51% White, 0.62% African American, 4.13% Native American, 1.77% Asian, 0.12% Pacific Islander, 2.33% from other races, and 4.51% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 7.30% of the population.
