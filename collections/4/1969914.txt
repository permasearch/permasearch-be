At its widest distribution in the U.S., the film was screened at 1,647 theaters grossing $8,100,840 in its opening weekend. During that first weekend, the film opened in second place behind Star Trek IV: The Voyage Home.[16] Revenue dropped by 41% in its second week of release, earning $4,721,454.[17] During its final weekend showing in theaters, the film grossed $1,040,729. It went on to take in a total of $42,724,017 in ticket sales during a seven-week theatrical run[17] and a worldwide total of $121,700,000.[2] It ranked 18th at the box office for 1986.[18]
