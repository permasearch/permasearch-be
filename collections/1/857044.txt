Jackman married Deborra-Lee Furness on 11 April 1996, at St. John's in Toorak, Victoria, a suburb of Melbourne.[68] They met on the set of Australian TV show Correlli.[69] Jackman personally designed an engagement ring for Furness, and their wedding rings bore the Sanskrit inscription "Om paramar mainamar", translated as "we dedicate our union to a greater source".[70] Furness had two miscarriages,[71] following which she and Jackman adopted two children, Oscar[72] and Ava.[73][74]
