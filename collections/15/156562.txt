Principal photography began in late January 2017 in Macon, Georgia, where Macon Coliseum was used as a set.[12][13][14] Throughout the shoot, Robbie suffered from a herniated disc in her neck and had routine MRIs to ensure it was safe for her to continue filming skating scenes.[9] Filming wrapped in late February of that year,[15] with pickups in Atlanta on May 16.[16][17]
