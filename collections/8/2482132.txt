Today, total operating budgets for the member museums exceed $80 million, funded almost completely from revenues and private donations. Museum District funding includes approximately $1.5 million in Hotel Occupancy Tax funds from the City of Houston and the Houston Arts Alliance;[26] contributions from all participating museums; foundation support from The Houston Endowment, Inc.;[27] The John P. McGovern Foundation;[28] The Wortham Foundation, Inc.;[29] The Brown Foundation, Inc.;[30] Susan Vaughan Foundation, Inc.; and corporate support from KHOU-TV Channel 11, KUHT-TV (PBS),[31] the Houston Chronicle, Texas Monthly, Exxon, and Cadillac.[2]
