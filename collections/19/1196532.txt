Quality adjusted price of IT equipment – The price of information technology (IT), computers and peripheral equipment, adjusted for quality and inflation, declined 16% per year on average over the five decades from 1959 to 2009. [124][125] The pace accelerated, however, to 23% per year in 1995–1999 triggered by faster IT innovation,[17] and later, slowed to 2% per year in 2010–2013.[124][126]
