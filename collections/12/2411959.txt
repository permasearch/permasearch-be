"Dumb Things" or "I've Done all the Dumb Things" was the fourth single released by Australian rock group Paul Kelly and the Coloured Girls from their second album, Under the Sun. The single was released by Mushroom Records imprint White Label Records in January 1989 and reached No. 36 on the Australian Recording Industry Association (ARIA) Singles Chart. In the US, it was released under the band name, Paul Kelly and the Messengers, which reached No. 16 on the Billboard Modern Rock chart. A music video, directed by Larry Williams, was provided for the single – a still from the clip is used as the single's cover.
