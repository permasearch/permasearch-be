The university offers both undergraduate (B.Sc., B.A., BBA and LLB) courses and postgraduate (M.Sc., M.Tech., MBA) ones, as well as engineering diploma courses.[14] Admission is based on national and state entrance exams and a personal interview.[15] Ph.D. positions are also available.[12] The university is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC)[2] which has also published an inspection report in 2015,[16] and a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).[17]
