9 October (Maspero demonstrations):[149][150] Late in the evening of 9 October, during a protest in the Maspiro television building,[151] peaceful Egyptian protesters calling for the dissolution the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces, the resignation of Chairman Field Marshal Mohamed Tantawi and the dismissal of the governor of Aswan province were attacked by military police. At least 25 people[152] were killed, and more than 200 wounded.
