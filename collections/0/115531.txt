Bakay served as a writer of Sabrina the Teenage Witch, in which he also voiced Salem Saberhagen, a warlock who has been transformed into the Spellman family's pet cat for trying to take over the world. Bakay also served as a writer for the cartoon series Sabrina: The Animated Series, also providing his voice as Salem in this series as well, and as the voice of Norbert on The Angry Beavers.
