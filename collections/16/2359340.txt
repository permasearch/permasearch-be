In 2016, the population of Windsor was 217,188. In 2017, the total population of the Windsor metropolitan area (consisting of Windsor, Tecumseh, Amherstburg, LaSalle and Lakeshore) was 344,747.[40] This represents an increase of 3.0% in the city population since 2011 and an increase of 3.1% in the metropolitan area population since 2011.[41] During the same period, Ontario grew by 4.6% and Canada by 5.0%.[42]
