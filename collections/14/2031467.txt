In a 2006 viewer poll conducted by Japan's Famitsu magazine of top 100 games of all time, Metal Gear Solid 2 was ranked at #42 in the poll.[107] In the 200th issue of Game Informer Magazine in 2009, its list of top 200 games of all time ranked the game at #50 on the list.[108] Metal Gear Solid 2 was ranked #7 in Game Informer's 2008 list of the top ten video game openings.[109] In 2010, GamesRadar included the game in its list of top seven games "with mega plot twists you never saw coming."[110] The game was included in Tony Mott and Peter Molyneux's 2011 book 1001 Video Games You Must Play Before You Die.[111] It has also been listed as one of the best games of all time by Electronic Gaming Monthly,[112] Empire,[113][114] GameFAQs,[115][116][117][118] GameRankings,[119] IGN,[120][121][122] Metacritic,[78] Slant,[123] and Stuff.[124][125]
