In the CW series Supergirl, Gotham is mentioned by a civilian after Superman saves him and his family from John Corben, telling his wife and son they should move back to the city. Gotham and its vigilantes are referenced by Supergirl during a battle with Master Jailer. It is also mentioned in Arrow, where in the same episode Oliver mentions Bruce Wayne by name. Gotham City will be incorporated into the Arrowverse as part of a 2018 crossover storyline among the shows, which will introduce Batwoman.[94]
