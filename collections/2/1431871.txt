Filming on the project began at Mehboob Studio in Mumbai on 5 October 2012.[28] additional filming was done at Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus railway station with a minimal crew.[29] Stills of the lead actor were unofficially released in mid-October 2012.[30] In November 2012, the cast and crew went to Goa to begin filming the scenes set there.[31] Vasco Da Gama Railway Station was used to portray as Kalyan Junction railway station. A romantic scene on board a train passing next to Dudhsagar Falls was also shot.[32] In December 2012, the crew went to Jakarta to film certain scenes.[33]
