Alternative band B.A.L.L. covered "Wah-Wah" on their 1988 album Bird, as part of their parody of early 1970s rock stars such as the former Beatles.[169] Buffalo Tom recorded "Wah-Wah" live on WMBR in Cambridge, Massachusetts in January 1991, a version that appeared on the band's Fortune Teller EP later that year.[170] Ocean Colour Scene covered the song on their 2005 album A Hyperactive Workout for the Flying Squad,[171][172] and in 2011 former Jefferson Starship vocalist Mickey Thomas released a version on his album Marauder.[173]
