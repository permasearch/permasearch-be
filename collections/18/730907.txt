Upon its release, "Gangnam Style" was an enormous hit. The song went straight to number one on the Gaon Singles Chart on the fourth week of July, 2012, with 816,868 digital downloads,[336][337] and spent five consecutive weeks at the top position of the chart, tying it with IU's "Good Day" for the most weeks at number one single in the chart's history.[338] In addition, the song became the first-place winner on various weekly chart shows such as M Countdown (three straight weeks)[339] and Music Bank (a total of 16 weeks including a record 10-consecutive-week).[340] According to the Korea Music Content Industry Association, "Gangnam Style" became the best selling song of 2012 in South Korea with 3,842,109 download sales.[341]
