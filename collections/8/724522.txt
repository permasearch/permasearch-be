Former member Damon Harris died on February 18, 2013, from prostate cancer at a Baltimore hospital.[59] Nine days later, former member Richard Street died of pulmonary embolism in Las Vegas, Nevada.[60] At the time of his death, Street was in the process of writing a book regarding his time with the Temptations entitled Ball of Confusion: My Life as a Temptin' Temptation. Completed by his co-author, Gary Flanigan, the book was published in 2014; it is the second autobiography regarding the group.[citation needed]
