A recent study on Y-chromosomes and mtDNA detected no African admixture in European-Americans. The sample included 628 European-American Y-chromosomes and mtDNA from 922 European-Americans[409] According to a genome-wide study by 23andme White Americans (European Americans) on average are: “98.6 percent European, 0.19 percent African and 0.18 percent Native American.”[401]
