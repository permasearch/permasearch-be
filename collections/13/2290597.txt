The city's treatment of shack dwellers has been strongly criticised by a report from the United Nations linked Centre on Housing Rights and Evictions[44] and there has also been strong criticism of the city's treatment of street traders,[45][46] street children[47] and sex workers.[48] Durban is known throughout the world for its strain of cannabis called 'Durban Poison'. It is one of the most common strains sold by car guards and street dealers throughout eThekweni.[49]
