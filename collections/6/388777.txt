For the 20th anniversary of Crisis on Infinite Earths, DC Direct released three series of Crisis action figures. The first series included Earth-Two Robin, Supergirl, the Monitor, Harbinger and Psycho-Pirate. The second series included the Flash (Barry Allen), Earth-Two Superman, the Anti-Monitor, Lex Luthor and Brainiac, and the third series included Earth-Prime Superboy, Batman, a Weaponer of Qward, Earth-Two Huntress and the female Dr. Light.[citation needed]
