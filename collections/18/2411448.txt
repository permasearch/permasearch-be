Critics have argued that evolutionary psychologists created a false dichotomy between their own view and the caricature of the SSSM.[147][148][149] Other critics regard the SSSM as a rhetorical device or a straw man[146][147][150] and suggest that the scientists whom evolutionary psychologists associate with the SSSM did not believe that the mind was a blank state devoid of any natural predispositions.[146]
