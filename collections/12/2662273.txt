Eurogamer writer Rich Stanton was pleased with the creation of this Metal Gear spin-off expanding the franchise. It was noted that the game was close to receiving a perfect 10 were it not for issues with the camera.[121] Other sites noted similar issues during their reviews due to the fact it could not keep up with the fast action sequences.[124][126] The game's action was praised by several publications, stating it would be appealing for casual players and Metal Gear fans.[124][126] Some focused on the cutting system which allowed players to slice not only enemies but also the environment,[125] while IGN's Mitch Dyer commented on how the number of subweapons improved the game's variety despite a lack of flow when changing them.[126] Boss fights have also been referred as one of the game's strongest points due to its use of cutscenes and music.[120][124]
