Halifax departed the War Cabinet for his meeting with Reynaud. Not long afterwards, Churchill adjourned the meeting and joined Halifax at Admiralty House along with the rest of the War Cabinet. Later that day, after Reynaud's departure, Churchill asked the War Cabinet to remain at Admiralty House for an "informal meeting". The surprise nature of the meeting makes the records of it incomplete. War Cabinet Secretary Edward Bridges did not arrive until 15 minutes into the meeting.
