Sergey Lazarev was announced as the Russian entrant to the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 by Dutch media on 10 December 2015.[4] The following day, it was announced that Lazarev's internal selection was decided upon by the editorial board of VGTRK with the executive producer of the Russia-1 channel, Gennady Gokhshtein, revealing that a song produced by Philipp Kirkorov had also been selected.[5] The song was later released along with its music video which features Miss Universe Russia 2015 Vladislava Evtushenko on 5 March 2016.[1] He performed in the first half of the first semi-final and qualified to perform in the final on May 14.[6]
