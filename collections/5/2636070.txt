The country has seven national parties: Bharathiya Janata Party (BJP), Indian National Congress (INC), Communist Party of India (CPI), Communist Party of India (Marxist) (CPI(M)), Nationalist Congress Party (N C P) Bahujan Samaj Party (B S P) and All India Trinamool Congress (TMC)[2] At the level of its states, many regional parties stand for elections to state legislatures, every five years. The Rajya Sabha elections are held every 5 years.
