Hungry Jack's products include burgers (meat and vegetarian options), chicken, chips, onion rings, salads, drinks (hot and cold beverages) kid meals and desserts, as well as a full breakfast menu. The only Burger King trademarks that are sold at Hungry Jack's are the Whopper and the TenderCrisp and TenderGrill sandwiches. All other Burger King products go by more generic naming schemes, such as "Hamburger", "Veggie Burger", or "Grilled Chicken Burger".[22][23]
