Production of "Locked Out of Heaven" was handled by The Smeezingtons, alongside Ronson, Bhasker, and Haynie. It was mixed at Larrabee Sound Studios in Hollywood by Manny Marroquin. Bhasker, Mars, Nick Movshon, and Homer Steinweiss played the instruments, with additional assistance by Haynie. The recording was done by Ari Levine, Wayne Gordon, ALALAL and Ronson, while Bob Mallory and Tyler Hartman served as the recording assistants for the latter two. Recording took place at the Levcon Studios in Los Angeles, Daptone Studios in Brooklyn and Avatar Studios in New York. Charlez Moniz provided additional engineering to the recording. David Kutch mastered the song at Mastering Palace.[8]
