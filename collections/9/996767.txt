"One Kiss" is a song by Scottish record producer Calvin Harris and English singer Dua Lipa. It was written by Lipa, Jessie Reyez, and Harris, who also produced the song. "One Kiss" was released by Columbia Records and Sony Music on 6 April 2018, as the second single from Harris' upcoming sixth studio album. It reached number one on the UK Singles Chart on 20 April 2018, a week after debuting at number three. The song would eventually top the charts for eight consecutive weeks, becoming the longest-running number one by a lead female artist in the UK this decade, and the second longest-running number one of the century by a female artist behind only Rihanna's "Umbrella".
