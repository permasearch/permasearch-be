Three bridges connect the spit to the mainland. U.S. Route 50 crosses the Harry W. Kelley Memorial Bridge and connects to MD 528 at N. Division Street. Ocean City is the eastern terminus of US 50. Near Sacramento, California along US 50 is a mileage sign stating the distance to Ocean City, MD as 3,073 miles (4,946Â km). Maryland Route 90, a two-lane freeway, crosses the Assawoman Bay Bridge and connects to MD 528 at 62nd Street. Delaware Route 54 can also be used to reach Ocean City, as it meets Coastal Highway just north of the border.
