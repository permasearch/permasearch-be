In July 2015, it was announced that Sonata Arctica's vocalist Tony Kakko would replace Jukka Nevalainen as the band's special guest at the 2015 edition of Rock in Rio.[95] In December 2015, they became the first Finnish band to headline a show at the Wembley Arena, in London.[20] The sold-out show had the guest performance of Richard Dawkins, who spoke his parts on stage. The Tampere and London shows were filmed for a future DVD release known as Vehicle of Spirit.[96]
