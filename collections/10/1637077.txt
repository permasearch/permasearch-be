In 2012, the programming block had its most ever total viewers in its debut week, with 2.9 million.[38] An airing of the film Dr. Seuss' How the Grinch Stole Christmas on December 2 became the lineup's most watched program ever, with 5.4 million viewers.[39][40] Other notable airings included, the network premiere of Despicable Me, watched by over 4.3 million viewers,[41] and a Christmas Eve airing of The Santa Clause 2, watched by 3.9 million viewers.[42] The programming block averaged 2.8 million viewers for 2012, on pace with 2010.[40]
