Trenton is located in eastern Edgefield County at 33°44′23″N 81°50′25″W﻿ / ﻿33.73972°N 81.84028°W﻿ / 33.73972; -81.84028 (33.739721, -81.840208).[7] South Carolina Highway 121 passes through the western side of the town, intersecting U.S. Route 25 at the southwest corner of the town. US 25 leads northwest 6 miles (10 km) to Edgefield, the county seat, and southwest 20 miles (32 km) to Augusta, Georgia, while SC 121 leads northeast 8 miles (13 km) to Johnston.
