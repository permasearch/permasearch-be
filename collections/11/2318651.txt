A major industrial agglomeration with about 9,000 industrial units, Lahore has shifted in recent decades from manufacturing to service industries.[107] Some 42% of its work force is employed in finance, banking, real estate, community, cultural, and social services.[107] The city is Pakistan's largest software & hardware producing centre,[107] and hosts a growing computer-assembly industry.[107] The city has always been a centre for publications where 80% of Pakistan's books are published, and it remains the foremost centre of literary, educational and cultural activity in Pakistan.[18]
