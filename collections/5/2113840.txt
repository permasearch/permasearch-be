The song is also featured in the soundtracks of several video games, including SSX on Tour (2005) as the title's main theme,[24] and Grand Theft Auto: The Lost and Damned (2009).[25] In addition, a cover version is featured in Rock Band (2007), in which it received an 'Impossible' difficulty rating,[26] while the original version was made available via download in June 2009.[27]
