Wanyama comes from a sporting family. His brother McDonald Mariga is also a professional footballer,[52][53] while his other brothers Thomas and Sylvester Wanyama are also footballers in the Kenyan Premier League. His father, Noah Wanyama, was a footballer for A.F.C. Leopards in the 1980s,[54] and his sister Mercy is a professional basketball player in the USA.[55]
