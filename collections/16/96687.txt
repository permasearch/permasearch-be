A new subsidiary, City Centre Terminal Corp., was set up in 2009 to manage Porter's new terminal at the Toronto island airport. The new terminal's cost of construction is estimated at $50 million CAD.[6] The first half of the new terminal opened on March 7, 2010.[7] The terminal was completed in early 2011. The new terminal has ten gates, two lounges, check-in and security areas, and food outlets.
