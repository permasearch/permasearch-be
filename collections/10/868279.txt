The Lake of the Ozarks is located at 38°12′09″N 92°37′35″W﻿ / ﻿38.20250°N 92.62639°W﻿ / 38.20250; -92.62639 (38.2025326, -92.6262966) at an elevation of 568 feet (173 m).[4] It lies in central Missouri on the Salem Plateau of the Ozarks.[9] The lake extends across four Missouri counties, from Benton County in the west through Camden County and Morgan County to Miller County in the east.[10]
