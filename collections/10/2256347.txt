The story was drafted by series creator I. Marlene King and Kyle Bown, while its teleplay was composed by King and executive producer Maya Goldsmith. This is the only season finale not to be entirely written by King herself. The episode was aired as a 2-hour special event.[1] Directed by King, the episode was filmed in October 2016 in and around Los Angeles, California, mostly on the backlot of the Warner Bros. studio lot in Burbank. Filming started in October 5[2] and wrapped in October 26, marking it the last day of filming for Pretty Little Liars ever.[3] Scenes at the set for the fictional Lost Woods Resort were filmed.[4]
