In the quarterfinals, which took place on August 18, Williams took part in the second heat. He again ranked third with a time of 20.28 seconds, defeating Malcolm from his previous heat (20.30 seconds) but falling behind heat leaders Dzingai (20.23 seconds) and the United States' Walter Dix (20.27 seconds). Takahira also was in Williams' heat, placing seventh (20.63 seconds). Of the 31 finishing quarterfinalists, Williams ranked fourth. He placed just ahead of Usain Bolt, who finished 0.01 seconds behind him. Williams advanced to the semifinals.[10]
