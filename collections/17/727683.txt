The Palmer House is a historic hotel in Sauk Centre, Minnesota, United States. It was built in 1901 and expanded in 1916.[2] The hotel was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982 for having local significance in the themes of commerce and social history.[3] It was nominated for being an example of a once-common hotel type catering specifically to traveling salesmen.[2] In 1994 the Palmer House was also listed as a contributing property to the Original Main Street Historic District.[4]
