WatchMojo.com has also placed Cowboy Bebop on multiple ranking lists. In 2013, the show ranked #6 on their "Top 10 Anime Series" list.[102] They also ranked the character of Vicious as #6 on their "Top 10 Anime Villains".[103] In 2014 respectively, the company also ranked the show's opening theme sequence as #9 on their "Top 10 Anime Opening Theme Songs"[104] and gave Spike Speigel an honorable mention in their "Top 10 Anime Anti-Heroes" list.[105] On a revised 2017 listing for Top 10 Anime Series of All Time, the show was ranked at #2.[106]
