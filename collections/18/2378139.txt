Francesca "Franky" Doyle is a fictional character from the television drama Wentworth, played by Nicole da Silva. She made her first appearance in "No Place Like Home", broadcast on 1 May 2013. Franky is a prisoner in Wentworth Correctional Centre. Her storylines have often focused on her relationships with Kim Chang, Erica Davidson and Bridget Westfall, her status as top dog of the prison, and her rivalries with Jacs Holt and Bea Smith. The character made her final appearance in the sixth season episode "Bleed Out", which aired on 3 July 2018.
