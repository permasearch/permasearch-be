The Last of the Street Survivors Farewell Tour is a concert tour by the Southern rock band Lynyrd Skynyrd. This will be the final tour conducted by the band.[1] It began May 4, 2018, at the Coral Sky Amphitheater in West Palm Beach, Florida[2] and will end January 8, 2019 after the Southern Rock Cruise concludes.[3] The band will utilize a plethora of opening acts, including Bad Company, Kid Rock, and Hank Williams Jr.,throughout the tour.
