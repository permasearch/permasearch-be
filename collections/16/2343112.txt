Bajrangi Bhaijaan was released on 17 July 2015, one day before Eid, on 4500 screens in India and 1000 screens in overseas respectively.[29][30] The film was also released in 50 countries outside India, on more than 700 screens.[31] Bajrangi Bhaijaan premiered at the 20th Busan International Film Festival (BIFF) on 6 October 2015.[32]
