"Hero" has been covered several times through live performances, as well as recorded studio versions. Additionally, since its debut, it has achieved strong media attention. In 2009, prior to the election results, Barack Obama's wife Michelle played him the song to give him strength and inner support.[71] A source close to Carey claimed that hearing the song's use meant a lot to her, saying "She was blown away when she heard. She is so honoured to have played her part in such an occasion."[71] At the 1994 Essence Awards, Aretha Franklin performed a live rendition of the song as a tribute to The Reverend Jesse Jackson.[72] In 2001, Neal Schon performed an instrumental version on the album Voice. Filipino singers Regine Velasquez and Sarah Geronimo both performed the song live on television, with the former playing a medley of the song alongside Carey's "Anytime You Need a Friend".[73] International male operatic pop group Il Divo included the Spanish version of the song on their second studio album, Ancora (2005).[74] Australian singer and actress Natalie Bassingthwaighte performed a live version of the song at the Rugby League World Cup Heroes 08 Campaign.[75] Michael Ball, British singer, actor and radio personality, covered "Hero" for his album titled One Voice (2006).[76] In 1994, Japanese singer Miho Nakayama released the song as a single with Japanese lyrics written by herself and became a big hit.[77] American Idol season 5 contestant Heather Cox performed the song during the top 20 performances week, only to be voted off that night for not being able to successfully carry the song.[78] Similarly, season 7 contestant Brooke White performed the song during the Mariah Carey themed week, while season 8 contestant Danny Gokey performed the song in the top 36 performances.[79][80] season 10 contestant Karen Rodriguez performed the song in both English and Spanish versions in the top 24 performances, making it into the top 13 the following night.[81] Japanese-American singer Yuna Ito included her rendition of "Hero" on her compilation album Love -Singles Best 2005â€“2010.[82] Barbadian R&B singer Rihanna sang "Hero" during a live talent show at her high school in Barbados when she was 15. Soon after, she was signed by Jay-Z to Def Jam.[83] The X Factor winner Melanie Amaro performed the song during the first season of the show, as a song chosen for her by the public. In 2013, Dami Im recorded a version for her album Dami Im. The album was #1 in Australia.
