Hazard is the eldest of four children. He has three brothers, all of whom play football, including Thorgan, who joined him at Chelsea in 2012 but then moved permanently to Borussia Mönchengladbach in 2015.[23] Thorgan had previously progressed through the youth ranks of Lille's biggest rivals Lens.[24][25][26][27] Hazard's other younger brothers are Kylian and Ethan. On 15 July 2013, Kylian joined White Star Bruxelles, but now plays for Hungarian side Újpest,[28] while Ethan remains playing in the youth academy of Eden's former club Tubize.[21][25][29][30]
