Achebe was the recipient of over 30 honorary degrees from universities in England, Scotland, Canada, South Africa, Nigeria and the United States, including Dartmouth College, Harvard, and Brown University.[202] He was awarded the Commonwealth Poetry Prize, an Honorary Fellowship of the American Academy of Arts and Letters (1982),[207] a Foreign Honorary Member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences (2002),[208] the Nigerian National Order of Merit (Nigeria's highest honour for academic work), the Peace Prize of the German Book Trade,[209] the Man Booker International Prize 2007[210] and the 2010 Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize.[211] He was appointed Goodwill Ambassador to the United Nations Population Fund in 1999.[212]
