A2 Corporation formed a joint venture with a major British milk supplier, Müller Wiseman Dairies, in November 2011 to process, market and sell its milk with predominantly A2 protein products in Britain and Ireland.[61] In June 2014 a2 Milk Company reported it had 20 dedicated farms supplying milk for processing in the UK.[62] In its first year the milk recorded ₤1 million in sales through 1000 stores.[63] On 1 January 2014, The a2 Milk company exited its joint venture with Müller Wiseman Dairies by acquiring MWD's stake for a "nominal" amount.[64]
