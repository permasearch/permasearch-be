As of June 2016[update], Opel/Vauxhall Ampera sales totaled just over 10,000 units since 2011,[255][256] with the Netherlands as the leading market with 5,031 Amperas registered,[257][258][259] followed by Germany with 1,542 units,[260][261][262][263][264] and the UK with 1,250 units registered by the end of June 2015.[265] The Netherlands is also the top selling Volt market in Europe with 1,062 units registered through December 2014,[257][258][259] out of about 1,750 Volts sold through 2014.[265]
