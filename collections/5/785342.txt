In 2014, Bush signed to producer Byron Gallimore's Streamsound Records as a solo artist.[64] His debut solo single, "Trailer Hitch," was released to radio and retailers on July 28, 2014. His debut solo album, Southern Gravity, was released on April 7, 2015. He wrote 300 songs for the project, which he refers to as a “mainstream country record that is meant to be played on the radio.”[65] Nettles released two solo albums in 2016, Playing with Fire on May 13, and To Celebrate Christmas on October 28.
