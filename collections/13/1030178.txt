While in the United States for the North American leg of their Love Yourself tour, BTS performed "Idol" on the second semifinal episode of America's Got Talent season thirteen, which aired on September 12.[32][33] The band sang "Idol" on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon and Good Morning America on September 25 and 26 respectively,[34] in addition to a sit-down interview on both shows.[35][36] A bonus performance of "I'm Fine" was also released on The Tonight Show's YouTube channel.[37]
