The film received generally positive reviews from critics, with a 68% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes based on 229 reviews and an average rating of 6.7/10. The website's critical consensus states, "As a visually stunning tribute to lives lost in tragedy, World Trade Center succeeds unequivocally, and it is more politically muted than many of Stone's other works."[23] On Metacritic, the film has a score of 66 out of 100, based on 40 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[24]
