Rakim Mayers was born on October 3, 1988, in the Harlem neighborhood of New York City.[3] His father was from Barbados, and his mother is African American.[4] He has an older sister, Erika B. Mayers.[5] Mayers' first name was taken from the hip hop artist Rakim, one half of the Eric B. & Rakim duo.[6] His cousin is fellow ASAP Mob member ASAP Nast.[7]
