Kamal Givens, also known as Chance, is best known for his work in reality TV, beginning with his role on season one of VH1's I Love New York, wherein he was one of 20 contenders for the affections of Tiffany "Miss New York" Pollard. On I Love New York, Chance appears with his brother Ahmad Givens, also known as Real. Chance is also a former Capitol Records artist.
