On May 31, 2017, it was reported that All the Money in the World had begun principal photography.[17] Filming continued at Elveden Hall in west Suffolk for a week at the end of July. The aristocratic Grade II-listed stately home was used to represent a Moroccan palace in the filming of a series of flashback scenes.[18] Kevin Spacey worked for just ten days on the film.[19] The original production reportedly concluded in August.[20]
