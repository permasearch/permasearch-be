There are two rugby clubs in the town; Weston-super-Mare RFC, formed in 1875, owned by Jonson Coles[139] and Hornets RFC, formed in 1962.[140] Hornets play in National League 3 [South West], whilst Weston were relegated from the same league in 2014/15 and now play in South West One division. These are national level 5 and level 6 respectively in the English rugby union system.
