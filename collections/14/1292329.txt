In mid-2002 Musharraf implemented a two-term limit on prime ministers. Both Bhutto and Musharraf's other chief rival, Nawaz Sharif, had already served two terms as prime minister.[289] She was in Dubai while the PPP contested the October 2002 general election; it received the largest number of votes but only 63 seats in the National Assembly.[270] Musharraf agreed to release Zardari in November 2004 as a symbol of good will; following his release, Zardari travelled to Dubai to be with his wife.[290]
