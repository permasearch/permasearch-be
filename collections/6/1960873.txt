After the success of the single "Good Girls Go Bad", which was their most successful single on the charts, Cobra Starship began to record what would be their fourth studio album. "You Make Me Feel..." was released before the album finish and it served as the first single from the album entitled "Night Shades".[1] It was released on May 10, 2011 and features  Salvadoran-American  pop singer/rapper Sabi.[2]  The singer/actress turned rapper first came to many people's attention after Britney Spears gave her a spot on her track  "(Drop Dead) Beautiful".[2]
