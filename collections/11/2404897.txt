The go-ahead for converting the six aircraft was given on 4 May 1982.[173] Just 50 days after being ordered, the first Vulcan tanker, XH561, was delivered to RAF Waddington.[157][173] The Vulcan K.2s were operated by No. 50 Squadron, along with three Vulcan B.2s, in support of UK air defence activities until it was disbanded in March 1984.[174]
