The county has a large population of Asian Americans, being home to the largest concentration of immigrants who are Burmese, Cambodian, Chinese, Filipino, Indian,  Indonesian, Korean, Sri Lankan, Taiwanese, and Thai in the world.[16] The largest Asian groups of the 1,346,865 (13.7%) Asians in Los Angeles County are 3.3% Filipino, 2.2% Korean, 1.0% Japanese, 0.9% Vietnamese, 0.8% Indian, and 0.3% Cambodian.
