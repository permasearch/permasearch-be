After the melodrama of the 2003 ALCS, a rematch in 2004 was hotly anticipated by baseball fans.[244][245] Yankees general manager Brian Cashman said "I think Boston...really are...a mirror image of us in terms of...aggressiveness and desire to win".[246] Yankees pitcher Mike Mussina summarized the buildup: "This is what everyone was hoping for...it's a rematch of last year, with the best two teams in the American League".[247]
