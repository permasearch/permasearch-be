In Canada, the series premiered on Family Channel on January 23, 2015,[14] and moved to Disney Channel on September 1, 2015. During the launch week of Disney Channel, the episode "Runaway Robot" aired on September 1, 2015, a week before the U.S.[15] The series premiered on Disney Channel in Australia and New Zealand on March 5, 2015.[16] In the United Kingdom and Ireland, the show premiered on March 27, 2015, on Disney Channel. On Disney Channel in Southeast Asia, it premiered in Singapore on March 13, 2015, and in the Philippines on April 10, 2015.[citation needed] The show debuted on the English feed of Disney XD in the Middle East and Africa on October 12, 2015.[17] In India, the series premiered on October 30, 2017 on Disney International HD.[18]
