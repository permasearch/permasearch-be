Menat (メナト, Menato, from Arabic منات) is a new character debuting in Street Fighter V. She is the Egyptian apprentice of Rose, and like her is able to wield Soul Power and foresee future events. She can summon multiple orbs that can be shot away or retrieved. Her name may be a reference to the menat, an artifact linked to the cult of the Egyptian goddess Hathor. WatchMojo.com included her on their list of the sexiest videogame characters of 2017.[74]
