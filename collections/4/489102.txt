Iain Matthews covered "Dirty Work" on his 1974 album Some Days You Eat the Bear and Some Days the Bear Eats You.[10][11] Allmusic critic Brett Haretnbach described it as a "now-classic tune" well suited to Matthews' voice.[10] The Herald writer Tom Van Malder described it as a "standout."[11] The Pointer Sisters included it on their 1978 album Energy.[8][12] Syrja called it an "intelligent" choice for the album.[8]
