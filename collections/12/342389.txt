In March, Farmer made a cameo appearance on the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards,[21] then returned to Little Big Shots in April as "a Little Big Shots legend", where she performed "Shout" by the Isley Brothers with Oscar.[22] In July, she participated in the 2018 Starkey Hearing Foundation gala.[23] In August, she returned to AGT as a guest performer, singing "Show Off" from The Drowsy Chaperone with Petunia.[24]
