In 1998, Banks co-authored a book entitled Tyra's Beauty, Inside and Out.[42] She announced in May 2010 that she would be writing a novel, titled Modelland,[43] loosely based on her own modelling experience.[44] Published in September 2011, it is the first of a planned three-part series.[45] Modelland topped The New York Times Best Seller list in October 2011.[46]
