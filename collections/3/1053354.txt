The second season has received positive reviews from critics. On Rotten Tomatoes, it has an 80% rating with an average score of 9.44 out of 10 based on 15 reviews.[35] On Metacritic, it has a score of 72 out of 100 based on 6 reviews.[36] Writing for Collider, Chris Cabin gave it a four star review and wrote that it's "one of the greatest seasons of television that 2017 has produced thus far."[37]
