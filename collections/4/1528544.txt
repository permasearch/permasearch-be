The 2014 meet-up occurred on July 17 in movie theaters nationwide in the United States, and aired the Grateful Dead's April 21, 1972 performance at the Beat Club television studio in Bremen, West Germany.[4][8] The screenings began at 7:30 pm local time nationwide.[8] The presentation was prepared from restored video from the original Beat Club broadcast and re-mastered audio from the original analog magnetic tapes.[8] It also included a behind-the-scenes look at the production of an upcoming Grateful Dead release, with Bob Weir on guitar and vocals, and Jeffrey Norman performing mixing and mastering.[8]
