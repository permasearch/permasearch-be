On November 20, the CDC reported that the number of STEC O26 cases, as determined by DNA fingerprinting, had increased to 45 with 16 persons requiring hospitalization and the total number states being affected had increased to six.[193] Besides Oregon and Washington, new cases were reported in the states of Minnesota, California, New York, and Ohio.[194] 43 out of 45 of the affected individuals had reported that they had eaten at a Chipotle in the week before they had become sick.
