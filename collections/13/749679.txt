The single sold 205,000 digital copies in the US in its opening week and its highest week sale was of 360,000 in December 2009.[59] "Empire State of Mind" has also peaked at number one on the Billboard component chart for the sales of legal digital downloads on October 3, 2009,[60] as well as topping the Billboard component chart for singles with the most radio airplay throughout the country for eight consecutive weeks from November 28 to January 23, 2010.[61] The track also peaked at the top position on the R&B/Hip-Hop songs chart for three consecutive weeks,[62] as well as topping the Billboard Rap Songs chart for nine consecutive weeks.[63] After five months of release "Empire State of Mind" was certified three times platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for the shipment of over 3,000,000 units in the US.[64] By August 2012, the single has reached its 5 millionth sales mark in the US,[65] and as of June 2014, the song has sold 5,513,000 copies in the US.[1]
