Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs 2014 was the fifth season of Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs, a singing competition television series, which premiered on 27 December 2014 on Zee TV. The series aired on every Saturday and Sunday nights for one hour.[1] The grand finale was held on 21 March 2015 at 8 PM IST for three hours, and the winner was Gagan Gaonkar.[2]
