The music video debuted at number eight on MTV's Total Request Live on February 9, 1999,[40] and peaking at number 1 on its thirteenth day, February 25.[41] and spent ten non-consecutive days at the top position until its "retirement", on May 11, 1999.[42][43] It won the Grammy Award for Best Short Form Music Video and the 1999 Metal Edge Readers' Choice Award for "Music Video of the Year".[44] It was also nominated for nine 1999 MTV Video Music Awards, including Best Direction. It won two, Best Rock Video and Best Editing.[45][46]
