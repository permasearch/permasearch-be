The application was available from 22 April 2013 to 31 August 2013.[63][64] This first application consisted of applicant's general information, a motivational letter, a résumé and a video. More than 200,000 people expressed interest. By 9 September 2013, 4,227 applicants[65] had paid their registration fee and submitted public videos in which they made their case for going to Mars.[66] The application fee varies from US $5 to US $75 (the amount depending on the relative wealth of the applicant's country).[67]
