On 14 July 2016, a truck was deliberately driven into a crowd of people by Mohamed Lahouaiej-Bouhlel on the Promenade des Anglais. The crowd was watching a fireworks display in celebration of Bastille Day.[13] Eighty-seven people were killed, including the perpetrator, who was shot dead by police.[14][15] Another 202 were injured, with 52 in critical care and 25 in intensive care, according to the Paris prosecutor.[16]
