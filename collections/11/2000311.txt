The Tower of the Swallow, known as The Tower of Swallows in the US (Polish original title: Wieża Jaskółki) is the fourth novel in the Witcher Saga written by Polish fantasy writer Andrzej Sapkowski, first published in Poland in 1997. It is a sequel to the third Witcher novel Baptism of Fire (Chrzest ognia) and is followed by the final entry in the Saga, The Lady of the Lake (Pani Jeziora).
