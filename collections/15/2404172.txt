Carry On Jatta 2 is a 2018 Indian Punjabi comedy film directed by Smeep Kang. The film is a sequel to the 2012 film, Carry On Jatta with Gippy Grewal, Gurpreet Ghuggi, Binnu Dhillon, Jaswinder Bhalla, B.N. Sharma and Karamjit Anmol returning to feature in the second instalment. It is the highest grossing Punjabi movie of all time. The film features Sonam Bajwa as the lead female and, Upasana Singh and Jyotii Sethi.[1]
