In May 2017, the group released their seventh Japanese single, "Chi, Ase, Namida" (血、汗、涙), which became their first release in Japan to be certified Platinum by the RIAJ. BTS' fifth extended play, Love Yourself: Her, was then released in September 2017.[7] The EP opened at number seven on the US Billboard 200 with 31,000 album-equivalent units, making it the highest-charting K-pop album on the chart and biggest sales week of a K-pop album to date.[8] In December 2017, BTS released their eighth Japanese single, "MIC Drop/DNA/Crystal Snow", selling over 500,000 copies within the month and becoming the only foreign artist to be certified Double Platinum by the RIAJ in 2017.[9]
