Both of these methods rely upon the cardholder accessing the account associated with the credit card, and confirming the value of the random transaction in order to prove that they are the owner of the account. PayPal's method however does not specifically relate to a transaction between an eMerchant and card holder, so unless it is augmented with another process that relates directly to a transaction, the method is not a form of strong customer authentication as is thus not an alternative to 3D Secure.[22]
