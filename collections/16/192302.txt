On 12 February 2015, Hazard signed a new five-and-a-half-year contract with Chelsea.[198] After signing, Hazard said: "I’ve signed a new contract and I’m very happy because I’m playing for one of the best clubs in the world."[199] He played the full 90 minutes in the club's victory in the League Cup Final at Wembley Stadium on 1 March, a 2–0 win over Tottenham.[200]
