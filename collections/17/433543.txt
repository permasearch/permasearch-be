Anjum is a major character of the book who is Muslim and a hijra. On her visit to a Gujarati shrine, Anjum gets caught in a massacre of Hindu pilgrims and subsequent government reprisals against Muslims. She is anxious about the future of her own community, especially the new generation.[6] [7] She is born as Aftab, the long-awaited son of Jahanara Begum and Mulaqat Ali.
