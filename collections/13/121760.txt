Under the principle of "one country, two systems",[17][18] Hong Kong maintains a separate political and economic system from China. Except in military defence and foreign affairs, Hong Kong maintains its independent executive, legislative and judiciary powers.[19] In addition, Hong Kong develops relations directly with foreign states and international organisations in a broad range of "appropriate fields".[20] Hong Kong involves in international organizations, such as the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC)[21] and the World Trade Organization (WOR),[22] actively and independently.
