The Governing Body of Jehovah's Witnesses is the ruling council , publicly and commonly known as Jehovah's Witnesses[1] based in the group's Warwick, New York headquarters. The body formulates doctrines, oversees the production of written material for publications and conventions, and administers the group's worldwide operations.[2][3] Official publications refer to members of the Governing Body as followers of Christ rather than religious leaders.[4]
