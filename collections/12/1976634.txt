Howard visits Madeleine, and she persuades him to watch a video of her husband playing with their daughter, who turns out to be Howard and Olivia. Howard is able to acknowledge his daughter's name and condition, and hugs Madeleine while crying. Brigitte is revealed to be the woman who had told Madeleine about Collateral Beauty. Amy, Raffi, and Brigitte proudly observe Howard and Madeleine walking through a park, but they are gone when Madeleine turns toward them.
