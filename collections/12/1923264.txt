Totonacapan was the name of a kingdom in Veracruz. In the late 9th or early 10th century, (probably during the reign of Ce Acatl Topiltzin) the Toltecs invaded Totonacapan. The conquered most of the Totonac area including El Tajin. Many of the Totonacs fled as refuges to Cempoala. Records of this are obscure but from what is known Cempoala was never conquered.[citation needed]
