In 1819, Bagyidaw became the king of Burma and decided to annex Assam. He sent Mingimaha Tilwa to Assam in  February 1821.  Patalang Borbarua was killed and Chandrakanta Singha fled to Guwahati.  Mingimaha killed a number of Ahom officials and installed Punyadhar (Jogeshwar Singha), a brother of Hemo Aideo, as the king.[9]
