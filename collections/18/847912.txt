Dangal is the first Indian film to gross $300 million worldwide, and one of the top 30 highest-grossing 2016 films. Dangal is also the highest-grossing sports film of 2017, and Disney's fourth highest-grossing film of 2017.[129] Dangal is also the first Indian film to exceed $100 million[130] and ₹1,000 crore overseas,[131] grossing around ₹1,459 crore (US$230 million) in overseas markets.[132]
