The five hundred euro note (€500) is the highest-value euro banknote and has been used since the introduction of the euro (in its cash form) in 2002.[5] It is one of the highest-value circulating banknotes in the world, worth around 559 USD, 3,831 CNY, 61,877 JPY, 545 CHF or 449 GBP.[6] The note is used in the 23 countries which have the euro as their sole currency (with 22 legally adopting it), with a population of about 338 million.[7]
