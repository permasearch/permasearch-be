The Last Airbender received nine nominations at the 31st Golden Raspberry Awards including Worst Picture.[105]  The film went on to sweep the Razzies with five awards: Worst Picture, Worst Director (Shyamalan), Worst Screenplay (Shyamalan), Worst Supporting Actor (Jackson Rathbone), and a special award, "Worst Eye-Gouging Mis-Use of 3D."[106][107][108]
