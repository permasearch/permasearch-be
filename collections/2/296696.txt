Pōtulūri Vīrabrahmēndra (Telugu: పోతులూరి వీరబ్రహ్మేంద్ర Pōtulūri Vīrabrahmēndra, also romanised Pothuluru Veerabrahmendra) was a Hindu sage and oracle. He is considered to be the author of the Kālaṅñānaṁ, a book of predictions about the future.[1] His prophetic texts are also known as the Govinda Vakyas.[2] He also wrote Jeevaikya Bodha.
