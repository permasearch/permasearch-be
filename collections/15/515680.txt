On November 9, a performance of the song "New Year's Day" was premiered during the broadcast of an episode of ABC's Scandal.[42] It was filmed in one of the secret sessions, held at Swift's home in Rhode Island on October 18, with an audience of 100 fans.[44] An extended version of Swift's performance was aired on November 10, during The DUFF's air on Freeform.[45] On November 11, Swift was the musical guest on the fifth episode of the 43rd season of Saturday Night Live, where she performed "...Ready for It?" and "Call It What You Want" from the album.[46] It was her first appearance on live television following the album's release, and she appeared on the show after eight years.[47][48]
