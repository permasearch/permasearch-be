Vardy met Rebekah Nicholson in 2014 while she was working as a nightclub promoter.[111] The couple married on 25 May 2016 at Peckforton Castle in Cheshire, with David Nugent as the best man.[112] Vardy has three biological children, two with his wife, and one from a previous relationship. He is also stepfather to two children from his wife's previous relationships.[113]
