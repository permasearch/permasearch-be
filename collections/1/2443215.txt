The Government of Andhra Pradesh on 27 November 2004 established its first DKC (District Knowledge Center), also called JKC (Jawahar Knowledge Center), in the state, which is governed by the Institute of Electronic Governance at the college campus. It was inaugurated by the chief minister Dr. Y. S. Raja Sekhar Reddy. The college JKC has been awarded the Best JKC for 2007. This campus has tremendous placements in IT field for both core and non-core. The average salary package is about 4.0Lpa.
