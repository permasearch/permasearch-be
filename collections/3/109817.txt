Other countries in the region have also contributed various levels of aid. Iran has been exporting between 500 and 800 tonnes of flour daily to Syria.[591] Israel has provided treatment to 750 Syrians in a field hospital located in Golan Heights. Rebels say that 250 of their fighters received medical treatment there.[592] Syrian refugees make up one quarter of Lebanon's population, mostly consisting of women and children.[593] In addition, Russia has said it created six humanitarian aid centers within Syria to support 3000 refugees in 2016.[594]
