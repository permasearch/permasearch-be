Rose scored his first goal for England U-21s on 14 November 2009 in a 2011 European Championship qualifier win against Portugal.[61] His second goal came in a 2–0 victory against Uzbekistan on 10 August 2010.[62] His third goal for the team came on 5 June 2011 in a 2–0 win against Norway, the final warm-up match before the 2011 UEFA European Under-21 Championship.[63]
