The C+C song was a success in the US, reaching number four on the Billboard Hot 100 and number 31 on the Billboard Hot R&B Singles chart. It also reached number four on the UK Singles Chart. The song contains samples from the songs "I'm Shook" by James Brown, "My Thang" by James Brown, and "Apache" by the Incredible Bongo Band.[1] The song was certified gold by the RIAA on August 13, 1991 for sales of over 500,000 copies.
