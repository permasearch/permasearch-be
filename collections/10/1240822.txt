After the 2018 presidential election the official salary or "personal remuneration" of the President will be €249,014.[43] The incumbent, Michael D. Higgins, chooses to receive the same salary although he is entitled to a higher figure of €325,507.[44][43] The President's total "emoluments and allowances" includes an additional €317,434 for expenses.[45] The Office of the President's total budget estimate for 2017 was €3.9 million, of which €2.6 million was for pay and running costs, and the balance for the "President's Bounty" paid to centenarians on their hundredth birthday.[46]
