The BCG vaccine was first used medically in 1921.[1] It is on the World Health Organization's List of Essential Medicines, the most effective and safe medicines needed in a health system.[6] Between 2011 and 2014 the wholesale price was $0.16 to $1.11 USD a dose in the developing world.[7][8] In the United States it costs $100 to $200 USD.[9] As of 2004 the vaccine is given to about 100 million children per year globally.[1]
