Willie Boy's grave monument can be found at 34°17′30″N 116°32′15″W﻿ / ﻿34.29167°N 116.53750°W﻿ / 34.29167; -116.53750.  The monument itself bears the inscription “The West’s Last Famous Manhunt”, alluding to the notion that this was the last effort of its type before the use of a posse was generally replaced by modern, 'fully' staffed and empowered law enforcement agencies.
