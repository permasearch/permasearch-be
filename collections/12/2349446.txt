The season premiere "The B Is Back" was aired on April 7, 2015, while the nineteenth episode "New Beginnings, My Ass" served as the season finale, and was aired on August 11, 2015.[7][8] It was followed by a three-part reunion that aired on August 18, August 25 and August 27, 2015,[9][10][11] and a "Secrets Revealed" episode on September 3, 2015, which marked the conclusion of the season.[12][13][14] Andrew Hoegl, Barrie Bernstein, Lisa Shannon, Pam Healy and Andy Cohen are recognized as the series' executive producers; it is produced by Ricochet and is distributed by Shed Media.[13]
