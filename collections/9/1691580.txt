This campus has three departments (unités de formation et de recherche (UFR)) of Arts, Law and Sciences, as well as a technological institute (Institut Universitaire Technologique),[3] the Ecole Nationale Supérieure en Génie de Technologies Industrielles [4](ENSGTI) and a School for Business Administration (Institut d'Administration des Entreprises) [5] with a satellite in Bayonne. There are two specialised university libraries, one for the Sciences Department, the other common to the Law and Arts Departments.
