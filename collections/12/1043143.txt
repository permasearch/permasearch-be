The Chinese subsidiaries of American companies Yahoo!, Google, (Google services are blocked but Google still has a presence in China[53]) and Microsoft comply with this condition of operating there.[54]  While the leadership of these companies regularly express their distaste for China's Great Firewall policies, in the same vein they consider it a necessary part of doing business in China and better than the alternative, which would be to not have any China business at all. Jerry Yang, a founder of Yahoo!, additionally has implied that the presence of foreign internet companies in China will eventually help bring about less internet restriction in China.[55]
