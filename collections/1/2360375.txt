Dallas Buyers Club premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival on September 7, 2013.[2] Focus Features initially gave the film a limited release at nine theaters on November 1 before expanding it on November 22 to over 600 theaters in the United States and Canada.[3][4] The film grossed a worldwide total of over $55 million on a production budget of $5 million.[4] Rotten Tomatoes, a review aggregator, surveyed 235 reviews and judged 94% to be positive.[5]
