The player with the most league appearances is Martin Hicks, with 500 from 1978 to 1991.[52] The most capped player is Kevin Doyle, who earned 26 for the Republic of Ireland whilst at the club.[52][55] The most league goals in total and in a season are held by Ronnie Blackman with 158 from 1947 to 1954 and 39 in 1951–52 respectively.[52] The player with the most league goals in a game is Arthur Bacon with six against Stoke City in 1930–31.[52] The first Reading-based player to play in the FIFA World Cup was Bobby Convey in 2006 with the United States.[52] The record time for a goalkeeper not conceding a goal is held by Steve Death at 1,103 minutes in 1978–79, which is a former English league record in itself.[56]
