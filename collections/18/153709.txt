Thor held its world premiere at the Event Cinemas theatre in George Street, Sydney on April 17, 2011,[118] with the film opening on April 21, 2011 in Australia.[119] The following weekend it opened in 56 markets,[120] while the premiere at the El Capitan Theatre in Los Angeles, California took place on May 2, 2011.[121] Thor opened on May 6, 2011 in the United States,[119] in 3,955 theaters (of which 214 were IMAX 3D and 2,737 in 3D, a record amount).[122][123][124]
