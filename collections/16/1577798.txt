"Cum On Feel the Noize" was released on 7" vinyl by Polydor Records in the UK, Ireland, across Europe, Scandinavia, Yugoslavia, America, Israel, South Africa, Australia, New Zealand, Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, Singapore, Japan and Lebanon.[17][18] The B-side, "I'm Mee, I'm Now, an' That's Orl", was exclusive to the single and would later appear on the band's 2007 compilation B-Sides.[19]
