The storyline has major consequences for the New Avengers, causing the team to split and the pro–registration heroes—including Ms. Marvel—form their own team, debuting in The Mighty Avengers.[44] Danvers enters into a relationship with fellow member Wonder Man,[45] appears in a crossover series with the robot Transformers,[46] and becomes leader of the Mighty Avengers.[47] The character makes an agreement with Tony Stark, director of S.H.I.E.L.D., to lead a covert strike team called Operation: Lightning Storm, its designated mission being the elimination of supervillains before they become global threats.[48]
