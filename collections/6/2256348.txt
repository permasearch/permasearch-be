On August 29, 2016, King with the principal cast of the show announced that Pretty Little Liars was ending after the seventh season. King also announced that the show would start airing the second half of the season later than usual, in April 2017 and that the series finale would be a two-hour episode event.[5] It was reported on October 26, 2016, that a tell-all special would be aired after the series finale "Till Death Do Us Part", where the main cast and King would talk about the show with behind-the-scenes exclusives.[6][7]
