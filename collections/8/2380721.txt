With a nominal gross domestic product (GDP) of up to £152 billion in 2015, total public sector non-North Sea current revenue in Scotland was estimated to be £53.7 billion in 2015‑16 approx. 36.5% of GDP.[74]  Current non-North Sea revenue in Scotland is estimated to have grown by 13.4% between 2011–12 and 2015-16 in nominal terms.[74] Total public sector expenditure for Scotland has been declining, as a share of GDP, since 2011-12, and in 2015-16 is estimated to be £68.6 billion which is around 46.6% FY2015-16.[74]
