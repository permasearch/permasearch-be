Brown has fathered seven children. His eldest, Landon, was born circa 1986[15] to Melika Williams.[16] With girlfriend Kim Ward, whom he met circa 1980, he had daughter La'Princia[17] (born 1989) and son Bobby Jr. (born c. 1992). Brown and Ward's on-and-off 14-year relationship ended in 1991, after two-months-pregnant Ward found out Brown was engaged.[18]
