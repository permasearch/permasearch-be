The film opened very well at the box office. It grossed ₹360 million (US$5.6 million) in the first week of screening all around India and US$1.6 million overseas.[27][28] The film grossed ₹550 million (US$8.6 million) in India by the end of its third week, being declared a super hit.[29] Delhi Belly grossed ₹920 million (US$14 million) worldwide.[30]
