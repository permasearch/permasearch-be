The single was released in several formats during its physical release. The standard edition, available as both a 7" and a cassette single, features the song plus B-side track "Here We Are at Last".[5][6] On the other formats, B-side tracks such as "Till I Loved You", "The Love Inside", and "No More Tears (Enough Is Enough)" were used.[7][8][9] On select CD releases, "Kiss Me in the Rain", "The Places You Find Love", and "Wet" were included.[10][11] In Spain, it was released as a sole A-side 7" single and the title was stylized as "We're Not Making Love Anymore".[12]
