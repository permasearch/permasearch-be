Most football analysts dismissed the Broncos' playoff run as a fluke resulting from bad luck and a weak AFC West division.[citation needed] Thus, on March 20, the Broncos signed Peyton Manning, who had just been released by the Indianapolis Colts. The following day, Tebow, despite being extremely popular with most of the Bronco fanbase, was traded to the New York Jets in exchange for a fourth-round draft selection.[83]
