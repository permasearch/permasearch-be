On 12 July 2011, Europe confirmed that Kevin Shirley would produce their ninth studio album. The recording sessions started on 3 October 2011.[40][41] On 24 January 2012 the band announced the album title, Bag of Bones.[42][43] The album was released on 18 April 2012 in Japan by Victor Entertainment[44] and on 25 April in Sweden by Gain Music Entertainment.[45] "Bag of Bones" debuted at number 2 on the Swedish album chart on 4 May 2012[12] and was certified Gold in Sweden on 12 July 2012.[12]
