Her television acting in the late 1960s and early 1970s included The Door of Opportunity (1970) with Ian Ogilvy,[45] adapted from W. Somerset Maugham's story, followed by August Strindberg's The Stronger (1971) with Britt Ekland,[46] and Terrible Jim Fitch (1971) by James Leo Herlihy, which once more paired Faithfull with Nicol Williamson.[47]
