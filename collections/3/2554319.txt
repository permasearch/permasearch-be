McFarland co-hosted a radio show with Marc Ryan that broadcast throughout the Tampa Bay area. It was a sports radio program on CBS radio station 98.7 The Fan, based in St. Petersburg, Florida, but was canceled when the station dropped its sports format in December 2014.[2] He joined the SEC Network in 2014 as a football analyst. McFarland was also a part-time guest analyst on ESPN's Mike & Mike morning program.
