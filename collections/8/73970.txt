Indigenous animal species in the state include 62 mammal species,[88] 93 reptile species,[89] 73 amphibian species,[90] roughly 307 native freshwater fish species,[85] and 420 bird species that spend at least part of their year within the state.[91] Invertebrates include 97 crayfish species and 383 mollusk species. 113 of these mollusk species have never been collected outside the state.[92][93]
