BT has the largest defined benefit pension plan of any UK public company. The trustees valued the scheme at £36.7 billion at the end of 2010;[71] an actuarial valuation valued the deficit of the scheme at £9.043 billion as of 31 December 2008.[72] Following a change in the regulations governing inflation index linking, the deficit was estimated at £5.2 billion in November 2010.[73]
