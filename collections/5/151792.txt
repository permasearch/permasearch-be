Lance Salisbury, played by Angus McLaren, made his first appearance on 17 April 2018.[7] McLaren's casting and character details were announced on 16 April 2018. Of joining the cast, McLaren commented, "It's very exciting. It hit home for me when I walked into the Diner and all the characters I've grown up watching were there."[7] McLaren was reunited with his former Packed to the Rafters co-star James Stewart, who plays Justin Morgan.[7] Lance is a federal agent and best friend of Robbo (Jake Ryan). Lance is involved with the circumstances surrounding Robbo's amnesia and he helps out as the "mystery slowly gets unpacked."[7] Lance also wants his friend to get justice.[7]
