In May 2013, the Indian Army requested 11 AH-64Es;[263] and has a requirement for 39 Apaches.[264] The Indian Ministry of Defence approved the procurement in August 2014,[265] as did India's Cabinet Committee on Security (CCS) in September 2015.[266] On 28 September 2015, a contract was formally signed; the first helicopter is expected to be delivered to India in the next three to four years.[267] India's Defence Acquisition Council approved procurement of 6 Apaches for the Indian Army in August 2017.[268][269][270]
