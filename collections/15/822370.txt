Lenovo's principal facilities are in Beijing, Morrisville, North Carolina and Singapore, with research centers in Beijing, Morrisville, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Xiamen, Chengdu, Nanjing,[137] and Wuhan[138] in China, and Yamato in Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan.[139] Lenovo operates factories in Chengdu and Hefei in China, Japan, and as of December 2011[update] has plans to start production in Argentina. A 700-square-metre (7,500 sq ft) flagship store opened in Beijing in February 2013.[32]
