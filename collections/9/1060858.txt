Paul is a character in The Final Chapter. He is the boyfriend of Samantha. In the summer of 1984, Paul goes with his friends to spend the weekend at Crystal Lake. While there, Tina - one of the twins - starts flirting with him, which angers Samantha who storms out of the house to go skinny dipping. He then rejects Tina and goes looking for Sam (whom he finds dead). He then panics and is stabbed in the groin with a harpoon gun by Jason which kills him.
