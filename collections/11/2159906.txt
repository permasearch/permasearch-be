Hall's film credits include the thriller Paycheck (2003), the science fiction thriller Gamer (2009), the 2011 drama The Trouble with Bliss (2011), the comedy Peep World (2012), and Kill Your Darlings (2013).[22] Hall performed in a film adaptation of Joe R. Lansdale's cult novel Cold in July,[23] directed by Jim Mickle.[24] The film premiered at the 2014 Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah.[25] In the next year, Hall voiced Batman in Justice League: Gods and Monsters.[26] Hall portrayed Abraham Lincoln's advisor, Leonard Swett, in the 2017 documentary film The Gettysburg Address.
