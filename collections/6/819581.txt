Newman and his wife, Krissie, operate Rescue Ranch, whose mission is to promote, through its education, respect for all animals, as well as agricultural, environmental, and wildlife conservation; and facilitates rehabilitation, rescue, and responsible pet ownership in order to enhance the human-animal bond.  The Newmans themselves have rescued and care for five dogs: Socks, Dunkin, Fred, Jasmine and Hollow.[citation needed] He helped fund the construction of the Catawba County, North Carolina Humane Society shelter, in the county where he once lived.[citation needed]
