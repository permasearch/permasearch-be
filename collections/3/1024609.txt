On Rotten Tomatoes, Assassin's Creed  has an approval rating of 19% based on 194 reviews, and an average rating of 4/10. The website's critical consensus reads, "Assassin's Creed is arguably better made (and certainly better cast) than most video game adaptations; unfortunately, the CGI-fueled end result is still a joylessly overplotted slog."[63] On Metacritic, the film has a score of 36 out of 100 based on 38 critics, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews".[64] Audiences polled by CinemaScore gave the film an average grade of "B+" on an A+ to F scale.[65]
