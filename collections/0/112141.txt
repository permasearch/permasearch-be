Mars's average distance from the Sun is roughly 230 million kilometres (143,000,000 mi), and its orbital period is 687 (Earth) days. The solar day (or sol) on Mars is only slightly longer than an Earth day: 24 hours, 39 minutes, and 35.244 seconds.[171] A Martian year is equal to 1.8809 Earth years, or 1 year, 320 days, and 18.2 hours.[7]
