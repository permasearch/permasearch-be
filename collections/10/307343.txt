Harris Teeter Supermarkets, Inc. is an American supermarket chain based in Matthews, North Carolina, just outside Charlotte. As of October 2017[update], the chain operates 245 stores in seven states: North Carolina, South Carolina, Virginia, Georgia, Florida, Delaware, Maryland and the District of Columbia. Supermarket News ranked Harris Teeter No. 34 in the 2012 "Top 75 Retailers & Wholesalers" based on 2011 fiscal year sales of $4.3 billion.[2]
