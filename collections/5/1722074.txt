Bakersfield's historic and primary industries have related to Kern County's two main industries, oil and agriculture. Kern County in 2013 was the most oil productive county in America.[63][64] Kern County is a part of the highly productive San Joaquin Valley, and ranks in the top five most productive agricultural counties in the nation.[64] Major crops for Kern County include: grapes, citrus, almonds, carrots, alfalfa, cotton, and roses.[65] The city serves as the home for both corporate and regional headquarters of companies engaged in these industries.
