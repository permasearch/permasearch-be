In January 2009, the company acquired a controlling interest in the largest grocer in Chile, Distribución y Servicio D&S SA.[134][135] In 2010, the company was renamed Walmart Chile.[136] As of October 31, 2017,[update] Walmart Chile operates 371 stores under the banners Lider Hiper (85 locations), Lider Express (84 locations), Superbodega Acuenta (110 locations), Ekono (88 locations), and Central Mayorista (4 locations).[1][2]
