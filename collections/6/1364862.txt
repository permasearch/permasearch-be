In 2003, 597 insurgents were killed, according to the U.S. military.[122] From January 2004 through December 2009 (not including May 2004 and March 2009), 23,984 insurgents were estimated to have been killed based on reports from Coalition soldiers on the frontlines.[123] In the two missing months from the estimate, 652 were killed in May 2004,[124] and 45 were killed in March 2009.[125] In 2010, another 676 insurgents were killed.[126] In January and March through October 2011, 451 insurgents were killed.[127][128][129][130][131][132][133][134][135] Based on all of these estimates some 26,405 insurgents/militia were killed from 2003, up until late 2011.
