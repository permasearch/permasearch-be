IMPETUS[12] is a national level Technical Extravaganza organised by IEEE UVCE. It is the biggest technical fest in UVCE. Every year the event witnesses the footfall of over 1000 students. Corporate companies like IBM, Yahoo!, NIIT, Motorola, Cognizant, Infosys etc. sponsor for this fest. The event will host both technical events like Circuit Debugging, OSP, Web Designing, Technical Quiz and non-technical events like Mad Ads, Ad-Venture, Team building event, Marketing event, Mock stock etc.
