Hamilton opened at the CIBC Theatre in Chicago on October 19, 2016, following previews from September 27, 2016.[66] It is currently booking through January 20, 2019.[67] Near the beginning of its run, lead producer Jeffrey Seller said the show may be in residence for two years or more.[68] On its opening, attended by author Miranda, the Chicago production received strongly positive reviews.[69][70][71] Miranda praised the Chicago casts' performance during a later television interview.[72]
