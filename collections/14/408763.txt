Smith married Sheree Zampino in 1992. They had one son, Trey Smith, born on November 11, 1992,[78] and divorced in 1995. Trey appeared in his father's music video for the 1998 single "Just the Two of Us". He also acted in two episodes of the sitcom All of Us, and has appeared on The Oprah Winfrey Show and on the David Blaine: Real or Magic TV special.[citation needed]
