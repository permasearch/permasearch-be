As of June 14, 2013,[28] Sony TV was still talking to other distributors, with Amazon, Netflix,[27] TBS, and NBC[26] being named. At this point, there was just over two weeks left to finalize a deal as Sony's options on the cast contracts were set to expire on June 30, 2013.[29][30] By June 27, 2013, there was no serious talks to revive the series on broadcast, cable or streaming networks, as both NBC and TBS had also passed on the show.[31]
