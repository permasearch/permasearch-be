The twentieth series of the British reality television programme The Only Way Is Essex was confirmed on 3 June 2015 when it was announced that it had renewed for at least a further six series, taking it up to 21 series.[1] The series launched on 5 March with the cast going to Tenerife,[2] and concluded on 3 May 2017 after eighteen episodes, making this the longest series to date.[3] Ahead of the series it was announced that James Argent had taken a break from the series having only appeared in the first half of the previous series.[4] It was also announced that Mario Falcone, Jamie Reed and Charlie King would all be returning to the series.[5][6][7] Lauren Pope also made a return to the series during the final episode,[8] and Fran Parman also made a brief return during this episode.
