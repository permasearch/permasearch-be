Clarkson also promoted "Stronger" in live television events, the first live televised performance of the song was on November 23, 2011, during The X Factor (US) results show.[94] On December 18, 2011, she performed the song on VH1 Divas Celebrates Soul television special.[95] On January 7, 2012, she performed it along with "Mr. Know It All" on the 37th season of  Saturday Night Live.[96] On June 6, 2012, Clarkson performed it on the British television series This Morning.[97] Clarkson also performed a medley of the "Stronger" with the succeeding single "Dark Side" on various television events, such as at the season finale of the fourteenth season of Dancing with the Stars on May 22, 2012,[98] and at the 2012 MuchMusic Video Awards on June 17, 2012.[99] On June 8, 2018 Clarkson performed it on The Today Show.
