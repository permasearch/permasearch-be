Journey 2: The Mysterious Island grossed $325,692,760[5] surpassing its predecessor.[6][7] In North America, the film earned a $6.54 million on its debut Friday, ranking fourth at the box office.[8] Over the weekend, it earned $27.3 million, coming in third place, much higher than the original's $21.0 million debut.[9] Outside North America, Journey 2 began its run three weeks before its North American release.[10] It topped the box office outside North America for two consecutive weekends[11] and three in total.[12] It surpassed the original's total outside North America.[13] Its highest-grossing region after North America was China ($58.4 million),[14] followed by Russia and the CIS ($17.6 million) and Mexico ($12.7 million).[15]
