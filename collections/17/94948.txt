On April 24, 2016, the Lakers announced that they will not to exercise their option on Byron Scott's contract for the following season.[246] On April 29, the Lakers announced another former Laker, Luke Walton, as their new head coach. At the time of his hiring, Walton was an assistant coach for the Golden State Warriors, who were in the playoffs, so Walton could not officially begin his duties as head coach until the Warriors playoff run was over.[247] The Lakers earned the second overall pick in the 2016 NBA draft,[248] and selected Brandon Ingram from Duke University.[249]
