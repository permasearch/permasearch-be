Built in 1986,[9][10] Four Seasons Hotel Austin is a nine-story hotel on 2.3 acres of land on the Lady Bird Lake's north shore.[11] In 1997, Four Seasons Hotel Austin became the first hotel to have "a high-speed wireless Internet network" after Wayport, Inc. set it up there for testing wireless Internet networks.[12] The hotel hosted Queen Elizabeth II in 1991 when she visited Texas.[13][dead link] It was acquired by Anbang Insurance Group from the Blackstone Group for $359.7 million in 2016.[11]
