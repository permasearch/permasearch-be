Isi Ka Naam Zindagi (English: This is called life) is a 1992 Hindi-language Indian feature film directed by Kalidas, starring Aamir Khan, Farha Naaz, Pran, Shakti Kapoor, Asrani and Tej Sapru. It was a flop at box office. However, actor Pran gave one of his best performances was used to promote the movie.
