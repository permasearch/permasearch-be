Zanjeer (English: Shackles) is a 2013 Indian bilingual action-drama film directed by Apoorva Lakhia, shot simultaneously in Hindi and Telugu. It is a remake of the 1973 Hindi film of the same name. The film marked the Bollywood debut of Tollywood actor Ram Charan in the lead role along with Priyanka Chopra.[1] Reliance BIG Entertainment bought worldwide distribution rights for ₹1.05 billion (US$15 million).
