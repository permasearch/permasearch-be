At 15, eager to find work as an actor, a high school friend Jesse Abrams introduced Drake to his father Norbert, who was an acting agent.[55][56] The agent secured Drake a role on Canadian teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation. On the series, Drake portrayed Jimmy Brooks,[57] a basketball star who became physically disabled after he was shot by a classmate. When asked about his early acting career, Drake replied, "My mother was very sick. We were very poor, like broke. The only money I had coming in was off of Canadian TV".[45]
