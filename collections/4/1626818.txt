In 1988, Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak earned a domestic net collection of ₹5 crore,[2] and was Blockbuster making it the year's third highest-earning film, after Tezaab and Shahenshah.[17] Adjusted for inflation, the domestic net collection of Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak is equivalent to more than ₹90 crore (US$14 million) in 2016.[a]
