The first episode of season two of BYUtv's AUDIO-FILES centered on the band and featured live performances of "Winter Song" (performed atop a Ferris wheel), "Sounds Like Hallelujah", etc.[34] The song "Let's Be Still" appears in a 2015 television commercial for Corona.[35] "Let's Be Still" was also featured in an episode of the second season of The Night Shift[36] and at the end of season one, episode three of Heartbeat.[37]
