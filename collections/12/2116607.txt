Bellamkonda Sreenivas[1] is a Telugu film actor. He made his debut in the 2014 Telugu film Alludu Seenu opposite Samantha Ruth Prabhu.[2] He is the son of Telugu film producer Bellamkonda Suresh. Sreenivas recently scored a hit with his third film titled Jaya Janaki Nayaka opposite Rakul Preet Singh and Pragya Jaiswal, directed by Boyapati Srinu.[3]
