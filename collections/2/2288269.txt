"Get the Girl" aired on March 15, 2012.[1] The episode was viewed by an estimated 4.88 million viewers and received a 2.3 rating/6% share among adults between the ages of 18 and 49.[10] This means that it was seen by 2.3% of all 18- to 49-year-olds, and 6% of all 18- to 49-year-olds watching television at the time of the broadcast.[11] This marked a decrease in the ratings from the previous episode, "Last Day in Florida".[11] The episode finished second in its time slot, beating a rerun of the Fox supernatural series Touch and The CW teen drama series The Secret Circle.[11] Despite this, the episode was defeated by the ABC medical drama Grey's Anatomy which drew a 3.0/6% rating.[11] In addition, "Get the Girl" was the highest-rated NBC television episode of the night, barely beating the return of Community.[11]
