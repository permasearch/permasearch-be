American tariffs on $34 billion of Chinese goods came into effect on July 6, 2018. China imposed retaliatory tariffs on US goods of a similar value. The tariffs accounted for 0.1% of the global gross domestic product.[40][41] On July 10, U.S. released an initial list of the additional $200 billion of Chinese goods that would be subject to a 10% tariff.[42]  China vowed to retaliate with additional tariffs on American goods worth $60 billion annually two days later.[43]
