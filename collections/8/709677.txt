The short premiered on 4 November 1989, at the Bristol Animation Festival at the Arnolfini Gallery in Bristol.[5][3][6][7][8] It was first broadcast on 24 December 1990, Christmas Eve, on Channel 4.[9][10] A Grand Day Out is followed by 1993's The Wrong Trousers, 1995's A Close Shave, 2005's The Curse of the Were-Rabbit and 2008's A Matter of Loaf and Death.
