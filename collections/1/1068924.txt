Mn/DOT announced on September 19, 2007, that Flatiron Constructors and Manson Construction Company would build the replacement bridge for $234 million.[167] The I-35W Saint Anthony Falls Bridge was opened to the public on September 18, 2008, at 5 a.m.[168] Using the innovative design-build project delivery method, the replacement bridge opened over three months ahead of schedule, and was awarded the "Best Overall Design-Build Project Award" for 2009 from the Design-Build Institute of America.[169]
