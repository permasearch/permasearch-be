Joan Baez included a cover of the song on her 1967 Joan album. She changed one of the lines to "Is the church really dead?" and Simon insisted that a line be inserted on the album's back cover that read: "Paul Simon asks Joan to note that the original line is, 'Is the theater really dead?'"(as you can read in the left side under arrangements on the backcover of the Original LP  https://www.discogs.com/Joan-Baez-Joan/master/160723#images/8518357)
