On 14 November 2008, Air France released the first picture[143] of an Airbus A320 with registration F-GFKJ that has been repainted in the full 1946 paint scheme[144] to celebrate the airline's 75 years anniversary. This heritage aircraft was planned to fly under the special colours until early 2010.[145] The aircraft was officially withdrawn from use and scrapped at Phoenix Goodyear Airport in September 2013 [146]
