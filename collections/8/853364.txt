On 16 May 2006, Jagex upgraded RuneScape's game engine, improving the game's loading times and reducing its memory requirements.[63] On 1 July 2008, Jagex released a beta of their "High Detail" mode for members, which was extended to free players two weeks later.[64] Before the launch, Jagex stated that it would be officially revealed at the 2008 E3 trade show.[65]
