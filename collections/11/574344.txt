Jones married American actress/singer Julienne Marie in 1968, whom he met while performing as Othello in 1964.[45] They had no children, and divorced in 1972.[46] In 1982, he married actress Cecilia Hart, with whom he had one child, son Flynn Earl Jones.[47][48] Hart died on October 16, 2016, after a one-year battle with ovarian cancer.[49] In April 2016, Jones spoke publicly for the first time in nearly 20 years about his long-term health challenge with type 2 diabetes. He has been dealing with diabetes since the mid 1990s.[50]
