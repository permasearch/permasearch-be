The David S. Pumpkins Animated Halloween Special is a Halloween television special that aired on October 28, 2017, on NBC. With the Saturday Night Live character David S. Pumpkins, it stars Tom Hanks as the title character, as well as Mikey Day, Bobby Moynihan, and Streeter Seidell, with Peter Dinklage as the narrator. It was produced by SNL creator Lorne Michaels and animated by Bento Box Entertainment with a score by Mark Mothersbaugh, and it was written by Day, Moynihan, and Seidell.
