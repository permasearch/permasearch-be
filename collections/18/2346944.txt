The QUT Business School is one of six faculties at the Queensland University of Technology.[1] It is home to the QUT Graduate School of Business, as well as four-discipline focused schools; the School of Accountancy, the School of Advertising, Marketing and Public Relations, the School of Economics and Finance, and the School of Management.[2] In 2015, the QUT Business School had enrolled a total of 8,971 students across the faculty, including 2,962 international students.[3]
