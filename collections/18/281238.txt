The house building company, Croudace, have their head office in Caterham. Caterham and DeStafford Schools and several supermarket chains are also large local employers. The Town has a large vacant building - The Rose and Young building - which is boarded up and has remained vacant for over twenty years. There is a plan for the council to compulsorily purchase this building but that's been a plan for a couple of years. [39]
