On the January 22, 2018 during Raw 25 Years, Jericho appeared backstage in a segment with Elias, putting him on The List of Jericho.[213] Jericho was scheduled to participate in the 50-man Royal Rumble match at The Greatest Royal Rumble, but later swapped places with Rusev, therefore Jericho will face The Undertaker in a Casket match while Rusev will be in the Royal Rumble match, only to swap places again where Rusev will face Undertaker in a Casket Match as WWE planned.
