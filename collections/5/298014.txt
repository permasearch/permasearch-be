Murray stated in an interview with IGN prior to release that VR "would be a really good fit" for No Man's Sky, as the immersive experience could create "really intense moments within a game".[100][101] Murray also commented on the potential for a remastering of No Man's Sky for a system with more hardware capabilities, suggesting that they would be able to both increase the texture resolution and the degree of complexity of the flora and fauna on the planets.[102]
