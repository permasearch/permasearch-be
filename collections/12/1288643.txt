Cape Verde is a stable semi-presidential representative democratic republic.[4][16] It is among the most democratic nations in the world, ranking 23rd position in the world, according to the 2016 Democracy Index.[17] The constitution — adopted in 1980 and revised in 1992, 1995 and 1999 — defines the basic principles of its government. The president is the head of state and is elected by popular vote for a 5-year term.[14]
