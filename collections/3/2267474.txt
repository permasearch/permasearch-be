The 2004–05 season saw Downing make 49 appearances, scoring six goals, as well as providing numerous assists.[citation needed] In a UEFA Cup match against Lazio in November 2004, both Downing and Zenden were in the starting line-up.[citation needed] Though Zenden scored both goals in the 2–0 victory, Downing drew praise for his performance down the left.[citation needed] Such performances that season saw him called up to Sven-Göran Eriksson's England squad in February 2005 for a friendly against the Netherlands.[8]
