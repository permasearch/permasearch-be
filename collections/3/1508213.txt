Nasir-ud-din Ismail Shah asked Zafar Khan to become the king with the title of ‘Sikandar- uth- thani Ala-ud-din Hasan Bahman Shah al-wali’. The new king was crowned on Friday Aug 3, 1347 in the mosque of Qtub-ud-din Mubarak Shah Khalji at Daulatabad. According to historian, Hasan was the nephew of Malik Hizhbar-ud-din titled Zafar Khan' Alai, who was killed in 1298 AD when Hasan was six years old.
