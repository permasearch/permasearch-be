The Low End Theory peaked at number 45 on the Billboard 200 and number 13 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.[9] Upon its release, the album's commercial potential was doubted by music writers and Jive record executives, including label CEO Barry Weiss.[29][30] However, on February 19, 1992, the album was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), with shipments of 500,000 copies in the United States.[9] On February 1, 1995, it was certified platinum by the RIAA, with shipments of one million copies.[9]
