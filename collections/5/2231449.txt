James Cook University (JCU) is a public university based in Townsville. Established in 1970, the main campus is located in the suburb of Douglas.[55] JCU was the second university in Queensland and the first in North Queensland. The University has a strong and internationally recognised expertise in marine & tropical biology.[56] The JCU Medical School was established in 1999 and is linked with the adjacent tertiary-level Townsville Hospital. The Veterinary Sciences undergraduate facility is the newest in Australia.[57]
