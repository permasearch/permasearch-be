Breslin also starred in the film adaptation of Orson Scott Card's novel, Ender's Game[74] The film received mixed-to-positive reviews from critics[75] but it was a disappointment at the box office,[76] grossing only $125 million worldwide from a $110 million budget.[77] In July 2012, she was cast in August: Osage County, the film adaptation of the Pulitzer Prize-winning play of the same name, alongside Julia Roberts, Benedict Cumberbatch, and Meryl Streep.[78] August: Osage County was released in December 2013, and received mostly positive reviews.[79] In 2014, Breslin appeared in the crime drama Perfect Sisters. In January 2015, she was cast in the first season of Fox's horror-comedy anthology series Scream Queens, which aired from September 2015 to December 2016.[80]
