The film was released theatrically starting on November 20, 2009, and set numerous records. It is currently[when?] the biggest advance-ticket seller on Fandango[71] and held the biggest midnight opening in domestic (United States and Canada) box office history, grossing an estimated $26.3 million.[72] Its sequel, The Twilight Saga: Eclipse, broke that record in June 2010, grossing $72.7 million on its opening day domestically, $709 Million Worldwide and becoming the biggest single-day opening in domestic history.[2] It is the twelfth highest opening weekend in domestic history with $142,839,137.[73]
