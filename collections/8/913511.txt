"I'm Just a Bill" is a 1976 Schoolhouse Rock! segment, featuring a song of the same title written by Bob Dorough. The segment debuted as part of "America Rock", the third season of the Schoolhouse Rock series. The song featured in the segment is sung by Jack Sheldon[1] (the voice of the Bill), with dialogue by Sheldon's son John as the boy learning the process. It is about how a bill becomes a law, how it must go through Congress, and how it can be vetoed, etc.
