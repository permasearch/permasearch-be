The Villages has become a popular spot for many artists. In April 2015, The Sharon L. Morse Performing Arts Center, a venue with over 1,000 seats, opened to much fanfare.[56] The Sharon has featured various popular artists such as Frankie Avalon,[57] Jerry Lewis,[58] Felix Cavaliere,[59] Little River Band,[60] Bobby Rydell,[61] Ray Stevens,[62] The Spinners,[63] Kenny G,[64] and Chubby Checker.[65]
