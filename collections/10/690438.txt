Djokovic is the first Serbian player to be ranked No. 1 by the ATP and the first male player representing Serbia to win a Grand Slam singles title. Djokovic has won numerous awards, including the 2012, 2015, and 2016 Laureus World Sports Award for Sportsman of the Year,[8] 2011 BBC Overseas Sports Personality of the Year, five-time ITF World Champion, and four-time ATP year-end No. 1.
