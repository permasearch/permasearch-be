GameRankings and Metacritic calculated scores of 88.47%[53] and 88/100,[54] respectively, for the Xbox 360 version, 86.11%[55] and 87/100[56] for the PlayStation 3 version, and 85.41%[57] and 85/100[58] for the PC version.
