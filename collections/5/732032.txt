The Bad World Tour began on September 12 that year, finishing on January 14, 1989.[150] In Japan alone, the tour had 14 sellouts and drew 570,000 people, nearly tripling the previous record of 200,000 in a single tour.[151] Jackson broke a Guinness World Record when 504,000 people attended seven sold-out shows at Wembley Stadium.[152] He performed a total of 123 concerts to an audience of 4.4 million people.[153]
