Iss Pyaar Ko Kya Naam Doon? (English: What Name Shall I Give This Love? ) is an Indian daily soap opera that aired weekdays on Star Plus from 6 June 2011 to 30 November 2012. It is the second installment in the 4 Lions Films series. The show starred Barun Sobti and Sanaya Irani. The show was first titled as Vaidehi but later changed its title. It replaced Rajan Shahi's Chand Chupa Badal Mein.[1] 'Iss Pyaar Ko Kya Naam Doon? was one of the top popular shows with high TRP ratings throughout its run in 2011-2012 and till date the series is available in multiple languages. The series is translated into Panamericana as "Duele Amar" and it began airing from 19 March 2018 onwards. Currently Iss Pyaar Ko Kya Naam Doon is rerunning on Star Utsav as Rabba Ve and Telugu version on Star Maa. IPKKND will be rerunning in Thai language as แผนรักลวงใจ from 27 June 2018 onwards on Thai channel 8 and broadcast on Hnow tv, a Pakistani channel from 3rd July 2018.[2].
