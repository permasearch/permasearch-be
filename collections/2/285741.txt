Review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes gives the film an approval rating of 94% based on reviews from 110 critics, with an average rating of 8.5/10. The website's critical consensus reads, "Enchanting, sweepingly romantic, and featuring plenty of wonderful musical numbers, Beauty and the Beast is one of Disney's most elegant animated offerings."[72] The film also holds a 95/100 on Metacritic, which indicates the reviews as "universal acclaim".[73] Audiences polled by CinemaScore gave the film a rare "A+" grade.[74]
