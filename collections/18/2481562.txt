The Level 4 observation deck was reopened July 5, 2007 to give visitors a view of Knoxville.[10] The observation deck can hold 86 people.[10] At the time of its reopening, Level 5 became a cafe with concession and an early evening drinks service. Level 6 served as an open space leased out for functions. As of October 2013, both the 7th and 8th floors are available for commercial rental.[11]
