After a three-month hiatus, Show returned to WWE on August 11 at a house show in Oakland, California.[177][178] Show made his return on the following night on the August 12 episode of Raw, and helped Mark Henry and Rob Van Dam from an attack by The Shield, and turning face in the process for the first time since May 2012.[179] On the May 16 episode of SmackDown, Show, Henry, and Van Dam defeated the Shield in a six-man tag team match.[180]
