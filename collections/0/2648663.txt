Tintagel and its castle are associated with the conception of the legendary King Arthur[35] and a 15th-century house that was later used as a post office.[36] The path continues to Trebarwith Strand, Tregardock, then to Port Gaverne, Port Isaac, and Port Quin, three small harbours. Overlooking Port Quin is Doyden Castle, a 19th-century folly.
