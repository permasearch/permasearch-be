The Republic of the Congo (French: République du Congo), also known as the Congo-Brazzaville, the Congo Republic,[5] West Congo,[dubious – discuss] the former French Congo, or simply the Congo, is a country in Central Africa. It is bordered by five countries: Gabon and the Atlantic Ocean to the west; Cameroon to the northwest; the Central African Republic to the northeast; the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the east and south; and the Angolan exclave of Cabinda to the southwest.
