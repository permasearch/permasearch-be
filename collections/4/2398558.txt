Sales of the Canadian Prius v began in October 2011, starting at a base price of CA$27,200 (US$27,270).[37] The Prius v sold 553 units during 2011, 4,077 in 2012, and fell to 2,640 in 2013, and to 1,442 in 2014. Sales climbed to 2,497 units in 2015. Cumulative sales through April 2016 reached 12,670 units.[29]
