A film that makes rich use of proverbs is Forrest Gump, known for both using and creating proverbs.[88][89] Other studies of the use of proverbs in film include work by Kevin McKenna on the Russian film Aleksandr Nevsky,[90] Haase's study of an adaptation of Little Red Riding Hood,[91] Elias Dominguez Barajas on the film Viva Zapata!,[92] and Aboneh Ashagrie on The Athlete (a movie in Amharic about Abebe Bikila).[93]
