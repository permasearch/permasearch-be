The largest collection of Dalí's work is at the Dalí Theatre and Museum in Figueres, Catalonia, Spain. The Salvador Dalí Museum in St. Petersburg, Florida, contains the collection of Reynolds and Eleanor Morse, and holds over 1500 works by Dalí, including seven large "masterworks". Other significant collections include the Reina Sofia Museum in Madrid; the Salvador Dalí Gallery in San Juan Capistrano, California; the museum Dalí - Die Ausstellung am Potsdamer Platz in Berlin, Germany; Espace Dalí in Montmartre, Paris, France; and Dalí Universe in London, England.
