Michel's parents emigrated from Haiti a year after his older sister Lamise was born.[1] Michel attended American Heritage School in Plantation, Florida.[2] Michel was the starting running back for the American Heritage Patriots, beginning in his eighth grade year there.  As a senior, he rushed for 1,833 yards and 24 touchdowns. Over three years playing on varsity, he rushed for 4,758 yards and 63 touchdowns. He was rated by Rivals.com as a five-star recruit and was ranked as the third best running back and 13th best player overall.[3] Michel committed to the University of Georgia on April 26, 2013 to play college football.[4][5] Michel's older brother Marken Michel is a receiver for the Calgary Stampeders of the Canadian Football League, and was the 2017 West Division Most Outstanding Rookie.[6]
