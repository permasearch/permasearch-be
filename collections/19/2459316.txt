It was announced in June 2008 that an animated adaption of Kiss×sis would be produced by Feel.[17] On December 22 of that year, the first OVA was released, bundled with the third volume of the manga, directed by Munenori Nawa.[18][19] Subsequent releases were packaged with subsequent volumes of the manga. The 12th and last episode was released on April 6, 2015.[20] The opening and closing themes for the OVA include "Our Honey Boy" (ふたりのハニーボーイ, Futari no Hanibōi) by Ayana Taketatsu and Yuiko Tatsumi and "Starry Sky Story" (星空物語, Hoshizora Monogatari) by Nana Takahashi, respectively.
