On March 4, 2016, Niantic announced a Japan-exclusive beta test would begin later that month, allowing players to assist in refining the game before its full release. The beta test was later expanded to other countries.[31] On April 7, it was announced that the beta would expand to Australia and New Zealand.[32] Then, on May 16, the signups for the field test were opened to the United States.[33][34] The test came to an end on June 30.[35]
