In the United States, the single topped the Billboard Hot 100 chart in November 1987 for one week and also reached number one on the Adult Contemporary for four weeks.[5] In the United Kingdom the song had two chart outings: in November 1987, after the film's initial release, the song peaked at No. 6;[1] in January 1991, after the film was shown on mainstream television, the song reached No. 8.[6]
