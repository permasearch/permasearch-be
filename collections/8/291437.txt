Visa requirements for Trinidad and Tobago citizens are administrative entry restrictions by the authorities of other states placed on citizens of Trinidad and Tobago. As of 18 January 2018, Trinidad and Tobago citizens had visa-free or visa on arrival access to 133 countries and territories, ranking the Trinidad and Tobago passport 33nd in terms of travel freedom according to the Henley Passport Index.[1] As a member of Caricom, Trinidad and Tobago passport holders have access to freedom of movement in all Caricom states.[2]
