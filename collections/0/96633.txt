Median longevity of Basset Hounds is about 10.3 years in France and 11.3 years in the UK,[15][18] which is a typical median longevity for purebred dogs and for breeds similar in size to Basset Hounds.[19] The oldest of the 142 deceased dogs in the 2004 UK Kennel Club survey was 16.7 years.[17] Leading causes of death in the 2004 UK Kennel Club survey were cancer (31%), old age (13%), gastric dilatation volvulus (11%), and cardiac (8%).
