The discography of American rapper Snoop Dogg of sixteen studio albums, four collaborative albums, seventeen compilation albums, two extended plays, twenty mixtapes, 127 singles (including seventy-four as a featured artist), and fourteen promotional singles. He has sold over 23.5 million albums in United States[1] and 37 million albums worldwide.[2] He has garnered fourteen top ten singles on the Billboard Hot 100 (including eight as a featured artist).[3]
