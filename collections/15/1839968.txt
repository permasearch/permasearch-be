In an interview with TV Wise, Rachel Boston revealed that before filming the pilot episode, she read many books on witchcraft to prepare for the role of Ingrid.[21] Boston described Ingrid as smart and sensitive,[21] and said that she "is ruled by her mind, intellect, and invention".[17] When Boston was asked if there were any similarities between her and Ingrid, she said that there was because she relate to being out of her "comfort zone and in an unfamiliar land figuring it out step by step".[21]
