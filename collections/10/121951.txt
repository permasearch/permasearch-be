Bednar's parents, Barry Bednar and Lorin LaFave, were born in the United States.[4] The family moved to England from the United States seventeen years before the murder.[5] Barry Bednar, 49, is an oil trader and shipping consultant, described by some sources as a millionaire.[4][6] Lorin LaFave, 48, is a teaching assistant.[3][4] The Daily Mail reported that the couple had divorced in 2006 and that Lorin LaFave was living as a single mother with the four children in their family home in Surrey.[7] The London Evening Standard reported that the family lives with their three surviving children in a family home in Caterham.[4]
