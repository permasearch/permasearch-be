Infosys Foundation In 1996, Infosys established the Infosys Foundation, to support the underprivileged sections of society.[57] At the outset, the Infosys Foundation implemented many programs in Karnataka. It subsequently covered Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, and Punjab in a phased manner. A team at the foundation identifies all the programs in the areas of healthcare, education, culture, destitute care and rural development.[58] The Infosys Foundation USA promotes science and math education in USA, with an emphasis on under-represented students.[59]
