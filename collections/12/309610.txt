Thorgan Ganael Francis Hazard (French pronunciation: ​[tɔʁɡan azaʁ]; born 29 March 1993) is a Belgian professional footballer who currently plays as an attacking midfielder or as a winger for German Bundesliga club Borussia Mönchengladbach and the Belgium national team. He is the brother of Eden and Kylian Hazard; Thorgan is the second oldest child of the family.
