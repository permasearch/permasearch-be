The WaterNSW now manages eleven major dams: Warragamba one of the largest domestic water supply dams in the world,[391] Woronora, Cataract, Cordeaux, Nepean, Avon, Wingecarribee Reservoir, Fitzroy Falls Reservoir, Tallowa, the Blue Mountains Dams, and Prospect Reservoir.[392] Water is collected from five catchment areas covering 16,000 square kilometres (6,178 square miles) and total storage amounts to 2.6 teralitres (0.6 cubic miles).[392] The Sydney Desalination Plant came into operation in 2010.[389]
