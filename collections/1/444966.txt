On 4 December 1985, the show transferred to the Palace Theatre, London and moved again on 3 April 2004, to the much more intimate Queen's Theatre, with some revisions of staging and where, as of August 2015[update],[24] it was still playing. It celebrated its ten-thousandth performance on 5 January 2010.[25] In October 2015, this West end version of the musical, celebrated its 30th anniversary.[26] The co-production has generated valuable income for the Royal Shakespeare Company.[27]
