On March 27, 2013, it was reported that NBC was considering plans for a spin-off of Chicago Fire. Deadline Hollywood revealed that the proposed spin-off would involve the Chicago Police Department, and would be created and executive produced by Dick Wolf, Derek Haas, Michael Brandt, and Matt Olmstead.[27][28] On May 10, 2013, NBC picked up the show for the 2013–14 United States network television schedule.[29] On May 12, 2013, the show was announced as an unscheduled midseason replacement.[30] On October 18, 2013, NBC announced that it would premiere on January 8, 2014, at 10:00 p.m. EST, after Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.[31]
