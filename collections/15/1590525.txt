Christina Chrissy "Monroe" Castranda was an official cast member on VH1 show Love & Hip Hop: New York. A notable reality television personality on the show Love & Hip Hop: New York, Chrissy moved to New York City at the age of 29 after signing a modeling and brand ambassador contract with Pabst Brewing Company. She subsequently launched her own company called LeJeu Entertainment which is a full spectrum development talent and modeling agency.
