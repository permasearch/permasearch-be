In August 2016, it was announced that the French private national TV channel TF1 is producing a remake of the series, titled Dans l'ombre du tueur ("In the Shadow of the Killer"). The French adaptation will star Emmanuelle Seigner and Melvil Poupaud as the leads, and supporting cast that will include: Jean-Hugues Anglade, Claire Keim, Patrick Chesnais, Bérengère Krief and Sofia Essaïdi. Filming began in Lyon on 12 September 2016.[76]
