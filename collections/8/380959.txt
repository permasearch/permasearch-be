Most recently she has been critically recognized for her performance in Deserted,[50] a psychological survival story written and directed by Ashley Avis. Barton plays Jae, a young woman recently released from prison who goes on a soul-searching journey into the desert with her brother Robin, played by Jackson Davis. "Barton is well-cast, grounded and burdened,"[51] and "...delivers one of her best performances in years."[52]
