The song has been used on the final sequences of the episode "I'll Try to Fix You" of the 2012 HBO's television series The Newsroom,[54] as well as an episode of Scrubs, and the final scene of the episode "Cargo" of the CBS series Cold Case. The song also briefly featured in the Australian family drama Packed to the Rafters. In 2013, "Fix You" was used in the final episode of the first season of British public television Channel 4's comedy-drama Derek. "Fix You" was played during an episode of Brothers & Sisters.
