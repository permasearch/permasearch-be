The ICTV has published reports of virus taxonomy about twice a decade since 1971 (listed below - "Reports"). The ninth ICTV report was published in December 2011[9]; the content is now freely available through the ICTV website.[10] Beginning in 2017 the tenth ICTV report will be published online on the ICTV website[11] and will be free to access with individual chapters updated on a rolling basis.
