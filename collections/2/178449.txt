The first episode was ranked fifth among the best selling PlayStation 3 and PlayStation 4 video games of February 2015.[125] Life Is Strange reached one million sales in July 2015,[126] having accumulated over 1.2 million unique players worldwide; the attach rate to units between the complete season and season pass proved to be "extremely strong", divulged Square Enix.[127] The retail edition made seventh place in the top ten UK game sales chart for the week ending 23 January 2016.[128] Life Is Strange was one of the top 100 best-selling games on Steam in 2016.[129] As of May 2017, more than three million copies have been sold.[130][131]
