Average costs for sleeve gastrectomy surgery are $19,000 in the United States, although this varies widely between states; South Dakota and Texas offer the lowest average cost at about $12,000 while the most expensive surgeries are performed in Alaska (average about $58,000).[10] In Turkey this operation costs $6,000 USD. In Jordan the total cost is around $4,000 USD, which includes all pre-surgery tests, x-ray and hospital fees. In New Zealand the cost is approximately $20,000 USD.[citation needed] Superannuation in Australia may be accessed by patients to fund the cost of surgery.[11]
