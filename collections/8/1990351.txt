In the second film his car is a gold color 1980 Pontiac Bonneville, with two single red emergency lights on the edges, two extra smaller ones (one red and one blue) and large megaphone in the center that completes this display: It gets its back right side fender buckled, the left side of the emergency light display destroyed, and megaphone buckled downwards.
