The Iowa 80 facility also has a two-story, 30,000 sq ft (2,800 m2) showroom, which is the largest trucker's store in the country.[4] Here customers may purchase chrome accessories, books, DVDs, CD's, cell phone accessories, apparel and cleaners, chrome stacks, and bumpers.  There are custom built show trucks, one on a rotating platform. A 20-by-40-foot (6.1 m × 12.2 m) wall displays 500 illuminated truck lights.  Also located in the store are a vinyl graphics shop, a custom T-shirt shop, and an  embroidery center.[1]
