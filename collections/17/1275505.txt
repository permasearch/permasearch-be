The first White Stig was introduced in November 2003, following the Black Stig's exit in the prior episode. His identity remained secret until it was revealed as Ben Collins in August 2010. During this Stig's time on the show, the character's role expanded from Power Lap times and "Reasonably Priced Car" training to include appearances in other show segments, such as producing timed runs in the Isle of Man road test;[ep 8] driving a Caterham Seven from Caterham to Knockhill;[ep 9] riding a London Bus, the DLR and the Tube across London;[ep 10] jumping a snowmobile off a ski-jump in Lillehammer, Norway;[ep 11] and playing a police pursuit driver in the White Van Man challenge.[ep 3][3]
