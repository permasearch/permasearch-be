Now with 2018, The Event is now called DaNang International Fireworks Festival, is one of the biggest, most exciting and original event in the world. Taking an extraordinary turn this year, the competition will no longer be just two nights, but separated into five (5) separate nights with eleven (11) displays from teams around the world to paint the skies of the night. Being themed The Legends of Bridges, the competition have 5 night. This long festival would be carried out from April until June with many fascinating side events.
