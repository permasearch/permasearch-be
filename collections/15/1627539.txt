Ti Saddhya Kay Karte (English: What Is She Up To These Days) is a 2017 Marathi language romantic drama film which is produced by Zee Studios and directed by Satish Rajwade.[3] It stars Ankush Chaudhari and Tejashree Pradhan Ketkar in lead roles.[4] Abhinay Berde, son of the late actor Lakshmikant Berde and Aarya Ambekar plays younger versions of character played by Ankush And Tejashree.[5] Movie had its theatrical release on 6 January 2017.[6]
