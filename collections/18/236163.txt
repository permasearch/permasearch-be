In April 2015, David Heyman, the producer of Paddington (2014), confirmed that a sequel was in development.[9] It was also announced that Paul King would return to direct, and co-write the script with Simon Farnaby.[10] Heyman's Heyday Films, and StudioCanal, produced the film.[11] By October 2016, most of the cast of Paddington — Hugh Bonneville, Sally Hawkins, Julie Walters, Jim Broadbent, Peter Capaldi, Madeleine Harris, Samuel Joslin, Ben Whishaw and Imelda Staunton — were confirmed to be returning for the sequel, joined by Hugh Grant and Brendan Gleeson.[8]
