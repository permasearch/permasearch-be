Thome experienced stiffness in his lower back in the Phillies' game against the Chicago Cubs on April 28,[103] and early in May, he was placed on the 15-day disabled list with a strained lower back.[104] At the time, he was batting only .100.[105] Thome returned to the club in early June, and prepared for interleague play against the Baltimore Orioles as the DH. Thome finished the nine-game interleague road trip with four home runs and 14 RBIs.[106]
