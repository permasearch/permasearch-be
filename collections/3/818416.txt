"Oops!... I Did It Again" is a song that lasts for a duration of three minutes and thirty seconds.[4] It is composed in the key of C ♯ minor and is set in time signature of common time, with a moderate tempo of 94 beats per minute. The song has a basic sequence of C♯m–A-G♯ as its chord progression, and features a vocal range spanning from C♯3 to C♯5.[5] Lyrically, the track discusses a female who toys with her lover's emotions, who mistakes Spears' flirtatiousness with a serious romantic interest.[6] During its bridge, Spears delivers a spoken-word dialogue that references the blockbuster film Titanic (1997).
