Yemen law does not recognize marital rape and does not provide a minimum age for marriage. The issues of child marriage and child rape inside marriage have made international news and have led to calls for legislative changes.[312] There have been several reports of deaths of young girls due to violent rape by adult husbands, as well as young girls dying during childbirth.[313][314][315][316] Human rights watch stated that "Child marriages and forced marriages remain widespread, exposing young girls to domestic violence and maternal mortality and truncating their education."[317]
