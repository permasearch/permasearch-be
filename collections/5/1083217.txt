Now the productive forces are truly free to develop, but in a democratically planned way, without the vast waste of anarchic capitalist society, its wars and its destruction of the planet. One of the primary tasks of the workers in the socialist society, after placing the means of production into collective ownership, is to destroy the "old state machinery". Hence the bourgeoisie's parliamentary democracy ceases to exist, and fiat and credit money are abolished.
