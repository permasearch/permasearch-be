The journey which started with 200 students has swelled today to a strength of 10,000+. Today DBGI has ten institution each having individual premises at Dehradun, (Uttarakhand), Kurukshetra (Haryana) & Saharanpur (UP) operating under Uttarakhand Utthan Samiti & Shri Krishna Education Trust, Haryana, which are professionally managed by the eminent academicians, industrialists, and scientists. These institutes are approved by AICTE respectively and are affiliated to the state Universities of Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh & Haryana.[1] Dev Bhoomi Institute of Management Studies was ranked No. 2 in entire Uttarakhand by Amar Ujala.[2]
