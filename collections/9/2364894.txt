An anime adaptation originally developed by DLE and Fields was scheduled to air in April 2016,[5] but it was delayed indefinitely until Netflix streamed the series worldwide on March 23, 2018.[6][7] Tomohito Naka directed the series while Takahiro Ikezoe served as the chief director, Inoue himself wrote the scripts, Atsuko Nakajima designed the characters, Toshiki Kameyama was the sound designer, and Kotaro Nakagawa composed the music. LandQ Studios, Production I.G, and DLE are credited with animation.[8] The opening theme song is "Sadame Goto" (サダメゴト, What is Predestined) by Yūto Uemura.[4]
