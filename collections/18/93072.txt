From 1972 to 1975, the program was taped at CBS Television City.[5] From 1977 until 1986—with the exception of the 1984–85 season, which was taped at the Production Group Studios near Columbia Square in Hollywood—the taping location was moved to Chris Craft/KCOP Studios. Taping returned to CBS Television City for the 1990–91 version. The current version of the show tapes at Sony Pictures Studios in Culver City, California.
