Henry split carries with veteran DeMarco Murray and run-oriented quarterback Marcus Mariota. On September 17, 2017, Henry ran for a then career-high 92-yards on 14 carries and scored a touchdown in a 37–16 victory against the Jacksonville Jaguars.[21] On October 16, 2017, on Monday Night Football, Henry ran for a career-high 131-yards on 19 carries and ran for a 72-yard touchdown late in the fourth quarter of the 36–22 victory over the Indianapolis Colts. In addition, he had 14 receiving yards in the victory. On December 3, 2017, Henry ran for 109 yards on 11 carries and beat his longest rush of the year with a 75-yard rushing touchdown late in the fourth quarter of the 24-13 victory over the Houston Texans. On December 31, 2017, Henry caught his first receiving touchdown on a 66-yard reception from Marcus Mariota.
