The song was ranked #5 on Pitchfork Media's Top 100 Tracks of 2006 list.[1] Pitchfork reviewed the song on 19 July 2006, giving it three out of five stars.[2] The song was also voted #2 in the 2006 NME Track of the Year poll.[3] Planet Sound named this their #2 single of 2006. The song was named the number one song of 2007 according to the iTunes Store. Y-Rock On XPN also named the song #1 of 2007. In October 2011, NME placed it at number 92 on its list 150 Best Tracks of the Past 15 Years.[4] It was included in Spinner's 10 Best Whistling Parts in Songs.[5] Rolling Stone put the song also on its list of the 15 Best Whistling Songs of All Time.[6]
