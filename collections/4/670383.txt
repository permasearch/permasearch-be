Collectively, the two territories are known as "St-Martin / St Maarten", or sometimes "SXM", the IATA identifier for Princess Juliana International Airport, the island's main airport. St. Martin (the French portion) received the ISO 3166-1 code MF in October 2007.[5] The Dutch part changed in status to a country within the Kingdom of the Netherlands in 2010 and was given the code SX.[6]
