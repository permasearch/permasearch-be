For both sexes, there have also been some successful players with lesser height, like Billie Jean King 1.64 m (5 ft 5 in), Justine Henin 1.67 m (5 ft 6 in), Chris Evert 1.68 m (5 ft 6 in), Pancho Segura 1.68 m (5 ft 6 in), Arantxa Sánchez Vicario 1.69 m (5 ft 7 in), Rod Laver 1.73 m (5 ft 8 in), and Michael Chang 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in). Shorter players continue to thrive. Present-day examples include Dominika Cibulková 1.60 m (5 ft 3 in), Sara Errani 1.64 m (5 ft 5 in), David Ferrer 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in), Kei Nishikori 1.78 m (5 ft 10 in), and Fabio Fognini 1.78 m (5 ft 10 in).[29]
