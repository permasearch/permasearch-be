Ocampo is Associate Professor and former Chairman of the Department of History, School of Social Sciences in the Ateneo de Manila University where he has been connected since 1998. He was Professorial Lecturer in the Department of Filipino and Philippine Literature of the College of Arts and Letters in the University of the Philippines Diliman from 1989 until 2010 and served on the Board of Regents of the Universidad de Manila (formerly City College of Manila), where he served as its President and Vice-President for Academic Affairs from 1996 to 1998. He has also held previous appointments at the De La Salle University, Far Eastern University and San Beda College.[18]
