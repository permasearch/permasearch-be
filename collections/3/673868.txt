Lionsgate Entertainment acquired worldwide distribution rights to a film adaptation of The Hunger Games, produced by Nina Jacobson's Color Force production company.[23] Collins adapted the novel for film herself,[23] along with director Gary Ross.[24] The cast included Jennifer Lawrence as Katniss, Josh Hutcherson as Peeta, and Liam Hemsworth as Gale.[25][26][27] The first film began production in Spring 2011,[28] and was released in March 2012.[29][30] For Catching Fire, Ross was replaced as director by Francis Lawrence;[31][32][33] the film was released in November 2013. Lawrence then directed Mockingjay, parts 1 and 2,[34] released in November 2014 and November 2015.
