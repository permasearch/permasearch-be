The film was released on Christmas Day 1992 in 1,066 theaters. It debuted at no. 5 at the US box office.[10] making $6.4 million in its opening weekend. In its second weekend, it dropped at #6 and grossed $4.8 million. It went on to gross $24.2 million in the U.S. and $5 million overseas, for a worldwide total of $29.3 million.
