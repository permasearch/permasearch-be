After the track was leaked online, Spears said she was "gonna do some sick remixes too" and asked "who would you like to remix Scream & Shout?"[11] On January 1, 2013, will.i.am said that he would release a club remix of "Scream & Shout" featuring American rapper Waka Flocka Flame and producer Hit-Boy.[105] An early version of the remix was leaked on January 25, 2013.[106] The remix features Waka Flocka Flame, Hit-Boy, and rappers Diddy and Lil Wayne.[106] Following the leak, will.i.am uploaded the finished version to his YouTube account.[107] It was released for digital download on January 29, 2013.[108] Because it is significantly different from the original version, the remix was tracked separately on the Billboard charts.[65] On the week ending February 16, 2013, it debuted at number 49 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs.[65] An accompanying music video was filmed in Los Angeles, California on January 23, 2013,[109] and will.i.am said that the remix is "so freaking fresh that we had to do a video for it."[110] The video was due to premiere on February 15, 2013 at BET's 106 & Park.[111] However, the day before its planned release it was leaked online through Youku,[112] and was therefore uploaded to VEVO the same day.[113]
