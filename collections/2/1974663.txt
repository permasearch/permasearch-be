The stand-off was ultimately resolved by civilian negotiators including Bo Gritz – who Weaver agreed to speak to.[87] Through the intermediacy of Gritz, Kevin Harris, who had earlier urged Randy Weaver to end his suffering, surrendered on August 30 and was removed via stretcher.[111] Likewise, Randy Weaver would allow the removal of his wife's body, the move of which, via body bag, Gritz oversaw.[111] FBI HRT Commander gave Gritz a deadline to get the remaining Weavers to surrender, else the standoff would be resolved by a tactical assault.[112][page needed] Randy Weaver and his daughters surrendered the next day. Both Harris and Randy Weaver were arrested. Weaver's daughters were released to the custody of relatives, although some consideration was given to charging Sara, who was 16, as an adult.[113]
