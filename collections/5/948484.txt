Modern Warfare Remastered was released worldwide on November 4, 2016 for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Microsoft Windows, alongside the Legacy, Digital Deluxe and Legacy Pro editions of Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare. At the time, the game was only available by purchasing a premium version of Infinite Warfare, with physical versions requiring the Infinite Warfare disc inserted to access the remaster.[45][46] Remastered was later released standalone for PlayStation 4 on June 27, 2017, and for Xbox One and Microsoft Windows on July 27, 2017.[47] Activision confirmed that premium versions of Infinite Warfare must be permanently installed in order to use the included remaster.[48]
