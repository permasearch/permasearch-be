The WWE Cruiserweight Championship is part of the Raw brand and is for wrestlers 205 lb (93 kg) and under. It is defended on Raw and the cruiserweight-exclusive 205 Live.[1] It was also defended once at an NXT house show.[2] The title is currently held by Kalisto, who is in his first reign. He won the title by defeating Enzo Amore on the October 9 editon of Raw in a Lumberjack match.
