On April 18, 2016, it was announced by BK Racing that sweetFrog would be sponsoring David Ragan and the No. 23 Toyota Camry for the Toyota Owners 400 at Richmond International Raceway.[22] sweetFrog returned to sponsor Ragan's No. 23 for the Federated Auto Parts 400 at Richmond in August.[23] sweetFrog later appeared in the video game NASCAR Heat Evolution.[24]
