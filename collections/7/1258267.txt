Oakbay Resources & Energy, is a mining and processing company, run by the Gupta family.[68] It supplies minerals and mineral products, such as gold, uranium, platinum, coal and diamonds and also conducts trackless/mechanised mining, track bound mining, breast mining, down-dip mining and open-pit gold operation.[68] Oakbay Resources & Energy began trading on the JSE on 28 November 2014.[69]
