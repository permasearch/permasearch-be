In the United States, "Anything Could Happen" debuted at number 17 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart on the issue dated 8 September 2012,[13] before rising to number three on 20 October upon its release to radio.[14] The song entered the Billboard Hot 100 at number 75 for the week of 27 October 2012, peaking at number 47 in its tenth week on the chart.[15] It also topped the Hot Dance Club Songs chart during the final week of 2012.[16] The single was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) on 17 January 2013, and platinum on 24 July 2013.[17] As of January 2014, the song had sold 1,166,000 copies in the US.[18]
