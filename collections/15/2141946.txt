By December 1961,[12] Caroll had opened the two-story Landmark Plaza shopping center,[13][14] built out in an L-shape[15][7] at the base of the tower.[13][16] The Landmark Apartments, with 120 units,[17] were also built near the tower and operational by the end of 1961.[10][18][19][20] In 1962, a bar known as Shannon's Saloon and a western music radio station, KVEG, began operating in the Landmark Plaza.[21][22][23] In addition to studios, KVEG also had its offices in the shopping center.[22][23]
