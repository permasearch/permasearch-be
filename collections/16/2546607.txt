Team Solomid acquired a team to compete at 2016 CoD: World League in February 2016. The team, which took less than 2 weeks to assemble,[82] consisted of Cole “ColeChan” Chancey, Jonathan “Pacman” Tucker, Jordan “ProoFy” Cannon, and Jamal “Whea7s” Lee.[83] On July 7, 2016 TSM announced their withdrawal from CoD, and that the team would be going separate ways. This is due to the team's performance in the World League Stage 2. The team finished near the bottom of their rankings.[83]
