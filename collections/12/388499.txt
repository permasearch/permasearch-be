The TV series Once Upon a Time that airs on the Disney-owned ABC, featured live-action versions of Snow White, Cinderella, Belle, Aurora, Mulan, Ariel, Rapunzel, Merida, Jasmine and Tiana in addition to Anna and Elsa. Snow White and Belle were previously main characters while the rest made recurring and or guest appearances. Beginning in season 7, Cinderella and Tiana were main characters. Many of these characters are patterned after the Disney versions, but a few draw inspiration from older stories.
