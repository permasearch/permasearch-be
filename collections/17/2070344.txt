"Way Down We Go" is a song written and recorded by Icelandic rock band Kaleo, released as the second single for their second studio album A/B by Elektra Records and Atlantic Records. The song was written and co-produced by the band. It was used in the movie Collateral Beauty [2], in a trailer for the movie Logan,[3] in the trailer for the fourth season of Orange Is the New Black,[4] as well as the shows Suits, Supergirl, Lucifer[5], Grey's Anatomy, Teen Wolf,[6] Blindspot,[7] Eyewitness and The Vampire Diaries,[8] and an advertisement for Boots UK.[9]
