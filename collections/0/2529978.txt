The Halo adaptations have been successful as well. Many of the novels have appeared on Publisher Weekly's bestseller charts and the Halo Graphic Novel sold more than 100,000 copies, a "rare hit" for the games-to-comics genre.[207] Ghosts of Onyx, Contact Harvest, The Cole Protocol and the first volume of Cryptum appeared on The New York Times bestseller lists,[123][208][209][210][211][212] and The Cole Protocol also opened 50th overall on USA Today's bestsellers list.[213] Tor's first three novels sold more than one million copies by April 2009.[214]
