She played the role of a vivacious and modern girl in Chhan Chhan (2013).[24][25] The show was loosely based on the Hindi movie Khubsoorat.[26] Irani then appeared in Rangrasiya (2014) as Parvati. The show was produced by Saurabh Tiwari, head of Tequila Shots Production. The story followed a border security personnel and an orphan girl who were ideologically different.[27] It ended in September.[28] She was seen opposite Ashish Sharma.
