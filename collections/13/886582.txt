On July 26, 2017, TVLINE reported that fan favourite Kaitlyn Black who plays Annabeth Nass, was promoted to a series regular for the third season.[25] Josh Cooke was cast as Zoe's new boyfriend, Joel. The casting was announced by The Hollywood Reporter on August 1, 2013.[26] Season three also saw the introduction to Zoe's estranged family, the Wilkes. TVFanatic reported that Lauren Bittner was cast as Zoe's cousin, Vivian Wilkes, a new love interest for Wade.[27]
