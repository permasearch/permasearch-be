Tu Aashiqui (English: You, my love) is a 2017 Indian musical romance television show based on two lovers effort to unite.[2][better source needed] It is produced by Mahesh Bhatt[3] under Gurudev Bhalla production house. The show has been stated to be inspired from the American film Endless Love.[4] It stars Jannat Zubair Rahmani and Ritvik Arora in the leads as Pankti and Ahaan respectively.[4]
