Columbus was ranked as the 15th most literate city in the country in 2008 by Central Connecticut State University,[90] and the 19th best educated.[91] In 2006, Columbus was ranked by CNN Money as the 8th best big city in the country to live in.[31] In 2012, Columbus was ranked by Bloomberg Businessweek as America's 20th Best City, the highest-ranked city in Ohio.[92]
