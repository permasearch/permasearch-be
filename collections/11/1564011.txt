Other areas which received a significant number of Yoruba people and are sites of Yoruba influence are: Puerto Rico, Saint Lucia, Grenada, Santa margarita and Belize, British Guyana, Saint-Domingue (Now Haiti), Jamaica[15](Where they settled and established such places as Abeokuta, Naggo head in Portmore, and by their hundreds in other parishes like Hanover and Westmoreland, both in western Jamaica- leaving behind practices such as Ettu from Etutu, Yoruba for Atonement among other customs of people bearing same name, and certain aspects of Kumina such as Sango veneration),[149][150][151][152][153][154][155] Barbados, Dominican republic, Montserrat, etc.
