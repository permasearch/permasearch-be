Of 194 stones, 95 are marble, 41 are granite, 30 are limestone, 9 are sandstone, with 19 miscellaneous types, including combinations of the aforesaid and those whose materials are not identified. Unusual materials include native copper (Michigan),[43]:147 pipestone (Minnesota),[43]:153 petrified wood (Arizona),[43]:213 and jadeite (Alaska).[43]:220 The stones vary in size from about 1.5 feet (0.46 m) square (Carthage)[H] to about 6 by 8 feet (1.8 m × 2.4 m) (Philadelphia and New York City).[43]:3, 90, 124, 218
