Middlebush Reformed Church known as "the church with the red doors", is located at 1 South Middlebush Road at the corner of Amwell Road in Franklin Township, Somerset County, New Jersey, United States.[1][2] It is the fourth oldest church in Franklin Township.[3][4] It was organized in 1834,[5][6][7] and the New York Times noted their first church was built in Colonial times and was one of the landmarks of the region.[8] The church is a contributing property of the Middlebush Village Historic District that was added to the National Register of Historic Places on April 24, 2007.[9]
