Sheeran was born in Halifax,[6] on 17 February 1991.[7] When he was a child, he moved with his family to Framlingham in Suffolk.[8] He has an older brother named Matthew,[9] who works as a composer. Sheeran's parents, John and Imogen, hail from London; his paternal grandparents are Irish,[10][11][12] and Sheeran has stated that his father is from a "very large" Catholic family.[13] John is an art curator and lecturer, and Imogen is a culture publicist turned jewellery designer.[11][14] His parents ran Sheeran Lock, an independent art consultancy, from 1990 to 2010.[15]
