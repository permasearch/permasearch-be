Tracey Donovan, played by Lisa Maxwell, made her first on screen appearance on 12 September 2016.[29][30] Tracey is the mother of established characters Clare Devine (Gemma Bissix), Grace Black (Tamara Wall), Adam Donovan (Jimmy Essex), Liam Donovan (Maxim Baldry) and Jesse Donovan (Luke Jerdy) and the ex-wife of the character Fraser Black (Jesse Birdsall).[30]
