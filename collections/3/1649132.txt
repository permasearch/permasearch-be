This branch descends from Robert Burnet, Lord Crimond, another brother of Sir Thomas Burnett, 1st Baronet. He was the third son of Alexander Burnet, above-mentioned, and Katherine Gordon.[35] Robert studied law in France for several years and in 1617 was admitted to the Scottish Bar.[35] He was a Scottish advocate and Judge of the Court of Session in 1661 as Lord Crimond.[35] His heir was his third son Alexander Burnett. His fifth son was Gilbert Burnet, Bishop of Salisbury.
