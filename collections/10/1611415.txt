The series' Blu-ray and DVD releases were handled in Japan by Aniplex.[41] The series released in six volumes between September 28, 2011 and February 22, 2012.[42] The series' English dub and North American licensing was handled by Funimation.[8] A box set containing the whole series released for Blu-ray and DVD on January 22, 2013.[43] In Australia, the series was licensed by Madman Entertainment, releasing as a box set for DVD and Blu-ray on April 17, 2013.[44][45] In Europe, the series was distributed by Anchor Bay Entertainment through Manga Entertainment. It released as a box set on DVD and Blu-ray on June 10, 2013.[46]
