In January 2013, Kudos and Shine France announced a joint United Kingdom – France production called The Tunnel, with the discovery of the body of a French politician at the half-way point of the Channel Tunnel.[53][54] Broadcast began in October 2013 on Sky Atlantic in the UK and Canal+ in France.[55] An eight-episode second season was broadcast in 2016 and filming for the third and final season is underway.[56]
