Renewal and reissue of the OCI document is required to on each passport renewal for holders up to the age of 20 year and once on the first passport issued after the age of 50. For other holders, it is generally advisable to renew, but not compulsory when obtaining a new passport. The OCI card has the old passport number, and that old passport may be required to be carried along with the new passport, and OCI document to avoid issues.[8]
