There is no airport in Tirunelveli.The closest domestic airport to Tirunelveli City is the Thoothukudi airport (TCR), located at Vaagaikulam, Thoothukkudi district, about 28 km east of Tirunelveli City. Connections to Chennai and Bangalore are  Spice Jet, twice a day. The nearest international airports are Madurai International Airport and Thiruvananthapuram International Airport, each about 150 km away by road. There is a demand for Tirunelveli International Airport which will boost the tourism, manufacturing and IT services in the district.
