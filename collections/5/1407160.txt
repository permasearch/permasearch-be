On Rotten Tomatoes the film has an approval rating of 68% based on 179 reviews, with an average rating of 6.3/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "While WTF is far from FUBAR, Tina Fey and Martin Freeman are just barely enough to overcome the picture's glib predictability and limited worldview."[25] Metacritic gives the film a weighted average score of 57 out of 100, based on 44 critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews".[26] Audiences polled by CinemaScore gave the film an average grade of "B" on an A+ to F scale.[27][24]
