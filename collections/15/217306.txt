According to the Digambara Jains, Mahavira was born in 582 BC.[36] According to the Svetambara Jain texts, he was born in 599 BC. Mahavira's birthday, in the traditional calendar, falls on the thirteenth day of the rising moon in the month of Chaitra in the Vira Nirvana Samvat calendar.[20][37][38] In the Gregorian calendar, this date falls in March or April and is celebrated by Jains as Mahavir Jayanti.[39]
