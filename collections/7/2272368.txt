With the signing into law of the Children and Family Relationships Act 2015 on 6 April 2015, same-sex couples have the ability to jointly adopt children and stepchildren, as well as be able to obtain parental recognition in assisted reproduction methods of child creation.[102][103] On 18 January 2016, key provisions of the law (including spouses, stepparents, civil partners and cohabiting partners being able to apply to become guardians of a child or for custody) went into effect, following the signing of a commencement order by the Minister for Justice and Equality.[104] Portions of the Act allowing for joint adoption have not yet come into effect.[105]
