Thaila Lucia Zucchi (/ˈtaɪlə/ [luˈtʃiːa ˈdzukki]; born 19 January 1981) is a British singer and actress of English and Italian descent.[1] She began her career as a member of the British band allSTARS* before transitioning into acting, appearing in Big Brother 8 in the UK, where she played the part of fake Australian housemate Pauline. She has also appeared in Balls of Steel and Star Stories.
