On 15 May 2005 at 07:00 local time, U.S. tennis star Venus Williams played a show game with Turkish player İpek Şenoğlu on the bridge, the first tennis match played on two continents.[12][13] The event promoted the upcoming 2005 WTA Istanbul Cup and lasted five minutes.[12] After the exhibition, they both threw a tennis ball into the Bosphorus.[12][13]
