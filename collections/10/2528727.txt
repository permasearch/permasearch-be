Fringe ended its third season with an average of 5.83 million viewers per episode, finishing in 99th place for the American network television season.[89] Time shifted viewing played a significant part in Fringe's third season ratings. According to a report released by Nielsen Company, Fringe was the only network television series among the top ten of most time-shifted shows of 2011. The report continued that time shifting increased the series' overall audience by eighty percent.[90]
