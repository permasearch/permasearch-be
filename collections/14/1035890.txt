The review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes gave an approval rating of 100% for seasons 1, 2, 3, and 4 based on 9 reviews for season 1, 5 reviews for season 2, 6 reviews for season 3, and 6 reviews for season 4, with an average rating of 8.17/10 for season 1, 7.5/10 for season 2, 9.0/10 for season 3, and 9.28/10 for season 4.[60][61][62][63] On Metacritic, the first season has a weighted average score of 78 out of 100 based on 4 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[64] IGN and Variety in particular had strong praise for the pilot film, Spark of Rebellion, with their only criticism being the appearance of the Wookiees in the film, being cited as not all that impressive compared to the rest of the animation.[65]
