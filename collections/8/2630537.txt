Through Harrison's friendship with the Band, Jonathan Taplin served as production manager, while Chip Monck was in charge of lighting.[27] Gary Kellgren from the nearby Record Plant was brought in to record the concerts, overseen by Spector,[10] and "Klein's people", led by director Saul Swimmer, would handle the filming of the event.[27] The official concert photographers were Tom Wilkes and Barry Feinstein,[77] the pair responsible for the artwork on Harrison's acclaimed 1970 triple album, All Things Must Pass.[78][nb 5]
