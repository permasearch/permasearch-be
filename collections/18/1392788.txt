As Special Education Act announced in 1984, students with special needs started to receive special education services in Taiwan.Two subgroups were further classified by professionals in special education, including students with difficulties and students with giftedness.In order to provide support that meet students needs, three main ways of special education services are provided: centralized special education class, decentralized resource room, and itinerant resource program.Students from age 2 to 22 can receive special education services in the process of assessment, placement and educational provision.[11][12]
