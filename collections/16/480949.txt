Panettiere was born in and partly raised in Palisades, New York. She is the daughter of Lesley R. Vogel, a former soap opera actress, and Alan Lee "Skip" Panettiere, a fire captain. She has Italian, and some English and German ancestry. She has one younger brother, fellow actor Jansen Panettiere.[3][4][5] Her mother's family lives in Indiana.[6][7]
