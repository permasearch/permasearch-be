UPS had over 1200 LNG fueled trucks on the roads in February 2015.[35] UPS has 16,000 tractor trucks in its fleet, and 60 of the new for 2014 large trucks will be placed in service in the Houston, Texas area, where UPS is building its own private LNG fuel center to avoid the lines at retail fuel centers.[36] In Amarillo, Texas and Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, UPS is using public fuel centers.[37]
