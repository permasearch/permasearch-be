Batman Returns was released in America on June 19, 1992, earning $45.69 million in 2,644 theaters on its opening weekend.[42] This was the highest opening weekend in 1992 and the highest opening weekend of any film up to that point.[43] The film went on to gross $162.83 million in North America, and $104 million in foreign countries, coming to a worldwide total of $266.83 million.[42] Batman Returns was the third highest-grossing film in America of 1992,[43] and sixth highest in worldwide totals.[44]
