There are other StarCraft 2 tournaments that may not bear the WCS name, but allow players to earn WCS points. A few such tournaments include some of the aforementioned tournaments such as Intel Extreme Masters (IEM) and DreamHack Open, but also ASUS ROG,[133] Red Bull Battlegrounds,[134] and Homestory Cup.[135] A full list of tournaments can be found on the StarCraft 2 Liquidpedia.[136]
