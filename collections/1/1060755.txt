Colleen Van Dueson is a character in Friday the 13th Part VIII: Jason Takes Manhattan, played by Barbara Bingham. Colleen is Rennie Wickham's teacher who accompanies her on the Lazarus for the students graduation trip. Her and Rennie are very close much to the chagrin of Charles McCulloch who disapproves of her bond with Rennie. When the group arrive in New York Colleen is forced to watch as Rennie is kidnapped by two thugs. Colleen dies in an explosion when the police car Rennie commandeered and drove at Jason crashes and blows up killing her instantly.
