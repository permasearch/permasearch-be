For much of its existence, the studio was recognized as the premier American animation studio;[9] it developed many of the techniques, concepts, and principles that became standard practices of traditional animation.[10] The studio also pioneered the art of storyboarding, which is now a standard technique used in both animated and live-action filmmaking.[11] The studio's catalog of animated features is among Disney's most notable assets, with the stars of its animated shorts – Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, Goofy, and Pluto – becoming recognizable figures in popular culture and mascots for The Walt Disney Company as a whole.
