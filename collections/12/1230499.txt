Thank Me Later was released on June 15, 2010,[78] debuting at number one on the Billboard 200 with sales of over 447,000 copies in its first week.[79] Upon the album's release, 25,000 fans gathered at New York City's South Street Seaport for a free concert, hosted by Drake and Hanson, which was later cancelled by police after a near-riot ensued due to overflowing crowds.[80] The album became the top selling debut album for any artist in 2010,[81] and featured Lil Wayne, Kanye West,[82] and Jay Z.[83]
