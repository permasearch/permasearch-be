Vick made his fourth Pro Bowl after the season and was named the Eagles' starting quarterback.[48] The Associated Press and Sporting News named him the NFL Comeback Player of the Year.[49][50] He also won the Bert Bell Award on March 4, 2011.[51] In early 2011, the Eagles placed their franchise tag on Vick.[52] He signed the one-year tender on March 2.[47] On August 29, however, Vick and the Eagles announced they had agreed on a 6-year, $100 million contract with almost $40 million in guaranteed money.[53]
