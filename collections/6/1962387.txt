Levine began dating Namibian Victoria's Secret model Behati Prinsloo in May 2012.[131][132] They got married in July 2014. The couple have two daughters. Prinsloo gave birth to the couple's first child, a daughter named Dusty Rose, in September 2016.[133] On September 13, 2017, Prinsloo announced her second pregnancy on her Instagram account.[134] On February 15, 2018, it was announced that Prinsloo had given birth to their second child, a daughter named Gio Grace.[135]
