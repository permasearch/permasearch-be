The General Assembly has 253 members, consisting of a Senate with 50 members and a House of Representatives with 203 members, making it the second-largest state legislature in the nation (behind New Hampshire) and the largest full-time legislature. As of 2014, members' base pay was $85,356,[1] making it the costliest state legislature per capita in the U.S.[2] Republicans hold a 34-16 majority in the Senate and a 120-83 majority in the House.
