Jenna Dewan-Tatum portrays Freya Beauchamp, Ingrid's younger sister.[5] She works as a bartender at The Bent Elbow.[18] Freya has lived many past lives, and in each one she dies young, only to be reborn again.[1] She has the powers of premonition, healing, telekinesis and limited weather control, and is particularly talented with making potions.[3][22]
