Independence Day: Resurgence premiered at the TCL Chinese Theatre in Los Angeles on June 20, 2016. The film was originally going to be released on July 3, 2015[55] but on November 12, 2013, it was announced that the sequel had been rescheduled for a July 2016 release.[56] On October 14, 2014, Fox changed the release date to June 24, 2016.[57] It was released in certain formats such as 3D, IMAX 3D, and premium large formats.[58]
