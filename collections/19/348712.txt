The song was nominated for two Teen Choice Awards in 2003 on the categories, "Choice Music Single" and "Choice Music Hook-Up",[35] but lost it for Avril Lavigne's "Sk8er Boi" and 50 Cent and Nate Dogg's "21 Questions", respectively.[36] In May 2003, "All I Have" received a BDSCertified Spin Award, signifying 200,000 radio spins.[37] In 2004, it received an ASCAP Pop Music Award and a BMI Pop Award.[38][39]
