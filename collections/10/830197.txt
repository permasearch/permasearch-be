Major rice insect pests include: the brown planthopper (BPH),[121] several spp. of stemborers – including those in the genera Scirpophaga and Chilo,[122] the rice gall midge,[123] several spp. of rice bugs[124] – notably in the genus Leptocorisa,[125] the rice leafroller, rice weevils and the Chinese rice grasshopper.[126] The fall army worm, a species of Lepidoptera, also targets and causes damage to rice crops.[127]
