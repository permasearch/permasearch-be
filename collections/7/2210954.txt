Canned coffee is ubiquitous in Japan, with a large number of companies competing fiercely and offering various types for sale. Canned coffee is already brewed and ready to drink. It is available in supermarkets and convenience stores,[78] with vast numbers of cans being sold in vending machines[79] that offer heated cans in the autumn and winter,[80] and cold cans in the warm months.
