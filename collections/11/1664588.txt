Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara grossed ₹120 million (US$1.9 million) overseas in three days, which made it the biggest opening for an Indian film in 2011.[66] The film reached top-twenty lists in the US and UK.[72] It grossed £896,289 in UK, $3,103,656 in USA, $387,384 in Australia and $136,380 in New Zealand.[73] As of January 2012, the film has grossed $7.25 million overseas.[74]
