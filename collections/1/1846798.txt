On 27 June 2001, ten minutes before she died, Norah Holland spoke to her gently about Kenneth Williams, Hattie Jacques and their time on the Carry On films.[15] Sims died from liver failure and diverticulitis, with diabetes and COPD cited as contributory factors.[16][17] She was cremated at Putney Vale Crematorium, and her ashes scattered in the grounds there.[18]
