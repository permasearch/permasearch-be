On 14 November 1994 Eurostar services began between Waterloo International station in London, Gare du Nord in Paris and Brussels South in Brussels.[29][30][31] In 1995 Eurostar was achieving an average end-to-end speed of 171.5 km/h (106.6 mph) between London and Paris.[32] On 8 January 1996 Eurostar launched services from a second railway station in the UK when Ashford International was opened.[33] Journey times between London and Brussels were reduced by the opening of HSL 1 on 14 December 1997.
