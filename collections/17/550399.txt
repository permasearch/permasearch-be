As of 25 May 2008[update], 79 facilities for the production of a wide variety of food products were in operation.[75] Of these facilities, 49 are located in the US, 12 in the Asia/Pacific region (8 of which are leased), 5 in Canada (2 of which are leased), 7 in Europe (3 of which are leased), 5 in Latin America and Mexico, and one in South Africa.[76]
