The Processes tab shows a list of all running processes on the system. This list includes Windows Services and processes from other accounts. Prior to Windows XP, process names longer than 15 characters in length are truncated.[2] Beginning with Windows XP, the Delete key can also be used to terminate processes on the Processes tab. By default the processes tab shows the user account the process is running under, the amount of CPU, and the amount of memory the process is currently consuming.
