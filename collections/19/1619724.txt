The Walt Disney Masterpiece Collection (Walt Disney Coleccion Maestra in Spanish and Collection Chefs-d'oeuvre de Walt Disney in French-Canadian) was a line of VHS tapes and laserdiscs released by Walt Disney Home Video from 1994 to 1999.[8][9] Similar to the Walt Disney Classics line, it consists mainly of Disney animated features, along with a few live-action/animated hybrid films. Spanish versions were also released.
