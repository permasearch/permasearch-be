Hugh Keays-Byrne (born 18 May 1947) is an English Australian character actor. He moved to Australia in 1973 and is well-known there as a television and film actor. Outside Australia, he is best known for his role as Toad in the 1974 film Stone, the main antagonist Toecutter in the 1979 film Mad Max,[1] the main antagonist "Immortan Joe" in the 2015 film Mad Max: Fury Road,[1] and Grunchlk on the science fiction television series Farscape.
