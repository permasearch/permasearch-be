Mohan Estate (Hindi: मोहन एस्टेट) is an elevated station on the Violet Line of the Delhi Metro. It is located between Sarita Vihar and Tughlakabad stations and close to the financial and industrial region of Mohan Estate in South Delhi. The station finally received safety clearance and opened as part of Sarita Vihar—Badarpur section on 14 January 2011.[1]
