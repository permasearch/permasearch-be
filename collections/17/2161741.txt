After a short period of interim government, municipal elections were held in May 2007. For the first time, the People's Party (PP) gained a majority, with 16 out of the 27 council seats. The PSOE won 10 council seats, United Left (IU) had 1.[76] In the municipal elections of May 2011, the PP won 15 seats, the PSOE 7, the IU 2, and three independents elected.[77][78]
