During 2013, additional voice cast members were announced for B.O.O.: Bureau of Otherworldly Operations, including Coolidge as Carol Sue, a transitions-relations officer.[33] Film distributor 20th Century Fox set November 6, 2015 for the film's release date (which was moved a few months later up to September 25),[34] In 2014, Los Angeles Times reported that the film had been delayed again, with no replacement release date set.[35] It was reported the film's concept has gone back to development.[36] Also in 2013, Coolidge and actress Megan Mullally joined the cast of Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day; Coolidge playing Ms. Suggs, the driving instructor.[37] The film was released in North America on October 10, 2014.
