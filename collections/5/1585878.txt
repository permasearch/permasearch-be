Snoop is a certified football coach and has been head coach for his son Cordell's youth football teams and also the John A. Rowland High School team.[93][94] Cordell played wide receiver and defensive back at Bishop Gorman High School in Las Vegas, Nevada, and received football scholarship offers from Southern California, UCLA, Washington, Cal, Oregon State, Duke, and Notre Dame.[95][96][97][98][99][100] Cordell committed and signed a letter of intent to play for UCLA on February 4, 2015.[101] On August 14, 2015, UCLA announced that Cordell had left the UCLA football team "to pursue other passions in his life".[102]
