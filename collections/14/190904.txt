From 2017 reviews, a plant-based diet reduced total and LDL cholesterol, with further evidence that shifting plant protein consumption to more than 50% of total protein intake may lower the risk of cardiovascular diseases, although further clinical research was recommended.[12][13]  A diet high in fruits and vegetables may decrease the risk of high blood pressure, stroke, and colorectal cancer.[14][15] A vegetarian diet may also help with weight loss.[16]
