What We Do in the Shadows received critical acclaim and has an approval rating of 96% on Rotten Tomatoes based on 163 reviews with an average rating of 7.8 out of 10. It is rated #68 on the website's list of Top 100 comedies of all time. The critical consensus states: "Smarter, fresher, and funnier than a modern vampire movie has any right to be, What We Do in the Shadows is bloody good fun."[16] The film also has a score of 76 out of 100 on Metacritic, based on 33 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[17]
