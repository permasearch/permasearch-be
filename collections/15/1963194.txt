Dr. Kirby keeps a boutique dermatological practice in Hermosa Beach, California, with an emphasis on aesthetic procedures and complexion enhancement catering only to friends, family, and select private patients and celebrity clientele. Office visits are accepted by appointment only. Bringing over a decade of experience in the aesthetic dermatology industry, Kirby also serves at the National Medical for LaserAway. In this role he selects dermatologists to serve as regional medical directors for the company, writes technical medical protocols for use in their exclusive EMR, investigates and assists in the acquisition of new aesthetic dermatological products and devices, and supervises the implementation of clinical policies and procedures.
