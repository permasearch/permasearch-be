Many guest stars from the earlier seasons reprised their roles. Proposition Joe (Robert F. Chew), the East Side's cautious drug kingpin, became more cooperative with the Barksdale Organization.[45][46] His lieutenant "Cheese" (Method Man) became involved in the Major Crimes Unit investigation.[47][48] Brother Mouzone (Michael Potts) returned to Baltimore looking for revenge.[49] Hassan Johnson reprised his role as incarcerated Barksdale enforcer Wee-Bey Brice.[50][51] Tray Chaney continued to portray Barksdale crew chief Poot Carr.[52][53] Leo Fitzpatrick returned as hapless drug addict Johnny Weeks.[54][55]
