Many familiar mammal species are crepuscular, including some bats,[2] hamsters, housecats, stray dogs[6], rabbits,[2] ferrets[7], guinea pigs and rats[8]. Other crepuscular mammals include jaguars, ocelots, bobcats, strepsirrhines, red pandas, bears,[9] deer,[2][10] moose, sitatunga, capybaras, chinchillas, the common mouse, skunks, Australian wombats, wallabies, quolls, possums[2] and marsupial gliders, tenrecidae, spotted hyenas, and African wild dogs.
