Murdered: Soul Suspect received an average reception upon release; most criticisms centered around the poor combat, short length, lack of replay value, and lack of difficulty. Aggregating review websites GameRankings and Metacritic gave the PlayStation 4 version 61.29% based on 41 reviews and 59/100 based on 60 reviews,[8][11] the Microsoft Windows version 58.00% based on 12 reviews and 59/100 based on 22 reviews[9][12] and the Xbox One version 55.76% based on 17 reviews and 50/100 based on 16 reviews.[10][13]
