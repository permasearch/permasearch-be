Later a child named Chiku is brought to Rawat House and left their with a letter. The letter is written to ‘Mr Rawat’ which suggests the child could be Suyash, Vidhaan or Jayant’s child. It is revealed Chiku is the illegitimate child of Suyash and Shreya, a girl who went to the same college as Suyash. The family cannot believe this and ask Shreya to leave, however Falguni asks her to stay.
