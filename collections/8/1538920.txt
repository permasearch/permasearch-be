In 2007, Airbus estimated a demand for 1,283 VLA in the following 20 years if airport congestion remains constant, up to 1,771 VLAs if congestion increases, with most deliveries (56%) in Asia-Pacific, and 415 very large, 120-tonne plus freighters.[290] For the same period, Boeing was estimating the demand for 590 large (B747 or A380) passenger airliners and 630 freighters.[291]
