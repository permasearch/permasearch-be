For the Thomas Cup draw, the teams were allocated to three pots based on the World Team Rankings of 22 February 2018. Pot 1 contained the top seed China (who were automatically assigned to Position A1), the second seed Denmark (who were automatically assigned to Position D1) and the next two best teams, Indonesia and Chinese Taipei. Pot 2 contained the next best four teams, meanwile Pot 3 for the ninth to sixteenth seeds teams.
