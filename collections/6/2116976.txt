In May 2013, Dolce & Gabbana released their third version of the “Light Blue” fragrance campaign. Mario Testino again filmed the commercials and adverts on location in Capri but this time with Italian model Bianca Balti in the female role opposite Gandy.[22] In 2015, InStyle magazine included Light Blue as one of 'The 10 Hottest Perfume Campaigns'.[23]
