Similarly owing to the live performance, "Love on Top" debuted at number 65 on the Canadian Hot 100 chart issue dated September 17, 2011.[70] The song also entered at number 75 on the UK Singles Chart and at number 23 on the UK R&B Chart on September 4, 2011,[71][72] It debuted at number 14 on the New Zealand Singles Chart the following day,[73] and spent seven non-consecutive weeks on the chart.[73] In Ireland, "Love on Top" made its debut at number 47 on September 8, 2011.[74] The song also entered at number 40 on the Australian Singles Chart on September 12, 2011.[75] The following week, it debuted at number 9 of the Australian Urban Singles Chart,[76] and was the most added song to radios in Australia.[77] "Love on Top" peaked at number 20 on the singles chart on November 6, 2011, and number 4 on the urban chart on October 17, 2011.[78][79] Having spent 16 consecutive weeks on the Australian Singles Chart,[78] the song was certified double platinum by the Australian Recording Industry Association (ARIA), denoting sales of 140,000 copies.[80]
