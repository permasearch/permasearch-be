Viperine Gorgon (voiced by Yeni Alvarez)[210] is a gorgon daughter of Stheno, whose sister is Medusa. She works as a makeup artist in Hauntlywood and is originally from Barcelgrona. She has pink hair, underneath which is a nest of vipers. She and Deuce Gorgon are cousins. She first appears in the 2014 movie Frights, Camera, Action.[76][211] Viperine's doll was designed by Natalie Villegas,[6] and was released as part of the film's characters.[77]
