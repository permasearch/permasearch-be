Mazatlán (Spanish pronunciation: [masaˈtɬan] ( listen)) is a city in the Mexican state of Sinaloa. The city serves as the municipal seat for the surrounding municipio, known as the Mazatlán Municipality. It is located at 23°13′N 106°25′W﻿ / ﻿23.217°N 106.417°W﻿ / 23.217; -106.417 on the Pacific coast, across from the southernmost tip of the Baja California Peninsula.
