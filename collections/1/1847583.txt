The episode received a great deal of praise from critics. The animation, Hori's storyboarding and voice acting of the "Here Comes a Thought" sequence was particularly lauded,[3][4][5] with James Whitbrook of io9 calling it "one of Steven Universe's best songs since 'Stronger Than You'",[6] and Eric Thurm of The A.V. Club, who gave the episode an A grade, singling out AJ Michalka as "the real MVP of the song".[7] The message of the episode was also praised,[7][8] with Vrai Kaiser of The Mary Sue comparing it to Pixar's Inside Out,[3] and ND Medina of iDigitalTimes calling it a "bite-sized dose" of cognitive behavioral therapy.[4]
