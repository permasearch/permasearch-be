The first voice actor to be cast in the series was Jeffrey Combs as Ratchet. Combs revealed this through his personal website on February 4, 2010.[4] On May 17, 2010 it was revealed that Peter Cullen, who had originated the role of Optimus Prime in the original series and voiced him in the live-action film series, would return as Optimus' voice in Prime.[25] During San Diego Comic Con on July 8, 2010, it was revealed that Frank Welker would also reprise his role as Megatron from the original series. Besides Optimus and Ratchet, further Autobots revealed were: Bumblebee, Arcee and Bulkhead. It was also revealed that Starscream and Soundwave would be part of the Decepticons.[26]
