Kathmandu is and has been for many years the centre of Nepal's history, art, culture and economy. It has a multiethnic population within a Hindu and Buddhist majority.  Religious and cultural festivities form a major part of the lives of people residing in Kathmandu. Tourism is an important part of the economy as the city is the gateway to the Nepalese Himalayas. It is home of 7 world heritage sites which include Durbar Squares of Hanuman Dhoka,Patan and Bhaktapur,Stupas of Swayambhunath and Baudhanath and temple of Pashupati and Changu Narayan .It is also the home of Newars.
