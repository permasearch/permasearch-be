In early 2017, five years after the film's initial release, the short film became somewhat viral, garnering a polarizing reaction due to its controversial themes.[3][4] Ivan Kander of the website Short of the Week wrote that if one "[takes] a quick perusal of the video’s comments on YouTube ... you’ll find everything from effusive acclaim to disgusted vitriol. In terms of the internet, that means it’s a hit".[5]
