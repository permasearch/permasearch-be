In May 2013, a Romanian flag that was the largest in the world was unfurled at Clinceni. The flag, weighing some 5,000 kg (11,000 lb) and with a size of 349.425 m × 226.917 m (1,146.41 ft × 744.48 ft), had an official surface area of 79,290.39 m2 (853,474.7 sq ft). This area surpassed the previous world record of 65,975 m2 (710,150 sq ft), held by a flag of Lebanon.[25][26][27][28][29]
