Pre-production commenced on 23 August 2010 at Cape Town Film Studios in Cape Town, South Africa.[32] During the 2010 San Diego Comic-Con International in July, Urban confirmed that he had been offered the role of Judge Dredd, and on 18 August 2010, it was reported that Urban officially had the role.[33] In September 2010, it was announced that Thirlby would play Dredd's telepathic rookie Cassandra Anderson.[4] In the same month during the Toronto International Film Festival, the film attracted $30 million in worldwide pre-sales to distributors in 90% of theatrical markets.[34][35] The sales included a $7 million deal with British distributor Entertainment Film Distributors.
