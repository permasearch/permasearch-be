Irvine was chosen in 2008 by CNNMoney.com as the fourth-best place to live in the United States.[32] In 2012, it was ranked sixth nationally.[33] In September 2011, Businessweek listed Irvine as the fifth-best city in the United States.[34] Irvine consistently ranks as the safest city in America with a population over 100,000.[35] In 2014, Irvine was named the best-run city in the U.S. by 24/7 Wall Street.[36] In March 2017, WalletHub listed Irvine as the third happiest place to live in the United States.[37] In June 2017, Irvine was named tenth best City in America by Niche.com.[38]
