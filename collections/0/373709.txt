Pixels received negative reviews from critics.[7] On review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes, the film has an approval rating of 17% based on 172 reviews; the average rating is 3.8/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "Much like the worst arcade games from the era that inspired it, Pixels has little replay value and is hardly worth a quarter."[74] On Metacritic, the film has a weighted average score of 27 out of 100, based on 37 critics, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews".[75] Audiences polled by CinemaScore gave the film an average grade of "B" on an A+ to F scale.[65]
