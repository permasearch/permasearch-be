The episode aired on American Broadcasting Company on January 25, 2018.[1] Upon initial release, it was viewed by 8.62 million people, an increase of 0.35 million from the previous installment and the mid-season premiere "1-800-799-7233"; it also garnered a season-best 2.3/9 Nielsen rating.[2][3] "Personal Jesus" was also the week's second most watched drama and ranked seventh on the list of most watched television programmes overall.[4] The 8.62 million audience was also the largest for Grey's Anatomy in over a year.[2]
