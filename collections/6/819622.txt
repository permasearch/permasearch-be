In Belgium, vehicle inspections are known as autokeuring (Dutch) or contrôle technique (French). All cars over 4 years old must be tested annually, and before selling them on the second-hand market. This annual period may be extended to two years if the following apply: The vehicles passes the inspection and receives a green certificate, the vehicle has covered less than 100,000 km, the vehicle has been tested within its date limit and if the vehicle is less than six years old.
