In December 2009, the New York Times featured articles on the possibilities for tourism in the Pamir area of Tajikistan.[16][17] 2013 proved to be the most successful year ever for tourism in the region and tourism development continues to be the fastest growing economic sector. The META (Murghab Ecotourism Association) website (www.meta.tj) provides an excellent repository of tourism related resources for the Eastern Pamir region.
