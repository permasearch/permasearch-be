Yo-kai Watch is a children's anime series based on the video game of the same name developed by Level-5. The anime began broadcast on January 8, 2014, on TXN and related stations.[1] An English dub, produced by Dentsu Entertainment USA, started airing on the Disney XD channel on October 5, 2015 and the Disney XD block on Disney Channel on December 18, 2015 in the United States, Canada's Teletoon on October 10, 2015, and 9Go! in Australia on December 14, 2015.[2] Episodes are split into multiple parts.
