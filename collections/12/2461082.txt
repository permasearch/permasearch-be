His articles have appeared in Salon.com,[25] the Washington Post Magazine, The Nation, Mother Jones,[26] Rolling Stone, the San Francisco Chronicle, and the Los Angeles Times. Talbot wrote about Robert Mugabe in an article for the Frontline/World website.[27] He has written about his daughter's home birth, "Call the Midwife," for KQED's website [28] In the 1970s, he was a reporter and editor for Internews, a radio and print foreign news service based in Berkeley, California.
