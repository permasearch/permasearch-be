Zack and Cody make a hole in a wall, and Arwin helps them fix it. Zack and Cody then discover that they can see soccer girls in the next room, so they invite their friends to look in the hole too. They later get found out by Mr. Moseby and Carey and they all end up with a sore eye. Meanwhile, London transfers to Maddie's school and causes her to get a detention, later they pretend to be nuns from Finland.
