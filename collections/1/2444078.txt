AIG offers property casualty insurance, life insurance, retirement products, mortgage insurance and other financial services.[98] In the third quarter of 2012, the global property-and-casualty insurance business, Chartis, was renamed AIG Property Casualty.  SunAmerica, life-insurance and retirement-services division, was renamed AIG Life and Retirement, other existing brands continue to be used in certain geographies and market segments.[98][99]
