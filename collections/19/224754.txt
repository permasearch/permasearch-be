The Jacksonville Jaguars Foundation was established in 1994, when the franchise deal was first announced.[109] Since then, the Foundation has given over $20 million to area efforts in community improvement.[110] The Foundation focuses on many initiatives, such as Honor Rows, anti-tobacco programs, NFL Play 60, and support for veterans.[111] The Foundation grants over $1 million annually to organizations that assist "economically and socially disadvantaged youth and families".[112]
