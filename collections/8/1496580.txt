In October 2017, multiple national media outlets reported that Spurs Sports & Entertainment, owner of the NBA's San Antonio Spurs and its WNBA sister team, the San Antonio Stars, had reached an agreement to sell the Stars to a Las Vegas-based group that would move the team to the area effective with the 2018 season. The NBA and WNBA officially approved the sale on October 17, 2017 and announced the Las Vegas WNBA team will be playing at the Mandalay Bay Events Center starting in the 2018 season.[16]
