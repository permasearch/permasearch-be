A free universal insurance system is in place.[32] Quality of medical care varies significantly by region[322] and is often low, with severe shortages of equipment, drugs and anaesthetics.[323] According to WHO, expenditure on health per capita is one of the lowest in the world.[323] Preventive medicine is emphasized through physical exercise and sports, nationwide monthly checkups and routine spraying of public places against disease. Every individual has a lifetime health card which contains a full medical record.[324]
