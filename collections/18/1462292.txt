Kerala Pradesh Congress Committee (Kerala PCC or K.P.C.C) is the unit of the Indian National Congress—one of the world's largest political organization—working in the state of Kerala. Its head office is situated at Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram). It first met in 1921 at the banks of the River Bharathappuzha.[3] M. M. Hassan is the current president. Ramesh Chennithala is the current parliamentary party leader
