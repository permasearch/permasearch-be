In November 2006, the University of California, Davis released a report analyzing business leadership by women within the state.[68] The report showed that although 103 of the 400 largest public companies headquartered in California were located in Santa Clara County (the most of all counties), only 8.8% of Silicon Valley companies had women CEOs.[69]:4,7 This was the lowest percentage in the state.[70] (San Francisco County had 19.2% and Marin County had 18.5%.)[69]
