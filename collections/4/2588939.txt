Bridge over Troubled Water charted in over 11 countries,[clarification needed] topping the charts in 10 countries, including the US Billboard 200 and the UK Albums Chart.[56][57] It was the best-selling album in 1970, 1971 and 1972 and was at that time the best-selling album of all time.[38] It remained CBS Records' best-selling album until the release of Michael Jackson's Thriller in 1982.[58] The album topped the Billboard 200 charts for 10 weeks and stayed on the charts for 85 weeks.[38] According to Columbia Records, 1.7 million copies were sold in the first three weeks in the United States.[36] In the United Kingdom, the album topped the charts for 35 weeks, and spent 285 weeks in the top 100, from 1970 to 1975.[38] Furthermore, it received 8× Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), and 4× Platinum in Canada.[38] Bridge over Troubled Water has since sold 3,163,789 copies in the UK,[59] and over 25 million copies worldwide.[60]
