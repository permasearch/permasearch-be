War Horse (1982) by Michael Morpurgo is set in World War I and won the Whitbread Book Award for 1982. It has been adapted into a play and film. US writer Mark Helprin's A Soldier of the Great War[25] and French novelist Sebastien Japrisot's A Very Long Engagement both appeared in 1991 and the latter subsequently became a film in 2004.[26] The novel Birdsong (1993) by Sebastian Faulks received much praise[27] and later became a TV mini-series in 2012.
