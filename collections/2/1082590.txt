Principal photography for Casino Royale commenced on 3 January 2006. The film was primarily shot at Barrandov Studios in Prague, with additional location shooting in the Bahamas, Italy and the United Kingdom. The shoot concluded at Pinewood Studios on 20 July 2006.[255][256] In the Bahamas filming took place at Paradise Island[257] and Albany House, an estate owned by golfers Ernie Els and Tiger Woods.[258] Footage set in Mbale, Uganda, was filmed at Black Park, a Country Park in Buckinghamshire,[259] with further UK location shooting taking place at the Dunsfold Aerodrome in Surrey and the Millbrook Vehicle Proving Ground in Bedfordshire.[260]
