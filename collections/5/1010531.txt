Duanne Olivier and Lungi Ngidi were added to South Africa's squad for the second Test after Dale Steyn suffered an injury during the first Test.[31] Dinesh Karthik was called up as replacement to India's Test squad for Wriddhiman Saha after Saha was ruled out of the series due to an injury in training.[32] Temba Bavuma suffered a finger injury in a domestic one-day game and was ruled out of South Africa's squad ahead of the third and final Test.[33] South Africa's AB de Villiers was ruled out of first three ODIs due to a finger injury.[34] He was added back into South Africa's squad for the final three matches.[35]
