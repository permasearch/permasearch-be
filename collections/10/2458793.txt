The graduating class of 2016, the largest class to date, had 801 students receiving degrees.[87] Of them, the youngest was 16 and the oldest was 70 with an average age of 36.[87] They were nearly equally split between the genders, with 52% being male, and came from 36 countries and 42 states.[87] Almost all, 96%, were taking classes for professional gain.[37] Half take a single course, and half pursue a degree.[37] The increase in online course offerings has fueled growth, and students from more than 150 countries are enrolled.[37]
