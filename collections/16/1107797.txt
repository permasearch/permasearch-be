Naa Peru Surya is a 2018 Telugu language patriotic action film produced by Sirisha and Sridhar Lagadapati under Ramalakshmi Cine Creations banner, written and directed by Vakkantham Vamsi. This marks the debut movie of Vamsi as a director who has earlier written stories for successful movies like Kick, Race Gurram, Temper. The movie stars Allu Arjun in the lead role while "Action King" Arjun Sarja plays an important supporting role and "Supreme star" R. Sarathkumar was roped in to play the antagonist. The soundtrack for this movie is composed by Vishal-Shekhar. Kotagiri Venkateswara Rao, Rajeev Ravi and Rajeevan handle the editing, cinematography and Art direction respectively.It will hit screens on April 27, 2018 along with the Malayalam dubbed version.
