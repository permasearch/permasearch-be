In the United States, the show debuted on Nickelodeon's Nick Jr. block on August 16, 2004.[17] The second season debuted in the United States on Nick Jr. in 2006.[18][19] It also aired in the United States on CBS as part of the Nickelodeon on CBS Saturday morning block from September 18, 2004 to September 9, 2006. The series would continue to air daily on the Nick Jr. channel until 17 July 2010.[20]
