Google announced on 31 August 2010 that it had started to index SVG content on the web, whether it is in standalone files or embedded in HTML, and that users would begin to see such content listed among their search results.[46] It was announced on 8 December 2010 that Google Image Search would also begin indexing SVG files.[47] On 28 January 2011, it was discovered that Google was allowing Image Search results to be restricted exclusively to SVG files.[48] This feature was announced officially on 11 February 2011.[49]
