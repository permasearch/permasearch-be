Therefore, "the business of her life" ever since Jane, the eldest, reached 16 years old, is finding husbands for her daughters in order to ensure their financial security.[18] Thus, she shows immediately interest in the arrival of an eligible bachelor in the region.[19] So she sends Jane to Netherfield in the rain to make sure they retain her there, she encourages Mr Collins to ask for the hand of Elizabeth, and she rejoices loudly about the marriage of Lydia, indifferent to the dishonourable reasons which made it necessary.
