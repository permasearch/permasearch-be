On October 24, 2015, Spears donated $120,000 to the Nevada Childhood Cancer Foundation.[337] In addition, $1 of each ticket sale for her Las Vegas residency show, Britney: Piece of Me, was donated to the nonprofit organization.[338] Spears also fundraised for the charity through social media, in addition to selling limited edition merchandise, with all proceeds going to the NCCF.[339] On October 27, 2016, Spears partnered with Zappos and XCYCLE to host the Britney Spears Piece of Me Charity Ride in Boca Park, Las Vegas to raise additional money toward her goal of $1 million for the NCCF, with $450,000 having already been raised from Spears' ticket sales and merchandise.[340] Participants were entered for a chance to win a spin class with Spears herself. The event ultimately went on to raise $553,130.[341]
