On July 22, 2017, David Ozer, president of IDW Entertainment, announced at the show's San Diego Comic-Con panel that the series had been renewed for a third season with the premiere scheduled for 2018.[21][22] Space announced on the same day that it had ordered 12 episodes for a third season, together with Syfy.[23][24] Andras credited Wynonna Earp's passionate fan base (nicknamed "Earpers") with gaining the renewal.[25][26]
