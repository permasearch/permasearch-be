In Northern Africa, deep-fried dishes are a part of the cuisine.[41] A common food in this region is the deep-fried fritter, also referred to as "sponges".[41]In East africa deep fried food is common, cooked in cast iron or earthenware pots.  Frying in batter is common. A Ugandan speciality is a kind of doughnut called Mandazi.  In areas of Southern Africa, street foods include deep-fried potato and cassava chips.[42] Deep-fried foods in the country of South Africa include fish and chips, vetkoek and koeksisters,[43] among others.
