A 12-episode anime television series adaptation by Diomedéa aired between January 11 and March 29, 2015.[20] The opening theme is "Hi no Ito Rinne no Gemini" (緋ノ糸輪廻ノGEMINI), performed by Petit Milady (Aoi Yūki and Ayana Taketatsu), and the ending theme is ""Magna Idea" (マグナ・イデア, Maguna Idea), performed by Fortuna (Aoi Yūki, Ayana Taketatsu, Maaya Uchida and Yui Ogura).
