Filming of Season 3 began January 15, 2018, and scheduled to wrap on May 7, 2018.[55][56] On February 5, 2018, the series announced the casting of Megan Follows in the role of Michelle Earp, the mother of Wynonna Earp.[57] On March 15, the addition of a bounty hunter character named Kate, with Chantel Riley cast in the recurring role, was announced.[58] Bell Media announced on April 24 that musician Jann Arden had joined the cast as Bunny Loblaw, a member of Purgatory's town council.[59]
