Flair's "Wooo" chant has been used throughout pop culture, in hip-hop music in particular. Rapper Pusha T has paid homage to Flair in numerous songs. For example, on the track "Sweet Serenade", he says, "Triple doubles, two hoes and check please (Wooo!), They love me on my Ric Flair shit (Wooo!), In that Phantom like I'm Blair Witch (Wooo!), Who are you to be compared with? (Wooo!)".[143] Rapper Killer Mike also has a track named "Ric Flair".[144] Rapper Offset played tribute to Flair in his hit song "Ric Flair Drip." [145]
