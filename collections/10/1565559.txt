On February 21, 2011 Microsoft announced that it would release a non-commercial Kinect software development kit (SDK) for Microsoft Windows in spring 2011, and the first beta was released for Windows 7 on June 16, 2011.[13][14][15] Beta 2 was released on the 1 year anniversary of Kinect for Xbox 360, on November 3, 2011.[89] Version 1.0 was released at the same time as the officially supported Microsoft Windows hardware on February 1, 2012.[90]
