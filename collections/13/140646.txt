As of May 2016, there are 1,094 personnel of all ranks in the Naval Service,[2] plus approximately 150 in the Naval Service Reserve.[44] The Naval Service is headed by a General Officer Commanding (GOC) known as the Flag Officer Commanding the Naval Service (FOCNS), who holds the rank of Commodore.[45] Non-Military training takes place alongside Mercantile Marine Personnel at the National Maritime College of Ireland in Ringaskiddy, near to the Haulbowline base.[46]
