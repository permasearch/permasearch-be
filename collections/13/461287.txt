On June 5, 2015, it was announced that after several seasons of being a recurring role, Jason George was upgraded to a series regular.[12] It was announced on June 15, 2015, that Martin Henderson, who played a doctor on the ShondaLand produced show Off the Map, would be added as a series regular for the twelfth season.[13] He will make his debut in the middle of the season, according to Rhimes.[14] On June 28, 2015, it was announced that Jessica Capshaw, whose contract expired after Season 11, had renewed her contract for another three seasons as Dr. Arizona Robbins. This means that her character will be staying on the show through seasons 12 as well as possible seasons 13 and 14.[15]
