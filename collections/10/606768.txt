In singles, Novak Djokovic (winner in 2012–13, 2015) and Andy Murray (winner in 2010–2011, 2016) hold the record for most titles with three each. In singles, Djokovic, Andy Murray and Magnus Norman share the records for most consecutive titles (two). Murray holds the record for most finals (four). In doubles, Leander Paes (winner in 1998, 2010, 2012) holds the record for most titles (three), and no player has collected back-to-back titles yet.
