In 2016, exclusively to India, Indian arm of South Korea's LG Electronics Inc started selling a TV that would reject mosquitoes.[23] It uses ultrasonic waves that are silent to humans but cause mosquitoes to fly away.[23] It was released on June 16, 2016. The technology was also used in air conditioners and washing machines.[23] The TV is aimed for lower-income consumers living in conditions that would make them susceptible to mosquitoes.[23]
