In 2009, the School of Medicine received 5,166 applications[88] and accepted approximately 4% of them,[89] while the average GPA and MCAT scores for accepted students from 2002 through 2009 were 3.74 and 34, respectively.[90][91] The School of Law accepted approximately 13% of its applicants for the Class of 2014, while enrolling students had a median GPA of 3.75 and median LSAT of 170.[92]
