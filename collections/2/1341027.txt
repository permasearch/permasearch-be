In March 2010, Penn called for the arrest of those referring to Venezuelan President Hugo Chávez as a dictator.[59] The two were friends, and when Chávez died, Penn said: "Venezuela and its revolution will endure under the proven leadership of Vice President (Nicolás) Maduro. Today the United States lost a friend it never knew it had. And poor people around the world lost a champion. I lost a friend I was blessed to have."[60]
