Capitaine de corvette Thierry d'Argenlieu[25] suggested the adoption of the Cross of Lorraine as a symbol of the Free French. This to recall, the perseverance of Joan of Arc, patron saint of France, whose symbol it had been, the province where she was born, and now partially annexed into Alsace-Lorraine by the Third Reich, and as a response to the symbol of national-socialism, the Nazi swastika.[26]
