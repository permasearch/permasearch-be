Peter Hein was chosen as the film's action choreographer,[66] while A. R. Rahman agreed to compose the film's background score and soundtrack.[67][68] Soundarya chose Saroj Khan, Raju Sundaram, Chinni Prakash and his wife Surekha Chinni Prakash, and Sivashankar to choreograph the songs.[69] Soundarya invited art professionals to send their work as she was forming an art department for Kochadaiiyaan.[70] She chose 42 students from the Government College of Fine Arts, Chennai for the task, besides 50 experienced persons from the Jacob College of Fine Arts. 60 concept artistes were already reportedly working for the film.[71]
