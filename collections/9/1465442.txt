Schur is married to Jennifer Philbin, who was formerly a writer on The O.C. and is the daughter of television star Regis Philbin. Their first child, son William Xavier Schur, was born on February 18, 2008.[22] His middle name, Xavier, is in honor of Regis's confirmation name.[22] On July 14, 2010, Philbin gave birth to their daughter, Ivy Elizabeth Schur, in California.
