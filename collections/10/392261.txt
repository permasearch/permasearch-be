On 27 June 2016, the film began filming in Liverpool and London.[7] It then moved to Pinewood Studios, where it wrapped on 8 August 2016.[8] To fill in for scenes set in New York City and Malibu, California, the crew used rear-screen projections.[2] On 11 November 2017, Elvis Costello released an original song entitled "You Shouldn't Look at Me That Way", composed specifically for the film.[9]
