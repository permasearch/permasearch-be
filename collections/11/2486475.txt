"Young Blood" debuted a top the New Zealand Singles Chart on 14 June 2010,[12] becoming the first time a New Zealand artist debuted at number one in over three years;[13] Matthew Saunoa's "Hold Out" did so in November 2006.[14] The song held the top spot for another week, however on 28 June it was pushed to number two by "California Gurls" by Katy Perry and Snoop Dogg.[15] "Young Blood" fell out of the singles chart in October 2010, having spent eighteen weeks in the chart.[16]
