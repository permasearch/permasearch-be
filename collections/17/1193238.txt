The nominees for most categories were revealed on July 16, 2018, via an IGTV video.  Nominees for Song of Summer, however, were announced on August 13, 2018. Cardi B had the most nominations with 12,[3] with The Carters behind with 8, while Childish Gambino and Drake both received 7 nominations each.[10][11] Winners were announced on August 20, 2018, on the Video Music Awards broadcast.[12]
