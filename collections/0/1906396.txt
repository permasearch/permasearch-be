Despite the large number of contributors, the greatest burden continues to be borne by a core group of developing countries. The ten largest troop (including police and military experts) contributing countries to UN peacekeeping operations as of August, 2016 were Ethiopia (8326), India (7471), Pakistan (7161), Bangladesh (6772), Rwanda (6146), Nepal (5131), Senegal (3617), Burkina Faso (3036), Ghana (2972), Egypt (2889).[21]
