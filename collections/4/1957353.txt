Lifetime first announced plans to adapt the second book in the Dollanganger series on January 9, 2014, just before they aired Flowers in the Attic.[2] It was also announced by writer Kayla Alpert that the film would take place ten years after Flowers in the Attic, whereas the book picked up directly after the events of the first.[3][4] Alpert stated that she started working on the script for Petals on the Wind, shortly after she had finished the script for the first film.[5]
