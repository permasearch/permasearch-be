Title XII, known as the "Improving Access to Mainstream Financial Institutions Act of 2010",[171] provides incentives that encourage low- and medium-income people to participate in the financial systems. Organizations that are eligible to provide these incentives are 501(c)(3) and IRC § 501(a) tax exempt organizations, federally insured depository institutions, community development financial institutions, State, local or tribal governments.[172] Multi-year programs for grants, cooperative agreements, etc. are also available.[173]
