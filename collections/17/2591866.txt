In 2012 Long led the Rams in sacks with 11.5 and again led the team in quarterback hits (24) and hurries (50) [35] Additionally, according to Football Outsiders.com Long led NFL defensive ends in hurries for the third straight season.[36][37] On December 30, 2012, Long tied a career-high of 3 sacks versus the Seattle Seahawks.[38] Long's additional honors included being named to the USA Football's All-Fundamentals Team for the first time.[39] He was also voted as a Pro Bowl alternate for the third time.[40][41]
