Richardson guest starred on the FOX television show House (Season 2. Episode 13 "Skin Deep"). She played 15-year-old supermodel Alex, having herself been a model before getting into acting. She is known for her role as Paula Hargrove in Point Pleasant, a 2005 series on Fox. She also guest starred on the HBO television show, Entourage in the episode "Strange Days" in which she played a waitress who has a tryst in the coat room with character Vincent Chase during a silent auction. In 2009, she was a series regular on Harper's Island, a murder mystery television series. Promotions for the series describe her character Chloe as The Flirt.[5] Richardson appeared in the film Alvin and the Chipmunks.[6]
