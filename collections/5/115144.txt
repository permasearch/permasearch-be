The Sports Turf Managers Association (STMA) presents various awards each year. Starting in 2001, its Sports Turf Manager of the Year Awards have been presented annually in the Triple-A, Double-A, Class A, and Short-Season/Rookie divisions of Minor League Baseball and are chosen from the 16 league winners.[12] STMA also presents the Baseball Field of the Year Award, which includes Schools and Parks, College/University and Professional categories.[13]
