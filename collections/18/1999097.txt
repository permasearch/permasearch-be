The game will have vast compatibility with controllers, including compatibility with the GameCube controller through the GameCube Adapter for Wii U.[16] Ultimate will also support existing Amiibo figures for any of the fighters to provide unique abilities when used with the Joy-Con and Pro Controller NFC sensors.[17] New Amiibo figurines will also be introduced alongside the game for characters that did not appear in Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS/Wii U.[18]
