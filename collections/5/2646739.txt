Other major languages include Mende, which is spoken by 37% of the population as a mother tongue and as a lingua franca in southern Sierra Leone, and Temne, which is spoken by 29.7% as a mother tongue and also as a lingua franca in northern Sierra Leone and some part of the Capital and the Western Area. Other languages include Kono, Kissi, Kuranko, Limba, Fula (Pular) and Susu.
