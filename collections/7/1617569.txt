Frank William Abagnale Jr. was born on April 27, 1948. He is one of four children and spent the first sixteen years of his life in New Rochelle, New York.[4] His French mother, Paulette, and father, Frank Abagnale Sr., separated when he was twelve[5] and divorced when he was sixteen.[6] His father was an affluent local who was very keen on politics and theater, and was a role model for Abagnale Jr.[7] His primary school education was in a Catholic school.[8]
