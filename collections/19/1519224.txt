In a 2005 Tom's Games interview, Maressa Orzack estimated that 40% of the players of World of Warcraft (an MMORPG) were addicted, but she did not indicate a source for the estimate.[47] She may have derived the estimate from the informal survey managed by Nick Yee at The Daedalus Project,[48] who notes that caution should be exercised when interpreting that data.[49]
