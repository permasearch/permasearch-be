In November 2007, Hamilton started dating Nicole Scherzinger, the lead singer of the American girl band Pussycat Dolls; it was announced in January 2010 that they split up to focus on their respective careers. However, they were seen together at the 2010 Turkish Grand Prix[279] and at the Canadian Grand Prix in Montreal, on 13 June 2010.[280] The couple split up and reunited numerous times between 2011 and 2013,[281][282] but appeared to have got back together in November 2013.[283] They split up again in February 2015.[284]
