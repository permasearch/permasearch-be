"Till Death Do Us Part" was seen by 19.05 million live viewers following its broadcast on May 15, 2012, with a 12/20 share among all households, and 3.6/11 share among adults aged 18 to 49.[2][5] A rating point represents one percent of the total number of television sets in American households, and a share means the percentage of television sets in use tuned to the program. In total viewers, "Till Death Do Us Part" easily won NCIS and CBS the night. The spin-off NCIS: Los Angeles drew second and was seen by 15.19 million viewers.[2] The episode also was the most watched television program the week it aired.[5] Compared to last week's episode "Up In Smoke", "Till Death Do Us Part" was up a bit in both viewers and adults 18-49.[6]
