On 3 December 2009, Lewis performed her version on the BBC Radio 2 show Live Sessions with Ken Bruce, as part of a set list which also included "Bleeding Love", "Better In Time", "Happy" and "I Got You".[68] She performed an acoustic version of the song on MTV Unplugged in Germany.[69][70] A reviewer for Neon Limelight felt that her acoustic performance lacked emotion and passion.[70] The singer performed the song again on the sixth series finale of The X Factor in December 2009.[71] On 21 January 2010, Lewis performed "Stop Crying Your Heart Out" and "I Got You" on BBC Radio 1's Live Lounge.[72]
