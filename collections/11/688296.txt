Bratz was universally panned by critics and fans alike. On Rotten Tomatoes, gives the film a rating of 9%, based on 78 reviews, with an average rating 3/10. The site's critical consensus, "Full of mixed messages and dubious role-models, Bratz is too shallow even for its intended audience."[7] On Metacritic, the film has  a score of 21 out of 100, based on 18 critics, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews".[8]
