Come Away with Me is the first full-length album by vocalist Norah Jones, released by Blue Note Records on February 26, 2002. The album received Grammy Awards for Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Album[1]  and was certified Diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) on February 15, 2005, having shipped over 10 million copies in the U.S.[2][3] As of October 2016, the album has sold more than 27 million copies worldwide.[4] Come Away with Me reached the top of the Billboard 200 chart and several jazz charts.
