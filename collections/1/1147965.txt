Aldean married Jessica Ann Ussery on August 4, 2001.[3] Together the couple have two daughters, Keely (born February 14, 2003) and Kendyl (born August 20, 2007).[3] On September 30, 2012, Aldean admitted to having "acted inappropriately at a bar",[25] with former American Idol contestant, Brittany Kerr, responding to reports linking her and Aldean. In a statement, Kerr said she had suffered "a lapse in judgment" and "would like to sincerely apologize to everyone that has been affected by this."[26] Aldean filed for divorce on April 26, 2013, citing irreconcilable differences and listing the filing date as the formal separation date.[3]
