Kash (Pej Vahdat) is the owner of the grocery store Kash and Grab, where Ian works. He has an affair with Ian despite being Muslim and married with children. When Linda, Kash's wife, learns of his sexuality, she allows it as long as he gives her another child. Kash is unable to stand up to Mickey, who constantly steals from the store. When Linda becomes pregnant, he tries to restart his affair with Ian and is shocked to find him now with Mickey. Faced with this knowledge, Kash finally managed to stand up to Mickey and shoots him in the leg, leading Mickey to be returned to juvie. However, Kash continues to feel trapped in his marriage and he leaves without word.
