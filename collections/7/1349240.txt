The Japanese opening song is "Alola!!" (アローラ!!, Arōra!!) by Rica Matsumoto with Ikue Otani. The Japanese ending song is "Pose" (ポーズ, Pōzu) by Taiiku Okazaki. The Japanese second opening is "Future Connection" (未来コネクション, Mirai Konekushon) by Japanese Band, ReaL. The second Japanese ending song is "Twerp, Twerpette" (ジャリボーイ・ジャリガール, Jari-boy, Jari-girl), again by Taiiku Okazaki.[2] The opening for the English dub is “Under the Alolan Moon” by Haven Paschall and Ben Dixon.
