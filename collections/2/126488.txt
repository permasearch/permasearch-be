After the two are held hostage in a grocery store, they begin dating.[20] Julie loses her virginity to Austin, realising that the relationship has to move forward if she wants to keep dating him. Unfortunately her mother and Edie catch Austin with Danielle Van de Kamp (Joy Lauren), and breaks up with him.[21] Eventually Julie agrees to give him a second chance, but when Danielle learns that she is pregnant with his child, Orson Hodge (Kyle MacLachlan) insists Austin leave town.[22]
