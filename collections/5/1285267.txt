The Sami language is an official language to the north, in Lapland or in northern Lapland, where are the Sami people, numbering around 7,000[54] and recognized as an indigenous people. About a quarter of them speak a Sami language as their mother tongue.[55] The Sami languages that are spoken in Finland are Northern Sami, Inari Sami, and Skolt Sami.[note 1]
