Jeter has appeared in national ad campaigns for Nike, Gatorade,[264] Fleet Bank, Ford, VISA,[265] Discover Card,[266] Florsheim,[267] Gillette, Skippy,[268] and XM Satellite Radio.[269][270][271][272] He endorses a cologne named Driven, designed in collaboration with and distributed by Avon.[273] Jeter has his own Jumpman shoe.[274] To commemorate Jeter's final year, the Jordan brand made a tribute commercial titled "#RE2PECT", which had many baseball players (such as Jon Lester) and celebrities, even rival Boston Red Sox fans tip their caps.[275]
