Gilman graduated with honors from Flintridge Preparatory School where she also achieved the Advanced Placement Scholar designation. She is athletic and played in a variety of school sports teams including volleyball, water polo, basketball, softball and soccer. Gilman also holds the distinction of being the only girl in school history to complete the intense training required to play on the Boys Junior Varsity Football team.[2] On June 1, 2014, she graduated from high school.[5] Gilman began attending the University of Southern California in the fall of 2014. She is studying film production, screenwriting, and theatre.[2]
