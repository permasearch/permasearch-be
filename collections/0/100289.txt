It was released worldwide on August 25, 2017, although those who pre-ordered the "G.O.A.T. Edition" were able to play it three days earlier. It is the first game of the series since Madden NFL 2005 to not be released on both PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360,[1][2] and also the first game in the main series to be available on only two platforms since Madden NFL '94.
