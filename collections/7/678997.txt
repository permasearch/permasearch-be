The General Assembly resolution establishing the Council, provided that "the Council shall review its work and functioning five years after its establishment”.[45] The main work of the review was undertaken in an Intergovernmental Working Group established by the Council in its Resolution 12/1 of 1 October 2009[46] The review was finalized in March 2011, by the adoption of an "Outcome" at the Council’s sixteenth session, annexed to Resolution 16/21.[47]
