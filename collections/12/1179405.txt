Several video game adaptations of the film have been published on various game console platforms, including Shrek (2001), Shrek: Hassle at the Castle (2002), Shrek: Extra Large (2002), Shrek: Super Party (2002) and Shrek SuperSlam (2005).[114] Shrek was also included as a bonus unlockable character in the video game Tony Hawk's Underground 2 (2004).[115]
