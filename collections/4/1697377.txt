Donovan's earliest film roles included Kevin Donaldson in SpaceCamp (1986), a recovering drug addict in Clean and Sober (1988),[11] 1st Lieutenant Luke Sinclair in Memphis Belle (1990)[12] and Paul Matthews in Love Potion No. 9 (1992).  Donovan voiced the title character in the Disney animated film Hercules (1997).[13]  Donovan played Jesse Zousmer in the Best Picture nominated film Good Night and Good Luck (2005).  In 2007, Donovan starred in the film Nancy Drew alongside Emma Roberts.[14]  Later, he played Bob Anders, one of the key supporting roles in the Oscar-winning film Argo (2012).[15][16]
