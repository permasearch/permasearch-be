As stated by the Mercer international surveys for quality of life in cities around the world, Bucharest occupied the 94th place in 2001[41] and slipped lower, to the 108th place in 2009 and the 107th place in 2010. Compared to it, Vienna occupied number one  worldwide in 2011 and 2009.[42] Budapest ranked 73rd (2010) and Sofia 114th (2010).[43]
