Tiger Child, the first IMAX film, was demonstrated at Expo '70 in Osaka, Japan.[4] The first permanent IMAX installation was built at the Cinesphere theatre at Ontario Place in Toronto. It debuted in May 1971, showing the film North of Superior. The installation is still in place,[5] however, Ontario Place is on hiatus for redevelopment. Cinesphere re-opened November 3, 2017, with IMAX. [6]
