On May 26, 2015, Antena 3 renewed the series for a second season.[3] After Antena 3 cancelled the show, on 6 July 2017, Fox Spain announced they had picked up the series for a third season, which premiered on April 23, 2018.[4][5] A fourth season will be released in 2019.[6] Maggie Civantos will reprise her role,[7] and Benjamín Vicuña, Cristina Marcos and Georgina Amorós will join to the series.[8] It will be shot in Spain.[9]
