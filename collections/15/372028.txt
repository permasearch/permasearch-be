The first three X-Men films and Deadpool set opening records in North America: X-Men had the highest July opening yet,[160] while X2 and X-Men: The Last Stand earned the fourth-highest opening weekends yet and Deadpool got the largest opening weekend in February.[161][162][163] The records for the first three films have since been surpassed. The next three X-Men films after X-Men: The Last Stand opened lower than their predecessor and didn't set opening records.[164] In North America, Deadpool is the highest-grossing film in the series, and it also has the highest opening weekend.[159][163] Outside North America, X-Men: Days of Future Past has the highest opening weekend and is the highest-grossing film in the series.[159] Worldwide, Deadpool is the highest-grossing film in the series and the highest-grossing R-rated film of all time.[165]
