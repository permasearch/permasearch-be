Siemens medical products include clinical information technology systems; hearing instruments; in-vitro diagnostics equipment; imaging equipment including angiography, computed tomography, fluoroscopy, magnetic resonance, mammography, molecular imaging ultrasound, and x-ray equipment; and radiation oncology and particle therapy equipment.[113] As of 2015[update], Siemens finalized the sale of its hearing-aid (hearing instruments) business to Sivantos.[114][115]
