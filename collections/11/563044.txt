Following the initial announcement of auditions for the team, over 5,000 people applied for Clue Crew posts.[52] The original Clue Crew members were Cheryl Farrell, Jimmy McGuire, Sofia Lidskog, and Sarah Whitcomb.[51] Lidskog departed the Clue Crew in 2004 to become an anchor on the high school news program Channel One News, and a search was held to replace her in early 2005.[53] The winners were Jon Cannon and Kelly Miyahara, who formally joined the crew starting in season 22, which premiered on September 12, 2005.[54] Farrell continued to record clues for episodes aired as late as October 2008,[55] and Cannon continued to appear until July 2009.[56]
