The film opened to $1.2 million in midnight takings from 1,003 theaters in the United States and Canada.[37][38] Throughout its opening day, the film's release was expanded to 3,055 theaters,[38] where it grossed a total of $8.2 million including midnight takings.[39] The end of the opening weekend saw the film take a total of $21 million—an average of $6,891 per theater[3]—finishing as the number-two-grossing film of the weekend behind the animated family film The Lorax ($70.2 million),[40] and exceeding expectations that it would finish with a gross in the mid- to high teens.[41] Project X was highly popular with males and youth; 58 percent of the opening-weekend audience for the film was male, and 67 percent of the audience was under the age of 25.[42]
