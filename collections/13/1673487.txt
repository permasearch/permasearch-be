04. "The Big Pimpin' Episode": Mona's music-video shoot for a rapper may end in disaster when he refuses to leave the dressing room until he meets Dee Dee. While Mona is pleased with the rapper's newfound enthusiasm for the shoot, she soon begins to worry that he's using Dee Dee. This episode marks the final television appearance of Merlin Santana who was killed 3 weeks after the episode aired. (10/14/2002)
