The third season, titled Natsume Yūjin-chō San (夏目友人帳 参, Natsume's Book of Friends Three) aired between July 5 to September 27, 2011. The opening theme is "Boku ni Dekiru Koto" (僕にできること, "I Can Do") by HOW MERRY MARRY and the ending theme is "Kimi no Kakera" (君ノカケラ, "Pieces of You") by Kousuke Atari featuring Emiri Miyamoto.
