Now ambitious, Wian was promoted to fry cook then manager.[10][6]:12 He learned the White Log system, its merchandising and pricing of foods, and use of a central commissary;[6]:36 Wian would later apply these to Big Boy.[11] He would also adopt White Log's pancake batter recipe.[6]:13 And at White Log he befriended fellow fry cook Bennie Washam, who would later sketch the original Big Boy mascot.[12]
