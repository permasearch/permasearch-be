The mysterious deaths of people linked to Vyapam scam triggered dark humour on various social media platforms across India. People compared it to fictional serial killer Dexter and termed it as India's version of Game of Thrones. Due to untimely and sudden death of a journalist who died soon after interviewing the family members of another dead accused, people termed it as inauspicious. An online perception was created that any sort of link to the scam could bring trouble or even mysterious death.[96]
