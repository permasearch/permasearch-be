In February 2012, Variety announced that Jessica Chastain and Joel Edgerton had been signed to star in The Disappearance of Eleanor Rigby, with screenwriter Ned Benson set to direct the two films.[14] Edgerton was later replaced with Scottish actor James McAvoy in May 2012.[15] William Hurt was also named to be starring in both films, but in an unspecified role.[16]
