Ashley is a fictional character from the American Pie series of teen comedy films. She is the love interest of Lube throughout the film. Lube tells her how he feels about her, giving a speech on how he guarantees sexual satisfaction, but she still turns him down. She goes on the school ski trip and while there she is riding up the mountain in gondola ski lift with Lube, but Nathan has sex with Dana in the lift control room and accidentally shut off the lifts' power. Lube falls out when trying to go for help. When Ashley climbs down to him, Lube tells her how he really feels about her, moving her to tears. Lube and Ashley also go back to the cabin and have sex.
