When his grandmother dies, Carlitos decides that he will not live with his aunt and uncle, he finds the two coyotes so that he can make his way across the border to his mom. Though he successfully crosses the border without being caught, the car that he hides in is towed away and he is separated from the two coyotes since they had parking violations and expired tags they did not pay for. Carlitos waits in the van at the impound lot until it is nighttime. When he gets out of the van, he unknowingly drops his container full of money.
