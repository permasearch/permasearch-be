Regarding Obama's federal district court nominations, the Senate approved 143 out of 173 as of November 2013, compared to George W. Bush's first term 170 of 179, Bill Clinton's first term 170 of 198, and George H.W. Bush's 150 of 195.[40][42] Filibusters were used on 20 Obama nominations to U.S. District Court positions,[43] but Republicans had allowed confirmation of 19 out of the 20 before the nuclear option was invoked.[44]
