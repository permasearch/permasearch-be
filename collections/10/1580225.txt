In 2006, 615,549 registered participants played Australian football in Australia.[96] Participation increased 7.84% between 2005 and 2006.[96] The Australian Sports Commission statistics show a 64% increase in the total number of participants over the 10-year period between 2001 and 2010.[97] In 2008 there were 35,000 people in 32 countries playing in structured competitions of Australian football outside of Australia.[98]
