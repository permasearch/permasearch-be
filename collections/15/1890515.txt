Marino was an analyst for CBS's Sunday pregame show The NFL Today, from 2002–2013. On February 18, 2014, it was announced that Marino, along with Shannon Sharpe were being relieved of their duties as on-air commentators on The NFL Today and were being replaced by Tony Gonzalez and Bart Scott.[39] He was formerly a studio analyst on HBO's Inside the NFL, from 2002–2007. On August 24, 2014, Marino announced he would return to the Dolphins as a special adviser.[40]
