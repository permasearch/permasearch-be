After Stan attempts to get Gracie and Freddy to say their first words many times, Gracie finally says "Dada", and Stan and the kids get excited, but know that Gracie's secret getting out will have the same effect as Stan's secret getting out. Ellen soon hears Gracie talking, makes a video, and posts it on the internet and soon she and Gracie are invited to be on live TV. When the children discover the news, they must find a way to stop Ellen before the secret is exposed. At the end of the episode, Freddy begins talking as well.
