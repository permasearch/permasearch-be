Williams is the youngest member of the board of directors at The Advancement Project, a civil rights think tank and advocacy group. He is also the executive producer of Question Bridge: Black Males, a multifaceted media project, art exhibition, student and teacher curriculum and website,[21] focused on the black male identity and the diversity within the demographic. He has written articles for CNN[22] and The Huffington Post,[23] and has been a guest on Wolf Blitzer's The Situation Room.
