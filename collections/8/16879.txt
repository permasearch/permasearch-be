The total number of Spaniards serving in the Republican forces was officially stated as 917,000; later scholarly work estimated the number as "well over 1 million men",[99] though earlier studies claimed a Republican total of 1.75m (including non-Spaniards).[100] The total number of Spaniards serving in the Nationalist units is estimated at "nearly 1 million men",[99] though earlier works claimed a total of 1.26m Nationalists (including non-Spaniards).[101]
