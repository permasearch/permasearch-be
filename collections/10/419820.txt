The Great Christmas Light Fight (originally titled Lights, Camera, Christmas![1]in development) is an American reality television competition show that premiered on December 9, 2013 on ABC and began its fifth season on December 4, 2017. New episodes air every Monday at 8 pm EST with another new episode airing immediately following for the first three weeks of December.[2]. At the end of the fifth season it was announced that that the show will be renewed for a sixth season in 2018.[3]
