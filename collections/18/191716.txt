On October 10, 2014, it was announced that Legendary TV was developing a new reboot of Lost in Space for Netflix with Dracula Untold screenwriters Matt Sazama and Burk Sharpless attached to write.[37][38] On June 29, 2016, Netflix ordered the series with 10 episodes.[39][40] The series hit Netflix on April 13, 2018. The series was renewed for a second season on May 13, 2018.
