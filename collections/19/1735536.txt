Kalamazoo has a higher crime rate than the national average. Crime is spread throughout Kalamazoo and is especially high in the inner neighborhoods such as Edison, the Northside and Vine. Crime is lower in the downtown and most of the southwest area.[80] The aggravated assault rate in 2015 was 793.3 per 100,000[81] - 3.3 times the national average. The murder rate was 12.5 per 100,000 in 2010 and 7.9 in 2014.[82][83] The 2016 Kalamazoo shootings by Jason Dalton claimed 6 lives and caused 2 serious injuries in Kalamazoo County. Between 2008-2013 a gun was used in only 61% of homicides in Kalamazoo county compared to 77% statewide.[84]
