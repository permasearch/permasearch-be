Ant-Man premiered at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood on June 29, 2015,[168] and opened the 2015 Fantasia International Film Festival on July 14, 2015, along with Miss Hokusai.[169] The film was released in France on July 14,[170] and was released in North America on July 17,[113] in 3D and IMAX 3D.[171][172] The film was released in the United States in 3,800 theaters, with the breakdown of 3,100 3D screens, 361 IMAX screens, 388 large format screens and 133 D-Box screens.[170] Ant-Man had originally been scheduled for release on November 6, 2015.[83] In September 2013, the release was moved to July 31, 2015,[93] before changing for a final time to July 17, 2015 in January 2014.[113] An unfinished version of the film was screened on June 24, 2015, at CineEurope.[173]
