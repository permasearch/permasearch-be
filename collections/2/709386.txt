"The Doctor, the Widow and the Wardrobe" was first broadcast in the United Kingdom on 25 December 2011, Christmas Day, on BBC One[22] and on the same date on BBC America in the United States,[23] and Space in Canada.[24] In Australia it was shown on 26 December on ABC1.[25] In the UK, overnight ratings showed that the special was watched by 8.9 million, coming in fourth for Christmas Day.[26] The final consolidated rating was 10.77 million viewers, ranking Doctor Who third for both Christmas Day and the entire week.[27] On BBC's online iPlayer, "The Doctor, the Widow and the Wardrobe" was watched 434,000 times by 6 January 2012.[28] The special received an Appreciation Index of 84.[29]
