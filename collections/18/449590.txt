The novel was translated into 38 different languages, including an English translation by George Goulding.[4] Like the previous novels, the English language translation is published by Quercus in the UK.[6] In March 2015, the US publisher Alfred A. Knopf announced the English-language title of the book, The Girl in the Spider's Web, and released their cover art. The first printing in the United States was for 500,000 copies.[7]
