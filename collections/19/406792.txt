Initially a supporting player, the TV character gained much popularity among viewers and critics, drawing recognition from Entertainment Weekly, CNN, MTV, and others, prompting producers to expand her role. In March 2015, PrettyLittleLiars.com named Mona the greatest Pretty Little Liars character.[3] MTV also named her one of the best characters on television in 2014.[4] During the TV series' run, producer I. Marlene King acknowledged Mona as a "beloved character."[5] By the end of the show's run, Dana Getz of Bustle.com declared that Mona had become "the most important character in the series."[6]
