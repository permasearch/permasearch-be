Fourth degree members belong to one of 3,109 assemblies, including 75 created in 2012.[41]  The first assembly in Europe was established in 2012,[41] and in 2013 a new assembly for Boston-area college councils was created at Harvard University.[145]  As of  2013[update] there were 335,132 Fourth Degree members, including 15,709 who joined the ranks of the Patriotic Degree the year before.[41]
