The band released their first new studio album in 14 years, Southern Drawl, on September 18, 2015 via BMG Chrysalis.[39] In 2016, Alabama were selected as one of 30 artists to perform on "Forever Country", a mash-up track of "Take Me Home, Country Roads", "On the Road Again", and "I Will Always Love You" that celebrates 50 years of the CMA Awards.[40]
