A potential hurdle for Detroit's MLS bid may be supporter-owned Detroit City FC, which debuted in the semi-pro National Premier Soccer League in 2012 and has since built up a fan base, many of whom fear their efforts to build a team would be destroyed by the arrival of MLS. DCFC supporters have publicly jeered Gores, Gilbert, and MLS itself at the team's games, and hijacked social media campaigns for the bid.[116]
