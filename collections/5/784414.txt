Selena Marie Gomez (/səˈliːnə ˈɡoʊmɛz/ sə-LEE-nə GOH-mez;[1] Spanish: [seˈlena ˈɣomes];[2] born July 22, 1992) is an American singer and actress. Gomez began her career starring in the children's television series Barney & Friends (2002–04). She rose to fame playing the lead role of Alex Russo in the Disney Channel television series Wizards of Waverly Place (2007–2012). Gomez has also starred in films such as Ramona and Beezus (2010), Monte Carlo (2011), Spring Breakers (2013), Rudderless (2014), The Big Short (2015), and The Fundamentals of Caring (2016). She is also known for voicing the character of Mavis in the Hotel Transylvania film franchise.
