Since 2012 McManus has performed her one-woman shows at the Edinburgh Festival [44]  This trilogy of shows "Michelle at the Musicals", "Reloaded" and "Pop Goes the Idol" were co-written by McManus with Bruce Devlin [45]  During 2013 - 2017 she has toured "Musicals" and "Reloaded" in Scotland and Northern England [46] [47]
