Lake met illustrator Rob Sussman in October 1993, on Halloween. The couple married in Las Vegas on March 26, 1994.[25] They have two sons: Milo Sebastian Sussman (b. March 22, 1997), and Owen Tyler Sussman (b. June 18, 2001), whose water birth was actually shown in The Business of Being Born, the documentary that she also produced.[26] They both filed for divorce in August, 2003,[27] and their divorce was finalized on February 25, 2005.[28]
