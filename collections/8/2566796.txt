"Long Live" was also performed on selected dates during the Red Tour, The 1989 World Tour and Taylor Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour.  On the Red Tour, an acoustic version of the song was played by Taylor while she sat on red stool with her guitar in an intimate moment with her fans. Swift played the song also in an acoustic version on the last date of the 1989 World Tour, in Melbourne, Australia. On the Taylor Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour, Swift performs this song from the piano along with a mashup of "New Year's Day".
