The album debuted at number 2 on the US Billboard 200, with first-week sales of 516,000 copies.[19] It also entered at number one on Billboard's Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.[20] The album was successful in Canada, where it reached number 2 on the charts.[21] The record was mildly successful in Europe, reaching number 4 in the United Kingdom, number 7 in Ireland, number 15 in France, number 17 in the Netherlands and number 26 in Norway. It peaked at number 11 on the New Zealand album chart.[22] Closing out the year of 2000, the album was number 5 on the Billboard Top Albums and number one on the Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.[23][24] It re-entered the charts in 2003, peaking on the UK Albums Top 75 at number 61 and on the Ireland Albums Top 75 at number 30.[25] The album was certified six times Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) on November 21, 2000.[26] It is Dr. Dre's best selling album, as his previous album, The Chronic, was certified three times platinum.[27] As of August 2015, the album has sold 7,800,000 copies in the United States.[28]
