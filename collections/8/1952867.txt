MTV Spiltvilla Season 10 started on 23 July 2017. It was hosted by Sunny Leone and Rannvijay Singh. The show consisted of 10 boys (now 11) and 10 girls who came to find true love. The theme of this season was Experiment of love, i.e. experimenting and testing whether love can be found through science or it's just the heart that finds. The show took place amidst the stunning surroundings of Jim Corbett National Park. Season 10 was the second season to appear on Voot, Viacom18's advertising-led video-on-demand platform, while the season aired. The season ended on 10 December 2017 declaring Baseer Ali & Naina Singh as the winners.
