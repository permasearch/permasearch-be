Worrywarts Hilda and Zelda grapple with freshman Sabrina's decision to fly the nest and live at college, where she has issues with new roommates Roxie, Morgan and Miles. Trying to get over her first love, Harvey, Sabrina decides to make some big changes in her life, starting with attending college and living away from home like a normal freshman-but she soon discovers a normal freshman doesn't have life so easy.
