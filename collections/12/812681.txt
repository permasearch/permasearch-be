"Glory of Love" peaked at number one on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart on August 2, 1986, remaining in that spot for two weeks. It also spent five weeks atop the U.S. adult contemporary chart.[3] Billboard ranked it as number fourteen on the Top Pop Singles of 1986,[4]:Y-21 and number four on the Top Adult Contemporary Singles of 1986.[4]:Y-27 The song achieved similar success in the UK, peaking at number three on the UK Singles Chart,[5] where it was the 26th best-selling single of 1986.
