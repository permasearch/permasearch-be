Depeche Mode were called "the most popular electronic band the world has ever known" by Q magazine,[152] "one of the greatest British pop groups of all time" by The Sunday Telegraph,[153] and "the quintessential eighties techno-pop band" by Rolling Stone[130] and AllMusic.[129] They were ranked No. 2 on Electronic Music Realm's list of The 100 Greatest Artists of Electronic Music,[154] ranked No. 158 on Acclaimed Music's list of Top 1000 Artists of All Time[155] and Q Magazine included them on their list of "50 bands that changed the world".[3]
