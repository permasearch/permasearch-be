About 1,000 people protested outside police headquarters in downtown St. Louis. The event was peaceful until a small group of protesters started smashing the windows of a Marriott hotel and other businesses. Police arrested 123 people.[19][20][21] Police also said unknown chemical substances were thrown at them and that a officer on bike was taken to the hospital for non-life threatening injuries.[9] Among those arrested included an undercover St. Louis police officer, a Getty Images photojournalist, and an Air Force lieutenant.[22]
