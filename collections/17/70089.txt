The production featured a series of "VIP guests" as the voice of The Book including Billy Boyd,[42] Phill Jupitus,[43] Rory McGrath,[43] Roger McGough,[44] Jon Culshaw,[42] Christopher Timothy,[45] Andrew Sachs,[46] John Challis,[47] Hugh Dennis,[42] John Lloyd,[42] Terry Jones[43] and Neil Gaiman.[42] The tour started on 8 June 2012 at the Theatre Royal, Glasgow and continued through the summer until 21 July when the final performance was at Playhouse Theatre, Edinburgh.[48] The production started touring again in September 2013,[49][50] but the remaining dates of the tour were cancelled due to poor ticket sales.[51]
