As of 2013[update] India census,[2] Mungaoli had a population of 40000. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Mungaoli has an average literacy rate of 69%, higher than the national average of 58.5%: male literacy is 74%, and female literacy is 58%. In Mungaoli, 20% of the population is under 2 years of age.
