What's the Worst That Could Happen? is the soundtrack to the 2001 film, What's the Worst That Could Happen?. It was released on May 29, 2001 through Interscope Records and consisted of a blend of hip hop and contemporary R&B. The soundtrack found some success on the Billboard charts, making it to 38 on the Billboard 200, 6 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums and 11 on the Top Soundtracks. The soundtrack is now out of print. Three singles of "Wooden Horse", "Music" and "Bang ta Dis".
