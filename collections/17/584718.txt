Baby Driver was co-produced by Working Title Films, Big Talk Productions and Media Rights Capital, and was distributed worldwide by Sony Pictures and by TriStar Pictures in the US. It premiered at South by Southwest on March 11, 2017, and was released theatrically on June 28, 2017.[6][7] The film received critical acclaim and has grossed $225 million worldwide against a production budget of $34 million, making it Wright's highest-grossing film as director.
