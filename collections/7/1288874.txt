Six public school districts and many private schools serve Portland. Portland Public Schools is the largest school district, operating 85 public schools.[254] David Douglas High School, in the Powellhurst neighborhood, has the largest enrollment of any public high school in the city.[255] Other high schools include Benson Polytechnic High School, Cleveland High School, Grant High School, Jefferson High School, Madison High School and Roosevelt High School. Established in 1869, Lincoln High School is the city's oldest public education institution, and is one of two of the oldest high schools west of the Mississippi River (after San Francisco's Lowell High School).[256]
