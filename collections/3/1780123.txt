Following the album's release, "By the Grace of God" entered the UK Singles Chart at number 179 on the week of November 2, 2013,[29] and peaked on the US Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart at number 10, which represents the 25 songs which failed to chart on the Billboard Hot 100.[30] It also appeared on the US Hot Digital Songs chart at number 71, where it stayed for only one week.[31] It also entered the South Korea Gaon International Chart at number 77 with sales of 2,104.[32]
