More recent campaigning by anti-nuclear groups has related to several nuclear power plants including the Enrico Fermi Nuclear Power Plant,[23][24] Indian Point Energy Center, Oyster Creek Nuclear Generating Station,[25] Pilgrim Nuclear Generating Station,[26] Salem Nuclear Power Plant,[27] and Vermont Yankee Nuclear Power Plant.[28] There have also been campaigns relating to the Y-12 Nuclear Weapons Plant,[29] the Idaho National Laboratory,[30] proposed Yucca Mountain nuclear waste repository,[31] the Hanford Site, the Nevada Test Site,[32] Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory,[33] and transportation of nuclear waste from the Los Alamos National Laboratory.[34]
