Karachi is home to a wide array of non-Urdu speaking Muslim peoples from what is now the Republic of India. The city has a sizable community of Gujarati, Marathi, Konkani-speaking refugees.[26] Karachi is also home to a several-thousand member strong community of Malabari Muslims from Kerala in South India.[130] These ethno-linguistic groups are being assimilated in the Urdu-speaking community.[131]
