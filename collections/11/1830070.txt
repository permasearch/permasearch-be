The American Film Institute ranked it 28th in its list of the top 100 comedies of all time (in their 100 Years... 100 Laughs list).[36] In 2006, Bravo ranked Ghostbusters 76 on their 100 Funniest Movies list.[37] Entertainment Weekly ranked it as the "Funniest Movie of the Past 25 Years."[38] In 2008, Empire magazine ranked the film #189 on its list of The 500 Greatest Movies of All Time.[39] In 2009, National Review magazine ranked Ghostbusters number 10 on its "25 Best Conservative Movies of the Last 25 Years" list.[40]
