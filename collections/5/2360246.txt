In October 1, 2002, Fr. Francisco M. Ungria, Jr. was appointed parish priest and school director. His responsibilities included the academic and financial running of the school. He was responsible for renovating the school, and the construction of the high school building in the vicinity. The school changed its name from Holy Child Parochial School to St. John the Baptist Catholic School, Inc. at the start of school year 2004-2005. He appointed Fr. Nolan Que, Ph.D. as Principal of the school. On September 26, 2005 the change of name was approved by the Securities and Exchange Commission.[1]
