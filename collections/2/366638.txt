In Australia, "One Last Time" debuted on the ARIA Charts on the week of March 8, 2015 at number 27 and went on to reach a peak of number 15 on April 5, 2015, becoming her fifth top 20 single there.[42] Meanwhile, in New Zealand the song debuted at number 32 and a week later it peaked at number 22. It was higher than her previous single, "Love Me Harder", which peaked at number 28, and "Best Mistake", which charted at number 29.[43]
