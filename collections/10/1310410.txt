The record for most test points for not only New Zealand, but any nation, is held by Dan Carter with 1,598 from 112 tests.[168] He surpassed Andrew Mehrtens' All Black record total of 967 points from 70 tests[169] in the All Blacks' win over England on 21 November 2009.[170] On 27 November 2010 Dan Carter scored a penalty against Wales to pass Jonny Wilkinson's previous world record of 1,178 points.[171] Carter also holds the record for points against Australia with 366.
