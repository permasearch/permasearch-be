"The Time (The Dirty Bit)" debuted on the Canadian Hot 100 at number 87 the week of November 5, 2010.[15] The song also debuted at number 40 on the Billboard Pop Songs chart the next week and has currently peaked at number 19.[16] Two weeks later, the song climbed to number one in Canada, the third time the group has achieved this, selling 31,000 downloads that week.[17] The song also peaked at number 1 for two weeks in a row in New Zealand, becoming their 6th number 1 and 12th top 10 single in the country.[18] While the song debuted at number ten in Australia, it reached number one in its second week.[19] On the singles chart for Ireland and the Netherlands the song has peaked at number two.[20]
