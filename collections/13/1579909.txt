Around 47.8 percent of Hamiltonians affiliate with Christianity and 8.3 percent affiliate with non-Christian religions, while 41.6 percent are irreligious and 4.2 percent objected to answering. Roman Catholicism is the largest Christian denomination with 12.0 percent affiliating, followed by Anglicanism (9.9 percent) and Presbyterianism (6.3 percent). Hinduism (2.9 percent), Islam (1.9 percent) and Buddhism (1.6 percent) are the largest non-Christian religions.[56]
