Break the Spell debuted at number eight on the Billboard Top 200 on November 28, 2011 with 129,000 copies sold.[17] As of January 2012 the album has sold 407,000 copies in the United States.[18] On January 26, "Crawling Back to You" debuted at number twenty-four on the Japan Hot 100. "Renegade", "Crawling Back to You" and "Outta My Head" are the three singles released from the album. The fourth single "Start of Something Good" was released on September 4, 2012.
