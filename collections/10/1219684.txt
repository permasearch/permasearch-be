In modern days this material is also used for False Ceiling. In this, the powder form is converted in a sheet form and the sheet is then attached to the basic ceiling with the help of fasteners. It is done in various designs containing various combinations of lights and colors. The common use of this plaster can be seen in the construction of houses. After the construction, direct painting is not possible, here comes the use of plaster. The walls are painted with the plaster which is nothing but calcium carbonate. After drying the calcium carbonate plaster turns white and then the wall is ready to be painted
