In response to the growth of craft breweries globally, some commentators,[6]:122[12] German brewers,[13] and even German politicians[14] have argued that the Reinheitsgebot has slowed Germany's adoption of beer trends popular in the rest of the world, such as Belgian lambics and American craft styles. In late 2015, Bavarian brewers allegedly voted in favor of a revision to the beer laws to allow other natural ingredients.[10]
