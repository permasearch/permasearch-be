The average height is around 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in)[citation needed]. 13 of the 14 F1 seasons in the 21st century have been won by a drivers under 1.80 m tall, including Fernando Alonso 1.71 m (5 ft 7 1⁄2 in), Lewis Hamilton, Michael Schumacher 1.74 m (5 ft 8 1⁄2 in), Kimi Räikkönen 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in) and Sebastian Vettel 1.76 m (5 ft 9 1⁄2 in) with the exception of Jenson Button (1.82 m (5 ft 11 1⁄2 in)).
