Episode Summary: 17-year-old Aubrey Wolters (previously Akerill) is from Prescott Valley, Arizona, and is pregnant by her boyfriend, Brandon Akerill. Prior to the birth, Akerill and Wolters got married. The episode focuses on the contention between Akerill and Wolters because he wants her to be a housewife and stay home to raise the baby while he works, which is not the path that Wolters wants for herself. On February 6, 2010, Wolters gave birth to their son, Austin Carter Akerill. Following the birth, the episode focuses on Wolters' and Akerill's job choices and them trying to move out and into their own home.
