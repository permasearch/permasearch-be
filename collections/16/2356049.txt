In 2011, she starred as Olivia White, the lead role in the Disney Channel Original Movie Lemonade Mouth,[34][35] watched by 5.7 million viewers on its premiere night.[36] Mendler performed numerous songs for the film's soundtrack, which was released on April 12, 2011 by Walt Disney Records.
