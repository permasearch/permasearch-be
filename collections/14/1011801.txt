In early 2013, it was confirmed that Storms was in a relationship with her former General Hospital co-star Brandon Barash. In August 2013, the couple confirmed to People that they had secretly wed in June, and were expecting their first child, a girl, in January 2014.[24] On January 7, 2014, Barash confirmed on his official Twitter account that Storms had given birth to a baby girl, Harper Rose Barash.[25] On April 6, 2016, Soap Opera Digest revealed that Storms and Barash had filed for divorce after more than two and a half years of marriage citing irreconcilable differences but they will raise their daughter together as they are splitting amicably.[26]
