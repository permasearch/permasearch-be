In some markets, the lower-end LT was equipped with the single carbureted four-cylinder 2T engine displacing 1,600cc, while the ST came with a twin Solex downdraft carburetor 2T-B engine. The 2T-G that powered the high-end GT / GTV model was a DOHC 1600cc engine equipped with twin Mikuni-Solex Carburetors.
