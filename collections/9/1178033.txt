According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 7.42 square miles (19.22 km2), of which 7.21 square miles (18.67 km2) is land and 0.21 square miles (0.54 km2) is water.[2] Waterways in addition to the Willamette River include Arrowhead Creek, Meridian Creek, Basalt Creek, Seely Ditch, Boeckman Creek, and Coffee Lake Creek.[31] The Boeckman and Coffee Lake creeks account for 85% of the runoff in Wilsonville.[32] Coffee Lake Creek is on the west side of the city and includes Coffee Lake and the Coffee Lake Wetlands. The foothills of the Chehalem Mountains lie to the west of Wilsonville, with most land within the city on level ground.
