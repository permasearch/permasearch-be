On June 12, 2014, G-Unit continued their release of new music with a remix of American singer Trey Songz' "Ordinary".[25] On June 16, 2014, G-Unit released a song titled "They Talked About Jesus".[26] The song, which only features verses from Banks, Buck and Yayo, heavily samples "Tearz", by East Coast hip hop group Wu-Tang Clan.[27][28][29] On June 17, 2014, G-Unit premiered the music video for "Nah I'm Talkin' Bout".[30][31] On June 25, 2014, 50 Cent announced they would be releasing a mixtape prior to the album.[32][33][34] On June 27, 2014, G-Unit released a remix to Jeremih's "Don't Tell 'Em".[35] On August 25, 2014, G-Unit surprised fans with an extended play (EP) titled The Beauty of Independence, which was released at midnight, via digital retailers.
