Chief Keef was born Keith Cozart in Chicago, Illinois when his mother was 16, and is named after his deceased uncle.[9] He lived at the Parkway Garden Homes, located in the Washington Park neighborhood on the city's South Side.[10][11] Keef is estranged from his biological father;[12] when he was a minor, Keef's legal guardian was his grandmother, whom he lived with during his residence in Chicago.[13] He began rapping at a very young age, regularly listed as 5, using his mother's karaoke machine and blank tapes to record his music.[14] During his childhood, Keef attended Dulles Elementary School and the Banner School, a therapeutic day school.[14] Keef dropped out of Dyett High School at age 15.[15][16]
