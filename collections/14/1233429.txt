Comparing other English-speaking developed countries,[3] the incarceration rate of Canada is 106 per 100,000 (as of 2014),[37] England and Wales is 148 per 100,000 (as of 2015),[38] and Australia is 151 per 100,000 (as of 2015).[39] Comparing other developed countries, the rate of Spain is 141 per 100,000 (as of 2015),[40] Greece is 120 per 100,000 (as of 2013),[41] Norway is 71 per 100,000 (as of 2015),[42] Netherlands is 75 per 100,000 (as of 2013),[43] and Japan is 49 per 100,000 (as of 2014).[44]
