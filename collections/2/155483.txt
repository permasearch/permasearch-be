Your Name was released in 4K UHD Blu-ray, Blu-ray, and DVD on July 26 in Japan by Toho Pictures. The release was offered in Regular, Special, and Collector's editions.[22] FUNimation announced on July 1 at Anime Expo 2017 that the film would be released on Blu-ray and DVD by the end of 2017 but didn't specify a date.[23] At Otakon 2017, they announced they are releasing the movie in both Standard and Limited Edition Blu-Ray + DVD Combo Packs on November 7, 2017.[24][25]
