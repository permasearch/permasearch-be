Archer married William Davis in 1969. They had one son, Thomas William "Tommy" Davis, born on August 18, 1972. The couple divorced in 1977.[6] She married Terry Jastrow in 1979.[6] They have one son together, Jeffrey Tucker Jastrow, born on October 18, 1984.[7][6] She was originally a Christian Scientist,[8] but her husband and she have been members of the Church of Scientology since 1975.[9] Between 1982 and 1986, she was a spokeswoman for Applied Scholastics, the literacy training organization sponsored by the Church of Scientology.[1][10] Her son Tommy was the head of the Church of Scientology's Celebrity Centre International in Los Angeles.[11][12]
