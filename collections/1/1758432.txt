On February 11, 2013, CW president Mark Pedowitz confirmed an early renewal of Supernatural for its ninth season,[29] with the season premiere debuting on Tuesday, October 8, 2013.[1] It was later confirmed, on February 25, 2013, that Misha Collins would return as a regular cast member, after being a special guest member for the previous two seasons. It was also announced that he would direct an episode in season 9.[30]
