I Give My First Love to You was first released in Japan on 24 October 2009.[4] It was then released in Taiwan under the name of (Chinese: 獻給你，我的初戀) on 10 April 2010,[1] and in South Korea on 5 August 2010 (as Korean: 내 첫사랑을 너에게 바친다)[11] Within South-East Asia, the film was released in Thailand on 24 December 2009 as (Thai: เพราะหัวใจบอกรักได้ครั้งเดียว)[12] and in Singapore on 20 October 2010.[13]
