Arash Labaf was born in Tehran, Iran. At age ten, he and his family moved to Sweden, where he still resides. In an interview with BBC Persian Television, he has mentioned that his mother and father are from the Iranian cities of Shiraz and Isfahan, respectively.[1] Also in one of his interviews, he stated his great grandfather is Iranian Azerbaijani.[2][3][4][5][6] In an interview with Arash emphasized: "My great-grandfather was an Azeri so I always feel my Azerbaijani roots."[7] In another interview Arash stated he is originally Azerbaijani.[8]
