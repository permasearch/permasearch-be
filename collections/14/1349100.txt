The character has appeared in most other media adaptations of the comics, often playing a prominent role. She has been adapted into live action five times: Jeff Donnell in 1977, Irene Tedrow in the weekly Amazing Spider-Man television series, Rosemary Harris in the Sam Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy, Sally Field in Marc Webb's The Amazing Spider-Man duology, and Marisa Tomei in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
