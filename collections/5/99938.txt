Flair's son David is a semi-retired professional wrestler. Flair's younger son Reid, who signed a developmental contract with WWE near the end of 2007,[170] was an accomplished high school wrestler and made several appearances on WCW television along with his sister Ashley and half-sister Megan. In 2004, Flair became a grandfather at the age of 55, when his older daughter, Megan Fliehr Ketzner, gave birth to her first child, a daughter named Morgan Lee Ketzner on May 9.[171][172] On May 17, 2012, it was reported that Flair's daughter Ashley had signed with WWE[173] adopting the ring name, Charlotte (which was later changed to include the Flair surname).[174] On March 29, 2013, Reid died from an accidental overdose of heroin, Xanax and a muscle relaxer.[175]
