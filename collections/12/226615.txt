The third season has a 100% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes, with an average score of 8.2 out of 10 based on 13 reviews. The website's consensus reads, "Still evolving in its third season, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. further hits its stride with a blend of thrills, humor, and heart."[233] The fourth season also has a 94% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes, with an average score of 7.2 out of 10 based on 16 reviews, and the consensus reads "Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. explores darker territory in its fourth season with the thrilling introduction of Ghost Rider, setting up an action-packed new chapter of Marvel's edgier mythologies."[237]
