The final season of George Lopez originally aired Wednesdays at 8:00-8:30 pm (EST) and Tuesdays at 8:30-9:00 pm (EST) on ABC. Season 6 of George Lopez was released on DVD by Warner Home Video. Masiela Lusha leaves the main cast although she was in the opening credits in the season 6 premiere. Aimee Garcia joins the main cast.
