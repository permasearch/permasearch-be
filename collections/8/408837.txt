In Storybrooke, he is Marco, the town's handyman. August Booth offers to work as Marco's assistant to which he agrees. His memories as Geppetto are restored when the curse is broken. Marco later takes in August, who is reverted to Pinocchio by Mother Superior, as his son again.[25] He is sent back to the Enchanted Forest after Regina undoes the curse and is brought back when Snow White casts a new one.
