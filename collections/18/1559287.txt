Axis: Bold as Love, opens with the track "EXP", which innovatively utilized microphonic and harmonic feedback.[25] It also showcased an experimental stereo panning effect in which sounds emanating from Hendrix's guitar move through the stereo image, revolving around the listener.[26] The piece reflected his growing interest in science fiction and outer space.[27] Author Keith Shadwick described the track as "some of the wildest music Hendrix ever released."[7]
