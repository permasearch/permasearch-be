On Rotten Tomatoes, the film has a rating of 87% based on 186 reviews and an average rating of 7.6/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "Nostalgic in the best sense, Everybody Wants Some!! finds Richard Linklater ambling through the past with a talented cast, a sweetly meandering story, and a killer classic rock soundtrack."[21] On Metacritic, the film has a score of 83 out of 100 based on 50 reviews, indicating "universal acclaim".[22]
