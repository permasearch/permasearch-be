Later Ingrid learns of a social media influencer named Taylor Sloane (Elizabeth Olsen) while reading a magazine article. Beguiled by her seemingly perfect life, Ingrid comments on one of her photographs and receives a slight response from Sloane. With an inheritance of over $60,000 from her mother, Ingrid decides to move to Los Angeles to become friends with Taylor. Upon arrival, she rents a house in Venice from Dan Pinto (O'Shea Jackson Jr.), an aspiring screenwriter and Batman enthusiast. She visits Taylor's regular hangouts and restaurants, and gets a makeover in her style.
