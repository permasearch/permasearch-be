Salman bin Abdulaziz has married three times.[61] As of 2017[update], he had 13 children.[62] His first wife was Sultana bint Turki Al Sudairi,[63] who died at the age of 71 in late July 2011.[64][65] She was a first cousin of Salman, being a daughter of Salman's maternal uncle Turki bin Ahmad Al Sudairi,[64] who was a former governor of the Asir Province.[66] Sultana Al Sudairi supported the Prince Fahd bin Salman Charitable Society for the Care of Kidney Patients and other charitable organizations in the country.[67] His children from this marriage are Prince Fahd, Prince Sultan, Prince Ahmed, Prince Abdulaziz, Prince Faisal and Princess Hassa (born 1974).[67]
