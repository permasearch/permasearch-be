T-Mobile Polska serves over thirteen million customers,[17] and owns licenses for 900, 1800, 2100 MHz bands which are used for GSM, WCDMA and LTE.[18] Formerly Era, rebranding took place[19] on 5 June 2011. T-Mobile Poland with Orange Polska have consolidated their infrastructure and used this opportunity to roll out 3G coverage using 900 MHz band.[20]
