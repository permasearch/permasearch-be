Kyla celebrated 15 years in the music industry with an album to be released in September and major concert on November 20, 2015.[41][42][43] Kyla launched her first YouTube channel in February 2016, featuring collaborations with other singers (known as "Kylaborations").[44] Kyla performs at ASAP with fellow R&B artists Jay R, KZ Tandingan, Jason Dy and Daryl Ong as part of the group ASAP Soul Sessions. The group disbanded in late 2017, after being replaced by ASAP Jambayan.
