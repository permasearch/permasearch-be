The Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man has been the recipient of many awards from the amusement park industry. It won Amusement Today's Golden Ticket Award for Best Dark Ride for twelve consecutive years from 1999 through to 2010. It has since placed second in that category, after fellow Islands of Adventure attraction, Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey, took the top spot in 2011.[43] In 2000, the ride won a Thea Award from the Themed Entertainment Association for outstanding themed entertainment and experience design.[44][45] It has also won numerous public-voted Theme Park Insider Awards and Screamscape Ultimate Awards.[46][47]
