As of 7 December 2017[update], Ballerina had grossed approximately US$106 million worldwide.[3] It opened in France on 14 December 2016 with over half a million admissions,[23] earning €2.2 million (~US$2.4 million) over the course of the weekend.[24][25] Its French gross eventually reached $14.5 million.[26] In Canada, it earned more than C$1.5 million during the first 13 days of its theatrical run, $1.1 million of which was made in Quebec.[27] It ultimately grossed a total of $3.6 million (~US$2.8 million) in Canada.[28][29] It was released on 25 August 2017 in the United States, earning $4.7 million over its opening weekend.[30] As of 7 December 2017[update] it had grossed $21.9 million in the US,[31] the film's highest-grossing territory.[26]
