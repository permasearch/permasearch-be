Altogether, approximately 8,300 new companies have been founded in Vienna every year since 2004.[77] The majority of these companies are operating in fields of industry-oriented services, wholesale trade as well as information and communications technologies and new media.[78] Vienna makes effort to establish itself as a start-up hub. Since 2012, the city hosts the annual Pioneers Festival, the largest start-up event in Central Europe with 2,500 international participants taking place at Hofburg Palace. Tech Cocktail, an online portal for the start-up scene, has ranked Vienna sixth among the top ten start-up cities worldwide.[79][80][81]
