The Super Kings have a 67.44% win record at this venue, which is often referred to as "Fortress Chepauk"[63] and "Lions' den".[64] In the 2011 season, the Super Kings won all their home games (8 matches) including the final against Royal Challengers Bangalore. The Super Kings thus became the first team to win all their home games in a season and also the first team to win the tournament at home.
