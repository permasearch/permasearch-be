The first store opened in 1989 on Patpong Road in Bangkok. The franchise in Thailand is the CP ALL Public Company Limited, which in turn grants franchises to operators. In January 2018 there were 10,300 stores in Thailand.[58] There are 10,268[59] 7-Eleven stores in Thailand as of January 2018, with approximately 50% located in Bangkok. Thailand has the 2nd largest number of 7-Eleven stores after Japan.[60]
