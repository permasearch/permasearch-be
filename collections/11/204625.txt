The team won their final four games of the 2012–13 season to earn the seventh seed of the playoffs. The Red Wings' 3–0 victory over the Dallas Stars on April 27, 2013, clinched a playoff appearance; had they lost the game in regulation. Detroit's win preserved their streak of 22 consecutive playoff appearances. As the seventh seed in the 2013 playoffs, the Red Wings faced the second-seeded Anaheim Ducks.[210] They survived a fierce battle that included four overtime games, winning the series 4–3 after a 3–2 Game 7 victory in Anaheim.[211] The next round pitted the Red Wings against the top-seeded Chicago Blackhawks. Despite jumping out to a 3-1 series lead, the Red Wings would ultimately lose to the eventual Cup champions in seven games.[212][213]
