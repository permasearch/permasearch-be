At the 90th Academy Awards, Janney won for Best Supporting Actress, while the film also earned nominations for Best Actress for Robbie and Best Editing. It earned three nominations at the 75th Golden Globe Awards, winning Best Supporting Actress for Janney,[5] and at the 24th Screen Actors Guild Awards, it won Best Actress in a Supporting Role for Janney and was nominated for Best Actress in a Leading Role (Robbie).[6] At the 71st British Academy Film Awards, the film earned five nominations, winning Best Actress in a Supporting Role for Janney.[7]
