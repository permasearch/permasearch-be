On 12 November, in New York, Harrison, Starr and McCartney came together for the last time.[184] Three weeks later, on 29 November 2001, Harrison died at a friend's home in Los Angeles, aged 58.[185] He was cremated at Hollywood Forever Cemetery and his funeral was held at the Self-Realization Fellowship Lake Shrine in Pacific Palisades, California.[186] His ashes were then scattered in the Ganges and Yamuna rivers near Varanasi, India, by his close family in a private ceremony according to Hindu tradition.[187] He left almost £100 million in his will.[188]
