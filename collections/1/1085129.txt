The Twins have been affiliated with the Rookie League Elizabethton Twins of the Appalachian League since 1974, making it the longest-running active affiliation in the organization among teams not owned by the Twins.[2] It is also the longest affiliation in the team's history. Their newest affiliate is the Chattanooga Lookouts of the Southern League which became the Twins' Double-A club in 2015.[2]
