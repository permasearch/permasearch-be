The first season of the American political drama series Designated Survivor began airing on September 21, 2016 on ABC. The series was ordered straight to series by ABC in December 2015,[1] with a formal announcement of 13 episodes in May 2016.[2][3] Eight days after the premiere, on September 29, 2016, ABC gave the series a full season order.[4] The series is produced by ABC Studios and The Mark Gordon Company, and is filmed in Toronto, Canada.
