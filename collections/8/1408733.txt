There has been a steep increase in the use of the English language over the years.[19] Singapore is currently the most proficient English-speaking country in Asia.[20] Then Education Minister, Ng Eng Hen, noted a rising number of Singaporeans using English as their home language in December 2009. Of children enrolled in primary school in 2009, 60% of the Chinese and Indian pupils and 35% of the Malay pupils spoke predominantly English at home.[21]
