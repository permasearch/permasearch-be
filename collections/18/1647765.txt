Evangelista is an activist for HIV/AIDS research and awareness, as well as for breast cancer research and awareness.[10][12][25] She was one of the icons in the Viva Glam campaign for the Mac AIDS Fund.[6][20][29] In October 2013, she was the host of amfAR's Inaugural Inspiration Gala in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.[178] Evangelista also supports the Elton John AIDS Foundation and is an advocate for LGBT concerns.[179]
