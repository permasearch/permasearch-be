The song peaked at number 6 on the Billboard's Mainstream Rock Tracks chart in August 2001,[10] and No. 12 on the Alternative Songs charts in September 2001.[11] The song was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America on June 24, 2008, indicating half a million digital copies sold.[12] The song re-entered the chart in April 2016 and reached No. 6 on the Hard Rock Digital Song and No. 30 on the Rock Digital Songs charts.[13][14] It has sold 1,751,000 digital copies in the US as of April 2016.[15] On September 22, 2001, "Bodies" peaked at number 19 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart. It also reached number 34 on the UK Singles Chart.[16]
