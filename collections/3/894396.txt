In 2008, she starred in the home invasion horror film The Strangers with Scott Speedman, a film about a young couple who are terrorized one night by three masked assailants in their remote country house.[64][65] Although the film garnered a mixed reception among critics,[66] it was a major box office success, earning over $80 million over its considerably smaller $9 million budget.[31] In an interview with Entertainment Weekly, she noted that The Strangers was the most challenging role of her career. "It was as far as I could push myself in every way: physically, emotionally, mentally."[22]
