The aircraft was a leased Boeing 737-201 Adv. aircraft,[9] operated by Mexican charter airline Global Air (Aerolíneas Damojh, S.A. de C.V.), on a scheduled domestic flight from Havana to Holguín on behalf of Cubana de Aviación.[10] The aircraft was manufactured in July 1979, was about 39 years old and had served with multiple airlines before it was acquired by Global Air in 2013.[11]
