The facelifted international version of the E170 Corolla was unveiled on 24 March 2016.[26] It features sleeker LED headlamps with new LED light guides, thinner upper grille with a chrome garnish, a full width lower grille that houses the foglamps. At the rear the chrome strip is thinner and the tail lamps adopt new LED clusters.[27] The facelifted E170 Corolla Altis was launched in Thailand on 21 November 2016,[28] in Malaysia on 9 December 2016,[16] in Indonesia on 16 January 2017,[29] in Singapore in July 2017, and in Pakistan in August 2017.
