Based on 166 reviews collected by review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, Friday the 13th has a 25% approval rating from critics with an average score of 4.2 out of 10. The consensus reads: "Though technically well-constructed, Friday the 13th is a series rehash that features little to distinguish it from its predecessors."[54] Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, calculated an average score of 34 based on 29 reviews.[55] CinemaScore polls reported that average grade cinemagoers gave the film a "B-" on a scale of A+ to F.[56] Exit polls showed that 51% of the audience was male and 59% were at least 25 years old.[57]
