The series premiered in the Republic of Ireland on RTÉ One on 12 May 2013,[1] and in the United Kingdom on BBC Two on 13 May 2013.[2] The second series began in the Republic of Ireland on 9 November and in the UK on 13 November 2014. The third series premiered on RTÉ One on 25 September[3] and on BBC Two on 29 September 2016.[4]
