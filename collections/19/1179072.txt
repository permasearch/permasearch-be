In March 2007, BSkyB and Chrysalis Group announced plans to launch a dedicated Sky News Radio station on the proposed bid by Channel 4's 4 Digital Group for the second DAB multiplex in the United Kingdom.[29] However, two months later Chrysalis sold its radio stations to Global Radio,[30] and in October 2007 following a business review Global's chief executive announced that it would be withdrawing from the joint venture.[31]
