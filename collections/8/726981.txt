On review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, the first season has received a 97% approval rating, with an average rating of 8.25/10, based on 43 reviews.[51] The website's critical consensus reads, "Black Lightning doesn't reinvent superhero TV, but it does give the genre a necessary jolt with real-world plots, scary new villains, and a star-making performance from Cress Williams."[51] Metacritic, which uses a weighted average, assigned a score of 79 out of 100 based on 24 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[52]
