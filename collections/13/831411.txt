Sarajevo, the capital city of Bosnia and Herzegovina has a large Irish expatriate community.[70][71] The community established the Sarajevo Irish Festival in 2015, which is held for three days around and including St. Patrick's Day. The festival organizes an annual a parade, hosts Irish theatre companies, screens Irish films and organizes concerts of Irish folk musicians.
