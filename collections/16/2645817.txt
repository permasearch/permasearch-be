Team Challenge 2: For this team challenge, this is going to be a special three-way team challenge in a university campus, serving poutine with the last two teams going into the pressure test. David and Sabrina will be team captains of the Red and Blue Team respectively. David chooses Line as the third team captain. David chooses Andrew, Christopher, Michael for the Red Team. Sabrina chooses Jon, Cody, Kwasi for the Blue Team. Line chooses Tammy, Kristen, Jennifer, for the Green Team. Each team will create a dish from different proteins selected by David, with pork for Red, beef for Blue and chicken for Green. Line's Green Team emerges victorious by a margin of seven votes.
