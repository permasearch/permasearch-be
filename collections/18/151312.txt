Justice League is the second most expensive film ever made with an estimated production budget of $300 million. The film premiered in Beijing on October 26, 2017, and was released in the United States in 2D, 3D and IMAX on November 17, 2017. Justice League grossed $657 million worldwide, against an estimated break-even point of up to $750 million.[4] It received mixed reviews from critics; the action sequences, visual effects, and performances (particularly Gadot and Miller) were praised, while the plot, writing, pacing, villain, and overuse of CGI were criticized. The film's tone was met with a polarized reception, with some appreciating the lighter tone compared to previous DC films, while others found it inconsistent.[5][6]
