"She Doesn't Mind" is the second single from the Jamaican recording artist Sean Paul's fifth studio album Tomahawk Technique. It was written by Sean Paul, Shellback and Benny Blanco and was produced by Shellback and Benny Blanco. It was released on 29 September 2011 on NRJ & Skyrock (French radios), and to iTunes on 31 October.[1] and was released to U.S. mainstream radio on 24 January 2012.[2] Like its predecessor, "Got 2 Luv U", which featured American singer Alexis Jordan, it debuted at number-one in Switzerland. Internationally, the song dominated the charts in Austria and Israel, and peaked within the top ten of the charts in France, Norway, Romania and the United Kingdom.
