She starred as Leslie Spencer in the indie crime-drama Meskada, which premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival and had no domestic distributor as of April 2010.[43] However, the film received a limited release on December 3, 2010 in the United States. Nichols was cast in the 3-D sword and sorcery movie Conan the Barbarian in March 2010, as the character of Tamara, a master of martial arts, a priestess, and also the potential Conan's love interest.[44][45] The film, released on August 19, 2011,[46] was a reimagining of the 1982 film of the same name which starred Arnold Schwarzenegger. It received mostly poor reviews,[47] and was a box office bomb.[48]
