Tyler Perry's Madea's Witness Protection is a 2012 American comedy film directed, written and produced by Tyler Perry.[3] It is the fourteenth film by Tyler Perry and the seventh in the Madea franchise. It is the fourth Tyler Perry film not to be adapted from a play, alongside The Family That Preys, Daddy's Little Girls and Good Deeds, as well as the first Madea film not to be adapted from a play.
