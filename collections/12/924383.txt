Most Filipinos are Malayo-Polynesian, a major family within the Austronesian language family. Other ethnolinguistic nations form a minority in the Philippine population. These include those of Spanish, Latin American, Japanese, Chinese particularly the Hokkien and Cantonese, Indians particularly the Punjabi, Tamil and Kerala, and other ethnolinguistic nations from other countries. Mixed-race or mixed-ethnicity individuals are known as mestizo.
