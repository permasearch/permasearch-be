On May 17, 2014, she performed at the Sony Centre for the Performing Arts in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.[42] There, she performed a medley of songs from "In Between Words," songs she performed on The Voice, and her two singles "Tumbling Down and "Everything Reminds Me of You" from her album "Count On My Love."
