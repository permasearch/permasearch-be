In a very special 25th Anniversary episode, the Super Ninja Steel Rangers team up with Legendary Rangers from the past 2 decades in an epic battle to save the world when a new monster known as Lord Draven tries to steal ranger powers to create his own army of evil clones. Leading the charge is none other than Time Force Red Ranger Wesley Collins, Ranger Series Operator Gemma the Silver Ranger, Blue Dino Charge Ranger Koda, and Tommy Oliver himself.
