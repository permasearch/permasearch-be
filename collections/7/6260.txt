The single debuted at number 50 on the UK Singles Chart a week prior to its physical release, due to download sales.[45] The following week, "I Think We're Alone Now" peaked at number 4 on the Christmas chart, being beaten by Leona Lewis' "A Moment Like This".[46] The song slipped to number 7 in its second week.[47] It spent a total of seven weeks in the top 75.[48] The song also peaked at number 11 on the Irish Singles Chart and spent six weeks in Ireland's top 50.[49] As their 17th best selling single it has sold a total of 85,000 copies.
