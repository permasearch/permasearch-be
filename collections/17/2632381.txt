On the October 22 episode of SmackDown, Ziggler started a feud with the debuting Tyler Breeze, who aligned with Summer Rae and attacked Ziggler.[219] Ziggler entered a tournament for the vacant WWE World Heavyweight Championship, defeating The Miz in the first round match[220] before being eliminated by Dean Ambrose.[221] Ziggler and Breeze continued their feud,[222] which culminated in a match between the two at Survivor Series, which Ziggler lost.[223] Ziggler would then enter a feud with Kevin Owens with the pair trading victories throughout the rest of December and beginning of 2016.
