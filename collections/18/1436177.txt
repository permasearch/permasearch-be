Smurfit gained fame for her role as Orla O'Connell in the BBC television series Ballykissangel from 1998–99. She was in the 2003 movie Bulletproof Monk. From 2003–09, she played the lead role of DCI Roisin Connor in Trial & Retribution.[3] She also guest starred in the BBC Radio 4 series Baldi. In 2011, Smurfit appeared in the Agatha Christie's Marple television episode "The Mirror Cracked From Side to Side".
