In October 1996, it was announced that filming would take place over five days in December at New Zealand's Fiordland National Park,[47][48] where the film's opening sequence was to be shot.[49][50] Scenes involving Hammond's residence were shot during the final week of filming, at Mayfield Senior School in Pasadena, California.[51][40] Also shot in Pasadena was a scene in which Vaughn's character emerges from a lake.[45]
