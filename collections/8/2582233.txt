Describing her singing style, Chauhan said; "I have no particular style; instead I only go with the music and the lyrics, and feel free to express my emotions through singing".[191] Though her singing style has a strong western influences, she has recorded some semi-classical songs in her career, including songs from Rang Rasiya.[192] She has not performed any classical songs or a ghazal and has expressed her interest on releasing a ghazal album.[192][193] Though known for her high pitch renditions, she personally prefers soft and soothing songs.[194] Believing that singing all kinds of songs makes her grow as a musician she revealed that her "weakness remains for soft melodies".[195]  "Even on live shows, I first sing my fast numbers to get the audience into the groove. But once they are happy with their favorite numbers, I switch on to slow scores".[195]
