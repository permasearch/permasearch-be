The Russian Government has earmarked a budget of around 20 billion dollars[53] which was later slashed to 10 billion dollars for the preparations of the World Cup of which half is spent on transport infrastructure.[54] A special emphasis was made on airports, with many of those in the host cities were renovated and modernised. In Samara, new tram lines were laid.[55] The city of Saransk got two new hotels, Mercure Saransk Centre and Four Points by Sheraton Saransk as well as few other smaller accommodation facilities.[56]
