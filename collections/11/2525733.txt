Elk Grove Village is home to the largest consolidated business park in North America.[citation needed] The business park comprises 5.9 square miles (3,776 acres) and continues to expand.  There are nearly 3,600 businesses operating in the business park, employing nearly 100,000 persons.  In 2018, the business park became the sponsor of the Bahamas Bowl college football game, renaming it the Makers Wanted Bahamas Bowl after the business park's advertising slogan "Makers Wanted".
