Official record temperatures range from −4 °F (−20 °C) on January 21, 1985 to 109 °F (43 °C) on June 29, 2012;[39] the record cold daily maximum is 18 °F (−8 °C) on January 30, 1966, while, conversely, the record warm daily minimum is 79 °F (26 °C) as recently as August 11, 2007.[39] Temperatures rarely fall below 10 °F (−12 °C), having last occurred January 7, 2014.[39] The average window for freezing temperatures is November 5 to March 24, allowing a growing season of 225 days.[39]
