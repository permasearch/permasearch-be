Winfrey was called "arguably the world's most powerful woman" by CNN and Time.com,[109] "arguably the most influential woman in the world" by The American Spectator,[110] "one of the 100 people who most influenced the 20th Century" and "one of the most influential people" from 2004 to 2011 by TIME. Winfrey is the only person in the world to have appeared in the latter list on ten occasions.[111]
