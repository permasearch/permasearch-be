By 2013 the Indian Banking Industry employed 1,175,149 employees and had a total of 109,811 branches in India and 171 branches abroad and manages an aggregate deposit of ₹67,504.54 billion (US$1.0 trillion or €860 billion) and bank credit of ₹52,604.59 billion (US$780 billion or €670 billion). The net profit of the banks operating in India was ₹1,027.51 billion (US$15 billion or €13 billion) against a turnover of ₹9,148.59 billion (US$140 billion or €120 billion) for the financial year 2012–13.[23]
