Following the release of Unapologetic, "Loveeeeeee Song" entered the US Billboard Hot 100 at number 100. It ascended to 88 its second week and to 63 in its third week. [19] It additionally debuted at number 14 on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs due to strong digital downloads.[20] The song has also peaked at 4 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay chart.[21] In the United Kingdom, "Loveeeeeee Song" charted on the R&B Chart at number 17.[3]
