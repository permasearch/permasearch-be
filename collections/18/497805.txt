UNC Chapel Hill's admissions process is "most selective" according to U.S. News & World Report.[76] State law requires that the percentage of in-state students per freshman class be at least 82%, making out-of-state admissions particularly selective. For freshmen entering Fall 2016, 9,386 were accepted out of 35,875 applicants, a 26% acceptance rate, and 4,228 enrolled.[77] Women constituted 60% of the incoming class; men 40%.[77]
