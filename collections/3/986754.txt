On 7 December 2009, it was announced that the show was planned to return in 2010 with a speculated run of 20 episodes.[3] During the 2010 premiere on Wednesday 14 April, it was indicated that the 20-episode run would be split into two groups, with a break between them, and the second group was broadcast on Saturday nights from 16 October 2010 with the season finale on 27 November 2010. The show was not renewed for a new season in 2011 and since then there has been no announcement of it ever coming back on air.
