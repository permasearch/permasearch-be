Pranati was born to Colonel Prem Prakash and Sadhana Rai.[2] She hails from Patna in Bihar.[3] Her father serves in the army. Due to her father's job, her family relocated to a number of places in India,[4] including Srinagar, Port Blair, Bathinda, Mhow, Delhi, Thiruvananthapuram, Shillong, Delhi, Dehradun and Patna.[5] She studied fashion communication from NIFT, Mumbai. She loves yoga, painting, designing and travelling. She is currently based in Mumbai.[5]
