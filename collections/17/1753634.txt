To become a detective in Victoria Police, members must be confirmed senior constables, with at least four years' service, have completed the Field Investigation Course and obtained sufficient experience to sustain the application and interviewing process. Upon obtaining a position at a CIU/Squad, members must then complete the training package (preliminary portfolio of work and course attendance) at the School of Investigation (Detective Training School) to confirm their position as a qualified detective. Detective positions within Victoria Police are highly sought after and awarded, generally, to only the best police applying.
