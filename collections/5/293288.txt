Critical views of the film were very positive. Based on 220 reviews collected by the film review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes, 87% of critics gave 28 Days Later a positive review with an average score of 7.4/10 and the site's consensus reads: "Kinetically directed by Danny Boyle, 28 Days Later is both a terrifying zombie movie and a sharp political allegory."[10] On Metacritic, the film received a rating of 73 (out of 100) based on 39 reviews.[11]
