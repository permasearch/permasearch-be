Rotten Tomatoes gives the film an 80% critic rating based on 35 reviews.[8] Roger Ebert of the Chicago Sun-Times praised the film, calling it worthwhile and entertaining.[9] Janet Maslin of The New York Times stated that Some Kind of Wonderful is the "much-improved, recycled version of the Pretty in Pink story".[10] Richard Schickel of Time, however, criticized the film for being unrealistic.[11] Masterson's performance was singled out for praise by several critics.[9][10][12][13]
