His second wife was Bai Indira Kanwar, the daughter of Maharajah Ajit Singh. [43] She married Farrukhsiyar on 27 September 1715, during the fourth year of his reign, and they had no children. After Farrukhsiyar's deposition and death she was removed from the imperial harem on 16 July 1719, given back to her father with her property and returned to Jodhpur.[44]
