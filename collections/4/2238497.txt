The song was featured in a slow dancing scene in A Quiet Place. Cassandra Wilson did a cover of the song on her 1995 album New Moon Daughter. Pearl Jam has performed this song several times during their 2005 tour. Ben Gibbard of Death Cab for Cutie performed the song in 2007.[1] The Mavericks covered "Harvest Moon" on their 2016 album All Night Live: Volume 1.[8]
