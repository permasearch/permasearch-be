Category 7 was the top rated network miniseries in 2006.[2] The first part of the miniseries came in number 16 among the top 25 network programs aired in the week of October 31-November 6, and was the second most watched program for that Sunday with 14.7 million viewers.[11][12] The second part of the film was also the second most watched program for its timeslot on November 13, with 13.85 million viewers.[13]
