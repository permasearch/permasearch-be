On December 15, 2011, FedEx announced an order for 27 Boeing 767-300Fs to replace its MD-10 aircraft. The 767s will be delivered between 2014 and 2018, with three aircraft being delivered in 2014, and with six aircraft delivered each year between 2015 and 2018. The airline also delayed deliveries of eleven Boeing 777 freighters currently on order, but converted two options to firm orders.[46][47]
