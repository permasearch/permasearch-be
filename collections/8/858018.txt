"Kaun Tujhe" (English: Who Would)[3] is a Hindi song from the soundtrack of the 2016 Hindi Film, M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story. The song was penned by Manoj Muntashir, composed by Amaal Mallik, and sung by Palak Muchhal.[2]The song is featured in the film in a scene involving Sushant Singh Rajput and Disha Patani.[4][5][6].
