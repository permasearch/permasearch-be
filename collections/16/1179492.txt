The car is offered with either a new six speed manual, four speed automatic transmission for 1.6 litre engine cars. The continuously variable transmission (CVT) with paddle shifters comes with a 2.0 litre I4 engine that makes 148 hp (110 kW; 150 PS) at 6,000 rpm. On standard six speed manual transmission the same engine produces 150 hp (112 kW; 152 PS) at 6,200 rpm.
