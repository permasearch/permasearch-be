Star Wars: The Old Republic has received generally positive reviews from critics, with a score of 85 on Metacritic[77] and an 84% on GameRankings.[76] G4TV gave a review of 5/5 and praised the game for "Top notch music and voice acting" and "hundreds of hours of content."[79] PC Gamer gave a 93/100, praising the story, voice acting, and the amount of content available.[86] Gamespy gave a review of 4/5, praising the story lines and companion system but criticising the "standard kill and fetch" quests.[88] GameSpot gave the game 8.0/10, saying "[The Old Republic] isn't the next step in online role-playing games. Instead, it's a highly entertaining refinement of what has come before it."[89] GamesRadar gave the game 8/10 calling it "an extremely satisfying experience that sets the stage for a bright future".[82] The game has received a 9.0/10 "Amazing" rating from IGN.com.[90]
