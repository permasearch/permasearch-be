In 1993 the faired and bagged FXRT Sport Glide was discontinued and the FXRS Low Rider was displaced by the FXDL Dyna Low Rider,[10] although the FXR, FXLR, FXRS-Conv Convertible and the FXRS-SP Low Rider Sport continued to be offered.[citation needed]  The FXDWG Dyna Wide Glide was introduced in the same year. The Low Rider Sport was discontinued in 1994,[10] and the Low Rider was discontinued after the 2009 model year.[citation needed]
