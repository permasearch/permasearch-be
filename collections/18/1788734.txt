Military bases usually provide housing for military personnel, a post office and dining facilities (Restaurants). They may also provide support facilities such as fast food restaurants, a gas station, chapels, schools, a hospital or clinic (dental or health clinics), and retail stores (usually a Supermarket and a Department Store). Family, Morale, Welfare and Recreation (FMWR) provides facilities such as fitness centers, libraries, athletic fields, basketball hoops, child development centers, automotive workshops, hobby/arts and crafts centers, bowling centers, and community centers.
