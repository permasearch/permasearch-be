The second season of The Great Australian Bake Off premiered on 13 October 2015.[1] The series sees twelve home bakers take part in a bake-off to test their baking skills as they battle to be crowned The Great Australian Bake Off's best amateur baker. Unlike the first season, season two airs on pay television channel LifeStyle Food and is produced by FremantleMedia Australia.[2] The season is hosted by Claire Hooper and Mel Buttle and is judged by Maggie Beer and Matt Moran.[2][3] The season will consist of 10 episodes.[1]
