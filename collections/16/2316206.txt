In 2001, he was awarded the Centenary Medal for "service to the Clean Up Australia Campaign and the Clean up the World Campaign".[10] In 2006 Kiernan received a Lifetime Achievement Award from the National Trust of Australia (NSW).[11] The 2007 Reader's Digest Most Trusted Poll voted Clean Up Australia as the country's most trusted environmental charity and chairman Ian Kiernan AO as the fourth "most trustworthy" Australian.[12] In 2008 the same poll Kiernan was ranked number three.[13]
