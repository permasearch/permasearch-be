At the federal level, Davenport is in Iowa's 2nd congressional district. As of  2013[update], it is represented by Democrat Dave Loebsack. The two Senators are Republicans Chuck Grassley and  Joni Ernst. At the state level, Davenport is represented by the 41st, 42nd, and 43rd Iowa Senate districts and in the Iowa House of Representatives by the 81st, 84th, 85th, and 86th districts.
