Aquaman and Mera spent the night together in the lighthouse of Amnesty Bay, but in the morning, Mera finds Arthur on the dock, looking at the sea and wondering why he was resurrected. Mera comforts her husband and invites him to swim with her, but Arthur is hesitant, seeing only his Black Lantern form reflected at him in the water.[24] While cleaning up an oil spill, she and Aquaman are attacked by soldiers from Mera's homeworld and leading them is Siren, who bears a striking resemblance to Mera herself. As Mera pulls her husband away from them, she reveals that she was sent to kill him,[25] proceeding to confess her real origins to him.
