"Best Thing I Never Had" received a nomination for Record of the Year at the 2011 Soul Train Music Awards.[63][64] At the 43rd NAACP Image Award presented on February 17, 2012, "Best Thing I Never Had" received a nomination in the category for Outstanding Song.[65] At the 2012 ASCAP Rhythm & Soul Awards, "Best Thing I Never Had" won in the category for Award-Winning R&B/Hip-Hop Songs.[66] The Guardian's critic Dan Hancox placed the song at number four on his list of the 10 best songs of 2011.[67] On The Village Voice's 2011 year-end Pazz & Jop singles list, "Best Thing I Never Had" was ranked at number 604.[68]
