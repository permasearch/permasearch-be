In 2009, Sania Mirza became engaged to childhood friend Sohrab Mirza, a millionaire businessman. However the wedding was called off shortly after.[51][52][53][54] On 12 April 2010, she married Pakistani cricketer Shoaib Malik in a traditional Hyderabadi Muslim wedding ceremony at the Taj Krishna Hotel in Hyderabad, India followed by a Pakistani wedding customs for a mahr of ₹ 6.1 million (US$137,500).[55][56] Their Walima ceremony was held in Lahore, Pakistan.[citation needed]
