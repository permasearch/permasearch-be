In present day, these cars are highly sought after.  As of 2008, auctions on eBay and at Barrett-Jackson have brought bids of over $375,000.  In 2013 a black 1969 Ford Mustang Boss 429 Fastback (unrestored) sold for $417,000 at Mecum Auctions in Kissimmee, Florida. In 2016 a restored black 1969 Boss 429 sold for $500,000 at a Barrett-Jackson auction in Palm Beach, Florida.
