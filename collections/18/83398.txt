Merchandise featuring the character proved popular. David Betancourt of The Washington Post called Phasma's role in the film "little more than a shiny new prop", viewing her as a victim of the film's need to introduce all its new characters, yet noted the popularity and rarity of the merchandise surrounding the character.[31] Captain Phasma's figure was the bestselling of The Force Awakens' action figures on Amazon.co.uk.[3]
