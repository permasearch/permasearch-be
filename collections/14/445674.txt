The series had its world premiere at the 2015 Comic-Con in July.[69] In late August, free screenings of the pilot along with two other new Fox pilots were held in select cities.[70] In the United States, it premiered on Fox on September 22, 2015.[37][71] In Canada, Scream Queens airs simultaneously with the U.S.[citation needed] On October 26, 2015, the series premiered E4 in the United Kingdom and Ireland. After the season two hiatus the show on return to E4 moved to a late night timeslot airing Thursdays at Midnight.[72] In Australia, the series debuted on Ten on September 23, 2015, before moving to its sister channel, Eleven, with episode two on the same day.[73]
