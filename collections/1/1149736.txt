Foods historically consumed by the Chumash include several marine species, such as black abalone,[29] the Pacific littleneck clam,[29] red abalone,[29] the bent-nosed clam,[29] ostrea lurida oysters,[29] Pacific littleneck clams,[29] angular unicorn snails,[29] and the butternut clam.[30] They also made flour from the dried fruits of the laurel sumac.[31]
