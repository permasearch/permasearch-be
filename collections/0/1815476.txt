Robert Lee "Robbie" Jones III (born September 25, 1977)[1] is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Quentin Fields in One Tree Hill. In 2009, Jones starred in the film Hurricane Season with Forest Whitaker. In 2010, he starred in the series Hellcats, in which he portrayed Lewis Flynn. His most recent film role was in Tyler Perry's Temptation: Confessions of a Marriage Counselor alongside Jurnee Smollett-Bell and Lance Gross.
