India chose to bat first, but lost Virender Sehwag and Robin Uthappa to Mashrafe Mortaza in his opening spell. Mortaza, described by the Cricinfo journalist as "at the heart of the impressive performance",[6] took two wickets in the opening spell and returned to remove the two final wickets, though he also bowled six no-balls in a spell of four for 38.
