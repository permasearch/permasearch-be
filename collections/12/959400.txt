Among his living descendants are his great-granddaughter, the actress Helena Bonham Carter (b. 1966),[543] and two great-grandsons, Dominic Asquith, British High Commissioner to India since March 2016,[544] and Raymond Asquith, 3rd Earl of Oxford and Asquith, who inherited Asquith's earldom.[545] Another leading British actress, Anna Chancellor (b. 1965), is also a descendant, being Asquith's great-great-granddaughter on her mother's side.[546]
