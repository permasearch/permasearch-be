Bryant was signed by the Atlanta Falcons on December 1, 2009, to replace veteran Jason Elam.[33] He appeared in five games of the Falcons' 9–7 season. In Week 14, against the New Orleans Saints, he had his best scoring performance of the season. In the 26–23 loss, he converted all three field goal attempts and both extra point attempts.[34] On the season, he converted all 10 extra point attempts and 7-of-10 field goal attempts.[35]
