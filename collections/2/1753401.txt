The music video for "Don't Say You Love Me" was directed by P. R. Brown. The video was previewed through a picture posted on Fifth Harmony's social media accounts showing the silluetes of the group standing in front of a window in a dark room on March 29, 2017.[16] The video was released as a farewell before they go into an indefinide hiatus following their final live performance at the Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in Florida on May 11, 2018.[17]
