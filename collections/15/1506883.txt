To promote the album, West released free songs through his GOOD Fridays series and four singles: the Billboard hits "Power", "Monster", and "Runaway", and the international hit "All of the Lights". It was also promoted with a short film set to the record's music, Runaway. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and eventually sold 1.3 million copies in the United States.[1] A widespread critical success, it was named the best album of 2010 by many publications and has since appeared on several lists ranking the greatest albums of all time.
