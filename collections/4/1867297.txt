The Grudge received mixed reviews. On Rotten Tomatoes, the film has a rating of 39%, based on 158 reviews (with 61 out of 158 film reviews counted fresh), with an average rating of 5.1/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "There's some creepy imagery to be found, but not much in the way of logic or truly jarring scares". On Metacritic, which uses an average of critics' reviews, the film has a score of 49 out of 100, based on 32 reviews, indicating "mixed or average reviews".[5] Classic FM's film critic Simon Bates deemed it the scariest film he had ever seen.[citation needed]
