Campanella was born in Lewistown, Pennsylvania to Philip and Mary O. Campanella, immigrants from Sicily. He appeared in such television shows as Combat, Decoy, The Eleventh Hour, The Doctors (1963), The Fugitive, Mission: Impossible, Marcus Welby, M.D., Gunsmoke, The Big Valley, Alias Smith and Jones, The Untouchables, Police Story, The Road West, The Invaders, The Mary Tyler Moore Show, Rockford Files, The Golden Girls, and Mama's Family.
