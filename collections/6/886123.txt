The Union Theatre in London revived Chess in 2013, following the story line of the 2008 Chess in Concert.[48] East West Players, the longest running professional theater of color in the US,[citation needed] staged a revival of the UK version of Chess with a multi-cultural cast in 2013 at the David Henry Hwang Theatre in Los Angeles, California, directed by Tim Dang. The production garnered an excellent review from the Los Angeles Times.[49]
