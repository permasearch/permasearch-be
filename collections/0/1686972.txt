On January 26, 2008, the band's cover of "What Hurts the Most" entered the Billboard Hot 100 at number ninety-five.[29] The song rose to number eighty in the following week.[30] On March 22, 2008, the cover peaked at number fifty-two.[31] The song serves as Cascada's lowest-charting single on the Hot 100 as well as their lowest-charting lead single.[31] Aside from charting on the Hot 100, the song managed to peak at the top of Billboard Hot Dance Club Songs and at number twenty-eight on the US Billboard Pop Songs.[32] Despite its low peak on the Hot 100, the song managed to sell more than 500,000 downloads, earning a Gold certification by the Recording Industry Association of America in October 2009.[33] In Canada, the song managed to peak at number fifty-four and lasted on the chart for eleven weeks.[34]
