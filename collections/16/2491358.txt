The Zheng family of Xingyang 滎陽鄭氏 claim descent from the Zhou dynasty Kings via the rulers of the State of Zheng. The Marquis of Xingyang rank was created for Zheng Xi.[92] The Xingyang Zheng spawned Zheng Daozhao and Zheng Xi.[93] Zheng Wanjun was a member of the Xingyang Zheng.[94] The Xingyang Zheng spawned Zheng Yuzhong (Zheng Qiao).[95] The Xingyang Zheng spawned Zheng Jiong.[96] The Zheng of Xingyang may have been miswritten in the records as the Zheng of Rongyang.[97][98]
