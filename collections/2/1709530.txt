Duke Ellington incorporated treatments of Hiawatha[46] and Minnehaha[47] in his jazz suite The Beautiful Indians (1946–7). Other popular songs have included "Hiawatha’s Melody of Love", by George W. Meyer, with words by Alfred Bryan and Artie Mehlinger (1908),[48] and Al Bowlly's "Hiawatha’s Lullaby" (1933).
