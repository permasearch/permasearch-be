The sixth season of the American television series Bones premiered on September 23, 2010, and concluded on May 19, 2011, on Fox.[1] The show maintained its previous time slot, airing on Thursdays at 8:00 pm ET and then moved to Thursdays at 9:00 pm ET in 2011. The season consisted of 23 episodes, and introduced a spin-off series The Finder in the 19th episode which has the same name.
