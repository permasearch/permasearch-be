An avid car collector,[95] Selänne has entered the World Rally Championship (WRC) twice, finishing the Rally Finland 33rd overall in 1997 and 24th in 1998.[96][97] He raced under the pseudonym "Teukka Salama" ("Teddy Flash" in English).[18] In 1999 Selänne was involved in a crash while practicing for a rally race in his native Finland, an incident that was national news in the country.[98] Due to Finnish laws that set fines based on an offender's salary,[76] Selänne received a $50,000 fine for the incident.[99]
