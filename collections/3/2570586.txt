Jet2.com operates flights to 57 destinations across Europe, with a notable focus on Spain, the Mediterranean Sea and France. It also offers services to North America through its connections to Newark Liberty International Airport. Its primary hub is Leeds Bradford Airport, and Jet2.com operates an additional six domestic operating bases across the United Kingdom alongside an overseas hub at Alicante Airport, Spain, one of its most popular destinations.[1] Two more UK bases, at Birmingham Airport and London Stansted Airport, were opened in March 2017.[2][3]
