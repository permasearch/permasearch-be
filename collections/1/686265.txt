In July 2006, Kohli was selected in the India Under-19 squad on its tour of England. He averaged 105 in the three-match ODI series against England Under-19s[34] and 49 in the three-match Test series.[35] India Under-19 went on to win both the series. At the conclusion of the tour, the India Under-19 coach Lalchand Rajput was impressed with Kohli and said, "Kohli showed strong technical skills against both pace and spin".[36] In September, the India Under-19 team toured Pakistan. Kohli averaged 58 in the Test series[37] and 41.66 in the ODI series against Pakistan Under-19s.[38]
