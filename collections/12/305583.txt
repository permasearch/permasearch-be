Balfe was born in Dublin, Ireland,[35] and grew up in the village of Tydavnet, near Monaghan, in a family of seven; her father is a retired Garda Sergeant.[1] She is currently[when?] based in Glasgow,[36] having previously lived in Los Angeles when she started acting professionally, and in New York City, Paris, London, Milan, Hamburg, and Tokyo while she worked as a model.[16][37][38] In addition to English, she is a fluent speaker of the Irish language and is conversational in French.[39]
