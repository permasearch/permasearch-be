On 29 September 1777, Cook sailed for Papeto'ai Bay on Mo'orea. Cook met Mahine in an act of friendship on 3 Oct, though he was an enemy of Tu. When a goat kid was stolen on 6 Oct, Cook in a rampage, ordered the burning of houses and canoes until it was returned.
