Lost & Found Music Studios is a Canadian musical-drama children's series created by Frank van Keeken and aired by Family Channel in Canada, CBBC in the United Kingdom and Ireland, and Netflix in all other territories globally. It is a music-centered spin-off of the dance-focused The Next Step, using a similar mockumentary format and sharing characters and locations; the main characters of Lost & Found initially appeared in The Next Step's third season. The series premiered December 11, 2015 on Family Channel;[1] its second season is available in Netflix territories and is currently airing in Canada and the UK.
