In March 2018 Netflix announced yet another collaboration with Rainbow S.r.l: The creation of a series, in which the plot and characters are played by real actresses and actors. According to Eric Barmack, VP of International Originals of Netflix, the series will be aimed at young adults and will offer deeper plotlines, while the main plot will be the same, as of the animated Winx Club series: the fairies will learn to train their magical abilities, while living together in a magical boarding school". The series will be produced in Italy and is planned for release in 2019/2020.[75]
