Command & Conquer, released on August 31, 1995, was the first game in the series and is widely considered as the title which originally defined and popularized the real-time strategy genre.[6][7][10][11][12][13] Command & Conquer introduced the warring factions of the Global Defense Initiative (GDI) and the Brotherhood of Nod. Command & Conquer was well received and was widely praised by critics: "Command & Conquer is one of the finest, most brilliantly-designed computer games I have ever seen" said GameSpot reviewer Chris Hudak.[14] Command & Conquer has attained 94% as an aggregate score from Metacritic[15] with the less well received Covert Operations expansion pack obtaining an aggregate score of 72% after its 1996 release.[16]
