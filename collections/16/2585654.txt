After the season, the Baseball Writers' Association of America named Posey the NL MVP.[73] He won the Silver Slugger Award for the catcher position.[74] He won the NL Hank Aaron Award, and Miguel Cabrera of the Tigers won the American League (AL) award, marking the first time in history that World Series opponents won the award in the same year.[75] He was named the NL Comeback Player of the Year,[76] and he received the Willie Mac Award from the Giants' organization.[77]
