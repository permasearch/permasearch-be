Principal photography on the film began on June 25, 2015, in Vancouver,[40] and Squamish, British Columbia, after several delays caused by multiple script rejections.[41][42][43][44] Additional filming locations were Seoul, South Korea, and Dubai, United Arab Emirates.[45][46] Principal filming ended on October 15, 2015. In March 2016, production underwent reshoots, with Aghdashloo added to the cast.[39]
