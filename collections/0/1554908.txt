On November 27, 2016, Herman was hired as the new head coach at Texas. He signed a five-year contract with a base salary of $5 million per year.[11] Texas would go 7–6 in Herman's first season at the helm, which culminated in a 33–16 win over Missouri in the Texas Bowl. 2017 marked the Longhorns' first winning season since 2013 and first bowl win since 2012 under then-head coach Mack Brown.
