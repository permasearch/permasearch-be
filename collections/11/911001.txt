The game was first announced as Jurassic Park: Project Genesis on February 19, 2002, with the game's release set for the fourth quarter of 2002.[5] On May 22, 2002, footage was shown at the Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3), where the game was titled as Jurassic Park: The Game.[6][7] Blue Tongue Entertainment pushed for that to be the final title.[8] In June 2002, the game's release date was set for December 2002.[9]
