In her sleep, Elise sees a vision of Dalton and the Lipstick Demon looming over him which frightens her awake. At that moment, her phone rings. The woman on the line (Lorraine) explains that Elise helped her son (Josh) a long time ago and that now her grandson, Dalton, is experiencing the same symptoms. Elise agrees to help, saying she will meet him in the morning, setting up the events of the first film.
