On August 28, 2017, Missouri Attorney General Josh Hawley announced a settlement with Charter.  The lawsuit alleged Charter Spectrum violated state and federal No-Call and telemarketing laws.  Under the settlement, Charter agreed to a monetary payment of $225,000 and to terms designed to prevent and quickly remedy any potential future violations. Unwanted telemarketing calls annually rank highest on the list of complaints received by the Attorney General's Office.[citation needed]
