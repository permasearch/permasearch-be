On January 12, 2018, the video debuted on Swift's Vevo channel. It is Swift's seventh video directed by Joseph Kahn. The video shows Swift partying in various locales - with Future on a yacht in Miami, Florida, Sheeran at a nightclub in Tokyo, Japan, and various friends on a double decker bus in London, England. In a Miami scene Future was seen driving a Lamborghini Aventador worth $500,000. In one of the London scenes Taylor is seen sitting on a bar playing Snake on a handheld game console, rides a motorcycle through Tokyo while wearing a snakeskin suit, and sips drinks through a snake straw, all references to her "snake" reputation.[12] The video gained 14.4 million views within 24 hours of upload on YouTube. As of May 2018, the video has over 160 million views.
