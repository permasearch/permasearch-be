Davis was born in Lakewood, California.[1] He played baseball at Deer Valley High School in Glendale, Arizona, where he was twice named an All-State player.[1] During his senior season at Deer Valley, Davis hit .592 with ten home runs and 50 RBIs to lead the team to a state title.[2] Davis attended California State University, Fullerton, where he played college baseball for the Cal State Fullerton Titans.[1] Davis excelled for Cal State as an outfielder, designated hitter, and pinch hitter.[2] In his junior season, Davis hit .328 with 16 home runs and 58 RBIs.[3]
