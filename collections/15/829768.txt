On September 28, 2010, Warner Archive released Thundarr the Barbarian: The Complete Series to DVD in region 1[13] as part of their Hanna–Barbera Classics Collection.  This is a Manufacture-on-Demand (MOD) release, available exclusively through Warner's online store and Amazon.com.[14] The DVD set is branded as part of the Hanna-Barbera Classics Collection[15] as Thundarr and the other 1978–91 Ruby-Spears programs were sold to Turner Broadcasting in 1991 alongside Hanna-Barbera by Great American Broadcasting.[citation needed]
