In 2006, the F22C1 was also introduced to the Japanese market, with an output of (178 kW (239 hp) and 221 N⋅m (163 lbf⋅ft)). The F20C continued in all other markets. The 2006 model introduced a drive by wire throttle, an electronic stability control system, new wheels, and one new exterior color, Laguna Blue Pearl. Interior changes included revised seats and additional stereo speakers integrated into the headrests.
