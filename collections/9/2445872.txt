Much like nearby Lubbock, Littlefield has a mild, semiarid climate. On average, Littlefield receives 18 inches (460 mm) of precipitation per year.[11] Summers in Littlefield are hot, with high temperatures in the 90s °F (32 - 37°C) and dropping into the 60s °F (15 - 20&°C) at nights. The highest recorded temperature was 112°F (44°C) in 1994.[11] Winter days in Littlefield are typically sunny and relatively mild in the mid 50s °F (13°C), but nights are cold with temperatures dipping to the mid 20s °F (-4°C). The lowest recorded temperature was -6°F (-21°C) in 1979.[11]
