In 2010 the municipality of San Cristóbal de las Casas had a total population of 185,917,[22] and the city of 158,027.[22] Other than the city of San Cristóbal de las Casas, the municipality had 110 localities, the largest of which (with 2010 populations in parentheses) were: San Antonio del Monte (2,196), La Candelaria (1,955), Mitzitón (1,293), and San José Yashitinín (1,109), all classified as rural.[22]
