Single study prevalence findings for other disorders include 63% in anorexia nervosa,[59] 56% in bulimia,[59] 45%[19] to 50%[60] in major depressive disorder, 34% in panic disorder,[61] 28% of social phobics,[61] and 50% in substance abusers.[62] Alexithymia is also exhibited by a large proportion of individuals with acquired brain injuries such as stroke or traumatic brain injury.[63][64][65]
