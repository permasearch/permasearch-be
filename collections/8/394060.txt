In February 2015, the Ministry of Tourism proposed extending the facility to citizens of China, United Kingdom, Spain, France, Italy and Malaysia.[47] Subsequently, the Minister of Finance announced that the facility would be extended, in stages, to citizens of 150 countries.[48] In March 2015 it was announced that 53 nations were shortlisted for the second round of expansion of the system based on the number of tourist arrivals in the previous years.[49]
