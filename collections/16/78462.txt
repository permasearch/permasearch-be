On the death of Newman in 2008, control of the company and foundation passed to CEO Robert Forrester, a person Newman felt shared his vision for the company. Since taking over, Forrester has expanded and diversified the company.[10] However, Newman's daughter, Susan, has alleged that Forrester has taken her family "hostage" and pushed them off the board of the Newman's Own Foundation, the body setup to distribute company profits to charitable causes. He says the company and foundation are continued to be operated in accordance with the expressed wishes of her late father.[11] Forrester's salary increased from $185,000 to $295,000 from 2010 to 2013.[12]
