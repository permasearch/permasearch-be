After the 10.11.4 update, many users started reporting that their MacBooks were freezing, requiring a hard reboot.[34] This issue mostly affects Early 2015 MacBook Pro computers, although many others have reported freezes in other models.[35] Several users created videos in YouTube which showed the freezes.[36][37][38] Soon after this, Apple released the 10.11.5 update, which contained stability improvements. Apple later acknowledged these problems, recommending their users to update to the last point release.[39]
