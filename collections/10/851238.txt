The series premiered with the episode "Dos pájaros de un tiro" and "Venganza fallida" being watched by 1.03 and 1.12 million viewers respectively.[19] The first ten episodes aired weekdays at 9pm/8c. Due to low ratings Telemundo moved Guerra de ídolos to Saturday's only at the same time of 9pm/8c. La querida del Centauro took over its original time slot.[20][21]
