Peddapalli-Nizamabad Railway Line is a single broad gauge railway line in the Indian state of Telangana. It connects Nizamabad city (Hyderabad Division)of Nizamabad District to Peddapalli town (Secunderabad Division) of Peddapalli District under South Central Railway zone. It reduces the distance between North Telangana Cities Nizamabad-Karimnagar-Warangal (262 km) when compared to present running route of Nizamabad-Secunderabad-Warangal(303 km).
