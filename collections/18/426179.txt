On March 19, 2014, images leaked for the next game, titled or code-named "Unity", showing a new assassin in Paris.[12] On March 21, Ubisoft confirmed the game's existence, having been in development for more than three years, by releasing pre-alpha game footage.[17] The game features up to four player co-op, a first for the series.[18] It was released in North America for PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Windows on November 11, 2014 and in Europe and Australasia on November 13, 2014.[14]
