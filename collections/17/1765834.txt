San Jose del Monte is a component suburban city in the province of Bulacan in Central Luzon, Philippines.[4] It is bordered by the cities of Caloocan and Quezon of Metro Manila in the south, by the town of Rodriguez, Rizal in the east, the towns of Santa Maria and Marilao in the west and Norzagaray in the north. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 574,089 people,[3] making it the largest local government unit within Bulacan province and the 18th most populated city in the Philippines.
