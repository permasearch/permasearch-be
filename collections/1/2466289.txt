Many departments and agencies of the U.S. Government have facilities in Wichita. The Wichita U.S. Courthouse, located downtown, is one of the three courthouses of the U.S. District Court for the District of Kansas.[145] The U.S. Air Force operates McConnell Air Force Base immediately southeast of the city.[146] The campus of the Robert J. Dole Department of Veterans Affairs Medical and Regional Office Center is located on U.S. 54 in east Wichita.[147] Other agencies, including the Federal Bureau of Investigation,[148] Food and Drug Administration,[149] and Internal Revenue Service[150] among others, have offices in locations around the city.
