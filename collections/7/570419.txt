Chelsea then faced Tromsø in the second round.[5] They lost the first leg by 2–3 which took place at Alfheim Stadion, with Gianluca Vialli registering a brace.[6] In the home leg, Chelsea thrashed Tromsø with a 7–1 victory, during which Vialli scored a hat-trick; the other goals were scored by Dan Petrescu, who contributed with a double, Gianfranco Zola and Frank Leboeuf to ensure an amassed score of 9–4.[7]
