Beckham has several eponymous video games, including Go! Go! Beckham! Adventure on Soccer Island, a platform game for the Game Boy Advance, and David Beckham Soccer, a football game for a number of different platforms,[241][242] and he was brand ambassador for exercise video game EA Sports Active 2.[243] Beckham featured in EA Sports' FIFA video game series; he was on the cover for the International edition of FIFA 98.[244]
