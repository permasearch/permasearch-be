Wataru Takagi (高木 渉, Takagi Wataru), Harry Wilder as dubbed by Funimation, is an officer who works for Meguire. He has feelings for his fellow officer Miwako Sato,[ch. 171] and gets jealous whenever he hears she is interested in someone.[ch. 208-210, 232-233] He sometimes deflects any praises and motivational compliments she might give.[ch. 232] Though he has an appearance of ineffectual nervousness, he is quick to assess a situation and take immediate and decisive action when necessary, and has a knack for getting out of situations out of sheer luck.[ch. 269] His Japanese voice actor is of the same name, while his English voice actor is Doug Burks.[12][28]
