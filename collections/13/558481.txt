Released as a single in 1980, the song reached No. 5 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart, where it remained for three weeks.[3] It also peaked at number 4 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart, No. 21 on the Adult Contemporary chart and No. 45 on the UK Singles Chart. It was the group's highest charting hit since "Kiss and Say Goodbye", which was released in 1976.
