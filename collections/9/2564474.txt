According to a 2010 estimate, the median income for a household in the city is $93,553 [23] and the median per capita income is $40,432.[24] Males have a median income of $63,135 versus $38,119 for females. 2.3% of the population is below the poverty line. Out of the total population, 0.8% of those under the age of 18 and 8.2% of those 65 and older are living below the poverty line.[25]
