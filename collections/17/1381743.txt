Welling was born on April 26, 1977, in the lower Hudson Valley, New York. He is of German descent.[2][3] His family moved frequently, making stops in Wisconsin, Delaware, and Michigan.[3] Welling attended Okemos High School in Okemos, Michigan, where he started acting in plays, but then switched into sports. Welling played baseball and soccer, but his favorite sport is basketball.[3] Welling is one of four children with two older sisters, Rebecca and Jamie.[4] His younger brother, Mark Welling, is also an actor.[2]
