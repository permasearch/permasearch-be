Line of Duty is a BBC police procedural television series created by Jed Mercurio that premiered on 26 June 2012. It is the most popular drama series broadcast on BBC Two in the multichannel era[1] and is a winner of the Royal Television Society Award for Best Drama Series.[2] Line of Duty was included in a list of the Top 50 BBC Two shows of all-time,[3] in a list of the 80 best BBC shows of all time[4] and was the highest ranked current series in a list of the best cop shows of all time.[5]
