According to Scientific Elite: Nobel Laureates in the United States by Harriet Zuckerman, a review of American Nobel prizes awarded between 1901 and 1972, 72% of American Nobel Prize laureates identified a Protestant background.[150] Overall, 84.2% of all the Nobel Prizes awarded to Americans in Chemistry,[150] 60% in Medicine,[150] and 58.6% in Physics[150] between 1901 and 1972 were won by Protestants.
