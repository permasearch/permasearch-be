P>The Texas Chainsaw Massacre was released in North America on October 17, 2003, in 3,018 theaters.[29] It grossed $10,620,000 on its opening day and concluded its North America opening weekend with $28,094,014, ranking No. 1 at the box office.[30] The film opened in various other countries and grossed $26,500,000, while the North American gross stands at $80,571,655, bringing the worldwide gross to $107,071,655.[31] The film's budget was $9.5 million.[32]
