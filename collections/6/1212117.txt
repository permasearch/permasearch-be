Pompeo’s early work included adverts and small independent films.[10] She made her television debut in 1996 by guest-starring in the NBC legal police drama, Law & Order.[10] She made her feature film debut in Coming Soon (1999), playing a small part. She made her second appearance in Law & Order in 2000, and then guest-starred on Strangers with Candy, Strong Medicine and Friends.[10] Pompeo then moved to Los Angeles in 2001. She participated in the movie Mambo Café[11] with Thalía.[12]
