"I'm Not Supposed to Love You Anymore" is a song co-written by Skip Ewing and Donny Kees, and performed by American country music singer Bryan White. It was released in February 1996 as the first single from his album Between Now and Forever. The song peaked at number 4 on the U.S. country chart[1] and at number 2 on the Canadian country chart. It also peaked at number 1 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart.
