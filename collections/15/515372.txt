Cumulative sales of light-duty plug-in electric vehicles in Europe passed the 500,000 unit milestone in May 2016.[270] Norway passed the 100,000th registered plug-in unit milestone in April 2016,[271] France passed the same milestone in September 2016,[272] and the Netherlands in November 2016.[273] The UK achieved the 100,000 unit mark in March 2017.[209][274]
