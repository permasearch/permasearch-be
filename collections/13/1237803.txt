For Programmes that are six years in length, entry is based on secondary school qualifications[57]. Programmes that are four years in length require previous university degrees[58]. The Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland and the University of Limerick were the first medical institutions to offer Graduate Entry Medicine of four years in duration in the Ireland[59]. This is now also offered in University College Dublin and University College Cork. The National University of Ireland, Galway also launched a graduate entry programme in 2010.
