In the United Kingdom, Sky Atlantic premiered the series on 16 October 2013 at 9 pm, and continued weekly until 18 December.[48] The debut was seen by an average of 362,000 overnight viewers, considered strong ratings for the channel.[49] With consolidated ratings taken into account, the first episode went up to 803,000 viewers on Sky Atlantic, with an extra 90,000 viewing from its catch-up channel, Sky Atlantic +1.[50] However, the second episode dropped a third of its overnight audience, leaving it with 236,000 viewers.[51] The finale was seen by 267,000 overnight viewers.[52]
