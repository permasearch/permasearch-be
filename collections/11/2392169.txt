Sapporo has five breweries in Japan.[7] The first, Sendai Brewery, was opened in 1971 in Natori, Miyagi, and was among the first to use a computerised brewing system.[8] Shizuoka Brewery in Yaizu was opened in 1980,[9] Chiba Brewery in Chiba was opened in 1988,[10] Hokkaido Brewery in Eniwa, Hokkaidō was built in 1989,[11] and the Kyushu Hita Brewery was opened in Hita, Ōita in 2000.[12]
