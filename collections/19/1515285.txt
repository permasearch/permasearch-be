On May 31, 2011, HarperCollins released Through My Eyes, an autobiography that Tebow co-wrote with author Nathan Whitaker. Tebow details his early life growing up in Jacksonville and the Philippines, as well as his college football experiences.[162] By March 4, 2012, it had spent 24 weeks on the New York Times best seller list.[163] It was named the #1 sports book of 2011 [164] and the best selling religion book of 2011.[165] Tim followed his memoir with a young readers’ edition titled Through My Eyes: A Quarterback’s Journey, also co-written with Whitaker.[166]
