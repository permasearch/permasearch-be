Stratford Emergency Medical Service (SEMS) is the state-licensed, advanced life support (paramedic) and basic life support, municipal EMS provider to the Town of Stratford. Founded in 1977, it provides emergency medical response and care using both paid staff and volunteers. It also has an operational Medical Bike Unit, a Special Operations Division and an honor guard. SEMS is an independent department within the Town of Stratford and is not associated with the fire department. The Stratford Fire Department is the basic life support first responder for SEMS and the town.
