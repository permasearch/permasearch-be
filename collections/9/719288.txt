The Two E-com Center is the second phase of the four BPO towers in the Mall of Asia complex. It has a gross floor area of 107,862 square meters and consists of 2 towers - 15 story office building with retail areas at Ground and 4th Floor Level. The parking areas are located at the 2nd and 3rd-floor level. The TwoE-com Center is designed by the firm Arquitectonica.[20]
