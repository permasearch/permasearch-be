So You Think You Can Dance is an Indian Hindi competitive dance television series, which went on air on 24 April 2016 and is broadcast on &TV. The series is produced by Deepak Dhar, CEO of Endemol Shine India. The series is aired on every Saturday and Sunday nights. The show is an officially incensed version of the So You Think You Can Dance franchise, based on the original American production created by Dick Clark Productions. The auditions of the show commenced in the second half of February 2016. The show is being hosted by Rithvik Dhanjani and Mouni Roy, with a jury of Madhuri Dixit, Terence Lewis, and Bosco Martis.
