On January 26, 2017, it was reported that Verizon Communications was in talks with Charter to discuss a possible buyout.[40][41] President and CEO of Liberty Media, Greg Maffei said that they were not interested in the deal.[42][43] The deal was rejected around the end of May 2017.[44] Charter claimed that the deal was too low for them to accept, and Charter's largest shareholder Liberty Media stated that they weren't ready to sell.
