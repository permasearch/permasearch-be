Patrick began her stock car racing career by entering an ARCA Racing Series race in the No. 7 JR Motorsports Chevrolet at Daytona International Speedway. She finished in sixth place after spinning early in the race.[75][76] At the season-opening Nationwide Series race, the DRIVE4COPD 300, Patrick started 15th and finished 35th after being caught up in a 12-car crash.[77] In the season's third race, the Sam's Town 300 at Las Vegas Motor Speedway, she finished 36th after colliding with Michael McDowell on the 82nd lap.[78] Although Patrick struggled during her rookie season,[79] she had her best finish of the year at the season-ending Ford 300 at Homestead-Miami Speedway where she came 19th.[80] She finished 43rd in the drivers' standings, with 1,032 points in 13 starts.[81] In September, Patrick entered the K&N Pro Series East race at Dover International Speedway to broaden her stock car racing experience.[79] She finished sixth after briefly leading the race.[82]
