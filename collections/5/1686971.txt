In Sweden, Cascada's cover of "What Hurts the Most" debuted on the Swedish Singles Chart on November 22, 2007 at number five, where it peaked.[26] The song fell to number eight the next week, where it stalled on the chart for three weeks.[26] On December 13, 2007, the song debuted and peaked on the Irish Singles Chart at number six.[27] In the United Kingdom, the song debuted on the UK Singles Chart at number sixteen on December 9, 2007.[28] In the following week, the song reached a peak of number ten.[28] It exited the chart at nineteen weeks, logging its last week at number eighty-five.[28] On March 22, 2008, the cover debuted and peaked on the French Singles Chart at number two. The song fell to number four in the following weeks and eventually fell off of the chart after twenty-seven weeks.
