Minnette is the singer and rhythm guitarist in the band Wallows. Before 2017 they were known as The Feaver and then The Narwhals. They performed at "Summer Meltdown", a concert for autism awareness in 2010[7] and won a battle of the bands contest the same year, sponsored by 98.7 FM.[8] They played at Vans Warped Tour 2011[9] and have since performed at many Los Angeles venues including the Roxy Theatre and Whisky a Go Go.[10] Their song Bleeding Man was used in the promo for season 2 of R.L. Stine's The Haunting Hour.
