Common side effects include hair loss, bone marrow suppression, numbness, allergic reactions, muscle pains, and diarrhea.[2] Other serious side effects include heart problems, increased risk of infection, and lung inflammation.[2] There are concerns that use during pregnancy may cause birth defects.[3][2] Paclitaxel is in the taxane family of medications.[4] It works by interference with the normal function of microtubules during cell division.[2]
