Grande was featured on Cashmere Cat's song "Adore", which was released in March during her Honeymoon Tour; a review in USA Today said: "Grande's towering vocals have never sounded better than on this offbeat R&B track."[113] Also in 2015, Grande guest-starred on several episodes of the Fox comedy-horror TV series Scream Queens as Chanel#2.[114][115] Grande filmed an episode for the Fox TV reality series Knock Knock Live, but the show was canceled before her episode aired.[116][117]
