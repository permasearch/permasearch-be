Kyla graduated from Philippine Christian University with a bachelor's degree in mass communication. Her father is a lawyer and, she has three siblings. As a songwriter, she is known for writing and singing female-empowerment themed compositions taken from personal experiences, gospel, and inspirational music such as her acclaimed songs "Always on Time" and "I'm All Yours". Kyla interpreted "Be My Everything", originally written by Jessa Mae Gabon during A Song of Praise (ASOP) Music Festival 2012. She also sang "His Eye is on the Sparrow" at a benefit concert[48]
