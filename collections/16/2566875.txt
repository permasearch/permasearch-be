On December 14, 2017, Delta announced a 100 firm and 100 option order for the Airbus A321neo aircraft with deliveries beginning in 2020 and continuing through 2023. The aircraft will be powered by Pratt & Whitney PW1100G geared-turbofan engines, and will seat 197 passengers. The aircraft will replace ageing Airbus A320s, Boeing 757-200s and MD-90s.[25][26]
