Sales of the Pandora brand began in Europe and it first entered North America in 2003. The company opened concept stores around the world before its franchising model began in Australia in 2009.[5] Pandora products are sold in more than 100 countries on six continents through approximately 7,700 points of sale, including approximately 2,200 concept stores.[7] The company employs over 22,300 people, of whom 13,200 are located in Gemopolis, Thailand, the company's sole manufacturing site since 1989.[8]
