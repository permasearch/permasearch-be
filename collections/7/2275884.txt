In 2007, he starred in The Invisible, a remake of the Swedish film Den Osynlige, which marked his first lead role.[6] The following year, he played a supporting role in Middle of Nowhere, a coming-of-age drama directed by John Stockwell, also starring Anton Yelchin and Susan Sarandon.[7] In 2009, Chatwin portrayed Goku in Dragonball Evolution, a big screen adaptation based on the Japanese Dragon Ball manga by Akira Toriyama.[8] He then returned to Canada to shoot Funkytown, alongside Patrick Huard and Paul Doucet.[9]
