The .375 Flanged Magnum (9.5×75mmR), also known as the .375 H&H Flanged Magnum but should not be mistaken for the older .375 Flanged Nitro Express,the .375 Flanged Magnum is the companion cartridge to the .375 H&H Magnum for use in double and single shot rifles and was released together with the .375 H&H Magnum by Holland & Holland. It is considered the flanged (rimmed) version of the .375 H&H Magnum. The flanged cartridge is loaded to a lower pressure of 3,250 bar (47,100 psi) in comparison to the .375H&H Magnum.
