On August 13, 1991, the City of Los Angeles declared the hotel building Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monument #545.[5][9] In 1995, the hotel was purchased from Clarion Hotels by Goodwin Gaw, with David Chang later becoming co-owner.[7][8][10] In 2005, the hotel's management was taken over by the Thompson Hotel Group. A $30 million renovation of the hotel was embarked upon in 2005, led by the Dodd Mitchell Design Group, and David Siguaw.[10][11][12] Since 2015, the hotel has been run independently by its own management company.[10] In 2015, the hotel completed a $25 million renovation with rooms designed by Yabu Pushelberg, and plans for a new poolside food and beverage outlet.[7]
