John N. Bahcall (BS 1956) worked on the Standard Solar Model and the Hubble Space Telescope,[243] resulting in a National Medal of Science.[243] Peter Smith (BS 1969) was the principal investigator and project leader for the NASA robotic explorer Phoenix,[244] which physically confirmed the presence of water on the planet Mars for the first time.[245] Astronauts James van Hoften (BS 1966), Margaret Rhea Seddon (BA 1970), Leroy Chiao (BS 1983), and Rex Walheim (BS 1984) have orbited the earth in NASA's fleet of space shuttles.
