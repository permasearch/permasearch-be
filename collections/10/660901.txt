The club has won the La Liga championship 33 times, the Copa del Rey 19 times, the Copa de la Liga once, the Supercopa de España 10 times (including Copa Eva Duarte), the European Cup twelve times, the UEFA Cup twice, the European Super Cup four times, the Intercontinental Cup three times and the FIFA Club World Cup twice.[7] The table details the club's achievements in the early regional championships and in all national and international first-team competitions for each completed season since the club's formation in 1902. The club has won at least a trophy for seventeen consecutive seasons (from 1953–54 to 1969–70), a record in Spanish football.
