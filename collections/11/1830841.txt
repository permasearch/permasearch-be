In the same month, Tough Mudder also announced Lucozade, as the company’s Official Sports Drink Sponsor of Tough Mudder's UK events, advertising the partnership on 50 million of its bottles.[119] Jeep also announced as part of its current deal, that they will be producing 100 limited edition Renegade Tough Mudder vehicles for sale only in the UK.[120][121] Also in the UK, Head & Shoulders became the official hair care partner for Tough Mudder’s 2017 UK events. As part of the deal, Tough Mudder will place the shampoo’s company’s branding on its Augustus Gloop obstacle, create original content together, and offer participants to clean up with Head & Shoulders products at shower stations after the events.[122][123][124]
