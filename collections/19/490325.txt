Heavy rain from the south-west summer monsoon falls between June and September,[52] supplying Hyderabad with most of its mean annual rainfall.[51] Since records began in November 1891, the heaviest rainfall recorded in a 24-hour period was 241.5 mm (10 in) on 24 August 2000. The highest temperature ever recorded was 45.5 °C (114 °F) on 2 June 1966, and the lowest was 6.1 °C (43 °F) on 8 January 1946.[53] The city receives 2,731 hours of sunshine per year; maximum daily sunlight exposure occurs in February.[52][54]
