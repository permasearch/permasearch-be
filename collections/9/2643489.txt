Housemates have to cycle on a stationary rickshaw and speak about the various prominent places in Telugu state of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana and elucidate about their cultures, customs. The rickshaw will travel over Vizag, Adilabad, Kurnool, Warangal and Hyderabad. The contestants will have to dance the local cultural dance of the cities they are passing through and also perform skits. The journey would culminate in Hyderabad. The contestants won the luxury budget task and treated with Hyderabadi Biryani.
