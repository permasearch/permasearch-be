There were 235 renunciants in 2008,[27] between 731 and 743 in 2009, and about 1485 in 2010;[28][29] In 2011, there were 1781 renunciants.[30] A total of 2,999 Americans renounced their citizenship in 2012-2013;[31] in 2014, 3415 have renounced their USA citizenship or long term residency.[32] The State Department estimates 5986 renunciants and 559 relinquishers during FY2015.[33]
