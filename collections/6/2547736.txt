"Battle Scars" received an ARIA Music Award nomination for Best Pop Release, and Sebastian was nominated as Best Male Artist for the song.[11] "Battle Scars" won Song of 2012 in the POPreplublic.tv. IT List Awards. The nominees are chosen by POPreplublic, with public vote deciding the winner.[55] The song was also nominated for a 2013 NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Duo, Group or Collaboration.[20] The Awards are presented by the American National Association for the Advancement of Colored People. "Battle Scars" won the R&B/Hip category in the 2013 International Songwriting Competition.[21] The judging panel is made up of musicians, songwriters and industry experts, and songs are judged on creativity, originality, lyrics, melody, arrangement and overall likeability.[56]
