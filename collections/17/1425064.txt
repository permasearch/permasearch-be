Gerrard played his final game for Galaxy on 6 November 2016 in a MLS Cup Playoff penalty-shootout loss to Colorado Rapids. Gerrard took and scored Los Angeles' first spot-kick but the club were eliminated after Giovanni dos Santos and Ashley Cole failed to convert.[123] Nine days later, he stated he would be leaving on the expiration of his contract.[124] He then had an interview for the vacant managerial post at League One club Milton Keynes Dons, but said that he was not ready for the job.[125] Gerrard announced his retirement from professional football on 24 November, at age 36.[126]
