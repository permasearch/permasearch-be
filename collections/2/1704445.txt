Glen Rose is located at 32°14′12″N 97°45′14″W﻿ / ﻿32.23667°N 97.75389°W﻿ / 32.23667; -97.75389 (32.236637, -97.753823),[11] around the junction of U.S. Highway 67 and State Highway 144 in central Somervell County. The city is situated nearly 17 miles (27 km) south of Granbury and 52 miles (84 km) southwest of Fort Worth.[8]  According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 2.7 square miles (7.1 km²), all land.
