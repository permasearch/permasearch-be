Ford acquired the British sports car maker Aston Martin in 1989, later selling it on March 12, 2007,[105] although retaining an 8% stake.[106][107] Ford purchased Volvo Cars of Sweden in 1999,[108] selling it to Zhejiang Geely Holding Group in 2010. In November 2008, it reduced its 33.4% controlling interest in Mazda of Japan to a 13.4% non-controlling interest.[109]
