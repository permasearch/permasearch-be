Three video game adaptations have been made for the franchise. The first is The Irregular at Magic High School: Out of Order by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is a 3D fighting game for the PlayStation Vita and was released on December 25, 2014.[105] The second game is The Irregular at Magic High School: School Magicus Battle[Jp. 48], a Japanese role-playing game by Mobage. It was made available for Android, iOS, and feature phone on June 9, 2014.[106] The third game is The Irregular at Magic High School: Lost Zero, developed by BeXide and published by Square Enix. It was made available for the Android and iOS on September 4, 2014.[107]
