Pelé, regarded by many football historians, former players and fans to be the best footballer in the game's history,[88] is the ambassador of the Copa Libertadores, having won the competition with Santos twice.[89] In 1999, he was voted as the Football Player of the Century by the IFFHS International Federation of Football History and Statistics. In the same year, French weekly magazine France-Football consulted their former "Ballon D'Or" winners to elect the Football Player of the Century. Pelé came in first place.[90] In 1999 the International Olympic Committee named Pelé the "Athlete of the Century".[91]
