On May 30, 2007, he asked to be released from the role, potentially in preparation for a presidential bid.[8] Due to concerns about the equal-time rule, reruns featuring the Branch character were not shown on NBC while Thompson was a potential or actual presidential candidate, but TNT episodes were unaffected.[40]
