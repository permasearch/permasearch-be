Filipino singer Charice Pempengco recorded a version of the song on her debut release, the self-titled Charice EP released in May 2008.[57] Korean singer SunMin has a cover of the song on her mini album Cover Girl, released in Japan, August 2008.[58] The Italian variety TV show, Ti Lascio Una Canzone alumna Sonia Mosca covered the song on the compilation album Ti Lascio Una Canzone - La Compilation, released in May 2009.[59][60] In 2012, the song is performed on the TV musical series Glee in episode 17 of season 3, "Dance with Somebody", by Chris Colfer (as his character Kurt Hummel). The whole episode pays tribute to Whitney Houston and her music.[61]
