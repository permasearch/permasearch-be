Eric Bolling, who had been a show regular since the series' inception, announced on April 19, 2017 that he would be leaving to start work on a new afternoon talk show, The Fox News Specialists, which aired during The Five's former 5 p.m. time slot.[31] Though it had no impact on The Five, Bolling was suspended from Fox News in August 2017 over sexual-harassment allegations, and later fired.[32] The Fox News Specialists was also canceled.[32]
