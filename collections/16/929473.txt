The song entered the Canadian Hot 100 at number 58, for the week dated December 8, 2012.[43] It re-entered the chart for the week of February 23, 2013 at number 37. The following week, it leaped to number three,[44] where it remained for a further week before climbing to number two for the week of March 16, 2013.[45][46] The following week, "Stay" reached number one, becoming Rihanna's eighth single to top the chart, and second consecutive following "Diamonds".[47][48] It remained number one for three consecutive weeks.[49][50]
