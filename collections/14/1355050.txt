Trans World Airlines flew the last scheduled 707 flight for passengers by a US carrier on October 30, 1983,[28] although 707s remained in scheduled service by airlines from other nations for much longer. Middle East Airlines  of Lebanon flew 707s and 720s in front-line passenger service until the end of the 1990s. Since LADE of Argentina took its 707-320Bs from regular service in 2007, Saha Airlines of Iran was the last airline to keep 707s in scheduled passenger service until April 2013.[29]
