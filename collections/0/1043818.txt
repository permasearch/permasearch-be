On March 2, 2011, Abby surprises her mother when she returns to Salem and expresses her dismay for her father Jack's absence.[17] She tries to talk Jennifer into moving on without Jack and encourages her to pursue a relationship with Dr. Daniel Jonas, the surgeon who saved her life.[18] Abby is instantly attracted to Stefano DiMera's youngest son Chad DiMera and realizes she should keep her distance.[19] Chad comforts Abby when she learns that her mother is divorcing her father and admits she wishes her parents were still together.[20] They soon begin dating, but Chad's step-mother, Kate DiMera starts plotting to break them up, but that plan quickly falls apart when Chad gets shot and 'disowns' his heritage.
