Elsa's performance of "Let It Go" became the central feature in Disney California Adventure's Winter Dreams,[73] a 30-minute, winter-themed adaption of the nighttime show World of Color, which showcases scenes from Disney films.[74] Disneyland Paris' nighttime spectacular, Disney Dreams!, also added Elsa's performance of "Let It Go" to their attractions,[75] and she was given a similar role during the Magic Kingdom show, Celebrate the Magic, with her singing interspersed with scenes from the movie.[76]
