After participating the 2011 AFC Asian Cup, India's quest to qualify for the 2015 edition of the tournament began in February 2011 with AFC Challenge Cup qualifiers. Bob Houghton decided to change the makeup of the India squad, replacing many of the aging players from the Asian Cup with some young players from the AIFF development side in the I-League, Indian Arrows.[44] Even with a young side, India managed to qualify for the AFC Challenge Cup with ease.[45] Despite the good result though with a young side, the AIFF decided to terminate the contract of Bob Houghton.[46]
