The first season debuted on January 16, 2015 on Netflix, when the first five episodes were released, with further episodes released in May and September 2015.[1][2][3] A second season was released on December 11, 2015.[4] The third season was released on July 15, 2016.[5] The fourth season was released on December 16, 2016.[6] The fifth season was released on July 28, 2017.[7] The sixth season is set to be released on January 26, 2018.[8]
