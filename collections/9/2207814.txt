This is a list of products manufactured by The Hershey Company. Some of these products began production over 100 years ago such as the Hershey Kiss and Hershey Bar. Hershey produces a variety of products that are chocolate or candy based, and The Hershey Company also produces gum. This list excludes licensed items such as Ice Cream and Chocolate Milk, which are made by Breyers, Good Humor, Klondike, and Natrel. The main markets are the Continental North America.
