Johnson became known for reinvigorating film franchises after portraying Roadblock in G.I. Joe: Retaliation (2013) and reprising his role as Luke Hobbs in Fast & Furious 6 (2013).[239][240] He also starred in true-story films Pain & Gain (2013) and Empire State (2013). That same year, he hosted and produced the TNT reality competition series The Hero.[241][242] He won the Favorite Male Buttkicker Award at the 2013 Nickelodeon Kid's Choice Awards.[243] In May 2013, it was announced that he would executive produce and star in Ballers,[244] an HBO comedy-drama series about NFL players living in Miami, Florida.[245]
