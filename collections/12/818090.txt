In October 2013, Sia released "Elastic Heart" featuring The Weeknd and Diplo for the soundtrack of the American film The Hunger Games: Catching Fire (2013).[59] Sia executive produced Brooke Candy's debut EP, Opulence, released in May 2014, and co-wrote 3 songs on the EP.[60] In July 2014, Sia released her own sixth studio album, 1000 Forms of Fear.[61] She again collaborated with Greg Kurstin.[46] The album debuted at No. 1 in the US Billboard 200 with first-week sales of 52,000 copies.[62] By October 2015, it was certified gold by the RIAA denoting 500,000 equivalent-album units sold in the United States.[63] The record peaked at No. 1 in Australia and reached the top ten of charts in numerous European regions.[64] It was certified silver by the British Phonographic Industry and gold by the Australian Recording Industry Association.[65] By early 2016, the album had sold 1 million copies worldwide.[66]
