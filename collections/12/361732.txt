The Kuroko's Basketball manga had sold nine million units in Japan by September 2012.[36] As of 2013, the manga had sold over 23 million copies.[37] This number grew to 27 million by April 2014.[6] Individual volumes frequently appeared on the lists of best-selling manga in Japan,[38][39] and many editions have been in the 2012 Top-Selling Manga.[40][41][42] Kuroko's Basketball was the third highest selling manga series of 2013, with 8,761,081 copies sold in a year.[43] DVD sales of the anime series have also been featured in the Japanese anime DVD ranking various times.[44][45][46]
