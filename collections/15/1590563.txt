Pasay City is served by several highways and major thoroughfares. Epifanio delos Santos Avenue (EDSA), Roxas Boulevard, Gil Puyat Avenue (Buendia Avenue) and Taft Avenue function as the city's main thoroughfares. Secondary thoroughfares include Andrews Avenue, Antonio Arnaiz Avenue (formerly called Libertad in the Pasay segment), Diosdado Macapagal Boulevard, Ninoy Aquino Avenue and NAIA Road (MIA Road).
