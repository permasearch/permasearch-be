The majority of content appearing in the Daily Mail and Mail on Sunday printed newspapers also forms part of that included in the MailOnline website. MailOnline is free to read and funded by advertising. In 2011 MailOnline was the second most visited English-language newspaper website worldwide.[161][162] It has since then become the most visited newspaper website in the world,[163] with over 189.5 million visitors per month, and 11.7 million visitors daily, as of January 2014.[164]
